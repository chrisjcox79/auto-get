Depravity and Indifference

30 Dec 2019 00:07

Hello there! This is a rewrite of SCP-138-ARC, as administered by the Rewrite Team. This is obviously quite a bit different than the original article, but should still be generally in-line with the canon established in the tale  by Dr Gears.

Shout out to whoever looked this over in chat - it's been a while since I got crit on this so I can't remember everyone involved, but if you were I appreciate it, as always. Also special thanks to Modern_Erasmus with once again trusting me with a rewrite.

Lot going on here, and before anyone starts asking about this article's similarities to popular anime franchises yes, there are distinct inspirations there and yes, they were intentional. I would hope there is enough going on elsewhere in this article that it is thoroughly distinct from any other licensed properties.

Thanks again, and I hope you enjoy it!
