+explanations

-F-ALL

-Authorities were closing in on 67 so she was attempting to assume a new identity in a very thorough and novel way. She had some records destroyed as prep-work, to lessen the required amount of reality bending. But she prepared too many contingencies so that when the effect was finally triggered all the mystical identity thefts happened together instead of just the one she intended. Should have consulted a better alchemist.

-81 was researching the practical applications of the many-worlds theory of quantum mechanics. But CI experimental protocols are much laxer than at the Foundation. There was an unexpected cross interaction between the multiple anomalies being used in her tests, causing a macro-scale collapse of multiple quantum wave-form potentialities into a single gestalt actuality, with the momentary effective absolute observer as the coalescence point. Things blew up and her parallel selves now share a body; think of a cross between 

-127 is a truly gifted spirit, greater than any other still trapped in the karmic cycle. Her advanced enlightenment and higher plane of spiritual energy was too much for a single reincarnation. But her soul was too pure and the Great Spirits could not bear to shatter it. So in their wisdom, she became the many-in-one.

-154 was a cantankerous, stubborn, spiteful, cruel, leather-souled, poor excuse for a human being. She died and went to the hell she deserved. But Satan couldn't stand her any more than her four ex-husbands could, and heaven surely wasn't going to take her. So she climbed up out of her grave. Fate was insulted and scandalized by this blatant disrespect for his perfect pattern, and so settled on an ironic punishment. 154 wanted another life? Well, Fate would see to it that she got a lot more than she could handle.

-Omega option. All these at once, each in its own parallel world.

I'll have to see if I can come up with something for the other cases. This is fun, thanks!

Oh, and it's also novel and well-written.

The warm glow of your approval has inspired me to complete the set.

-14 and 15 never really got along. Sibling rivalry between twin sisters can be a blood sport But that didn't matter to the man the papers dubbed  seemingly a new one every day. They had nothing at all to say when the records and valid birth certificates for each of them.

-19 was not adjusting well to her new situation. She wasn't exactly depressed, which is common for new double amputees, she was just no longer engaged in the world. She spent hours alone in her room, just imagining what her life would be like if she had turned left instead of right. Or took the bus instead of driving. Or had gotten her brakes looked at like the manual said. Eventually she was constructing elaborate fantasy lives, each rooted in just a single change to the circumstances of her past. Sometimes, her imaginings were so detailed that she would forget herself entirely, just wandering in her could-have-been selves. Until one day her fantasy was so believable that everyone else, the whole world, reality itself believed it as well.
