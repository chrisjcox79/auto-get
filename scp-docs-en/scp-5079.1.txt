So, quarantine really is getting to me if it’s not clear from this article. Hopefully you’re all keeping mentally healthy (as best as we can manage in isolation).

Honestly, taking part in helping expand this wiki has been a welcome distraction and creative outlet desperately needed.

►◄

▼▼

The high levels of oxytocin and melatonin affect the instances in that they are often fatigued, and content. They feel no need to reach out for that connection anymore, or no ambition to join with others.

Bell is as much alone as anyone that is affected by SCP-XXXX, as is clear from his condition upon discovery. He intended this ritual as a way to connect together with others into a hive mind, thinking that if he could spread the ritual wide on the internet, then he would have forced a community to materialize and he could finally connect with someone. But he screwed up, the ritual wasn’t meant to be used like that, and the people are dying. Without joining together physically, there is no hive mind just a vague empathetic sense of the others. So long as the Foundation is keeping them separate, then the hive-being won’t be formed.

In-depth Crit: MalyceGraves
