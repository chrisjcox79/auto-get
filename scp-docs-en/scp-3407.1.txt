04 Jun 2018 21:34

I'm glad you enjoyed it! During one of the early drafts, I wrote the age of the girl in projection 4. Someone pointed out to me, however, that the foundation wouldn't know the exact age of the girl just looking at her, so I replaced it with her height instead. I didn't want the foundation to look like they're making assumptions, so I stuck with her height to allow the girl's age to be up to reader's interpretation.
