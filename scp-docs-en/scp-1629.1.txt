14 May 2014 02:26

A bit off base. The Locked Box Test is a good rule of thumb, but can't apply to everything.

There are two ways something is considered Safe. The first is the obvious one: if it only does something when you metaphorically poke it, it's Safe.

The other way something is Safe is predictability. If you can predict what will happen when it does X 100% of the time, and if the Containment Procedures give a 100% chance of keeping it secure so long as they are followed, it's Safe.
