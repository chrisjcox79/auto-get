Tubular to the max, Autobots!

15 Mar 2020 18:53

 was one of the first thoughts I had when the third theme (More Than Meets the Eye) was announced for the second 144 Hour Jamcon, but I wasn't going to do it until I watched some transformers commercials and got the idea for that secondary effect.

If there's any 80s slang you feel like I missed out on, please let me know in the comments here. This was originally gonna have a longer ending, and if I decide to expand it after the voting period for Jamcon is over it would be great to have a little repository of dumb words to pull from. Plus, I love these words and they make me giggle.

Thanks to Omino for bouncing ideas with me, giving me feedback some snippets of my first draft and putting up with me leaving the light on until 4.30AM to get this done while we shared a room. Love you 3.
