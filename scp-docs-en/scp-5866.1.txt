I really like the interactions between Brenda and Tiamat, but the in universe commentary between the two scientists needs to be redone. It starts flat and is only hurt more by how much time you spend lampshading your own esoteric class. The payoff that each argument makes the goddess more real is only made less satisfying as a result, especially because those arguments keep interrupting the interviews. You should refocus their debate on some aspect of 5866 that actually feels worth arguing about and reinforcing in universe; it would make the jailbreak more cathartic and could even give me something to remember about Brenda’s colleague.
