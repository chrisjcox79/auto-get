27 Jun 2017 04:53

Yeah, I rarely have the patience to write long articles. 3003 took me weeks, even after I'd been brainstorming about it on and off for years.

When writing this, I had two choices if I wanted to expand it: show the champagne working without a hitch, or show it having a problem. The first seemed boring and unnecessary D risk losing clients and reputation by putting out a malfunctioning product, and why can nothing in the Foundationverse work as advertised?

Ultimately, I felt like focusing too much on the effects was a dead end narratively, so I redirected my efforts to telling a story about MCD and the Foundation, using the champagne as a reference point. And, well, that one just didn't take many words to write.

If it makes you feel better, with this and SCP-3113 and maybe others, I'm laying the groundwork for a story. I don't know what that story is, or if I'll ever write it, but this might not have to stand on its own forever.

And MCD, knowing that they could use the jawbone to move and set up shop somewhere else, didn't feel like bribing the Foundation enough to look the other way about this stuff indefinitely.
