Actually, I am the one that started the collapsible hate, and he has simply repeated my points.

Collapsibles break flow and tone.
Collapsibles prevent pages from being printed properly.
Collapsibles prevent pages from being converted into PDFs.

If it's only a few lines, a collapsible isn't necessary in the first place.

If it's long enough to merit a collapsible, a supplementary page would exert no more stress than it does now (seeing as there's no images or anything else to bloat the site's footprint), reduce the load on initial render, and be easier to index.
