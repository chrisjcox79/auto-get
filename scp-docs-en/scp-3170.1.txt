I was hoping to see some negative/neutral discussion of this scip, because I can't see why its rating would be so low. I guess there's just not enough here for some folks? The only issue I found in the article was here:

Exhuming her body showed that its stomach had been stuffed with approximately 150 meters of Super 8mm footage of SCP-3170; portions of the film had been replaced with corneal tissue matching that of SCP-3170.
附加记录：    

It wasn't entirely clear to me why they'd exhume and inspect her body. Other than that, I think this is well-written, and probably just needed more content to hook people in and get us to care more about the anomalous actress.
