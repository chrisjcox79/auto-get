One thing I noticed...

28 May 2020 18:13

There are a lot of  that get used in various ways, and sometimes it's just to be more colloquial, but sometimes it's not clear on whether you're actually trying to quote someone. This one's doing fine, but maybe something to give some consideration to for improvement down the road. Look at each of those quoted passages and decide what it's really doing and contributing, and if it could be done in a way that doesn't use a source of uncertainty like quotes.

Re: One thing I noticed...

29 May 2020 03:57

One of the things I picked up on when analysing the original SCP-106 in preparation for this was an almost gratuitous use of  not so much (which I thought was a bit funny in itself)).

So in turn, I peppered them into this piece - perhaps gratuitously. Some of them may be subversions of lines from the original article, some of them may be that line of thinking extending into the rest of the writing.

I will give some thought as to whether all of them necessarily contribute to a gag or punchline, or maybe could stand to be replaced with more typical/clinical wording. Thank you for your comment!
