With that picture I wanted to like this, but the tone and dialogue failed me too many times for me to like this.

Here are gripes:

+block

–block

The containment procedures are too long for such an easily containable guys, and are a slog to get through.
Tone breaks at multiple intervals, saying vague or unprofessional things like “is knowledgeable on several subjects, especially those of a scientific nature,” or “it outright refuses to answer.”
You use an IQ test for intelligence, which is a pet peeve of mine. IQ tests really aren’t conducive to intelligence.
The skeleton (almost literally) says “good sir” every other sentence and if it was supposed to be humorous I’d like it but this is supposed to be historically accurate? It really put me off
Dialogue problems:

can you explain what are you?
附加记录：    

perhaps anunique specimen
附加记录：    

I shall satiate you thrist for information.
附加记录：    

Conceptually it’s a skeleton researcher who makes cool gadgets. Not incredibly interesting.

This article isn’t low-quality, but still fails to work for me.
