Ostensibly this frequency resonates with quarks or alters gluons so that they are no longer bound by or convey the strong force, without which matter falls apart at a fundamental level (i.e. disintegrates into lone quarks).

The resulting energy release has a similar effect on other quarks/gluons, sort of like tin pest. Which means this could easily destroy the Earth/solar system/universe as we know it, depending on the limits of the chain reaction.

At least that's how I interpret it.
