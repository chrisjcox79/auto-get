30 Jul 2017 02:21

叔母ちゃんワありがとう
附加记录：    

Actually, '叔母ちゃん、ありがとう” will do. When 'wa' is used as a particle, it is written as ’は’,1 instead of 'わ/ワ’. I don't think a particle is needed since the SCP is directly addressing the agent.

Truth be told, the reason why I suggested the parent and child thing was that the Japanese words reminded me of what I imagine a Japanese toddler's (who has not fully grasped formal speech) dialogue would be like.

Footnotes
1. It's still pronounced as 'wa'.
