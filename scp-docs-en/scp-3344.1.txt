Yeah, this was a risk I was aware of when the idea first struck me. The consensus I got in chat was that it could still stand on its own, as the effect was similar but not identical to other SCPs, and the format gave it a very different angle. It's a bit simplistic as far as where I took the core idea, and even in my own eyes it does feel weak in that regard. Unfortunately I'm rarely a strong enough writer to turn my ideas into something as impactful as I'd like them to be.

I've never linked 1048 to any of my other works, but most of my other skips are linked, and this felt like an okay place for a light hearted call back to my first article, as well as turning Dr. Carver into something slightly less one dimensional. It also shows that SCPs can be affected, and implies that SCP-3344 has been over every inch of the site in his search for an answer.
