#Team Sugary Sweet

09 Sep 2018 03:44

A big, big thank you to keyii for co-authoring this with me, helping me with 3744's character and whatnot. Team Sugary Sweet for life! #diabetesisaseriousproblem

So, I was really, really unhappy with the reception of the previous SCP-3744, and I wanted to make amends. I didn't feel like this SCP was worth your time. So hopefully I've fixed that right up.

The following are a bunch of folks who gave crits to this SCP: Alces_alces. You are all fine lads!

The image was taken by Sean Ellis, and you can find a link to it here: https://www.flickr.com/photos/s_w_ellis/3607970602/in/photostream/

Hello! I'm Keyii  Jan contacted me about doing a collaboration after deciding that he disliked the previous SCP-3744. This is the third work that I've had a hand in on this site, following Grin and Bear It.

My part in the collab was mostly regarding characterization in the interview and a complete rehaul of the session log. It is now very different from its original incarnation, and hopefully in a good way! Thank you so much to everyone who critiqued this in chat, and I hope you enjoyed reading this.
