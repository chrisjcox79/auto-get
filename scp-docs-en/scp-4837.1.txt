While you have certainly made the character more interesting since I read this in the original draft, there really isn't very much here. I said it once before, but I wanted a reason to care about this character. You two did better at explaining the character's background, but with the interview log it felt more as though it was just shotgunning details about the duck instead of building it up throughout the article.

The ending felt tacked on; why not develop more of a background on the  in different addenda? I get it that this duck wants to keep with the times, but why would the Foundation even consider trusting it with technology? How does this thing even know what a Google Pixel is when it is supposedly a medieval being?
