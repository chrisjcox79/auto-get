02 Jun 2019 21:29

Spoilers!

Woweeursefl

So, yeah. I banged this out in around half an hour last night after watching a video by Sam O'Nella Academy] dealing with the history of animals in space. As an aside, he mentions the fact that Big Bird was almost going to be sent into space, suit and all, as a publicity stunt. NASA found out that they couldn't fit him into the shuttle, so they went with a teacher instead.

And then the Challenger exploded.

The title of this comes from a quote from that video.

The SCP number is, as of writing, the number of episodes of Sesame Street that have aired.

Thanks to everyone who looked this over on chat, including ARD, AKFrost, LordOfLaugh, Fitnessgram, Crashington, and Grimmcreeper, for making sure this wasn't a complete coldpost.

Can't believe this is the first thing that I've posted in months, though.
