Explanations

25 Nov 2012 13:18

I have put an inordinate amount of thought into how this power works.

You are exactly right about the whale thing. I went with a cow as it seemed a more reasonable test subject, but he could drag around a blue whale just as easily.
Not eat so much as to have it inside him. A bit of an unfair loophole, but I was deadset to make this guy chomp some Shias.
Glad the accidental cannibal thing came across, that was one of the few 'horror' elements I tried to get in. The interesting thing is, although he must've felt like a cannibal every time he ate meat, when he actually did cannibalize someone, he wouldn't have know, as he didn't recognise the taste.
I worry the mushroom thing seems tacked on, but it links in to the [REDACTED]s as a bit of a pseudo-explanation for the effects.
All visualisations (good word, might sub it in for 'instances') have Shia's scars, tattoos, and so on. I hadn't though of make-up, but I'll say yes. The effect stops somewhere between a coat of mud and clothing.
A cut off worm arm would disappear in his mouth.
I was inspired by a song about Shia Labeouf. It was a bit of a meme a while ago, which is why I'm a bit uncomfortable. If anyone has a better suggestion for a person to take his place, send 'em at me.

Glad I managed to get you thinking!

Explanations for Redactions (Redaxplanations)

25 Nov 2012 13:45

Names, place names, and dates:  To prevent people getting in contact with this fellow's family. also, to dehumanise the SCP.

Atypical brain activity:  Part of this guy's brain is straight up gone, replaced with a mushroom. This is theorised to be what causes the effects, the Foundation would rather the low-level researchers not get too worked up about it. Everything about that has been redacted out of the article whilst the higher-ups take a closer look at it.

Why is this guy's brain a mushroom and what does that have to do with Shia LaBeouf? Dimensional portals.

Ooh, loved ones is a good idea. I'll definitely have a think about that. It's a little more horror than what I've got now, and I'm not quite sure if I've got the horror chops to pull it off, but definitely an excellent suggestion. Still open to others, though!

My original idea was that the guy saw himself as every animal, which would certainly be a mind-mongler. I turned to Shia, as I thought that that, although less traumatic, would mongle minds even more. Eating yourself or a loved one, you would ask  and could probably come up with an answer. When it's someone you've never even met, there's no answer, which is all the more monglery.

Dr Adams, the idea of killing the real Labeouf is so blaringly brilliant and frankly obvious that I'm amazed I never even thought of it. The only reason I'm not stealing it is that poor old 3-Black-Boxes would never be able to find the real one. it's like that one awful page in Where's Wally where he's surrounded by Wally clones.

Fridge Logic on the vegetarianism, but the sad truth is that I just considered poor old 3BB to be too dumb to survive in the forest through scavenging alone. He's a bit of a dolt, and the mind-mongling didn't help.

Mongle.

Edit:  azzleflux got in before me on both of Dr Adams's points. Death of the author is not so bad when you hit the nail right on the head!
