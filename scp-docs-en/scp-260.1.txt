I don't mind the shortness…honestly, some short articles are better than ones with five support documents and a ton of pictures. Killing-type SCP will always have a home here, but they do need to have some quality that makes them special.

The idea of a small stone slowly digging into a human body is very creepy, especially with it being slow. still, there are some questions: why not smash it? Why not try and just throw it away, or seal it in a box when it comes after you? just a little more information, and a edit for tone would go a long way.

Potential for greatness, but it needs some work yet.
