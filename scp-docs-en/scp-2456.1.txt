+block

–block

附加记录：    

Thou claimest 

But’s given the heart to its sole son

Is ? It ought to be but hast 

The stars and the Earth command to thy wishes.
附加记录：    

It seems the sense in the verb  is reversed here.

You alone are but their lifetime, a candle in the dark
附加记录：    

Why the switch to second-person plural pronouns here?

Thou cycles through the sky, sending visions from the heavens
附加记录：    

Thou cyclest 

Thou lives in the deepest drenches of the Great █████████ Sea
附加记录：    

Thou livest , though dwellest  is more likely in this construction. Is ?

Thou perished amongst the harlots; in the Fifth day of the solstice,

And the Fifth Year of the Summer Harvest, you suffered.
附加记录：    

You switch back from second-person singular (thou) to second-person plural (you) in the same sentence here. Also, though not incorrect, Thou didst perish  may be a more likely construction, as might thou didst suffer .

Thou shall live on to indulge the next world with thine divine antipathy!
