30 Aug 2017 14:52

I really liked this. Any skip that can provide an in-universe explanation for its existence gets a big boost toward a +1 from me, and this does so.

My only minor quibble is that it sounds like each one of these tricks could almost be considered a separate anomaly, as there's nothing that suggests they rely on the book itself to function. Ie; if somebody read the book, learned a trick, and demonstrated and taught it to somebody else who has never seen the book, does the anomaly not now exist entirely independently? Not saying they need their own SCP designation, but maybe number them as 3079-1, -2, etc.

Overall a +1 from me.
