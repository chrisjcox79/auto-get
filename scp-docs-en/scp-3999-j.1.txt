SCP-3999-J...

...Himself

Site-42 OFFICE INTERIOR, DAY

Director Lyceus and Researcher James Talloran stand in the office of the Chief of Security. The Chief gestures in frustration at the documents spread across his desk.

Folksy Narrator: Well, it looks James and the gang have done it again.

Chief: Surely you see there must have been undue expenditures-

Lyceus: Settle down Chief! This man is a hero.

Talloran grins modestly

Lyceus: Come with me, son. I know this must be a demanding assignment for you; I'd like to discuss some potential avenues of compensation for your dedicated spirit.

Talloran: Thank you sir. Truly it's an honour to serve this cause.

Lyceus and Talloran exit the office. The door closes behind them

The Chief shakes a fist in the air and bellows angrily

Chief: TALLORAAAAAAAAANNN!!!
