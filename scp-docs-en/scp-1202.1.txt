The one correction that would make this SCP readable...

11 Jan 2012 19:10

Indent your paragraphs in the  section of the SCP. Nobody, (not even the faction known as Nobody.)

wants to suffer through 'blocks' of text. When you indent at the beginning of a paragraph, you are giving the reader's brain a place to begin reading. Indentation also implies that a written piece has an air of professionalism, and is expected to be read as a story; rather than a text message from a friend.

On the other hand, kudos for correct spelling and punctuation.
