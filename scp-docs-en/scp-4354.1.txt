Hurray! My second solo article!

The idea for this one is almost as old as the first one. Only a few days younger.

This one is mostly about some body-horror and seeing if I can use expungements effectively, as they, in theory, always tickled me. The fact that this is expunged, not redacted, is very deliberate. I tried to make it clear what happens in the superbia event, but that's not the interesting part anyway. The interesting part is the  section. Because what do you do now?

As for -A treat is as worldbuilding, no need to pay them any attention yet.

Image and critique attribution is in the infobox.

Special thanks to stormbreath for making these awesome and actually functioning tables.

Novote

14 Aug 2019 15:04

I enjoy the idea behind the article and it is quite obvious what the author is trying to achieve with expungements. However, that section is a bit off. I suppose it just doesn't work for me:

Whyme

–block

I initially missed the implication of the SCP-4354-10 tattoo that the tattoos are creating the symbols that they represent, not the image itself. So initially I was like - ok, a Jesus-looking guy is born, not a big deal.
After realizing this implication, I am still not exactly worried. As far as I understand, Jesus is not an established character in the Foundation universe, so what we have is a reality bender and not a very powerful one at that. He can transmute and multiply matter, go through walls and levitate, but it is not like he can destroy the world in the matter of seconds or something. And even if he could, the symbols part comes in and Jesus is generally a benevolent character (as far as I know). So the worst that I could imagine is the Foundation having to kill Jesus, but that's not a big deal.
And if the Jesus is malevolent - the Foundation kinda knows how to contain reality benders and religious figures, so I am not sure why it is a big problem.

Overall, of course, it is a neat idea and execution, I am just not that enamoured with the expungements.

Myintent

–block

So, from how i intended this, this isnt about him being a reality bender, or potentially dangerous, but him being literally jedud, with all the memories and implications. This is the son of god and they had to do something with him right? A big part of foundation employees are probably Christian. How do you explain this to them? They canr just release him to the public, they canr just put him in a normal containment chamber. How would you explain that? So no matter what they did, they had to ensure that people dont know what it was.

Thats how I intended this to be interpreted. Cant guarantee what you take from it of course, but maybe this cleared it up a bit at least.
