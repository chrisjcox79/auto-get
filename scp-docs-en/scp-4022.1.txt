I also enjoyed the phrase, though I think it was repeated one too many times and started to lose its effect.

Ultimately, this is like a nice diet mix of Taboo's nomenclative hazard and Bush v Gore's Garber-speak. I think there is a conceptual inconsistency here; that SCP-4022 can be understood implies a minimal degree of syntactical and logical structure, which seemingly could be tGBNitMoHH.

But this is well written, well paced, and I feel that the angst you portrayed in trying to make as much sense with as little communicative structure as possible is a real horror to imagine, so +1.
