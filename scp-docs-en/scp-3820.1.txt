▶about

◀Collapse

In a parallel dimension where everybody keeps anomalous pets, a bunch of scammers pretended to be a high end pet hotel and saved on expenses by teleporting their clients to Earth for the Foundation to take care of, before teleporting them back to their unsuspecting owners.

The mysterious caller caught on to this scam and decided to confront the scammers, who denied being the masterminds and distracted her with Site-19's phone number, so she would be busy going after the Foundation while they bring back all the pets and make their escape.
