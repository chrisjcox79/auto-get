Much improved from the early draft - same basic idea, but much better writing.

I find myself asking though, what exactly is the effect of this Cthulu-like being? Does it take ordinary morons with their vague and confused prejudices and turn them into  - David Icke, David Duke, David Emory, or similar obsessed conspiracy theorists? Not really - they're crazy but focused and consistent, which the interview subject isn't.

Does it turn them into Fred Phelps - a man whose entire consciousness was warped around a single hatred, changing everything else to fit? Or does it turn them into Jesse Ventura or Alex Jones, men who never met a conspiracy theory they didn't like? Again, not really.

There's no breakdown of social order, or change in power structure. The people aren't deranged, or fanatical, justhicks. Creationists, tea partiers, congregations of tiny evangelical chirches. It's like Stepford transposed to Iowa.
