Fold

31 Jan 2014 17:56

It has plenty of merit for containment either way. This particular JPEG may smell like flowers, but the final thought of the article, about the sweaty fingers, shows that it might be transmuting molecules into something else. What if the next JPEG by this one's creator, changed it to Anthrax, or some other sort of biological terror? This could simply be a precursory test to a much sinister motive. Containing how many people have the anomolous version, what ruins the effect, and tracing it's source could lead to avoiding impending disaster. I know you already mentioned something along this line in your post, but it more of seems to be worried about hidden effects of this exact Jpeg. I'd be more worried about, what if more appear, with worse effects.
