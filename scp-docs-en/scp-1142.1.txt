The word is used that way frequently in some science fiction, but that doesn't make it any less inacurate, particularly in a (mock) scientific document. A dimension for our purposes refers to height, width, depth, and so forth. It does not refer to other universes / timelines / whatever. Though I may be butchering the physics, I believe it's accurate to say that a reality has dimensions; it is not a dimension itself.

My apologies if I'm coming on strong on this point, but this is a serious peeve of mine.
