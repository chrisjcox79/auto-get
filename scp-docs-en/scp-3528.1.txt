Spoilerknow.

Fromviolence.

Basically, this SCP was designed by the obskurakorps (Nazi GoI) to fight against the Russian .

They were an all-female bomber regiment using old WWI-era biplanes (made mostly of wood and canvas) due to aircraft shortages. They would fly at night, and stop their engines when close to their target to drop ordinance. IIRC, from the ground, the only thing you'd know is the night sky lighting up as bombs exploded around you. Without hearing their engines, there was almost no time to defend against them.

This SCP would've been a nightmare to the Night Witches; it would disable the pilots with its screech and then clamp on the flammable biplanes with its feet.
