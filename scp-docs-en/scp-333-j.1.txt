This is not a joke.

25 Mar 2010 16:09

I've just removed an addition… Now can someone explain to me how it qualifies as a joke?

A billy goat enters the bar. It is immediately accosted at the door by a large, misshapen humanoid with a club.

"Ohhh noooo, you don't want to eat me!" the goat immediately cries, "I'm much too small and scrawny. You will want to wait for my older brother."

The humanoid retreats, allowing the billy goat to find a place at the bar without further incident.

A second, slightly larger billy goat enters the bar fifteen minutes later and is accosted by the large humanoid in the same manner.

"Ohhh noooo, you don't want to eat me!" the goat immediately cries, "I have goat flu and dandruff and [DATA REDACTED]. You will want to wait for my older brother, who is not only overweight but has a bum leg and won't be able to get away or fight back."

The humanoid retreats and the goat joins its companion at the bar without further incident.

A third billy goat, much older, fatter and limping on its front leg hobbles into the bar. The humanoid leaps from the shadows and scoops the goat up.

"Ohhh noooo, put me down!" the goat immediately cries, "I'm far too fatty and my meat is tough and gamey because I am so old. The creature you wish to catch, my good troll, is the Ram. It will be coming down the road at any moment and you'll miss your chance if you are holding on to me."

The humanoid drops the lame billy goat to the ground and scratches its chin, then exits the bar and charges down the street to confront the Ram.
