17 Apr 2019 09:50

One can certainly argue that  is a concept that only exists from the human perspective, but then you're forced to apply that same concept to everything. SCP-2935 doesn't work because death isn't a discrete, meaningful state. SCP-173 doesn't work because 'line of sight' doesn't have concrete definition at the reductionist level. The SCP universe operates, and will continue to operate, at a level where human-created concepts have real physical meaning, and this article is no exception.
