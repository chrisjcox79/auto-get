SCP-969's packaging claims that approximately 19% of SCP-969 is composed of an unknown isomer labelled ████.
附加记录：    

From Wikipedia:

In chemistry, isomers  are compounds with the same molecular formula but different structural formulas.
附加记录：    

So you can't say something is simply , it has to be isomers of something. I think the label from the photo must mean that 1% of the repellent is isomers of the main active ingredient.

Also, shouldn't the SCP do something if you use it in a manner against federal regulations for insect repellents? Like cause you to be arrested and convicted of a crime?
