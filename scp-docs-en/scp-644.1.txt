For some, they have no qualms over Site names being censored since those with access to SCP articles would likely be workers of that particular Site (and it can be a failsafe against spies finding out the locations of particular SCPs).

In this particular case, we should take note that it is a Little Mister SCP. Their trademark (as seen in their counterparts) is the blackboxed Site where they are kept. I suppose it is to maintain canon among the Little Mister SCPs.
