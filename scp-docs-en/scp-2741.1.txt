19 Oct 2015 16:49

I definitely recommend reading and comparing the Moth Helmet. The basic idea of a box that does nothing more than make you left-handed isn't bad, in and of itself, and the item doesn't have to be particularly useful (it's actually a bit more interesting because it isn't). However, as it stands, the object begins and ends with just the pun, and that's not enough to carry the story.

Some possible ways to expand on it:

Why would a box that makes you left-handed exist?
Did someone get the definition of  wrong in its development?
Did someone get the definition of  right in its development?
Why is it so hammy?
What happens if it's powered by a deasil engine instead of a AA battery?

It doesn't need to be long (and probably best if it's short). But we need more than just the one pun.
