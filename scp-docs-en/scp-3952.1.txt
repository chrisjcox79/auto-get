Fold

23 Feb 2018 13:22

I don't think a cubic measurement makes sense. A measurement of area tells the in-universe reader roughly where this operates and by extension how many people might see this and how much trouble it would take to hide. Adding height into the mix removes this information (if the flight altitude wasn't specified, which adds additional fluff).

For example, if you were asked where a particular type of bird lives, your answer would be in square km.
