Hello and welcome. This one has been sitting in my sandbox for some time and I've reinvented the final 1/3 of it about half a dozen times. Family is neat, even weird ones.

Spoilers

Closethis!

The implication here is supposed to be that Ronnie is out of control and they made the Ronald McDonald House Charities to try to re-tame him and bind him to good deeds, since they were no longer able to control him or disperse him.

Removing the tulpas association to the corporate, and attaching him to a charity.
