Fold

27 Jan 2018 11:42

I've tried to touch up the entries so the last one comes as less of a shock, hopefully without losing its suddenness.

I'm glad you pointed this out, because I really want to get the ending right. My deepest fear is losing interest in the people I love, but there are plenty of traditions that not only encourage but demand severing emotional ties to your family. That was the tension I wanted to get at with the article, particularly since it suggests that having the power to define fear can come dangerously close to cultural imperialism. I do want it to be somewhat shocking, because it comes as a complete shock to Agent Bartell, but I don't want it to feel like a random bonus thrown into the story because the whole story is supposed to center on that moment.
