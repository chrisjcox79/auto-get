+block

–block

Instances of SCP-2941 superficially resemble their non-anomalous counterparts in appearance only, as their behavior and growth patterns do not correlate with any other known specimens of similar species
附加记录：    

This sentence is redundant, as superficially implies some of the rest. I'd split this into two sentences, with 

Each instance of SCP-2941 appears to be aware of its surroundings, capable of independent movement, and furthermore, some have shown the ability to speak, though how SCP-2941 instances manage to vocalize is currently unknown.
附加记录：    

I'd change this to be a bit more clinical.  Also, specify that they're sentient/sapient and if they do or do not decay as usual, and that they move around by rolling, if that's the case.

general size
附加记录：    

Average size?

Letting individual instances perform activities that they individually seem to derive pleasure from will also initiate accelerated growth
附加记录：    

and through Foundation computer modeling
附加记录：    

That should be , I believe.

negative morale inducement
附加记录：    

any instance left alone for a period of between three to five hours will begin growing at a noticeably rapid rate
附加记录：    

Left alone in any conditions? Or when it's allowed to move around and entertain itself? Does it just gain self esteem from introspecting? Also,  perhaps in cm^3 per second.

pleasure vector
附加记录：    

Is this an existing term? I really don't know. If it is then keep it, I guess. If you're just making it up, you should probably remove it. I really don't know if it's a thing or not.

waken
附加记录：    

Is very quick to notice nearby individuals, and will repeatedly vocalize questions regarding the quality of its roundness.
附加记录：    

This isn't very clear. Maybe change to 

prohibiting -4 from ever accomplishing this goal.
附加记录：    

I would change this to  or something. More clinical.

SCP-2941-8
附加记录：    

Since there's two, maybe this should be -8 and -9?

Class D
附加记录：    

This should probably be hyphenated.

That probably looks like a lot of critique, but most of it is just tone or grammar things. I actually really like this article, the humor, and the idea of the objects. It's a relatively simple idea, but it is executed very well. I laughed out loud at multiple points, and that's enough to earn my +1.

EDIT:

Establishing morale levels low enough to re-contain SCP-2941-3 took eight hours
附加记录：    

So Foundation personnel told a giant melon that it was a cube for eight hours. I like this article. This a good article.
