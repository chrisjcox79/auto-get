17 Aug 2018 23:08

+block

–block

I really enjoyed the article. The interview logs are cute and help to establish a reader-character bond. I think the beginning of the article has its put-offs, namely the containment procedures. A 6x6x8m chamber is enormous. Assuming it’s lxwxh, that would make the chamber 8 meters tall, which is about as high as a two story building. What does he need all that space for? He could probably just as easily survive in a 6x6x6m chamber. Also, when I read the revision early on, I jumped to thinking: “Why isn’t this Keter?” Because any object that the foundation CAN’T contain is generally classified Keter. However, after I read the rest of it it made more sense. I don’t know what I would do to the object class in your scenario. It’s not in containment, and it can’t be contained which would make it Keter. It’s not present around humanity and doesn’t pose a risk to normalcy (for all we know) so it probably wouldn’t be Keter. Neutralized wouldn’t work because it’s still active to the best of our knowledge, and Explained obviously wouldn’t work. I don’t know. I’m sure a veteran will be able to give more insight on the scenario than I can, but the object class confusion is no grounds for a downvote. The article is well written, well-thought out, and rightfully deserves a place on the mainlist in my opinion.
