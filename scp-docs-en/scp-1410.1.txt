Let me think

+block

–block

Counter-culture artist (possibly involved in the anomalous scene, but not necessary) gets caught doing something, chased by police/authority figures wearing gasmasks. Artists hides in a cave, gets drawn in by the mind-effecting Ichor. The Ichor fucks with his brain to the extent that he feels artwork made of mutilated human body parts is fascinating and beautiful, and the artist then acts on this, bringing people to his cave, mutilating them, and making art for the Ichor. The Ichor gets bored, and turns the artist into a rock with variable/shattered sapience. The artist is brought back to his home somehow - he might have been home when the change happened - and his children ignored him, because they didn't know the rock was their father. Rock somehow ends up outside the Foundation. Red is the chase, Yellow is the creation stage, Green is the horror at the realization of what he's done, and Blue is him trying to reach out to his children.

Close, overall. A group of artists makes something that royally pisses off the Foundation, and hid in a cave complex. One of them begins to get affected by the Ichor, until he goes mad and starts doing its/her bidding, which is to create art from the body parts of his fellows. After the Ichor gets bored of him and his arts, she turns him into 1410, and embeds it into the wall of Site-30, for her own reasons. The Blue Subtype are indeed the memory of the artist's family, but he never got to see them again after feeling to the cave. Whatever is left of him misses them, but realizes it went too far and got too twisted (which is the Green realization) to ever be with them again.
