Fold

16 Jan 2014 17:14

They are custom-made bodies. You'll find that the woman is not butch at all, but rather 'athletic looking' - which implies slim but fit. The appearances are all based on people I know (and in some cases also the personalities). The fact that the non-Caucasians die is entirely co-incidental. -2 survived until a very recent iteration, when I needed another one to die during testing, and as the one with whom communication was least possible, she made sense.

Allow me to explain -6, as an example of how I believe I am not being offensively stereotypical. Remember that these are returned items. They're the ones with whom the customers were not satisfied. So a man decided he liked the idea of lesbians, and thought it would be cool to have one as his slave/girlfriend. He doesn't specify much about her beyond 'lesbian' and 'fit'. What he doesn't realise is that lesbians, by definition, do not want to have sex with men. He happens to get a woman who is particularly damaged in her past relationships with men (spoiler: this is based very heavily on someone close to me) and she suspects, every time she's turned on and sees a man, that she has been kidnapped by this man for some nefarious purpose.

I apologise if I have come across in a way that has offended you. It was very much not my intention to, and indeed stereotyping is something I myself suffer and consider very harmful. You are correct to point out that it is something of a lazy and harmful notion to have the Japanese man killing himself. I needed someone who was going to commit suicide every time, I noticed I didn't have any East Asian instances yet, so my mind jumped to Japanese. I will consider making a change to that once I get home (on a train at the moment).
