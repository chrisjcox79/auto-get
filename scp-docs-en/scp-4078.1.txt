While I personally enjoy both termination logs and the Foundation dealing with real world politics, this could use some more writing.

The buildup is kinda meh, while we learn about all the shit he did not a lot of time is spent discussing them, and none of them feel especially ominous or interesting, so it feels awkward when we spend several paragraphs discussing how important killing him is. Perhaps expand on the list of his creations, and exploits, maybe hint at some atrocities he committed.

The termination log itself is kinda underdeveloped. Firstly, it's kinda brief. Conducting international assassinations of high ranking political figures is a big deal and should involve a long, very detailed log. Also, how did the foundation send a group of bombers into Russia during the hight of the cold war without starting a massive geopolitical incident? Earlier it was mentioned they managed to take photos of him, if they can get that close couldn't they find a way to put some delay acting poison in his smoked tea or something?
