P. Pigeon, Esq. 

ClicheCon Collab. Entry

Category 3

By:

Reverend Fox

Zyn

Cliches:

Anomaly Cliche:  An anomaly so helpful/harmless, the Foundation lets it roam the Site and has minimal containment procedures. Being helpful/harmless is optional.
Secondary Anomaly Cliche:  An item that is very good at doing the thing its supposed to do, too good in fact.
Story Element Cliche:  An interview in which the anomaly is overly cryptic, followed by the interviewer suffering.

~ The SCP Foundation
