I'm afraid the crusty edge stuff isn't as clear cut as specific passages: it's a matter of overall feel. This feels bogged down with a lot of terminology that, while there, it doesn't add anything but extra text to the article. it takes my focus away from the person who jumps dimensions and puts it on Protocol This and Operation That. 507's got a simple, memorable image: everyday shmuck who randomly jumps through the multiverse. This is just too busy to get a coherent feel for it. I walk away with a bunch of random terminology in my head, rather than the doomsday research bot.

I think that's my biggest issue. The core thought, the core image, is muddled thoroughly.
