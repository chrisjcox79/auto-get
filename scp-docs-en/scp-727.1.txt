22 Jun 2011 13:37

Well I never saw Talos or the Brass Man in any of the lists of Hephaestus's crafts, but they did say he constructed automatons.

The indesctructable metal was going to be Zeus's Aegis, but it's vague enough to be anything really.

The door-frame, door and lock was supposed to be the ones that were made for Hera, and only she could open the door.

And the statue was supposed to be Pandora, as in one of the lists it said that Hephaestus made her by mixing water and earth, so I reckoned clay was good enough to use.

I'll probably add more to the list, or detail it, if I can think of any other ways for people to fail the crafts without using 'died during engraving' again.
