Re: Thaumielvis

18 Sep 2019 17:22

+Spoilers

-Spoilers

The SCP is a body lodged in the Hoover Dam. The Foundation needs holy water to fight demons in Las Vegas. Since water that touches a saint becomes holy water, the Foundation asks a cardinal to quietly canonize the body and make it a saint. Then they have an unlimited supply of holy water, as all the water that touches the body comes from the Hoover Dam.

In return, the cardinal asks for an intimate encounter with a succubus. Succubi are described as demons that enter intimate encounters quite frequently. Since the cardinal has taken the vow of chastity upon becoming a priest, he cannot enter an intimate encounter if he follows his vow. If he doesn't follow the vow and word leaks out, he'll be sacked from his post as a cardinal. So in return for canonizing the SCP, the Foundation will help distract the cardinal's guards, suppress information, and help the cardinal meet with a succubus. If you Google the term , you will also understand what the succubus does very quickly.

The joke is all of this hidden subtext and what Ruini is trying to say without saying it. Also, before Ruini mentions succubi, people may assume that he wants to fight a demon or something like that. When he does mention succubi, the surprise is funny.

(I'm extremely squeamish about mentioning any specific details of what the succubus and the cardinal are going to do.)
