Short and sweet, I love this one for what (I think) was the conclusion based on what the mother stated.

One minor nitpick.

D-4657 told that he saw a SCP-4927-2 instance resembling his father.
附加记录：    

Told who?

http://www.learnersdictionary.com/qa/when-do-you-use-said-and-told

When using the past tense of tell, you need to point towards someone. X tells Y. X told Y.

With said, the receiver isn't needed as its just what X is saying at that moment.
