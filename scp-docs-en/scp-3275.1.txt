The image was taken by myself, with my own phone, of a pizza I ordered.
附加记录：    

I hope it tasted pretty good, because it looks terrible.

As a former pizza cook, I'd honestly have been more than a bit ashamed to send that out for delivery. The toppings are an absolute mess, what appear to be bacon strips are practically charcoal, the crust is unevenly cookedit makes me sad to look at it. Plus, bacon should always be small pieces, not strips, so it can be spread evenly over the whole pizza.

(Not joking even a little bit. Even when we were slammed we never made a pizza that looked like that. But we were consistently one of the highest rated shops of the local chain I worked for.)

((EDIT: Which, now that I think about it, bringing that up is funny, because I started reading the SCP wiki during that time period, and introduced a number of my coworkers to it via the shop computer during slow periods.))
