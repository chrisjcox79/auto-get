Some things I don't really care for, but my suspension of disbelief breaks really easily when people just have the number for the Foundation. You don't just have the world's biggest secret in the yellow pages.

That aside, the angle this is approached at isn't really too appealing, either? I guess I don't understand why Lorance needs these sprouts so bad. He even says that it's possible for him to just make more:

[] and if I don't get them back I'll just as well grow more myself! If I can. And I will.
附加记录：    

So I guess I don't understand why it's so important that he gets these specific sprouts unless they are his life force, and if they are, why is he feeding them to people? The logic is inconsistent, from what I can tell.
