No, this is wrong. What happened is that for several years we backlashed against a very popular and crosslink-happy author who had all his shit deleted (along with being sick of everything linking to such skips as 682, 231, 076, 343, 105, etc). Staff went around for ages forbidding crosslinks. We later realized that was a poor artistic decision. There is a reason the How To Write An SCP Guide encourages this.

The 'cross-references take away all on their own' is a mostly just meme that keeps replicating itself.
