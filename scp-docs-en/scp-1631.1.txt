About a year ago, I decided to write an article about flowers. I spent some time on it, got some thoughts, and then posted it. It failed.

I tried again. It failed again. And then again, same thing.

I'd probably still be in this continual circle of rewriting and reposting if it wasn't for Mayd, who gave me some very valuable core criticism and advice on how to restructure things. We even discussed collaborating on it, but unfortunately life (and covid) got in the way before we could make it a reality.

So yeah. Here's to persistence, and a morning influx of voters that hopefully wont make me eat these words.
