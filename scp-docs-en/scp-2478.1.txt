Secondly, some attribution notes:

IJAMEA was from SCP-2937 by Mazyrian
Holy Emperor's Council on Unearthly Matters is taken from Documeny 2237 Bravo by MacLeod. Bureau of Onmyo is my take on it, since MacLeod's name for the Japanese precursor was tangential at best in my headcanon.
Sarkicism Hub by Metaphysician
