26 Nov 2018 21:59

The object's effects are mildly interesting (or at least, how the one incident plays out is) for what's at its heart a standard item with an OTT anomalous effect directly relating to its function.

I could do without the terrible bore of the phone call logs, which don't amount to much, and are very much a retread of 4988. I'm hoping if people continue to write this goi, they can at least offer a bit of diversity.

The final reveal, while neatly incorporating the anomaly for some creep, doesn't offer anything fresh. It's weaksauce SCP-2535; hitting on all the basic tropes of spoopy anomalous business, but without anything truly original and outstanding. There's not much I can care about or want to come back to, it just reminds me of other things I'd rather read.
