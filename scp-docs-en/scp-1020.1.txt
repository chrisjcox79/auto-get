I personally think it doesn't have a game. Sending ransom notes is what it does because it's what it does. A ransom note needs demands, so it makes demands. The demand has to be for something the extortee would otherwise be very adverse to doing, so that's what the demands are like. But it doesn't need anything, since it's self-repairing, self-re-supplying, and indestructible. So it just has to make its demands something that can't be reversed/undone. It tried to blackmail the President because it's tired of the Foundation thwarting its purpose in existence.
