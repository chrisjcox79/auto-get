Brief google shows that it may be , or a brand name for a type of blended yarn made from Merino wool and semi-synthetic spun-cellulose fibre.

However, the third option appears the most likely: Cotton. Its name in a lot of European languages, particularly Germanic and Scandinavian, either literally translates to  (e.g. Baumwolle), or has an etymological base traceable back to it.

As it's one of the world's most common fabrics, and is otherwise missing from the list, it would also be the default assumption even without that.

Heh. I wonder if there's some way we can work the SCP initials into the name of a nudist camp?

Anyway it would seem prudent that the recapture and containment procedures for this scip should be extended slightly to include a) incapacitating it remotely, b) stripping it naked whilst knocked out, and keeping it unclothed whilst in containment, c) only interviewing it remotely, possibly using audio links only if video is too much of an ability-vectoring risk, d) possibly keeping it permanently sedated, seeing as it appears to have normal human susceptibility to being tased, so it may well be possible to keep it knocked out in a similar fashion (but using tranquilliser / coma-inducing drugs). Simple physical restraints might not be sufficient if it can alter form even without clothing, and we don't know the extent of its reality bending - after all, it was able to strip an agent of her skin even though she was herself naked. But it can't use either ability if unconscious (GoC engagement rules, somewhat, but they seem valid).
