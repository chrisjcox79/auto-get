+commentary

-Close

I have no idea how this popped up in my head. I just remember thinking  Naturally, it spiraled a little out of control and this ended up being written.

The original draft for this was off the walls silly. However, I ended up getting a different image in my head of 4272's character along this time. I ended up actually that I can respect. The second draft ended with a somewhat edgy rant alluding to an aspect of 4272's backstory where he snapped and hurt the kids who bullied him. This didn't end up working well for his character, so I switched it to an accidental, but still angry action brought through poor control of his teleportation. There's some other stuff there to be unpacked, but I don't want to do a death of the author thing and tell-alls kinda ruin the point of having subtle clues.
