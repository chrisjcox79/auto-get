This SCP was basically inspired by the fact that I'm not the kind of person who tolerates rollercoasters all that well, and the idea of being on one for what feels like a long-ass time fundamentally disturbs me, especially how people would react to such a situation. There's a bit of The Jaunt in there, now that I think about it.

I realize this skirts the edges of Bigger On The Inside stuff, but I don't think its an issue.
