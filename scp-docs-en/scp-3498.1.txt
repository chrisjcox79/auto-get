I hope I've achieved some semblance of a working production. Upon potential success this will be my second SCP, which has been in production since 2017. Even after a year in production, I still beat Seagull's rewrite.

Acknowledgements are in order:

YossiLiener: My main man. This would be a marked a collaboration if not for his declination, because of the amount of effort he's put in to help.

TheeSherm: Not only providing constant and exceedingly helpful critique, this person was one of the few present when the idea was sparked.

Cimmerian: Much help in the more recent edits.

The SCPDeclassified server: Too many to name individually. Every one of you has been an instrumental part of this process.

The IRC Chat  Draft Forums: The same to you. Each of you has helped tremendously.

And to you, the one who took the time to read. You're just as important.
