Trying something a bit different on this one: explicitly rejecting the old  wisdom in the most extreme way I can think of (making an SCP that is dependent upon another to make sense). This might get downvoted, but it's an experiment I had to try once.

+Spoilers

-Spoilers

SCP-3445 exists in the same universe as SCP-3519. Dr. Rory Jones/O5-6 has been receiving automated information updates on SCPs from the Foundation's surviving servers. He's currently editing most object classes to  and saying their current status is unknown because, well, almost everyone is dead.

As for why he's doing this? I don't think he could tell you. Boredom and obligation, maybe.
