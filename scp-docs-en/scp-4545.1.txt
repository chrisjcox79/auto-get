Like most of my ideas, this came from a random throw-away comment in chat (about trojan horses, obviously) so I decided to see if I can make the concept work as an SCP. Hopefully I succeeded! Also, I was super happy when I found the image for this under creative commons!

Thanks to CaptainKirby, Taffeta, Dora, stairfallinstitute, and cabbage for reading the draft of this!

Image source - https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Replica_of_Trojan_Horse_-_Canakkale_Waterfront_-_Dardanelles_-_Turkey_(5747677790).jpg
