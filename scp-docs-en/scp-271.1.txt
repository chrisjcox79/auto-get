I have three reasons why that is a bad idea.

Second question: are you certain of the consequences of turning, for example, Mars, into an anomalous metal with unknown properties? And wouldn't that be rather masquerade-breaking for the Foundation if it happened?

Third question: given that we don't actually have complete control over this object at the moment, do you have a plan to get it safely and completely out of CBG hands and into Site 19, without risking a containment breach at any stage and exposing any number of currently Safe and Euclid SCPs to a Keter-class object?
