So this made me chuckle more than a main list article has any right to, as there is no getting around how silly the basic premise is. However, for me it is that silly premise that makes the creepy aspects of this article shine all the brighter.

People falling from great heights, turning into bricks and clobbering people. Bricks shattering on pavement and then turning into shattered people. For how brief this article is, you sure do pack in a lot of vibrant imagery for the reader to pick through.

+1. This was fun, TG.
