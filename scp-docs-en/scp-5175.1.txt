Rieview - Site Crit

28 Aug 2020 20:36

I think is absolutely exemplary of mainlist articles that are funny. Not everything has to be a -J!

The heart of this article lies in the well written and appropriately absurd dialogue, and as Grigori points out above, there are absolute gems in here. It's a great blend of Katana from Suicide Squad and some more self aware taking the piss.

That being said - it's not perfect. As I mentioned above, this is is carried by the strength of the dialogue and interviews. I feel that it drags a bit towards the end. The final two logs, especially, feel like they're building up to something, but ultimately happens in this article. But that's ok here, because it is otherwise a delight.

28 Aug 2020 21:31

This is greatly improved from the draft version that I read. I think portraying Damien as more sympathetic is a vastly better narrative take than the original version, and I think the article is more internally consistent for it.

Still, as I mentioned, there's a knife's edge of making an annoying but still somewhat sympathetic character, and I think this just leans too far into the annoying for my own taste. I completely understand that this is meant to be a satire of a certain mindset, but I think it's possible to have a satire of something annoying that is so close to reality that it bleeds over into making the writing itself difficult to read. It's not quite in that territory since the writing is, all in all, quite solid, but something about the character is still a bit too much for my own taste.
