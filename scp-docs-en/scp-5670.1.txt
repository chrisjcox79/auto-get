Hello everyone! This is my submission to CorpseCon, and took at least a week to complete. And what a week it has been.

This SCP is a story detailing a certain kind of relationship between a 'pet' and his master, with a focus on what happens when the 'pet' disappoints. Hope you enjoy!

Special thanks to cybersqyd, Meska, not_a_seagull, caspian2, DrAkimoto, HunterDog, plaguepjp, Penguin6, Popsioak and Ekronak for helping me polish this up!

+1 I liked it, but wish the ending was a bit better

09 Jun 2020 03:16

Overall I really like both the containment procedures and the SCP. I do have a bit of a problem with the ending though.

+Open

-Close

It shifts gears from the decommissioning of the SCP to having it be really sad and broken down at the President's grave. Is it a coincidence that they choose to axe 5670 on anniversary of Johnson's death? The article ends with 5670 outside of containment in a public place. Did they finish the decommissioning? (at which point it should probably reclassified to Neutralized) Or are they going to bring it back into containment? I do have problem with the idea that attempting to neutralize the SCP because it isn't cost effective to contain it anymore. Isn't the whole point of SCP's like SCP-1609 that the foundation doesn't try to destroy things because of unintended consequences?

I get that the contest does lock your object class to keter and overall I still really like the article
