I'm generally of two minds on this. Yes, the story is in there somewhere. It might even be a pretty nice story. Unfortunately, it is both too distant and not initially interesting enough to get me invested. I'm not saying every story needs to be fully explored of course. I think she said something, but I took off running, so I don't remember exactly what her exact words, but
