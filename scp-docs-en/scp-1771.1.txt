I would like here to note that the inspiration of this SCP is from a newbie in a chat who mentioned something about a house which had people swap according to the seasons. Said newbie disappeared and hasn't appeared in many, many months and I found the initial premise interesting so I took my own spin on it. If you are the newbie who mentioned the idea and don't like how it's being used, let me know and I'll remove it accordingly.

Living the dream, or dreaming the life?
