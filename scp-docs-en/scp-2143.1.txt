Based on Researcher Dios and PeppersGhost's comments, it looks like there might be other people who are unclear on the nature of which songs SCP-2143 plays.

Basically, the Foundation has almost NO control over what songs SCP-2143 performs. SCP-2143 randomly picks and performs a song of its own (lack of) volition. They can ask it to replay an already-performed song once more, but that's it.

I will clarify that in the article.
