I haven't been this torn on an SCP in a while

On one hand, I really like this. It's simple, straightforward, and kinda funny. No attempt was made to make it overly scary, which I like. Also, I'm a cyclist so I love the karmic justice.

That said, the tone is a bit off, I think the note at the end could use some work, and I feel like there is potential to develop the personality of the bike (or it's creator) that you didn't fully capitilize on. As well, the teleportation bit is kind of weak; I see a lot of SCPs include teleportation as their means of being dangerous, and I just feel like there is a better way to execute that which better keeps in the spirit of the SCP. I mean, it's a bicycle. It can roll. Teleporation seems almost sacreligious.

I want to upvote this, but with the issues I just can't. I will, however, be eagerly checking back every few hours to see if improvements have been made.

No vote for now.
