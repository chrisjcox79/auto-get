Wow, second scp, I'm really getting the hang of this. I created this SCP for two reasons, one, for my dog that sadly passed away from cancer, she helped me a lot through tough times, it'll won't be the same without her. It feels like I've been cheated, I can't explain it.

Two, I've been struggling what I really want ever since I reached my teens. This pandemic just really puts things in perspective. But I'll think I'll find my path one day. It just scares me that I might make a mistake I can't come back from. But I'm doing good so far I suppose.

I want to thank DrDromeus for helping out with this piece. He pointed out a lot of good points and helped me craft the draft. Thanks.

The image that is used in the article was created and owned by me. I made some wings and taped it on the stapler.

This is really, really close for me, but I think there a few problems that need ironing out before I could upvote.

+critique

-critique

Shortly after capture, it became apparent that the objects that SCP-5070 has  led a trail from the golf course all the way to the suburban home of Dominic White (PoI-1877)
附加记录：    

This is a good example of one of areas where I sense a lack of clinical tone. Putting a word in quotation marks that isn't actually a quote is just putting in an off-tone word. Also, the 'all the way' in the same sentence feels unnecessarily opinionated.

In the interview log, some of the dialogue could use another pass. I noticed the POI strangely seems to avoid using pronouns when referring to Vamp.  was used a few times, so it's not a gender problem. When the subject is obvious, English speakers tend to use pronouns much more often. If this was intentional, the rest of the POI's dialogue didn't cue me to this person having a particularly strange speech pattern to justify it, just a few little things like lack of contractions that could also just be chalked up to stilted writing.

These are just a sample of the issues I see with the execution of the article. I see potential in the theme, even the narrative, but I can't upvote in the article's current state, I'm taken out of the narrative too often. Potential though!

SCP-5070 is able to use red coloring as a substitute for sustenance. When given objects colored red (or other shades of red), it will approach and 'bite' down on the widest surface possible. The color will recede to where the teeth meet until disappearing completely, leaving neutral colors (white, gray, black etc.) behind. This process only affects the color red; other colors will be unaffected.
