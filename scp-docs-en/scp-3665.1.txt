This subverted my expectations really well - by setting up 'thing that summons demons' in a setting where that's totally believable, I then felt properly disappointed and tricked at the slow realisation that it's just a cheap perception trick. This nicely emulates the real-life experience of buying crappy knockoff toys and tricks as a kid.

There are some definite tonal issues here (your use of 'succ' feels particularly forced in context), but this is excellently structured.
