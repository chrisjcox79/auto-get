This is the closest Ill ever get to a Series I slot

10 Sep 2018 22:00

Here's my first go at doing an explained SCP. Thanks to Tufto for reading and critting!

Image source:

https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Oort_cloud_Sedna_orbit.svg

This is part of Ad Astra Per Aspera, a project I've been working on with some other users set in a future where humanity and the Foundation are expanding away from an apocalypse-wrecked Earth and into space. There's no veil and colonization of the solar system is underway.

+project

-project

By Cyantreuse

Autonomy 

DIRECTIVE A-42

SCP-4069  Out of Range 

By 9Volt

SCP-3070  Highly Unusual Attempts at Solar Conquest 
SCP-3230  Intergalactic Isolation 
Untitled Terzan 2 Series 

Faster-Than-Light Prelude 
SCP-3417  The Preachers of Globular Cluster Terzan 2 

By MaliceAforethought

For those wondering, aside from being part of Ad Astra this article's narrative is largely self-contained. It does reference one other wiki article, though (I'm not telling which one).

(Also, note that the main goal here is to generate a mystery without any big reveals, though I’m hoping there’s enough hints to guve some sense of what is happening)
