06 Feb 2019 00:06

I genuinely quite loved this.

It absolutely has its issues but there's so much here that I enjoy. The dejection when its gifts are refused, its behavior when locked in a room, the poor thing's reaction to being yelled at :[

There are a few issues, though, I think you should think about. First one is:

There is currently no plan to capture SCP-4719, and it is doubtful there will ever be one.
附加记录：    

Lines like this crop up in a lot of SCP drafts I've seen. It's not the sort of thing that would be said as rarely do procedures speculate about the future like this. This reads more like an opinion than a fact.

Also, the acquisition. I am not sure if I'm missing a reference or if you're just intentionally being cagey here but I don't get much from it. There's not enough information there even for my imagination to run wild. And since the redacted SCP is not given any information, really, I'm just not sure what I'm supposed to take from it.

A human kidney
附加记录：    

I don't think the Foundation would let the transplant happen. The Kidney just got anomalously created out of thin air. Who knows what might be wrong with it. I imagine the fellow would still get a transplant, but I don't think it would be from this specific kidney.

There may be a bit more, but this really was great. Thanks for the super charming read! :D
