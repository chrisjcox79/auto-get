Sorry to downvote, I really am. I actually like the concept of making Santa an actual SCP, but I can't believe that the future Foundation would create Santa Clause, making so much work for their past selves. I know it's to prevent a paradox and potential reality shift, but this is already sort of a bootstrap paradox. They only do it because they already did it, but where did this start? Why would they do this in the first place (if a time loop can be said to have such a thing)?

You also state that he travels at a hundred kph and has been known split into up to 47 simultaneous instances, but 47 Santa's moving at 100 kph is still not enough to visit every Christmas Celebrating household in one night.

But this was an overall decent enough SCP, so please don't be discouraged by my downvote.
