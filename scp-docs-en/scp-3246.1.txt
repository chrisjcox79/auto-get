Fold

09 Apr 2018 21:23

I didn't enjoy this, for a couple of reasons.

Firstly, I dislike Thaumiels becoming this mundane. They're supposed, in my mind at least, to be big things that facilitate the containment of dangerous anomalies  a Granny who knits wholesome clothes doesn't fill that niche in the slightest, and given that (I thought) they were supposed to be classified below high-level Researchers, it doesn't jive well with me. I know some people think differently, but that's how I feel. It's not enough to cancel an upvote, but it definitely contributed.

Secondly, there's not much story here, or even imagery. It is, at the heart, a reality bender that helps the Foundation, which is hardly a unique idea. The logs are cool, but I didn't come away feeling like I'd explored any aspect of the universe: it didn't have the mystery or charm of older articles, or the in-depth narrative/exploration of newer ones. The 173 test came close (it was creepy and clever), and so did the Researcher Kiryu test (it was extremely cute), but in the end they just kind of cancelled each other out and left it feeling bland.

The writing is almost flawless though, and despite the problems I didn't actively dislike it, so no vote. Sorry.
