There are quite a few issues here with pacing, wording, dialog and tone - all very small, but taken altogether weaken what has the trappings of a gripping and atmospheric article - more than I could reasonably address in a single post while confined to mobile.

It'd be a lot easier for me to discuss some of this via irc/discord - and I'd be open to collabing on a rewrite, if that is something you'd be interested in.

As far as larger issues I can point out real quick, I'll second mickeyten that the  sections should probably use imperial - but more importantly, they don't really seem to contribute all that much. Just having several snippets that repeat the same thing adds useless fluff.

My biggest issue though is the final collapsible. The reveal, and the linked SCP, just feels random and disconnected to everything that's been built up prior to that point. It was a huge letdown.
