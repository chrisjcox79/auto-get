I’m sorry that you didn’t think it was decidedly spooky enough, but I’m at least glad you thought it had its moments.

I’ll try to explain a few confusing details

1. The servers can only be kept up so long as bills are paid, and cryptocurrency is the hardest form of currency to track. Since there’s no concrete location discovered, the only containment procedures are to effectively starve it out economically. If it has no means of funding, it can’t run.

2. It wasn’t a focus more than it was just a detail. They literally just said “I just wanted a cool acronym”.

3. Yes, it’s expected that there are AIs considering the big reveal. Though in reality, it’s just two.
