Minor stylistic comment

18 Jun 2016 09:07

SCP-2339 is the collective designation for an anomalously large Bombus terrestris (buff-tailed bumblebee) nest and the bees residing within
附加记录：    

I first misread this as saying the bees themselves were anomalously large. It might be less clunky to write something like:

SCP-2339 is an anomalously large nest of bees, of genus Bombus terrestris (buff-tailed bumblebee)
附加记录：    

85000
附加记录：    

I suggest inserting a comma for readability: 85,000

usually of the classical genre.
附加记录：    

, or something that suggests the Foundation documenter knows about the topic.

Flight of the Bumblebee
附加记录：    

Do they prefer to play in the key of B?
