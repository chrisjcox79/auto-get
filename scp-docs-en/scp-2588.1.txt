22 Jul 2016 00:57

The central idea is pretty neat, but there are a lot of hoops to jump through.

For instance, the organ transformation degrading them into mental zombies feels like a pointless  is something you act on quickly.

The interview didn't really do anything for me. The description of what he perceives from the process wasn't strange or interesting, especially given that he often repeats  feels.

Plus, it doesn't help that you've made him a standard-issue reality bender. All we know about him before death is that he bends reality and murders people and we filled him with bullets, and the fact that the Foundation thought that was all that was necessary to include in this file suggests that he's a really, really boring trope. I guess even that wouldn't bother me if it wasn't stated so plainly, giving it a quotidian, feel.

Ultimately, I just feel like this over-tells a fairly neat story with segments that don't improve it. No vote.
