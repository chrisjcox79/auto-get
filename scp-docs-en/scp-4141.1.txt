10 Dec 2018 21:46

The concept is beautifully simple, but the execution is very dry. The otherwise unexplored wi-fi connection is too obviously an authorial solution to the issue of “how would the Foundation know?”, except that it paints the Foundation as far too credulous. Why is this not described as “shortly after entering, online messages appear claiming to be a person trapped in the room” - how does the Foundation know that these messages are genuine?

The explanation that AWCY did it was a bit lacklustre. Ultimately, SCP-4009 explored this concept in a much more interesting way.
