Critiques and suggestions regarding the region being made from :

1) I can't see how that would lead to the  sort of thing as you present it. That is, there being some sort of illusion which covers up the edge until you go past it, being unable to return once past the edge, and there being air and sunlight (but nothing solid) present beyond the edge.

2) Similarly, I can't see how that would lead to copied animal life all becoming nocturnal, aggressive, and impossible to find during the day.

3) Buildings are either copied perfectly or copied such that have noticeably bizarre design and strange looking furniture. If the same happened when copying plant life the results would either have no mutations or be completely nonviable; likewise, copied computers would either be copied perfectly or be completely non-functional. On the other hand, copied plants having lots of mutations suggests that copying causes lots of little mistakes rather than a few large scale mistakes.

4) Presumably the electricity flowing through the power lines in the regions is actively and continuously being copied into the region, rather than being generated within the region. If so, you could the Hz frequency and randomly varying from a perfect sine-wave.
