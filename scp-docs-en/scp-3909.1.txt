This is beyond Star Signals-level though. 36 Chambers has set the blueprint for the entirety of hardcore hip hop, has been sampled in numerous songs, referenced in lyrics to numerous songs, has had documentaries and behind-the-scenes about it, has sold millions of album copies (not to mention bootleg tapes of it and its demos circulating the music underground scene, as I found out first-hand last time I went to Brazil) and has been used in countless media.
