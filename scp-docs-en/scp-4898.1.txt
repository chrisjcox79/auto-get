At first, I thought I was going to downvote this, due to the similarities with the recent SCP-4844. However, I found the execution substantially different enough to like it. It gives Saul more concrere abilities that make more sense given his occupation, and the pacing in this matches the progression of the story.

However, the last couple of interviews seem a bit cliche? I don’t know how to put this, it just feels like I’ve read them before. In addition, there are some grammar issues here, such as:

The next day, POI-4898-1 was observed leaving his apartment at 09:10. Then again at 09:24. And a third time at 09:41.
附加记录：    

This part could be tightened up.

In the end, this skip definitely distinguished itself from other skips of the same genre. +1
