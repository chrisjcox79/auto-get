One of your great strengths as a writer is your characterization, psul, and this SCP is no different. It was chilling to read about the effects on the AIs, and how frightful it all is.

Depression is the sort of thing I wouldn't wish on my worst enemy, and one thing we don't really think about is how we feel that machines, computers, and the like, because of their construction, are immune to this sort of psychological ravaging. To make us ponder the question What if they weren't immune? tells us just how horrendously unjust it feels. It hurts in ways we didn't know we had.

Excellent work, psul. A very easy +1.
