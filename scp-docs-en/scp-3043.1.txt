She's the dedicated researcher with nothing to lose. He's an anomalous typewriter with dreams too big to last. And in the middle of it all is Murphy Law — the man you call when everything that can go wrong… does.

Watch them explode across the silver-screen in tonight's premier: Murphy Law in… TYPE 3043 — FOR MURDER!

On Murphy Law

27 Jun 2017 18:23

If this article succeeds, and you want to use Murphy Law for your own skip, go right on ahead. Just keep in mind four things:

If this article succeeds, I'm probably going to write an SCP document on him at some point in the near future.
Murphy Law plays by his own rules. He's out of control! He's a loose canon!
Whenever you include Murphy Law in a story, you must write that story while smooth jazz is playing. Also, it helps if you're at least slightly drunk.
If you write Murphy Law without his trilby, he will find you. He will find you, and he will make you add the fucking trilby into your goddamn story.

FADE IN:

EXT. DOCKS - SUNSET

MURPHY stands at the edge of the docks, watching the sun go down on just another day. Behind him, two sleek black cars roll up. A dozen men start pouring out of them, but he doesn't bother to look back.

The men are smartly dressed in black suits; they crowd around a short old woman dressed in white. She slowly approaches MURPHY, leaning heavily on her cane. One man follows her closely  her personal secretary and bodyguard.

The man is AGENT JULY; whatever it is, he does it by the books. The woman is O5-5  and if we told you anything else, we'd have to kill you.

O5-5 moves to watch the sunset besides MURPHY. AGENT JULY is clearly displeased; he'd rather her not get too close.
O5-5 

You did some good work today, Mr. Law.
附加记录：    
MURPHY 

Yeah.
附加记录：    

O5-5 reaches to pull a cigarette out of her pocket, placing it between her lips.
O5-5 

If we ever need your services how might we contact you?
附加记录：    

MURPHY leans forward and plucks the cigarette out of O5-5's mouth. AGENT JULY immediately reaches for his firearm, but O5-5 lifts her hand to stop him.
MURPHY 

I'll be around.
附加记录：    

MURPHY turns, sliding the cigarette into his mouth and pulling out his lighter. As he walks away, he starts to light it  and a fog begins to roll in.

AGENT JULY steps forward, as if to go after MURPHY. O5-5 grabs him by the shoulder.
AGENT JULY 

But 
附加记录：    
O5-5 

(shaking her head)

Forget about it, July. It's Chinatown.
附加记录：    

AGENT JULY slides his hand away from his firearm. Together, they watch MURPHY walk off into the foggy night.

The camera focuses on MURPHY's silhouette as the view fades to black.

FADE OUT.

THE END
