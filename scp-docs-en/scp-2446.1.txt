SCP-2446-1 tend to appear in dress similar to Foundation-issue Mobile Task Force combat gear. Style and composition of such dress (as well as worn equipment) vary with some frequency. Certain physical factors, like the weight of an instance, vary between duplicates , however genetic makeup never seriously differs. I had initially downvoted when this first appeared, based on the fact that it was lacking supplements and that it employed humes1. However, with the other material supplied, I could see that this was something that was heavily context-dependent, so I went back to read again with an open mind.
