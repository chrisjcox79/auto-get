And a million dirty waves

Now I see you lying there

Like a lilo losing air, air

Black rocks and shoreline sand

Still dead summer I cannot bear

And I wipe the sand from my arms

The Spanish Sahara

I think that this is presented in far too straightforward of a manner. You've got a mysterious and enigmatic object but then simultaneously the reader is being handheld through the story. In my own SCP-703, I gave the object simple but easily identifiable personality traits. The object was less a vehicle through which the story was told and more the protagonist of the story. I don't really feel anything from the cloud, it's delivering the weekly mystery box subscription. It's delivering story but doesn't feel like a part of it. That's why this doesn't work right now. -1

Whether you like it or not, history is on our side. We will bury you!
