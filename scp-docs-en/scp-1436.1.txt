Alright, one of my pet peeves is the misuse of entropy. Just because something goes on forever does not mean it has anything to do with entropy. In fact, this is quite clearly shown to have positive entropy during most stages, and a static entropy overall (which is not anomalous, as the phoenix is not a truly isolated system).

tl:dr- Don't use entropy unless you understand it.
