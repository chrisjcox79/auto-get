22 Jun 2018 07:39

I'm not going to minus vote, but I do have a criticism here. It doesn't make sense to me that they'd test this with D-Class people. There's an obvious danger in allowing dangerous individuals to attain extreme intelligence, even temporarily, and testing this instead with research personal would allow the foundation to attain insights that might further all sorts of research. It just feels natural to me that the Foundation would throw researchers at this thing to get answers to all sorts of stuff and just kind of accept that they're putting those folks into retirement as the tradeoff.

I think if you centered this idea around a conflict like that it becomes much more interesting and might get a better response.
