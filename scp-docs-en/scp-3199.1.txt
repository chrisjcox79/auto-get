24 May 2017 11:45

There is no real structure here. It feels like you have put in a smorgasbord of standard SCP elements - recovery, mysterious note, breach procedure, doctors' notes, interview log and experiment logs - without having a real purpose or literary goal. SCPs don't need to be stories - sometimes the convenience of documents just happening to form a narrative is distracting - but I much prefer them when they have a point. This just sits there without bringing much to the table other than the picture.

As a side note, how can an audio log include details such as:

visibly anxious
附加记录：    

looks grave
附加记录：    

shuffles uncomfortably
附加记录：    

looks grave [again!]
附加记录：    

[Shivering.]
附加记录：    

places his head in his hands
附加记录：    

leaning forward in his seat
附加记录：    

stares for a second or two, leaning back in his chair
附加记录：    

shows visible signs of distress
附加记录：    

now standing, gradually sits back down. Trembling.
附加记录：    

Edit: video / audio - easy things to confuse, even for me!
