If my interpretation is correct, I love the emotional core of this article. A father wanting to create safe anomalous objects for his daughter's joy is heartwarming. But, him seeing and being paranoid about outside groups (nefarious or otherwise) interested in his work, thus causing him to separate himself from his family, is heartbreaking.

Even so, the article as-written does not facilitate the emotion upon reading it; I added things and pictured the father-daughter relationship in my head to make it give me that strong emotional punch, but not all readers do that.

+1, nonetheless.
