Re: Yes, but...

If you don't note that the anomaly turns blue at 3:45 AM on Tuesdays, someone is going to die
附加记录：    

In that scenario, it was because the anomaly always turned blue at 3:45 AM on Tuesdays. And even if it wasn't, turning blue usually isn't normal and would be noteworthy in itself.

Finally, I don't think that's ever been a rule of thumb, just a thing from a tale that can be used to highlight the importance in not leaving out critical information in the procedures about a SCP's properties that can affect how it's contained.
