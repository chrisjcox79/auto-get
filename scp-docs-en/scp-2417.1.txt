Welp, here is my third scip! After 2 months of drafting (and some lack of motivation), it is finally out. For those wondering, this is a start at connecting some the Foundation mythos together, which has always been fragmented. About the Church of the Second Hytoth, they are basically the Daevites but much more benevolent, with a bit of collecting ancient stuff. I don't fully intend to make them of GOI, but if you guys want to do so I won't object at all.

Also if someone can correctly identify every cross reference they get 1 Voltbuck! (Worth 0.0001 US dollars)

Oh, almost forgot! Big shoutout to shaggydredlocks and buttfranklin for helping my write this, and to everyone else who has reviewed it!

Couple nitpicks with the description that I missed in drafting due to focusing more on the addenda:

附加记录：    

I have no idea why this is in quotes, and don't think it needs to be.

附加记录：    

This strikes me as odd. Throughout the rest of the article, it's clear that Alison is only trying to support Rakmou, who would weaken and die without blood. It doesn't make sense for her to fear its wrath when her cult has it on cosmological life support. The rest of the piece unfolds with her acting out in desperation, but her fear then isn't from being smote by her god, its from the things it defends them from.

There's one or two bits of her dialogue I think you could have broken up to let the conversation flow better. Otherwise this is fine, and I like new structure. +1
