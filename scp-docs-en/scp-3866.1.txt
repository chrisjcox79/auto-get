26 May 2018 08:52

Something I don't like about dado is how it could quite easily become formulaic, in the vein of 'poor English skills + medication + pun/wacky misunderstanding/wordplay = article'. Much like a mediocre sitcom character, the appearances of dado don't serve to say something new or interesting, but rather as a set up for a that's-our-dado joke with a laugh track.

I think this skirts the line of that. I didn't downvote because the obvious pun I was expecting about Asian youths fortunately didn't materialise and I liked the tie-in to the doomsday as an amusing and natural way to link something silly to the end of the world and evidence against this being simply application of a formula. I don't think this is a bad article, it just happens to be a possible herald of a trend I wouldn't like to see.
