Fold

29 Nov 2018 02:27

Glad the test log was entertaining.

With respect to your comments RE: The CI plotline and its ramifications.

Basically, the SCP Foundation used this thing like mad shortly after its discovery since it was a very useful device for tracking down PoIs and seemed like it was low risk. However, by having several MTF agents bonded to several CI agents via SCP-3460, they may have inadvertently caused a massive CI raid on their own facility via the devices probabilities altering abilities. This caused them to reel in their use of the device, requiring O5 approval, and large amounts of down time between uses. In short, SCP has a super useful device, overuse it, and have it bite them in the ass in a bad way.

My plan was to foreshadow this with the initial update to the containment procedures, and then hint that things were heading towards catastrophe with the final entry of the test log, though I can see why that might be a little weak sauce.
