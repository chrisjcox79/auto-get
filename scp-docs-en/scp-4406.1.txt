12 Aug 2019 08:53

Glad to see there are other people with similar interests working on computer-based SCPs. This is very well written, avoiding most of the pitfalls of computer/digital virus-based SCP listings (namely that their writers as a rule have no idea how computers or viruses actually function in real life) Though one would imagine that anti-memetic properties would lead to the complete failure of any attempt to decompile or reverse engineer it Maybe GHIDRA (https://ghidra-sre.org/), being from the NSA, has hidden anti-thaumaturgic obfuscation countermeasures?
