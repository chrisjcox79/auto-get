Here are some of the things I said in chat, that I'm not sure if you've seen.

Things I like:

I think it has a very nice sense of progression, and ollie is effectively sinister in spite of his speech patterns.

Problems:

This is not a whole lot more than an anomaly that makes people into the most negative caricature of furries. I feel like adding a few more, weirder symptoms that clearly differentiate victims of this anomaly from passionate, non-anomalous furries would make this feel more creative.

The final log is excessively vague. I understand what you were going for, but the people writing up the report would know what Perry was doing to the MTF guy, and how said MTF guy turned out. Withholding it is a bit cheesy. Also it sort of strains credulity that a deranged furry would be able to get the upper-hand on an elite MTF agent.

Overall, +1, but a sort of narrow one.
