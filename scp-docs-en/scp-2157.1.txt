I think I'm with Dr N. on this one. It's quite good, well-written and pleasantly devoid of unnecessary fluff, but it does feel as if a bit of polish would really bring out the shine

Ideas/thoughts:

Perhaps a bit more of a hint as to why they're there, why they're facing west, or what their connection to the forest/earth is.
Give the readers a hint as to what happened to the missing researcher; why did that one vanish when the other ones died? Even a vague justification would give the reader a better sense of what these things are and if they're inherently hostile, indifferent or beneficent (as long as nobody, y'know, hurts them).
Perhaps a reference to a local legend, an ancient myth or something like that would help.
