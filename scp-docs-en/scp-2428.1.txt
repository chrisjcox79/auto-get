We all go a little mad sometimes

So the little misters tend to be pretty hit and miss with me. This one easily is my favorite of the existing misters so far, however, and there are two main reasons for that.

First off, I really dig the implication of all the little misters being normal people before they became the misters. That puts a really human face on a series that was all pretty human to begin with.

Second, I really like the interaction Mad has with the auditory hallucinations. That was my favorite part of the article, so much so that I could easily read an entire supplement log of similar interactions.

+1 TG.

Glad you got this posted.
