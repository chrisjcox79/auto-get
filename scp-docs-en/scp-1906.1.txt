Explanation

28 Jan 2013 05:17

There's a joke, often attributed to Groucho Marx, but apparently it's not actually his.

Outside of a dog, a book can be your best friend.

Inside of a dog, it's too dark to read.

So I started thinking what if that were not the case? What if, inside of a dog, there was a tastefully-furnished, well-lit reading room?

And once I had that utterly ludicrous idea, I decided to see where it could go if I played it completely seriously.
