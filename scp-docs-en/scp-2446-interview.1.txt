10 Mar 2016 00:58

Dr. L████ N█████
附加记录：    

I'm going to hammer on this point repeatedly until this stops showing up. Why would you redact something like this? Are we cleared to know the doctor's name? If not, just strike it entirely. Why would you provide a hint like this if we're not to know this person's identity? What is the purpose of narrowing someone's search to  This isn't the reason I downvoted, but the sheer pointlessness of this device is immersion-breaking for me.

As to the interview, I'm not sure there's really enough here for a standalone document. It provides some background info, and perhaps because of that it comes across as pretty directly expository. That's kind of an inherent problem with the interview format, I know. There's some lines that sound a little wrong, like they wouldn't be things that come out in natural conversation. , these sorts of things. The whole exchange could use a once-over to check for how this would sound as an actual conversation.

As to the size-zero text, I didn't bother. That's a gimmick that I don't see the point of, and I've never felt it's incumbent on me as a reader to go digging through source code to find things that are integral to enjoyment of the piece. If the writer is making it actively harder for the reader to read the piece, then I'm inclined to say that the writer is not doing their job.
