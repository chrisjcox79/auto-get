As most, I just think it have some writing issues, but overall is great. I'll try to make some suggestions on how to improve it, though.

+block

–block

Inflated SCP-3551-1 instances are to be kept in standard humanoid containment chambers, and their SCP-3551-2 instances confiscated and stored in Anomalous Weapons Locker-52. Guards assigned to SCP-3551-1 instances are to carry standard Foundation combat knives to ensure continued cooperation. SCP-3551-1 instances are to remain under the impression that any sharp object will result in their immediate fatality, and all recovered instances are to be led to believe in the ruse as well.
附加记录：    

Since the pistols have their own -2 designation, it seems odd to word this that way. Also, ensuring their cooperation seems like a very low priority and easy task to take so much specification on the Containment section. Maybe you could simplify to something like:

Inflated SCP-3551-1 instances are to be kept in standard humanoid containment chambers, and guards assigned to them can create any ruse to ensure cooperation if necessary. SCP-3551-2 instances are to be stored in Anomalous Weapons Locker-52.
附加记录：    

_
_

SCP-3551 refers to a line of inflatable toy products which are designated SCP-3551-1 through 3 that are sold together. SCP-3551 come in packaging that identifies the product as  with a specific name of the items contained inside the packaging below it.
附加记录：    

It is a little off tone. Maybe something like:

SCP-3551 is the common designation for three distinct inflatable products, designated SCP-3551-1 through 3. Non-inflated SCP-3551 instances are commonly found in packages with a  logo in the upper side, and a brief description of the items contained inside in the down side. To date, sealed packages contain one of each designation of SCP-3551.
附加记录：    

_
_

SCP-3551 anomalous properties do not occur until it is inflated with air or helium. When fully inflated, the object will remain inflated. This is due to the plug containing the air within the item sealing on its own, and therefore can not easily be deflated.
附加记录：    

Can be:

SCP-3551 anomalous properties do not occur until it is inflated with air or helium. When fully inflated, the plug containing the air within the item seals on its own and resists attempts to open.
附加记录：    

_
_

SCP-3551-1 instances have the appearance of a generic  though they are harmless and somewhat incompetent. Though instances are highly intelligent in some respects (see Incident 3551-01) despite being generally foolish and inept towards achieving their goal.
附加记录：    

To

SCP-3551-1 instances resemble a generic , though their actual actions are harmless and somewhat incompetent, apparently for comical purposes. Despite seeming generally foolish and inept towards achieving their stated goal, SCP-3551-1 instances can demonstrate advanced knowledge and technical skill, demanding constant supervision (see Incident 3551-01).
附加记录：    

_
_

The items have external LED lights that light up when firing. Instances fire a weak red laser beam that is relatively harmless. Though

The items have external LED lights that light up when

Change:

The Foundation since has been having encounters with instances.

Since then, the Foundation has already captured ███ instances.

That is mostly of it. The Incidents are good, but could use some clarification and technical details that would be of interest to the Foundation, like how they performed the hijack. The joke on using knifes to ensure cooperation when they are actually harmless to them could also be better served as an Incident or other kind of log, like an interview. Also, there are several problems with spelling and punctuation, but I'm in no position to really address them properly.
