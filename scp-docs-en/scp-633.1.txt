26 Aug 2017 13:14

I'm not really sure about this one. It has all the qualities of a successful article- good use of images, consistent clinical tone, solid pacing, a very ominous undertone- but somewhere in the middle it starts to feel disconnected, and by the end I felt slightly empty. As if there's not really a whole lot going on.

Perhaps I'm just being dense, but whatever narrative is being formed here doesn't feel as though it's been presented with much clarity. There's this overwhelming feeling of 'we should be scared of this thing' riddled throughout the article, yet even upon re-reading I couldn't really figure out why. Maybe my lack of knowledge regarding all of the terminology has killed my understanding of how this SCP works, or maybe you just haven't delivered on the hype (for lack of a better term) being built up through the documents successfully.

In short, I can't really put into words what I dislike about this article. For that reason, I'm going to have to settle on a no vote for now.
