Clinical-ism error

16 Jun 2020 20:23

I would recommend changing parts that refer to the victim of the ritual from 'they' or 'them' to 'it' or 'the subject'. For example,  The Foundation is supposed to have a clinical tone even when it is describing a victim that is not yet anomalous. I have not read a single article besides this on the site (in recent memory) that uses 'they' since, like I said, skips should be clinical.
