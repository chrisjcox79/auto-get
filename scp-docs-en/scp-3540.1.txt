This is a story about the dead. Or more specifically, the people who lead the dead to whatever lies beyond. This was inspired by a coldpost I saw several years ago about a person who appeared in abandoned houses during Halloween and killed the children who came to the house. I wanted to invert that idea, so I wrote a pair of grim reapers who take the month of October off to celebrate the season that is the most relevant to them.

I was actully kinda inspired by Ingmar Bergman's film Seventh Seal and it's rather casual portrayal of the death figure. I wanted to take my own crack at it, but with a decidedly more lighthearted take on it. Fear of death is intrinsic to being a human, so I wanted to show how human Death truly is.

Thank you to Shaggydredlocks, Fieldstone, Captain Kirby, and Ratsy for feedback.
