I think the photo caption would read better if , since the former word is much less common than the latter.

That's my only complaint, though. This is an intriguing article. To me, the most fascinating thing about it is that whatever is going on involves more than simple time travel. Something (someone?) is seeing to it that the notes are discovered in order. But who or what is doing that, and why? There's an additional anomaly involved besides the time travel, and the inscrutability of that anomaly and its motives (if any) is what makes this an SCP article and not just a time travel story. +1.
