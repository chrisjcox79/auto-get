Site Criticism Team - Junior Staff Post

04 Jul 2019 06:51

I found myself super mixed on this one, which is a shame considering how much I enjoyed the tale.

I think the main place where this article suffers for me is the pacing. It goes through periods of rapid action (namely two the exploration logs), interspersed with what I found to be a lot of slog. All of the stuff about farmers disappearing, about the development of mental and physical disorders in those who returned, the influx of farmers getting sent into 4475, etc all felt like they should have built up tension, but they just didn't. I think part of that hinges on how niche the anomaly was, only affecting dairy farmers who do very specific things from a specific angle in a specific order to a cow.

The good bits of this article were fantastic. Both logs were riveting and gross in equal measure, and were easily the highlights of the article, but given the hard time I had even just getting to those bits, I can't really do anything but downvote.

SoullessSingularity, for any questions or concerns relating to this review.
