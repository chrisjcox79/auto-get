I hate Tim Allen.

This is a trilogy. All three parts are entries in the 144 Hour Jam Contest. God have mercy on my soul.

1. SCP-4844 - Last Man Standing  (you are here)

2. What's The Deal? 

3. Clash of the Cosmic Comedians 

EDIT: It has officially tied for 2nd Place  for Day 1 of the contest! Thank you everyone who read it, liked it, or disliked it.

ShowCommentary

HideCommentary

I was originally not planning on participating in JamCon at all, but as soon as I saw  as a theme, I immediately thought of the movie with Tim Allen. And then, out of nowhere, the concept of this article hit me, and I wrote the first draft in about an hour.

Thanks to everyone who read it before posting, and special thanks to A Random Day who helpfully pointed out a few places to streamline.

Additional thanks to my best friend, who logged in to my account in order to post this article at the precise moment that posting opened, so that I could go to a party instead. Not only did he succeed, he managed to post before literally anyone else, so this article is the first entry in the entire contest. That achievement is entirely his.

This article was in part inspired by, for some god-forsaken reason, the entirely non-comedic comic Irredeemable, which also features an incredibly petty antagonist with godlike abilities. There's a small reference or two sprinkled throughout the trilogy.
