19 Jan 2018 10:30

This is a fun idea but I have a lot of issues with this.

1. the amount of black boxes, redaction's and expungement and their placement makes it feel like they're thrown in and that what is behind them isn't actually known. The don't feel very natural especially when you have:

melting of the limbs and torso, decomposition of their body as they are still alive, transformation of their living tissue into a glass-like substance, the continuous generation of metal sharpnel inside the body and [DATA EXPUNGED]
附加记录：    

2. I feel the multitude of effects that can happen in 3509-B is a bit overkill. I think one of these effects would suffice, having multiple effect that can happen seems a bit unnecessary and doesn't really add that much to the article.

3. Addendum 3509-01 honestly makes me wonder if this article wouldn't work better as a joke scip. This part was the best bit for me as I enjoyed seeing all the things the SCP ordered and how the foundation had to scramble to fulfill them.

4. Document 2 bothers me. I really don't feel like the Junior Researcher is an actual person giving actual dialogue. It feels more like he's just giving exposition. I think it's down to the way he talks and the fact he doesn't seem panicked in any way. Lines such as  just don't sound like something any normal person would say, it sounds like maybe an internal monologue or something someone would write in a dramatic letter, not someone who has literally jsut had his legs disappear and is now locked in an SCP.

Overall the idea is fun and the writing itself is fine, I think the tone is a bit all over the place though, too many redactions, expungements, black boxes and I really don't like the dialogue towards the end.

-1 for now but I really hope this gets re-written and comes back as it is an enjoyable idea.
