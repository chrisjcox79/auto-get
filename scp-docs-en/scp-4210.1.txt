The interview was reasonably effective at eliciting an emotional response (likely due to the content of it being surprisingly believable and something I could easily imagine as a reader), though it didn't really develop much of a character or go anywhere that hadn't already been covered by the description.

The other narrative focus was noticeably weaker, especially as it was almost entirely told through a single, overly-expository letter at the end. It attempts to bring a whole new angle to this article, but without either proper justification for suddenly introducing a new take or enough development for it to not feel entirely bolted on.
