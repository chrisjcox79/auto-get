15 Sep 2013 03:07

I was initially a little iffy about this, because it seemed like the Foundation was being unnecessarily cruel to poor Agent █████. But looking at it again, that's not really the case. They gave him a proper bed after it became obvious he might not get out for some time, if ever, and the initial bad vibes I was getting off Dimaccio's footnotes (and subsequent site transfer) went away once I read them in the context of a former MTF superior watching his friend and teammate sink into depression and anger without being able to do anything about it. Realistically, Agent █████ is probably being treated quite well for an SCP victim-turned-object. Whatever privileges he's given are just not mentioned in the article proper, since he's a special case and therefore they're not necessary for containment.

Anyway, even before I came to that realization, I was still going to upvote. Whether I agreed with their treatment of Agent █████ or not, you told the story you wanted to tell and I enjoyed it.

One thing confuses me a little, though. This collapsible:
