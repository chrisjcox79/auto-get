Craptivism

21 Jun 2020 08:06

For CorpseCon, I decided that I wanted to write something about Jewish ideals and power imbalances and what it means to be in control of your own life. It’s also about a guy who shits other people’s pants, but that’s not as pithy a summary.

Thanks to all of my critters for making this less of an absolute mess than it originally was! That includes (but is probably not limited to) Yossipossi. Critique is an important and thankless job, and all critters (ESPECIALLY those on the Butterfly Squad) should be given 6-figure salaries and luxurious offices. I have no ulterior motive here.
