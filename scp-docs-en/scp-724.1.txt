Major conceptual rework

14 Nov 2010 18:12

I had a talk with Erk about this on IRC;

here'slog

bleh

 Erk - you saw my post on the discussion for that RadioKinetic Anomaly ?

 the newer one voct?

 yeah

 Raccoons.

 picture it

 a room full of angry caged raccoons

 all of them talking

 saying random stuff that they've pulled out of radio communications

 hahaha

 Do they have a speaker-crackle voice, or a strange squeaky racoon voice?

 Like, do they act like speakers for the signal

 a combination of (the voice of the person who originally spoke it) and (the raccoon growl)

 That would certainly give the scp a bit more pizzazz

 oh, and because it affects all raccoons *in the vicinity*

 once you capture all the raccoons in a given area

 more will move in

 so the Foundation has to send people out every night

 to hunt down all the raccoons in the area

 Hahaha

 and check

 to see if they speak English.

 Do they remain affected if they leave?

 I think raccoons that get affected should remain radio receivers

 maybe less powerful, but they'll still wander around quoting cell phone communications and things

 At the epicenter they're far more potent though.

 radio racoons?

 yeah

 yeah

 aelanna, we're figuring how to make !scp-724 actually interesting

 raccoons that leave the radius of effect will only quote cellphone stuff if they're way close to a transmitter or something?

 I saw that last night

 hm

 Lei is the original author?

 Yeah

 hm

 maybe they remain receivers, but not speakers?

 how do you mean? If they don't speak anymore it wouldn't be clear they were receiving would it?

 Erk - they get all upset if you broadcast static

 well hey maybe they'll respond and let me do this tongiht?

 dunno

 Personally I think it'd be more fun if they just randomly started speaking on occasion.

 Much more rarely though

 Erk - it's just stuff they intercept from the radio communication

 Erk - that's a wonderful image.

 ?

 okay

 Radio Raccoons, definitely.

 The raccoon crews could disguise themselves as homeless people

 or total rednecks depending on the environment

 neither homeless nor rednecks would have tranq darts

 wouldn't they have enough?

 Oooh

 unless each raccoon was 'tuned' to a certain frequency when it was exposed to the SCP

 Why does this remind me of a Studio Ghibli film?

 Erk - we don't know. So we keep them.

 Just in case.

 so the storage facility consists of rows upon rows of cages labelled with frequencies, each cage containing a raccoon

Voct a VERY ANGRY raccoon

 quoting someone's cell conversation

 don't you love it?

 Or, rather than frequency

 it stops talking and starts eating.

 Each raccoon locks onto a specific signal, changing even when that signal is modulated

 so, like, if it starts picking up the radio transmissions of an al qaeda cell in afghanistan, it will always pick up that cell's transmissions even if they change walkie talkies

 which is useless unless you have someone who recognizes Pashto

 and it's all linked back to the nearby confluence of towers

 and then the coon takes a drink.

 Leading to a massive issue with data sifting

 Erk - the worst part would be when two raccoons are spouting different sides of the same conversation

 hah they probably have a few where they've realised they're tuned to people who often talk to each other.

 so they keep the cages side-by-side

 and then the raccoons start trying to attack each other through their cages

 all the while talking romantically to each other

 For the sake of humour, SCP infection should make the raccoons more ornery

 well

 we're not sure if it's because of the infection

 or because, y'know

 they've been captured and are being kept in small cages

 not *too* small

 we don't want to risk damaging their health

 Actually to be a little more scp'ish, maybe receiving radio transmissions is uncomfortable to them as well

 mildly activating pain centers in the brain consistent with a constant itching sensation

 Erk - then raccoons wouldn't be constantly entering the territory

 they wouldn't feel the itch until they'd been infected, though

 I still don't like it

 Fair enough.

 It's also possible that they are constantly itchy, but emitting the radio signals reduces the feeling

 hmm

 so infected raccoons tend to return to the area if they are clever enough to make the connection, because it's easier to broadcast there

 I gotta go though, battery is dead and no plugin

 ttyl

 good luck with the crazy coons

 [having a reason for them to return to the area might be a good plan, because otherwise it's hard to imagine the Foundation being able to keep this one secret]

 crazy white-trash rednecks babbling about talking raccoons?

 I think it should just be a reasonably good territory for raccoons to live in

 no itch, no pain

 just yummy garbage, safe attics, and the goddamn Foundation shooting them with tranq darts and sticking them in cages.

14 Nov 2010 20:19

I'll try a rewrite based on that idea, since it does sound cool. Not sure I want to have a wide area-of-effect (since that would be sufficiently obnoxious to contain that the Foundation would have just as likely had one of the radio towers shut down), but there are almost certainly some raccoons still out there that were affected before the Foundation moved in and put a building on top of the anomaly. Perhaps even include a suggestion that the effect includes genetic/epigenetic alterations, so that affected specimens can potentially pass it on to their offspring?

Edit: Rewrite complete! I'm tentative about the second part of the last addendum, since it might venture into "too useful" territory. I may pull it depending on what other people think.
