The miniature ecosystems within dead whales is a fascinating topic, and extending that to a social ecosystem is a clever concept.

Crit

Crit

That said, this ultimately falls flat for me. You overload the con-procs and description with a lot of needlessly bulky scientific terms.

There's a lot of ?

The punctuated, short-spoken conversations lack a lot of personality, and you feel the need to pre-establish gendering and age-labeling, which feels unnecessary. The concept itself is also a bit muddled. If these creatures speak through infrasonic vocalizations, aren't we just translating them into English? Why would be imbue gender, age, and language differences like Latin vs. English?

Overall, novote for me. Keep at it though!
