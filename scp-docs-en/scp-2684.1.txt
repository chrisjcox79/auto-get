Wheres the Beach?

16 Oct 2017 01:27

Once, when I was swimming, I got knocked down beneath the waves. When I came back up, I had somehow turned myself around without realizing and was looking out to the ocean. In the seconds before I wheeled around, I thought the beach was gone. This SCP is that panic.

This was an attempt to write an SCP based more on exploration logs than on the actual document itself. I hope that I wrote each of the characters well - each of the five in the article is supposed to have their own voice.

The title is a bad reference to the Wendy's  ads.

Spoilers

If1176.

The ending for the first log was my least favorite, and I've decided to cut out everything after 8872 says that she's already in Hell until she says Help and drowns. Hopefully that makes it a little better.

I do see the similarities between this and 3008, but I think those similarities are less important to the actual SCP - they're both extradimensional places that take people from other realities, but this is quiet, boring and empty unlike the fear and crowds of 3008.
