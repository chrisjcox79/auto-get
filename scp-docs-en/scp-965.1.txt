I'm probably going to go in and fix that up a bit; it seemed to flow better in my head when I read it out. Basically it's thus:

The  they're basically getting carved up from the inside out, rather than from the skin down to the core.

Quite frankly, there is simply too much here. It is a massive wall of text that describes every detail of 965 in a very uninteresting way. It's like you wrote 965, thought to yourself,  and began piling on details.

It stares at you through the window, making you paranoid. Oh, and it also can move between windows. Oh, and it also starts cutting people in their sleep.
