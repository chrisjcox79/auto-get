Reserved for OTC Author Post

09 Mar 2018 00:31

After months upon months of working with Rimple to complete this skip, we've finally finished. I've always been passionate about having a Latvian skip in the database, considering the fact that there are literally zero of them, and I particularly wanted to involve the Latvian Demi-god Lāčplēsis, arguably one of the cooler mythological figures in Latvian folklore.

Between the society revolving around amber, jokes about bread and beer and singing, and even a few Latvian puns, this is a skip I am incredibly passionate about, and I couldn't have done it without the help of Rimple. He has been patient with our difficult time zone differences, and has always been willing to help me learn and grow my ideas until they could reach the point they have.

So from me, a Latvian person, please enjoy the first ever Latvian skip.
