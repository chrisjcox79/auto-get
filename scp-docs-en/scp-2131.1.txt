10 Nov 2014 22:09

I am upvoting for what I freely admit could be seen as an insufficient/shallow reason.

It us cooperating D. Not at gunpoint, not extortion, not forced. Just both agencies working for a common purpose. The biggest thematic failing of the Foundation/SCP-verse, in my opinion anyway, is how rarely the larger Anomalous-interested groups work together. They're at almost out and out war 99% of the time, for honestly petty reasons.

MCD is an especially egregious example. Yes, they are ran and staffed almost entirely by amoral, nihilistic inclined hedonists. But it should be obvious that they don't want our world to burn. They want to make money and have a good time. Maybe at the expense of others, maybe doing unspeakable things to other people, but not wrecking the planet and skull-fucking the remains.

We need more Foundation and other group cooperation. I was ecstatic to see that the Foundation was working with the CotBG in that skip a month or so ago.

So yes, this gets a +1 for the simple joy of seeing the Justice League and Secret Society of Supervillains work together.

As for the object itself, I actually kind of like it on its own merits as well. It's not spectacular, but I love the fact it's an Antipope.
