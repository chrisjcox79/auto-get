Hey 4esthetics, I have a few pointers I'd like to share.

Okay, so this spot is where I would normally point out the missed spelling and grammar mistakes but this has far too many for me to list out. I highly recommend a program called Grammarly it helps greatly for minor SPaG mistakes and I believe a free version is available on most platforms.

So, in my opinion, the dialogue here falls a bit flat, I would suggest going over it with someone experienced in writing authentic dialogue since the interviews are really the bread and butter of this article. While on the subject of the interviews In the second interview, the one with andy, it says both Andrew and Cynthia are being interviewed and only Andy says anything.

I feel you had a really neat concept here, but more could have been done during the writing process to really make it shine. I highly suggest taking advantage of the writing forums and #thecritters on IRC chat; proper critique and thorough reviewing is a good way to really bring out the best article you can. After all, we have a community of great writers here and its a shame not to use that to your advantage. For the reasons listed above its a no-vote from me though I hope to see more articles from you in the future

SoullessSingularity, for any questions or concerns relating to this review.
