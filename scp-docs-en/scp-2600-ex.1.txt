Like this, but given the timeframe the refrence to genetic testing is way out of place and threw me out of the story. Just screening for particular genetic diseases wasn't available until the 70s, the kind of technology implied by saying  seems anachronistic, even assuming the Foundation is a decade or two ahead of the rest of the world technologically.

Still, upvote because the idea's good, and aside from the above, I like the execution.
