Heres hoping this does better than my 3kcon entry

01 Jul 2018 05:24

Sorry it took me so long to post here.

I'm glad a lot of people are liking this. I kind of deliberately avoided writing a Herman Fuller 4000 entry just because that would have been utterly predictable, though if I wanted to I probably could have thought of something since the Circus is old enough and Herman no doubt has had his fingers in a lot of pies over the years.

After Dread C Plastics is the Canon I have the most experience with, so I decided to write an SCP about the hole in the ground that started it all. A Nexus were anomalies all revolved around narration seemed like it had a lot of potential for a 4kcon entry.

Thanks to IHP especially, as well as to everyone else who read this over in chat.
