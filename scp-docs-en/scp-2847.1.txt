Fold

06 Feb 2017 15:34

Well mainly because the thing is in a very delicate situation right now? If -2 were to be neutralized, -K will break free, and Foundation doesn't have a completely safe backup plan in case that happens. And -2 could just be very easily killed so Foundation doesn't want to draw any attention to it.

The fake version is possibly the  on the Foundation database, which aims at misleading people into thinking the monster is already sealed safely in a thousand-meter deep iron lake, and is unlikely to break free just like it has been for thousands of years. The fake version undermines -2's role so that people won't notice and the interview gives just enough info to present yet another vague story.

And in a narrative sense, all the actual Sarkic/CotBG are emphasized in the second version. I was hoping to create a sense of, presenting the conventional vague myth story in the first version, and revealing a much detailed and grounded truth in the second one ._.

Still, it's fair that if you feel like it's repetitive, and thanks for reading.
