Site Criticism Team - Staff Review

07 Jun 2020 20:26

Alright, let's check this out.

+Crit

-Crit

The object is kept in a medically induced coma, and its brain activity is to be monitored at all times through EEG.
附加记录：    

No comma needed here.

Should SCP-5474 exhibit brain activity above a level of 3.8hz, or any other indications of having entered REM sleep, Site Command should be notified immediately and a yellow-alert status will be maintained until activity subsides.
附加记录：    

No first comma needed here.

All on-site personnel are to be issued handheld mirrors to carry on their person at all times, for the purposes of identifying instances of SCP-5474-1.
附加记录：    

No comma needed here.

SCP-5474 is former Foundation field medic Janet Corbyn. A .30 caliber round is lodged 3cm deep within SCP-5474's cranium. SCP-5474 is alive, although it has sustained severe damage to the occipital lobe. All prior attempts to remove the bullet have been met with failure
附加记录：    

I think these sentences could be played with to make them more complex, and therefore more interesting to read. Just something as simple as combining a few of them would do the trick. Honestly, this is also a piece of general advice, as most of your sentences read as super short and stiff.

Despite the chemical composition of the liquid (blood and water)
附加记录：    

Water is definitely a chemical composition, but blood really isn't. I'd cut out  for accuracy.

it lacks a single color
附加记录：    

single - distinct

This fluid, if allowed to escape from the cranial hole, becomes an SCP-5474-1 instance.

SCP-5474-1 instances originate from the wound sustained by SCP-5474.
附加记录：    

You're just saying this twice. I would cut out the first sentence and leave the second, perhaps giving it more detail.

SCP-5474-1 instances are humanoid in form, and are dressed in Foundation-issue fatigues.
附加记录：    

Unnecessary comma.

2. Most commonly is the color of manifestations' hair.
附加记录：    

This doesn't have to be a footnote, just put it after a comma. Tbh, all three of your footnotes contain information that could be easily worked into the sentence itself. I don't think they justify their own existence.

Only non-direct methods of viewing
附加记录：    

non-direct - indirect

instance logically should appear
附加记录：    

Cut .

Individuals who have conversed with an SCP-5474-1 manifestation will profess a deep feeling of grief and, in some cases, minor amnesia, upon the termination of the instance.
附加记录：    

profess - express, and also the comma after amnesia is unnecessary.

some members of staff noted a person making their way out of the cranial tissue feet first.
附加记录：    

 or something similar.

Camera monitors contacted site security, citing delusions on part of the medical staff.
附加记录：    

I would argue that site security are the ones manning the cameras anyways.

Working with security, the team utilized night vision goggles to locate the SCP-5474-1 manifestation, and terminated it.
附加记录：    

It's unclear who .

modern containment procedures were put in place.
附加记录：    

Modern - present or current.

Upon arrival, nearly all known anartist and GoI hideout locations had been found emptied of their contents.
附加记录：    

All as in all? Like even unrelated ones? If not, definitely clarify this.

Overall, this was a fun little article. It sets up a fun little anomaly with a decent bit of body horror and it resolves everything nicely. +1 from me.
