Author Notes

06 Apr 2014 03:28

Many thanks to CryogenChaos, weizhong, Dmatix, Athena_Grey, EdAWACSdenyY, Pig_catapult, Devereaux, Silberescher, and Vincent_Redgrave (and probably someone else I forgot…) for taking a look at this in chat.

Did you know that 12-19km/h is the speed of a “gentle breeze” according to the Beaufort scale?

Author’sNotes

hide

For the record, the goddess Kokio is a deity I made up. She’s the youngest of five, and all her older sisters are more popular and accomplished and it ticks her off so she made this little raincloud to try and show off how great she is, too. Both she and the cloud have no idea what they’re doing.

Anyway, my thought process was basically, “what if ancient deities looked to modern-day commercials for religious recruitment strategies?” That said, the “most interesting x in the world” line is obviously not mine, it’s a shout-out to the “Most Interesting Man in the World” commercial line from Dos Equis. I also did not come up with the horse and boat bit, which was done by Old Spice.

Also I wanted to write an SCP where the containment procedures are essentially “hang out on a tropical island and drink flower tea, bro”.

…on a tropical iiiiisland

underneath the molten lava moon…

And also there was this time when I was in high school and there was a huge storm and it poured and everything was miserable and a tornado/waterspout warning was announced over the intercom and people were told to be cautious, of course everyone pressed their faces up to the windows to try and see if there actually was a tornado outside. Most interesting storm in the world.
