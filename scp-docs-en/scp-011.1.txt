Fold

04 Oct 2011 00:07

The residents do not seem to know about it, as the cleaning of the statue is performed in the dark. One guy reporting weird behavior isn't the same thing as this being the whole town's secret. If they do know about it, why? That goes against the way the Foundation acts, especially since all it would take is one person alerting the media. Continuing, the object is now apparently in the custody of someone it loves. Even though it's being monitored, this seems somewhat dangerous as the custodian might someday return the affection and perhaps smuggle it off somewhere.

Also, there is no containment facility, all they ever did was clean the place. Which makes me ask, WHY was there never a containment facility? Usually anomalous items of this nature are smuggled away under cover of darkness and replaced with the kind that don't do weird things. As this statue is capable of removing itself from its pedestal, it's clearly movable. So what's the deal here? Why is this SCP allowed to run free when all it takes is one little thing going wrong for the Foundation to have to clean up the mess and bust out the amnesiacs.
