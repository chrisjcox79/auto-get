You know, I wanted to make the 'base' frequency 432Hz for that reason, but then I thought: what if the effect of SCP-814-1/2 is to .

In terms of description of what's happening, there was a deliberate choice made to couch that in scientific terms. I removed some of that during the drafting process  potential.

By the way, the only Easter egg in the frequencies, other than the choice of 440Hz as the base tone, is that they are factors of 4 apart, except 55Hz. And I picked that one, rather than 110Hz, because I imagined people with decent speakers trying it on tone generators to see what it sounded like, and then: bass!
