This one is dedicated to my stepdad, who loves wine and hates reading instruction manuals.

Image Credit: public domain image from here, modified by me.

Thanks to ModernMajorGeneral in chat for reading over this!

Spoilers/Commentary

–block

I honestly haven't decided whether these wine bottles actually come from Alagadda or that's the marketing angle MCD have decided to use. I might write a MCD-format article that explains that, either as the result of a (possibly millenia-long) trade agreement with one of the Masked Lords or as a clever marketing trick to spin some boring old blood rituals into something more exotic.

Originally this actually was going to be about a millenia-old relationship between MCD and Alagadda, but then Enzo appeared and I decided to cut all the ancient stuff about transport amphorae and ancient Roman religion. Thanks Enzo.

–block
