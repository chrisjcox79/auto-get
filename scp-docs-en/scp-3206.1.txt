telemarketing skips
附加记录：    

A quick search of the wiki reveals only one mainsite article that explicitly mentions telemarketing. There are other articles that involve phone calls, but none that specifically revolve around people trying to sell stuff over the phone. I'm not sure where this fatigue is coming from.

even kidnapping the person making the calls is an old move.
附加记录：    

The last addendum serves to establish that this isn't necessarily true, and while this John Ochieng is never found, it isn't ever established that this is even a real person either. Thomas Holloway alludes to this ambiguity above quite aptly. Yeah, they might have kidnapped someone, although it's equally possible that it's just a ploy to sell more VNOs - and it evidently worked. It's about taking expectations and using them to achieve the sole driving goal of whoever is behind this.
