I feel like I should clarify my position a bit. My breaking point for interviews is reached when they are used to carry the SCP rather than enhance it, which seems to be not uncommon lately, souring me a bit on the use of them.

Maybe it's a quirk in my preferences, but IMO the core anomaly of an SCP should usually be the metaphorical cake, with interviews and other such stuff the icing. I'm probably not gonna eat a piece of stale toast slathered in a two-inch thick layer of icing.

Bringing this back to the article, it succeeds in capturing my interest long before we even get to the interview (good!) and the interview adds to the skip and doesn't feel like a waste of time/space (also good!).
