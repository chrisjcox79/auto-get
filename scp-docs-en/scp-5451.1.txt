Rieview

28 Jul 2020 14:06

I can't believe this is up already!

Firstly - I think this is an amazing job for your first SCP. The formatting, the forums, offsets, usernames, everything is incredibly well done. I had to double check that you hadn't written anything before.

Gonna put this in a collapsible before a mod gets me for lengthy comments. :P

+Rieview

-pun!

So - here are the other positives.

The idea is cute, quirky, and handled well. The vector of communication, eg the forums and chat logs, feels natural and it allows us time to see Cook change in their motivation.

The pacing is also mostly good - 3 pages does feel long, but everything is interesting enough to sustain. That's not to say there aren't places where it doesn't drag, but I found it snappy enough.

And the humour is appropriately cute but never really hit me over the head with its quirkyness.

Now - things to work on in the future.

Pacing. There are Many chatlogs. How many of them are necessary? What's their purpose? Every time you include one, that's a choice to balance pacing and humour. You need to make sure people are happy with the loss of pacing.

Ending. This wraps up really quickly. Somewhat to the detriment of the article. You build up too little for this let-down to be intentional, but too much for it to satisfy the reader. I'm at a loss for what happened.

Naming your addenda - super minor, but they are all variations on . I understand you want the logs themselves to reveal the information, but it's a bit repetitive.

A happy +1.
