I'm not sure how to improve the execution - there could be more content on either results of the 'contest' or a recovery story but I'm not sure if either of those would be an improvement. There is always the not-started experiment log to do, I originally conceived this as a log-based sort of SCP.

I don't think SCP-114 is particularly similar but yeah SCP-946 is. I didn't realise I gave them almost the same name (no this is not actually named internet forums). I think I get away with the similarities considering that 'discussion' is not a very common SCP topic, if this were a 'scary door' this similar to another scary door I would think differently.

As to the XK classification, I was under the impression there were a bunch of classifications and XK is sort of a 'everything blows up' event, which this isn't so I made one up. If it's too silly I'll change it.

Actually - history lesson! - XK started out as religious apocalypse, and turned into 'salted earth'. And CK is generally thought to mean reality restructuring (though, again, no canon). Of course, if an article decides a CK-class restructuring is an invasion of psychedelic locusts and an XK-class end of the world is the Republicans/Democrats winning the next presidential election, there's nothing wrong with that (besides the lameness of the examples, of course :P).
