This alignment must be upheld at all costs. SCP-2995-1 must maintain a distance from one another of exactly 9.56 meters; to facilitate this, the instances are bound to one another by a series of reinforced steel supports.
附加记录：    

Matter will expand or contract when temperature changes due to thermal expansion, and metal is especially susceptible to this, so the temperature of the containment chamber would have to be kept constant. Though it would probably be better to make the connecting supports out of a material that undergoes little thermal expansion.
