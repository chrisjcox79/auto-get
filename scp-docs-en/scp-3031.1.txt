Fold

11 Apr 2017 16:23

I will address your points individually.

On the convenience of the time travel: I knew that would be a sticking point for some when I started this, as the acclaimed Tim Duncan skip received the same criticism. For all we know, Reality 1 already had an EXPERTISE collecting dust, with no real reason to use it. They are several centuries ahead, after all.
On the precise targeting of 3000: I did a lot of research into neurology when writing this, but I can only fudge so much science. I'm gonna have to leave that as plot convenience, though I tried my best to at least make it somewhat plausible.
On the world-ender: I don't think my writing skills are developed enough to write the next 2317. I knew if I focused on the entity itself, it would likely fall flat as  bit is me trying to customize it, as well as tie it into the narrative. It's not described much, as I was attempting to use the indirect horror as seen in Kalinin's 2798.
On the note: Yes, I'm very aware that final notes are a controversial plot device. I am not used to writing prose like this. I started out by repurposing the Note from the Administrator, and by the end had added several other allegories and threads. Esperanza is meant to sound somewhat flowery, but that's fair if it's not your taste. If I had had more time, I might have tried overhauling the note to arrange the threads better, because I can see your point that it's kind of Frankenstein'd together. And out of universe, I was trying to find a killer finishing metaphor.
On the explanatory power of the note: Esperanza has now realized that even if the rewritten reality can beat the entity, there will be so much more they have to stand up to. Therefore, best to describe the general mission, and the weakness of the entity. I don't feel that  would help as much.

Thank you for your thoughts, though; I'll certainly take them into account if I polish this up after the contest.

The original future doesn't have a Foundation. Note that different terminology is used, most significantly that Esperanza isn't an O5.
