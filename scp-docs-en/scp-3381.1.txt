Really interesting concept! I think it would be good of closing a few minor loopholes however:

- When (non-lucid) dreaming, things like interviews prepared while awake are hard to do, since the dreamer might not even remember anything about their awakened self.

- You can't actually read in dreams if I remember correctly. You can use dream logic to your advantage here however; the dreamer can magically know that this is a movie studio and never question why they actually know this.

Would it be better for the narrative consistency if the dreams while using the SCP are always lucid?

Apart from that, well done! +1
