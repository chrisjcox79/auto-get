+block

–block

This little detail

~~ - Sam Walton~~
附加记录：    

this observation

Typing and easy listening jazz can be heard.
附加记录：    

and this visual

Stomach wall contracts on the left of the frame as gas bubbles shift in the digestive acid. Cheering and excited vocalization by a group of about 15 voices can be heard in the distance.
附加记录：    

and an all-dolphin MTF are highlights for me.

My only note for correction would be a fine, split hair over the anatomy presented:

19-278 seconds: Camera rests against sphincter leading into large intestine

279 seconds: Camera passes through duodenum into large intestine.
附加记录：    

The duodenum is the very first part of the small intestine that connects with the stomach. The most distal segment of the small intestines is called the ileum and is what connects with the large intestine.
