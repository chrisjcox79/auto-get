31 Dec 2016 02:22

While I do enjoy the extent (and futility) of David the balloon's escape attempts, the SCP is multiple balloons. What about the rest of the balloons? This feels to be a big plot hole. If the article focused on that one balloon from the get-go, it would be a non-issue. But do other sentient balloons feel the same way? Do they empathise their friend? Do they help David? Do they hate David?

Thus, I think the other balloons should also be accounted for in the article beyond broad mentions and definitions.
