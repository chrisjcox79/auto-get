I think the number and length of designations may be contributing to this. Perhaps there's a cleaner way to name everything?

+block

–block

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Would it be feasible to simply cut  when discussing residents, and rearrange what's left to place their location over their individual designations (For ease of reference, I figure it'd flow easier to know immediately which town they belonged to rather than which resident they are of the town.)?

For the agents, maybe a symbol could be used to further differentiate them from residents? Also, unless the names are super important, I'd consider cutting them; see how that affects readability.

So:

SCP-3322-1568-A (Mr O. Vidal), wielding a hunting rifle, shoots and kills his wife, SCP-3322-1569-A (Mrs M. Vidal), during a heated argument. SCP-3322-0-B (Agent B. Ganbold) is informed of these events 2 hours later by SCP-3322-0-A (Agent G. Martin) and he investigates the residence of SCP-3322-1568-B (Mr A. Gantulga) and SCP-3322-1569-B (Ms O. Bayarmaa).
附加记录：    

Would look like:

A-1568, wielding a hunting rifle, shoots and kills his wife, A-1569, during a heated argument. Ω-B is informed of these events 2 hours later by Ω-A and he investigates the residence of B-1568 and B-1569.
附加记录：    
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

This:

SCP-3322-0-A (Agent E. Santos) and B (Agent V. Navalny) are discovered to have been involved in an intimate relationship with SCP-3322-491-A (Ms C. Herrera) and B (Ms O. Erdenchimeg) for the previous 3 months.
附加记录：    

Would look like:

Ω-A and Ω-B are discovered to have been involved in an intimate relationship with A-491 and B-491 for the previous 3 months.
附加记录：    

(Hell, you could also call the personnel: Operative-B and Operative-A)

Operative-A and Operative-B are discovered to have been involved in an intimate relationship with A-491 and B-491 for the previous 3 months.
附加记录：    
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

This:

SCP-3322-A. SCP-3322-2201-A and SCP-3322-3977-A visible in foreground.
附加记录：    

Would appear as:

SCP-3322-A. A-2201 and A-3977 visible in foreground.
附加记录：    
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

In cases where name matters, simple rewording should provide sufficient context:

On 1/03/1995, SCP-3322-1-B (Mayor C. Batu) announces he will use his personal funds to construct a house for the use of SCP-3322-0-B (Agent B. Ganbold)
附加记录：    

On 1/03/1995, the mayor of SCP-3322-B announces he will use his personal funds to construct a house for the use of Ω-B.
附加记录：    
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Hell, maybe you can drop the designations altogether in some places and go with names instead:

Mr O. Vidal, wielding a hunting rifle, shoots and kills his wife during a heated argument. Agent B. Ganbold is informed of these events 2 hours later by Agent G. Martin and he investigates the residence of the corresponding couple in Buyant.
附加记录：    

Agents E. Santos and V. Navalny are each discovered to have been involved in an intimate relationship with corresponding civilians for the previous 3 months.
附加记录：    

On 1/03/1995, the mayor of Buyant announces he will use his personal funds to construct a house for the use of Operative B.
附加记录：    
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Not perfect, but you could stand to play around with it until you find something workable.
