Special Containment Procedures:  SCP-3-J is to be kept in a fries-lined containment chamber located in Fort Knox, where it it is to be guarded at all times by no less than 7 Speech Language Pathologists armed with boxes of wine.

In the event that SCP-3-J ever begins running it's face, Gears is to bites SCP-3-J untill it ceases this behaivor. In the event of a contaiment breach, Mobile Task Force Ξ 7 (''The River'') is to be dispached to SCP-3-J's last known location.

Description:  SCP-3-J is a truck-shaped Angler fish. Like most members of it's species, it is able to molest boys, and regularly eats twice it's own weight in fries each day.

SCP-3-J's unusual properties manifest whenever it comes in contact with moose, which causes it to turn into gecko. Whenever this happens, all jellyfish within a 2 kilometer radius will begin to was uncontrollably, usually leading to civilian casualties.
