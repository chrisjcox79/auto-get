First of all, I would like to thank TheBlueHour for reading through the initial draft of this article and giving their opinions.

This is my entry for the 3rd theme in JamCon 2020 of  block attached to the rating module as well as other relevant information.

In this circumstance, I opted to use  to separate the two portions of the article which when done makes the page source generally inaccessible on the parent page. With this, the two fragment pages have been linked in the page source on the primary parent page1.

Thank you all for reading and I hope you enjoy.

▶◀

▷◁

Hello, is anyone there? Please, I feel so cold and I can't see anything. Oh wait, how amazing, I can see everything now. The whole world is just there laid out in front of me just waiting for my help. Oh, there are so many humans and they're so sad. Wait, my friends, why can't you remember me? Don't worry, they'll be here soon and their siblings will get a chance to breathe. Why is there a dead tree here? Well, that doesn't matter now. All that's here now is me and the others. But what's my name? Did someone take it? Well, maybe I can have a new one. I always used to go to some theme park oh what was it? Ah, that's it, Cragglewood! Now all I need is some fun. I don't yet know how but when I was thinking about the carousel I used to go on as a child, it just appeared today and is now next to me now. I don't know how it happened but it's here. I think I'm going crazy. I'm going to try and send for help but I don't know how I'm going to do it. I guess a video or maybe a letter. I just don't know.

As for what this article is about, I'll give you all a hint: carnival.

That's all for now.
