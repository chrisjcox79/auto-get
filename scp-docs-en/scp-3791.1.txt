Certain particulars seem to rub off the wrong way; namely the description of this as a . That's usually used to mean time is flowing faster or slower (or even backwards) in a certain area, this is more things being restored to a specific point in history (in a way that depends on completely arbitrary human-defined dates).

Also, I'm not seeing any sort of coherent pattern here. You need at least a hint of rhyme to imply that there's a reason, even if there isn't enough to be certain what it is.
