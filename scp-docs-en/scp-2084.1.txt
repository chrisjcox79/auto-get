Either I'm being too subtle or I'm stupid (most likely one of those)..

SPOILERS

Itagain

The real agents are catching glimpses of what was happening in the schoolkids being vaccinated against this bio-weapon, the dead being cremated, etc.

The fictional agent is (or was) on-location to the actual events, describing the effects of the bio-weapon, and the botched vaccinations. Due to time-space shenanigans, the real agents caught brief glimpses, and one of the agents managed to slip his report into our reality.

The implication was that this school building was being used to test a vaccine against the bio-weapon, but then became an emergency aid station due to war with an unknown enemy. The point of the  was to shift the school building (and presumably other buildings) into an alternate reality in order to avoid the war and the spread of the bio-weapon (as presumably they had contained it within the school) thus giving them either more time to work out a vaccine, or at least let the uninfected escape alive.

Except the people keep blinking in and out of existence, with inconsistent chronologies, indicating the  either failed or wasn't thoroughly tested before use.

If none of that got through, I'm sorry. Maybe should I dump more exposition into the last log, or re-work the explorations to be more informative?
