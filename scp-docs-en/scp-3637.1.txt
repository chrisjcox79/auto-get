04 Jul 2017 08:23

I'm really glad you liked it!

Deep down I've always wanted to write an SCP inspired by the Song of Solomon passage, but could never find the right concept. I made the connection to the Jeremiah verse quite a bit later on, when I wanted a thematic subdesignation for the event.

Maiasaura fossils are often found in herds and communal nests; my personal canon is that the mother was caught in a massive flood while out searching for plants to cover the nest in, and this flood was big enough to wipe out the colony as well. I admit to a tiny bit of guesswork as to the distance between her body and the nest, but if anything, the plot convenience is that only her nest was recovered, which is easily fixed if you imagine that the rest of the nests are also close by, dug up but not included in this file because they're not important.
