15 Feb 2018 14:32

It's been a while since I've seen an object that pulls off the escalating-WTF factor so effectively, to me at least.

Each of Jessica's iterations made my brows rise exponentially higher, and the lack of story is made up for by the sheer fucking weirdness. I love every part of this, especially how it ended. I saw it coming, but I'm very curious how Jessica could possibly top herself. I imagine she might go back to Euclid, since her human forms seem consistently human-sized, but her screams transform people who hear them into domestic pigs the size of Research Installations.

Or a Kaiju-sized Jessica Lambert, forever cursed to wander the Earth with the power to scream buildings (and everyone in them) into pigs. Or pigs into buildings. Or people into pigs with buildings for organs Oink.
