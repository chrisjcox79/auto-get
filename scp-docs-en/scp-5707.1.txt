16 May 2020 20:28

There's no reason this anomaly is Uncle Sam narrative-wise besides just being a hook which fails to follow through. Besides that, the meat of this article is the characterization of the anomaly1, which fails to capture me due to a lack of tension and conflict. Sure, he disagrees with the ASCI over whether he should be contained, but it's never developed beyond  The Foundation is secretly using his ability to essentially feed human meat to D-Class, but he never gets a chance to respond to it and grow as a character. This article is thwacked off at the trunk, so the anomaly never gets the chance to grow into a truly fleshed-out character.

This is a -1 from me.

Footnotes
1. Along with some minor gore in the testing log, which I have to admit that I enjoyed.
