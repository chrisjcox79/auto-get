Thanks to all who looked at this for me! I’ve always wanted to make a skip chase the Foundation, and by happy coincidence, I found that the best way to do that would be to have it take place in a time where they weren’t as big and powerful. Here, they are just starting to get big.

All of these pictures originated from the visual records, Agatha (SCP-1913-1) from artifacts, Telly (SCP-1913-2) from humanoids, and Freddie (SCP-1913-3) from animals.

Anyway, I hope you all enjoy! ЖωЖ
