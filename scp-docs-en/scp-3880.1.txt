I've never made an SCP with audio before. I'd never even used audacity except to directly record my voice and then send that raw, no editing. Therefore it was very wise of me to decide I'd learn all that, PLUS learn how to stream on YouTube, in one day, while also attending lectures and doing an exam that counted for 20% of a module.

This is my third entry for the 72-hour Jam, and probably my favourite of the 3 - DJ Chaac has very quickly come to mean a lot to me. Fingers crossed that love comes across, even if half of what I'm actually saying doesn't. My other entries are SCP-3224, so go check those out.

Thanks to Stallmantic and my offsite friend Grimble for feedback in the early stages, when this was just a skeleton with audio. Thanks to LordStonefish for being the only person to read the first draft, and liking it so much they didn't notice I hadn't written any containment procedures.

DJ Rimp out.

PS: That stream is real. I'm playing chill music on it rn. Who knows what it'll be when you click.
