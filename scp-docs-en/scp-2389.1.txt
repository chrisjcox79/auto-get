The idea of using this to finance the Foundation's operations was actually a suggestion on my first draft in the forums (believe me when I say the original addendum was way more lolFoundation), but personally I think it would make sense. The Foundation needs to build secure facilities, purchase the materials to do so, pay their employees ridiculous sums and probably a load of other stuff I've failed to mention, all of which require ridiculous sums of money.

If the Foundation can be sure an anomaly is, to a degree, safe to use and can be used to finance operations of the Foundation then I feel that this would be exploited. Just disposing of the pure gold would be putting it to waste - The Foundation could easily make money from selling it. Although the Foundation contains things that are very abnormal, the Foundation needs to think realistically - long term budgeting, logistics and so forth.

But thank you for liking the article, it's much appreciated.
