I was really tempted to end it at the podcast. The final two addendums were, ultimately, kinda tacked on at the end. Together, they serve two purposes: To establish that this horrible thing has horrible consequences (the teeth aren't the only thing that grows back), and to establish that Lucien Marchand is a hypocrite and a fraud.

I'll give some thought as to whether or not to trim it. I feel like I wanted to go a little over the top for once (rather than something restrained), but if more people feel like the last two addendums aren't needed, I'm very comfortable just junking them. The final line in the log is a pretty good (albeit weird) stinger.

Also, thanks for the compliment! I mention it in the author's post, but the conversation is based on this interview (which inspired the skip).
