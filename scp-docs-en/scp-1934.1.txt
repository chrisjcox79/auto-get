There's some awkward wording in there:

SCP-1934 is located in Site-19 behind a locked door specifically labeled as SCP-1934 in Corridor 6 of the seventh floor.
附加记录：    

The office was in the process of being cleaned out when the anomaly was discovered by custodial staff as they attempted to enter the office to remove the remaining items.
附加记录：    

No further information on SCP-1934 can be detected given the technological capabilities of the Foundation.
附加记录：    

All sentences that could probably benefit from a rewrite. There might be more, but these jumped out at me.

Also:

Access is restricted outside of testing.
附加记录：

Anyway, none of those things bother me too much, because in the end this is some weird shit and I - like Kalinin - can almost taste Marlowe's discomfort as she sees herself mutilated over and over again.
