Hi. This is a reworked version of SCP-5395 I posted a while ago. This version is mainly meant to solve two core issues that I think lessened the quality of the original piece. Those being a bit too global incident log (which was previously about Ennui Protocol) and lack of action on the O5's part after them supposedly realizing what they just stumbled upon.

Spoilers: 

+block

–block

The place itself is an observable part of the noosphere. It looks like this mainly because peoples' brains are filling the gaps in terms of visual representation because the conceptual realm kinda lacks it in the physical sense. Or maybe it's the anomaly itself making it look this way, choose the version you find more appealing.

Huge thanks to everyone who checked out the draft for the first version of this, also thanks to MaliceAforethought for looking at the new draft.
