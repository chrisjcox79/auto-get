18 Dec 2012 21:45

I'm kind of wondering why this isn't an EX-SCP. Sure, it's unheard of, but isn't it scientifically explainable? The venom is a liquid that undergoes an extreme endothermic reaction on contact with the air, and there are other creatures that can survive being frozen with no ill effects (i.e. cockroaches, tardigrades).

I guess I'm saying that the only really anomalous thing about this SCP is that it doesn't seem to have come from any sort of evolutionary progression - that it seems to have just come out of nowhere; and while all the genetic mutations it would take to make this kind of tarantula happening all at once in one embryo is extremely unlikely, it's not quite impossible, is it? If it's completely scientifically explainable, it's not an SCP…right? Am I missing something here?
