Soooo this has been in the works for five months – it’s my my first actual skip and my second article on the mainlist! Thank you to the kind people who critted this on the forums and on Reddit, your input made this article much better!

+block

–block

I’ve left a number of things deliberately ambiguous, but I can say that:

The Archivists are involved with archiving the multiverse. They preserve low-entropy objects (such as technology and biological organisms) from the heat deaths of universes.

If there’s anything you found confusing, or you anythng you think needs more fleshing out, please mention it here!

+block

–block

All resources are mine, except those listed here:

Logashkino is https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Abandoned_factory,_Railway_Tulcea-Medgidia_01.jpg
The paper texture is http://bashcorpo.deviantart.com/art/Grungy-paper-texture-v-8-37941453
The laboratory background is https://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/Wikilegal/Copyright_Status_Of_Images_Hosted_On_US_Government_Websites)
The Chinese logo is https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Ministry_of_State_Security_of_the_People%27s_Republic_of_China.svg
The trendy cube-building for Site-62 is https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Montreal_-_QC_-_Habitat67.jpg
The guy with his eyes blacked out is from a bw photo of Sean Connery, I wasn’t able to find the original creator, but I will remove it if need be.
The alien font used in the last section is called “Alien font”, and I got it from a guy on /r/deepdream (https://www.reddit.com/r/deepdream/comments/5k92bg/my_holiday_gift_to_rdeepdream_3_free_fonts/?ref=search_posts)

Also all of the images here are hosted in imgur, I couldn’t figure out how to upload them on the sandbox, so I’ll try and upload as many as possible. (all done now!)

Edit: just to add some context for why this article goes a little nuts on formatting and multimedia, one of the ideas which I had early on in writing this was to make something that had elements of an SCP document and elements of a tale – I'm not sure if I was successful in blending them together, but in any case I'm unbelievably happy to have my first skip upvoted (at the time of writing) to +70! :D
