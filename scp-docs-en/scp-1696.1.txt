I realize this has a somewhat similar effect as SCP-536, but think the look and feel of it are different enough. Also, even if you consider this the goofy kid's version of 536, there are some scary implications:

One of the things I am hoping people get from it is that if you use it with the settings of our own universe, there is probably a little Earth in the box, possibly with an alternate version of you. And then you're going to hit the Reset button

Conversely, we could all be in a giant version of one of these things, and have no way of knowing it.
