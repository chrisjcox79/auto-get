Fold

24 Jan 2018 04:19

Glad you liked it. The theme of this was intended to be 'humans build some really shitty things' (which was incidentally why I included the lead-in descriptions of normal landmines, which I find scarier than a lot of monsters) - the letters and Vietnam refs were meant to maintain that connection.

I also thought the article might need a personal touch to a dry subject, but I can see why people would think the letters are out of place. Depending on how this pans out and what other feedback I get, I might consider replacing them with a more clinical document (like a manifest of items produced and buyers) or getting rid of them.
