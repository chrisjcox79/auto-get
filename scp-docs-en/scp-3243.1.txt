Re: Thank you to Zyn and Friends!

07 Apr 2018 00:40

Oh man, I had a ton of fun with this one. Bardeen did an amazing job with the imagery in the description, and all the little details that made for this being a really heartwarming article full of little easter eggs/fridge logic . I basically just filled in the gaps with the containment and addenda here.

All names were created by Bardeen. I think Dr. Hunter Miles is a cool dude. Here's a quote from chat from Bardeen describing the doctor:

I imagine Hunter Miles is some eccentric weird dude who got an obscure high-level degree in Cryptozoology and then just got hired by the foundation like a stuntman

附加记录：    

I wrote most of my portion while listening to an orchestral version of an Undertale soundrack medley, and in between reading a fanfiction involving a shark-merman twist on the traditional selkie/mermaid tales. I think that's impacted this light, fairytale-like mood for an entity that's thousands of years old, and is basically a chill old god hanging out in the swamps with its little mushroom servants. It likes and looks out for humans because it sees them as kindred spirits.

we all armslugs now /wiggle
