What I get from this and the interview log is that this thing is like a monk fish, it attracts its pray with a lure (the shipwrecked sailor) than goes in for the kill once the pray has taken the bait. While this has been done before, this instance seems to have enough uniqueness to stand out on its own. The interview log however is a bit unwieldily, I would recommend shortening it, removing some of the drama, and making the reveal a bit less blatant and more interesting (The lure isn't charismatic in its trickery, it does not need to be as in is merely a worm on a line. maybe make the lure more of a insentient, poorly painted guise; constantly thanking its , and inconsistent in its story.). Most importantly the two pages should be melded.

+1 for the SCP
