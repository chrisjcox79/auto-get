Fold

11 Sep 2010 16:01

Even if it doesn't reflect IR, it's not stated to actually be transparent to it, and both it and the air around it are cooler than ambient temperature. That said, it should be made clear in the article whether 630 and 630-1 are opaque or transparent - if they're opaque, then they'd still be visible, just totally black.

In response to Tom90deg below, I'm assuming that the emergency containment protocol would basically entail surrounding 630 with normal water ice, which appears to be unaffected by it. I do agree that the ambiguity about whether 630's meltwater is normal or not could stand to be addressed.

My own little quibble - I honestly think it's slightly less effective that once 630-1 freeze up, they turn into pure ice. Removing that note would leave the possibility that there's something or someone frozen into the main glacier somewhere. Mysteries like "is someone or something inside there", "what is it", and "why is it there" can be very nice to have in an SCP article.

Upvoted in spite of the quibbles, because I love the concept.
