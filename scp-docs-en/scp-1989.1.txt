An interesting, weird idea that's well-written and executed. I'm giving this an upvote, but I have a few nitpicks:

I don't like that the last numeral of the year that this was recovered was redacted. There's no reason for it.
Die Hard came out in 1988, not 1974.
I don't get the note. Who's Carter? It sounds like Derwent Masterson is sending the SCP as a gift to someone named Carter, but it's recovered among Derwent's effects.
Also, I don't particularly care for the  angle here. It's either implying that the SCP made him lose his mind or something, which feels a bit much, or it's an extraneous red herring.
