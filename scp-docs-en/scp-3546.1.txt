Fold

24 Dec 2018 03:32

I don't think she's sad simply because they're not original. She started painting as something to do now that her husband was dead. Founding out that they were, in a way, after-the-fact copies took that special quality from making them.

And notice, this isn't one after the other. There is significant time between most of these. She's not home with her cats. She doesn't have her husband. The Foundation won't let her live her life her own way, other than to do the thing that is simply no longer special to her. She has nothing. So she took a way out, and even that was a copy.

At least, that's how I see it. Still tragic as hell. And in a way that doesn't really fall into the classic SCP tropes. Great stuff.
