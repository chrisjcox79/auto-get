Fold

21 Jul 2020 11:22

This feels like a start of a very good scip with tons of interesting exploration

Writing is good, idea solid. But when I got hooked, I received a hint of what is in 3 out of 12 floors of the anomaly.

Floor 11- Foundation would definitely try to crack into the file storage to find out what is/was going on. Possibly thing if they can use the computing power.

Floor 6- Habitating area of what definitely does not look like humans. DNA tests, Chemical tests, All sorts of test regarding the material, residual materials, food, and technology used to prepare the food. Again nice hook. But there is nothing behind it.

Floor 1- A lot of door opening, some medical tools and human skeletons. Someone or something definitely studied the humans. + some green unopenable doors which feels like connection to the owner of the place.

For me this is downvote. Because there is so much hooks that the article just ends abruptly, leaving reader disappointed after a lot of promises. I am curious about the anomaly, I can see its potential, but its not there.
