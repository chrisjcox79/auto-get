23 Jan 2017 03:45

Last line of the SCP reads  Jude lied about creating it to get the Foundation off his back.

However, other than the implication he was blackmailed into activating it, Jude's being genuine.

The main impetus for him creating the statue, then, and why it mirrors 1800 so closely, is because it was designed to get catharsis from having  be a victim to it.

As well, the mirroring is a catharsis due to having been a part of AWCY to begin with, to satirize how inane The Minotaur was while claiming it art. Specifically, the Chuck-E-Cheese tokens takes a potshot at The Minotaur taking coins, the middle finger point to Minotaur's point, the  being nothing but a meme, etc.

As for the character, he's further characterized in both the crosslinks to Different Kinds of Nihilism and The Assassination of Beleaguered Normalcy by the Coward Gamers Against Weed, as well as different GAW tales, if you'd like to further understand him. I'd recommend it; it might give some further depth to this, and they're all wonderful reads.
