21 Sep 2019 10:42

I have two main issues with this.

+block

–block

The first is that I've always been iffy about the use of 'Pataphysics as a synonym for Metafiction, since that wasn't its original intended meaning within the SCP-verse. Rimple, The Great Hippo, and a few other authors have constructed specific narratives regarding the true fictional/metafictional nature of 'pataphysics, which I feel this sort of thing  dilutes slightly. But in the interest of breaking out the high-energy Therisno Cannons, I'm not gonna let that minor gripe stand in the way of an upvote.

The second is something I've been wavering about, and is why I originally no-voted: because this is 'pataphysical/metafictional/metaphysical/'patafictional/whatever, the antagonist here is the author of the original SCP-166, or at least their fictional analogue. This isn't a defence of the original by any means; it's just that this blurs the line between in-universe fiction and out-of-universe fiction in a way that makes me a little bit uncomfortable. But, then again, I suppose that's the point.

Regardless, those things aren't enough to stop me from thoroughly enjoying it. It's certainly better than the original :P
