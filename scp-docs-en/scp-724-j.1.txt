The good: 

Apparently, D-class can just wander around, poke their heads into interviews, and answer the door for pizza.

The bad: 

Almost all of the jokes here feel highly derivative. Both the very first and the very last jokes in this article, the object class and , are directly taken from Sad Roach, and don't even stand on their own as anything other than references to sad roach.

Unfortunately, that's how most of the jokes turn out. They're either standard site jokes (). Rather than putting the original in a new light, you just put it in the exact same light, but dimmer.

I love Sad Roach (still miffed about that Darkblade match), but this just comes across as the off-brand knockoff. The jokes that are references to Sad Roach come off as far too derivative, and the jokes that aren't references to Sad Roach are far too standard. It needs to put in more work to actually be its own thing before it'll be anything other than .
