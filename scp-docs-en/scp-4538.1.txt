I really like the tie in with the Hawaii false alert. I’m a sucker for explaining weird modern phenomena, especially when it’s done as just a footnote.

On the other hand, I don’t really like the lack of narrative tension generated by a SCP that automatically neutralises all risk of nukes ever at no cost.

If I just look at it as a cute and quirky thing that does stuff, rather than a narrative, the concept of devices causing reality to correspond with lyrics of songs has been done before, and with more depth.

I’m gonna no-vote, since there’s elements I really like.
