The story here, about a titanic global AI battle, is pretty good. It is let down by the prose, which reads to me as somewhat stilted in English, I'm afraid. The other issue is with how the story is told - essentially in a recap where the reader knows the outcome in advance, it's not as involving as direct testimony.

A really good version of this story (albeit perhaps in tale form) would have the whole thing as a log from the POV of the scratch MTF trying to break into Sentinel's location and disable it. They would hear snatches of communications from the Foundation AIs, other sites and the O5, but would be continually cut off. You'd have immediate action (getting through the physical infrastructure, the arrival of the gynoids etc), interaction with Sentinel (to give it personality, if you wished) and escalating tension in the same way as in the Incident report as currently drafted. You would also have a neat reversal of the usual log format (which are typically from the perspective of the Foundation, with limited information about the MTF's situation) - this could have full information about the MTF's actions, with limited snippets from the rest of the Foundation to show how important their mission is.
