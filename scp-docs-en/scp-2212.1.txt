Welcome to SCP-2212! 

This is an SCP puzzle hunt, inspired by such things as the MIT Mystery Hunt and its ilk. You solve puzzles to gradually unlock parts of the main page. There's five puzzles:

Page image
Object Class
Special Containment Procedures
Cross-test Log
Inteview

These may be solved in any order you like, and are entirely independent of each other. Solving all five will make clear a puzzle in the Description section (don't worry, a much simpler one), and when that's solved, that will unlock the finale, which is in Twine (and not yet quite done, but it should be ready before anyone but a handful of people reach it).

Note that you do not need to know anything about SCP to tackle these. You will need to read some SCPs for the Cross-Test puzzle, but this was written to be approachable even for people unfamiliar with the site.

I would greatly appreciate it if you don't post spoilers in the discussion threads without putting them in a collapsible, like so:

[[collapsible]]
The answer is [REDACTED]
[[/collapsible]]

I will try to answer people's questions, and may adjust puzzles that people are having difficulty with. The answers to the puzzles will not change, so don't worry.

Tips 

+spoilers

–block

Figuring out exactly what the puzzle is is part of the puzzle — in certain cases, the bulk of it.
There's usually some clear puzzle that you need to solve (e.g. translating the interview), and then a second step where you need to turn that answer into a single English word. This latter step I'll refer to as the .
These require a mix of insight and plugging away. Don't worry if you don't know how to proceed with a puzzle; let it percolate at the back of your brain and come back to it later.
Use pencil and paper! For the page image and the cross-test, I made tools that can help you out, but those are just a substitute for pencil and paper.
Read all of the puzzle. If something doesn't seem relevant at first, it's likely used for the extraction.
I'm not trying to deceive you, just make you think. I tried to avoid blind alleys you can go very far down, and I tried to put up guardrails to stop you from getting too far off track. If it feels like the puzzle is fighting you, you might be approaching it from the wrong direction.
Check the first post of each puzzle's discussion thread for tips specific to that puzzle.
If you've tried and tried on something and still can't make headway, feel free to ask about it in the thread, or by PM. If you ask in the thread, and it concerns something not immediately apparent about the puzzle, please put it in a collapsible to avoid spoiling others.
This has some technical stuff I haven't worked with before, so it's quite possible there's glitches. If you think you've found a glitch, please let me know. If you think there's a way the user experience could be improved, also let me know.
What puzzles you've solved will be saved by a cookie. If you've blocked cookies, this won't work, so be sure to write down the passwords. Otherwise, don't worry about closing SCP-2212.
