The stone, it called to me 

And now I see the things the stone has shown to me 

A rock that spoke a word 

An animated mineral, it can be heard 

This started out as an attempt to collaborate with Djoric on a Horizon Initiative skip five or six years ago. I invited several other authors to contribute over the years, but nobody else really took to the idea the way I did. After years of tinkering, I felt like it was high time I pulled this out of storage and put it on display where it belongs.
