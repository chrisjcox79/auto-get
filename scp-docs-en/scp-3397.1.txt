SCP-3397: My First SCP

01 Jun 2018 01:09

Thanks for reading my first SCP. I've spent over a month working on it and I hope it entertains you or at least doesn't bore you enough to downvote it.

The image in this article is: Death of Pliny the Elder, Roman Naturalist by Louis Figuier, from Vies des Savants Illustres, 1866.

Thank you to all who critiqued and helped me to refine my SCP!
