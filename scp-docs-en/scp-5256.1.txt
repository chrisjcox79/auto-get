It really is quite strange how humans will act even towards the ones closest to them with such anger when faced with something they don’t understand.
附加记录：    

Hi everyone, I would like to give a special thank you to Veralta for their help in critiquing the drafts of this article.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

◎◎

◉◉

Okay, so after reading this — you may be a bit confused. So first off, the thing in the corner of the room is the dead body of a reality-bender; the boy in the window — to be exact and the  are nails. The reason they died was that his parents were deeply religious and in them not understanding their son's anomalous properties, they believed him to be demonically possessed and as stuff like exorcisms didn't work, they resorted to killing him to free his spirit in a way.

All of the weird things in the room are the process of the boy being killed with poison, weapons, and religious objects. PoI-5256 was a friend of the boy who witnessed the murder in the last time she saw him. With the boy not wanting the girl to have to live with the knowledge, he gave himself an anti-memetic filter to remove all knowledge of him. The term  refers to what they call anomalies in general.

With all of this weird stuff happening, the parents fled and are currently being searched for by the Foundation.

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Due to the lack of knowledge regarding SCP-5256's true nature, entry to the anomaly is limited — only being permissible per a formal request to the current acting research head of SCP-5256.
附加记录：    

Stylistic choice maybe, but the em dash isn't necessary here. Put a comma instead, like this:

Due to the lack of knowledge regarding SCP-5256's true nature, entry to the anomaly is limited, and it is only permissible per a formal request to the current acting research head of SCP-5256.

Only a single book pertaining to ceasing witchcraft and exorcisms being deemed  of any interest.
附加记录：    

Weird wording here. maybe this will fix:

Only a single book pertaining to  and exorcisms deemed interest.

This mixture along with stomach acid is also present in small amounts within the air inside SCP-5256.
附加记录：    

Needs some commas, also there's too many preposition phrases in a row:

This mixture, alone with stomach acid, is present in small amounts in SCP-5256's atmosphere.

A wooden crucifix along with stakes made of the same material.
附加记录：    

Along what though? the wall? And just saying  doesn't mean the reader will assume it's the Aconite mixture. Fix:

A wooden crucifix with stakes made of the Aconite mixture.

A plastic syringe containing  medical-grade anesthetics containing  trace amounts of neural tissue which possessed low Hume levels.
附加记录：    

Word choice fix:

A plastic syringe containing medical-grade anesthetics with trace amounts of neural tissue, which registered low Hume levels.
