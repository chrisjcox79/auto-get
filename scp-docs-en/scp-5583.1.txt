This was a tough one for me, I ultimately decided to upvote because I like the idea and parts of the execution. but I have a few issues:

The first log could be trimmed down and the humorous beats within it need to be combed through a bit. I really liked Skippy’s entrance, everyone ignoring it, and the upskirting scheme. My issue here is that, at the end, the humor boils down to Skippy cursing and insulting people. After the first few times, it’s not funny.
While this is a matter of opinion, I feel like Skippy should be characterized more along the lines of a disillusioned performer who doesn’t feel respected. There are definite hints of this, but it needed to be developed further because the main character trait in left with at the end is, angry Scottish man.
The logs at the end do drag a bit. The first three serve pretty much the same purpose, ie, Skippy is clumsy. For pacing’s sake, one should be cut or combined into another one.

I wanted to like this one much more. What’s here is good just it can be better.
