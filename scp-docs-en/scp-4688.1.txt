Author Post - Reviewers and Thanks

28 Jun 2019 01:25

Thanks to these dudes for the crit: pxdnbluesoul

Team Birb, well the Team Birb for Collab-Con came together really quickly, I’d been partner searching for three hours and then Seagull showed up, and this iteration of Team Birb was born. A Mew is even a type of Seagull. It was fate.

I wanted to expand on Eric’s lore, cause I love that kid, and he hasn’t gotten any love in 10 years. His thing is being a kid that has anomalous items, so we tried not to stray too far from that.

I think we’ve got a crazy enough entry for collab con, Maxwellism and Eric, only Team Birb would do that. This concept doesn’t work, but to me it does.

Thanks for reading!
