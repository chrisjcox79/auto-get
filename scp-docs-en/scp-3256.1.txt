This is what happens when you marathon Criminal Minds and Mindhunter.

27 Feb 2018 22:25

Before anyone asks: no, this was not originally going to be an entry for the Murder Mystery contest, though I wish I could have put that in, but it only accepted new work, and this had been on my sandbox for yonks since before I wrote Black Autumn, even.

As the title of this post suggests, this is the result of me marathoning several TV shows of varying quality, form trashy procedural shows like Criminal Minds to uncomfortable masterpieces like Mindhunter to the excellent Fringe.

My thought process was:  and then the murder mystery contest happened and now we have about ten. :T

Any errors are unintentional. If you see any glaring ones, please drive to my house and punch me in the face with corrections (by .)

Thanks to my beta readers: MayD, and anyone else who I've missed over the last several months of me getting this picked over.
