First of all, very special thanks to Lordstonefish for creating the audio track for this, my first multi-media SCP.

For everyone who didn't click on each of the many crosslinks in this SCP, here's a list of all the Cameos:

The two Elf girls are Ellette Carling and her sister (the girl who met her when she got home) from my own SCP-3903. Ellette's an Elf now as well, having retaken her Fey name (I will write a tale about this eventually).

The staff member is FallenZealot from the tale Iris Dark. He apparently survived whatever she and Percy did to him in Osteontronic Cubes.

The demon is H'rasm'Kal, who works for MCD, and the Clowns are obviously Eugene and Pius from the Circus of the Disquieting.

The girl with the zombie-thing is Katrina, a necromancer created by ninevolt.

The Man in the hat is supposed to be smiling man from SCP-507.

Chaz is Chaz Ambrose, who owns a chain of anomalous restaurants, and the girl scouts are probably from Night Vale, which I've implied exists in my Canon before.

Thanks to all my beta readers: ihp, lordstonefish, IAmTheOoga, Leveritas, KindlyTurtleRock, Othello and uncle nicolini.
