Initially i was going to give this a down vote because of how unreasonably vague it felt. But that ending definitely made up for it in my opinion, that was a great little twist on an otherwise mildly weird bird.

For me personally there is a conflicting feeling, i think it might need a bit more development at the beginning lest it lose a reader too early, though it is short enough that may not be an issue. You eat the egg and act strangely and then there is an  at the base of your spine, but then of course too much development would ruin the rather fun ending of the interview.

Just my own personal thoughts on it, i can't say i have a solution to suggest, after finishing the article i very much enjoyed it so +1 for sure.
