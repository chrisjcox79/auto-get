As I read this, I could tell the author had an advanced sense of literary composition and abstract thought, but I was pulled out of my immersion due to several uncharacteristic terminological redundancies.

+block

–block

“Containment operations are considered finished once SCP-4018-A is no longer operational”

“Its construction is consistent with similar models constructed in Europe in the 17th century.”

“This effect makes it difficult to ascertain the exact nature of the correlation between control SCP-4018-A's control over SCP-4018 and its success,”
