This is a fairly straightforward article but the anomaly is memorable and I like the idea of an anomalous organization punishing people involved in copyright.

Minor quibble:

If the subject fails to retrieve their book within twenty-four hours, steals or damages the book, or fails to send it to the literary foundation mentioned within forty-eight hours, they will disappear and cannot be recovered.
附加记录：    

How does the Foundation know that this is what causes subjects to disappear? The letters don't mention this and it doesn't seem like the subjects who succeed know what will happen if they fail, so it isn't clear how the Foundation would figure this out.

Other than that, +1.
