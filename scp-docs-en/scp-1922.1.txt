You mean 1440? It's not really that similar. 1440 is incredibly destructive, while this one is harmless, 1440 is undying while this one resurrects himself, and 1440's body is indestructible while this one degrades. They both cheated death in very different ways, and both suffer for it differently- in 1440's case it was hubris, and the suffering he suffers is of loneliness and guilt, of seeking death and never finding it, while 1922 reason for undeath is unknown, and is more about physical pain and the terror of constant death without end. I think they could co-exist.

As for the article itself, I feel about it much like the others- the interviews are good, but the body of the article is lacking. Neutral vote for the time being.
