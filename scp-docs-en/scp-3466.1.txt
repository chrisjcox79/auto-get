caCAW

Okay, accidentally posted this early (don't ask how you accidentally post something, I feel really stupid), so I'm writing the author's post now instead of later. Yay! Yay Anyways! This came out of Draft Swap, which is a little experiment put on by Rimple in which people traded drafts that they had lost motivation for to other people. If it goes well, this could become a regular thing (and maybe get its own tag in the future, which would be awesome). I actually got stuck with Rimple's draft, which was just some lines of description and scribbled notes about a call in which someone is attacked by birds wanting money, and another log in which a D-Class enters a Burger King run by crows.

Everything past those key points is conceptualized by yours truly, and Jimminy Christmas it has been fun.

Rimple had a one off line about Wilson's Wildlife Solutions, and then I turned them into a whole GoI with a (what I felt to be) unique relationship with the Foundation, and furthermore a unique approach to anomalies (or unusualities). Special thanks to a whole slew of people who reviewed this, who I actually don't want to mention by name because I will forget some of them but I'll do it anyways.
