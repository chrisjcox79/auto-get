There's a lot here. I apologise if some of it is unclear; I'm pretty tired right now but if I didn't do it now I'd just forget.

Lotsnotes

–block

fresh supply of square papers
附加记录：    

most of its time searching for papers
附加记录：    

Only papers below 50g/m²
附加记录：    

In each of these cases it should be .

square papers with a dimension of 15 x 15cm
附加记录：    

This would be cleaner as .

Only papers below 50g/m² can be given to SCP-4393 to slow down the rate of degradation.
附加记录：    

Adding a comma after  will make this easier to read.

may cause unforeseen irreversible damages.
附加记录：    

Should be .

SCP-4393 is a sentient, origami crane
附加记录：    

No comma is necessary here.

SCP-4393 uses it two, relatively manipulative
附加记录：    

Remove the comma here.

and will be collectively referred to as SCP-4393-1 instances.
附加记录：    

You can remove the word  here. It's not necessarily wrong, but it's just a little redundant and the sentence reads better without it.

Destroying an SCP-4393-1 instance or when SCP-4393 is handled by any personnel triggers an aggressive behavior
附加记录：    

This whole fragment here is a little clunky to read. It would be better phrased as something like 

This also removes the word , which is used again later in the sentence. Repeated words like this can sometimes make a sentence read fairly awkwardly, so it helps the whole thing flow a bit better without it.

towards the personnel responsible for any of the conditions.
附加记录：    

You can remove the  part here. It's a little self-reinforcing, which is unnecessary since it's still part of the same sentence describing the situation in the first place.

However, due to their lack of substantial physical strength
附加记录：    

No need for the word  here. It's an unnecessary qualifier as it implies that substantial strength is required to be a threat (which clearly isn't the case as these things become very dangerous later on in the article.)

Most of the contained SCP-4393-1 instances will continuously collide itself with the containment walls, possibly as an attempt to escape. The instance will repeat this behavior until it becomes immobilized due to crumpling.
附加记录：    

There are a few issues with these 2 sentences. The ), and then in the second sentence you begin to refer to them by the singular.

Consider rewording to something like 

If SCP-4393 is prevented from creating any SCP-4393-1 instances, the entity will display self-destructive behavior such as repeatedly flying into walls and personnel.
附加记录：    

You don't need the word , as it is already the subject of the sentence.

Additionally, and this isn't wrong but just a thought, since you already described this behaviour for the -1 instances, you could just refer back to it here. For example, 

Restricting its movement via trapping it between a heavy object or attaching it to an adhesive surface also triggers the destructive behavior, resulting in tears and further degradation particularly at its creases.
附加记录：    

I don't think you need all of these examples in here, they just kind of bloat out the sentence. You can just say 

Possibly due to substantial degradation, SCP-4393 ceased movement on 23/5/1958 at exactly 19:55 and was confirmed to be neutralized. Prior to this, SCP-4393 was halfway done folding another SCP-4393-1 instance.
附加记录：    

Beginning the sentence with speculation rather than the actual subject of the sentence makes it feel indirect and slow-paced. The second sentence here also reads very unclinically (phrases like .

After seven seconds had passed, all instances were observed to begin shaking
附加记录：    

Unless it specifically being seven seconds is important, I'd just say .

finished the uncompleted origami crane which immediately gained sentience.
附加记录：    

Add a comma between .

All the SCP-4393-1 instances began
附加记录：    

You can remove the word  here. It's already clear you are referring to all of them.

The aggregate momentarily collapsed in on itself creating a loud shockwave, composed of multiple crane calls which instantaneously caused all paper
附加记录：    

You've got the comma in the wrong place here; move it from after .

began to move in a westerly direction from the site and accelerate at an unprecedented speed of 1200 kph
附加记录：    

This reads a little incredulously, which isn't something you want in a clinical document. Additionally, .

SCP-4393-1 instances began mobbing on the aircraft
附加记录：    

Remove the word  here.

resulting in the destruction of three of the aircraft due to engine failure and severely damaging the other two which were ordered to retreat.
附加记录：    

Due to the repeated use of the word 

witnesses were amnesticed
附加记录：    

Generally  is used in this case.
