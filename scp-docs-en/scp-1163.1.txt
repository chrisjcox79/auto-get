Yeah, I guess I found it sort of hard to describe the anomalous behavior in a clinical way. I still haven't found an appropriate synonym for . Basically, whenever he switches bodies with a person, his face goes onto their body, and their face goes onto his previous body. I purposefully made the face only sort of strange. I wanted it to be distinct, but not impossible. I was sort of afraid it was too extreme, but I guess not.
