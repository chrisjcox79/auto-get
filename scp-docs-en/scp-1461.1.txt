Ultimately the concept behind the scip itself is the nature of the Broken God. Is this scip the origin of the Broken God? IS it the Broken God? Is the Worm the Broken God? Is the Man the Broken God? Which came first? What IS the Broken God, besides being broken? Is the Worm something the man trapped and was trapped by, or did he create the whole thing in a fight of existential madness? Maybe his insanity lured something primordial to him and he caught it, but in doing so transformed it, tarnishing its purity or simply giving a shape to its wrongness. Or maybe he found a piece of the Broken God, and created a mythology of his own around it, one that his madness helped shape?

There are many clues in this entry. I don't want to come out and say it, because 1) there is no canon and 2) I want people to build their own conclusions as to what it is. I want to reward astute readers like yourself who recognize the clues and look deeper into other parts of the site. Once they see more of the whole picture, they might come up with inspirations of their own, upvote other scips, and generally build on the mythology of the site.

Plus, it was mostly Wishun's idea, which admittedly was a really neat idea and I felt it needed writing down. :)
