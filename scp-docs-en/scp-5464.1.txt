ME: 

NEIGHBOR:

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Anyway, this is stupid. This is beyond stupid. And it is supposed to be stupid.

The whole thing started as an inside joke between me, Jakob17 on the SCPD Discord server. Though I usually don't really like articles that sprung out of stupid jokes, I just couldn't resist the urge to write this one.

Special thanks to these folks for critting this absolute monstrosity:

J Dune

The punctuation mistakes in the message at the end are intentional, please DO NOT change them.
