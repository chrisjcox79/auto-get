Attribution, Thanks and Other Matters

29 May 2016 01:56

This was inspired Aoneareyou60 of reddit, who issued a prompt on  here ://www.reddit.com/r/SCP/comments/4kpv98/another_scp_idea_roadkill_revenge/

I did modify the initial prompt heavily since then

Thank you to reviewers such as Hercules Rockefeller, Scorpion451, Steeltitan, Moen and others.

This article features the following:

SCP-2372 (Soulmates) by MrWrong

+block

–block

So this SCP somehow ended up in my re-using , a character mentioned in SCP-2372. I think it is befitting, as both SCPs deal with Chinese supernatural elements to some extent.

The LTE-1985-Grey Bluechild are reminiscent of Chiyou's followers as described in Document-2481, who essentially sided with the Brass Dragon and gained bodies of metal.

The road here is a residue of different thaumaturgies mixed onto one place, the Taoist-style reanimation and Brass Dragon's blessings.
