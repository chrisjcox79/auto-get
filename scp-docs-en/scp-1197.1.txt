22 Jun 2012 01:10

The windows on the far wall of the bedroom cannot be opened or broken by any known means.
附加记录：    

I assume this applies to the walls, floor and ceiling? Since otherwise a spy-hole could be drilled through a surface to try to look inside while it's active.

When viewed from through the windows from outside SCP-1197 appears empty and undisturbed despite any occupants.
附加记录：    

Always, or only when the room is active?

Opening SCP-1197-1 will reveal an identical version of SCP-1197 and a duplicate of any current occupants, who have opened their version of SCP-1197-1 at the same moment.
附加记录：    

Can SCP-1197-1 only be opened when the door to the hallway is closed, or does opening SCP-1197-1 cause the hallway door to slam shut? If the later, and (for example) the hallway door was jammed open with a steel girder, would the hallway door slam shut in a manner which cuts through the girder like it was made from butter?

Should 1 instance of each occupant expire, SCP-1197-1 will close and the entrance door will open.
附加记录：    

If a subject enters the room, opens SCP-1197-1, crosses into the duplicate room and kills the duplicate, will the entrance door open onto the duplicate room, with the original room vanishing? If the subject remains in the original room and kills the duplicate while the duplicate is still in the duplicate room (like with a gun), will the corpse of the duplicate vanish, or will it teleport into the original room?

Subject described meeting his duplicate and exchanging questions until both accepted their situation.
附加记录：    

Do recording devices not work while the room is active, forcing the foundation to interview the subjects?
