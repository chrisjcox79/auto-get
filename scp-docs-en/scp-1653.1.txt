Fold

12 Apr 2014 08:31

Heh. It's not bad but it's not really good either. The thing is, it seems to me to be weird for weirdness' sake. I know this may be a strange criticism to make for anything SCP related, but what I mean by that is that the various strange elements don't seem to have any connection (other than the tetrahedrons trying to reach the column).

I like ambiguity but the thing is, ambiguity only works if you give the audience enough material to speculate or interpret your work, otherwise it's not ambiguous or even absurd, it's merely random and the reader has nothing to work with. And if they have nothing to work with, they have no reason to care.

It does have potential though, so I'm not voting either way for now.
