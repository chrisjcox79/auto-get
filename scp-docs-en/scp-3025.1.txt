Okay, I went ahead and made a few edits. 09 May 2017 13:12

Radar trackers and multi-spectral cameras are supplied. Objective is to determine the nature of the being encountered during Expedition Gamma.
附加记录：    

I'd refrain from using the word 'being', especially at this point. They have no idea about that yet.

Command: It’ll be okay, ████. Just try to find shelter. There should be something in the mountains
附加记录：    

Is command using her name? Bit unprofessional, but understandable I guess.

Are they sending in D-Class in a tank? Even if they don't want to lose MTF personnel, I can't imagine the Foundation trusting D-Class with this. What if they return and decide that they've had enough of their imprisonment? They'd be in a freaking tank. It's going to be hard to explain to your superiors why the Site suddenly has a gigantic hole in it and a tank is missing.

The rest of it is really interesting though. I just hope it's not Hell. That would be cliché. And on the nose.
