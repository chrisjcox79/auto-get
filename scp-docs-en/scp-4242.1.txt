Regardless, and although it's not really your fault, it seems to be a losing idea to include characters that won't render on the default install of a huge proportion of the site readership (Windows and Chrome are respectively the world's most popular OS and web browser, and the number of people who install additional character sets to either is likely so small as to be meaningless for SCP purposes). It may be a better idea either to find an alternative character that looks close enough to it to pass, but that is a default include in all common character sets, or just to render it as a small, inline image instead?
