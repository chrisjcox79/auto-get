I feel like the first two sections of the description make it very unclear whether the anomaly is a phenomenon in the movie theater, or the theaters themselves. In addition, the final addenda feels like a letdown and further introduces confusion because it makes this anomaly seem like a one-time event and not a widespread anomaly.

The containment procedures feel lacking, you barely give any time to the most interesting part of the anomaly that would need the most unique containment procedures, instead focusing all but a sentence on the infiltration of the media which could be reduced down to a sentence itself. There's some really good imagery here but it's dragged down by unclear description and a focus on the least interesting parts of the concept. -1

Whether you like it or not, history is on our side. We will bury you!
