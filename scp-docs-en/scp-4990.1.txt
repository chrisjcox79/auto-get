SLEEP DEPRIVATION WOOO

So on the night after I posted my round 2 OCT draft I was staying up late, felt tired, and wanted to write something to see what my tired brain could come up with. I asked for ideas on an IRC sidechannel, Captain Kirby came up with  and it quickly turned into this. I only wrote the first full draft the day after and spent a few days revising so I can't claim this to be tired posting, but this still counts for something, right?

Thanks to Captain Kirby for their feedback and for providing the initial nugget of an idea that evolved into this weirdness.
