I've been trying this one for a while now. I had the idea for ghosts who want a friend for dinner back in July. My original draft had the ghosts be gold miners in California, the D-Class was exploring a section of the Redwood Nation Forest, and the Ghosts were in a cabin. I ended up feeling like I didn't do enough justice, mainly because of the lead up where I went in-depth on the people who disappeared and the log was about twice as long with just build up. I also had a weird thing where the D-Class would go into shock and refuse to speak, so the doctor would have to take charge.

After I finished up SCP-5595, I kinda fell into a rut and couldn't figure out what to write. I finally hit this one back up last week, wrote it in a night, and spent the last few days getting critiqued. Many things from the previous draft remained, the D-Class number, doctor's name, the first names of the ghosts, people disappearing, and so on. I wanted to make this a bit more character-based around the ghosts, hopefully, that came through well.

This is based on a mining town in Pennsylvania known as Centralia. This was initially the locale, but I changed it due to the Philly joke. The mines were actually set on fire, how this happened is unknown but it was believed to be an out of control fire from a controlled burn. The mines are still burning today. For some reason, 11 people are still living there as of 2019. That population actually increased for the first time in 60 years. From 10.

Nagiros

A bunch of people looked at this, so if I forgot you please let me know.
