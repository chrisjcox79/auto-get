The addendum is, tonally and structurally, everything you want from the ending of a short-form article - mysterious, understated, and troubling. The anomaly is a small, but interesting and compelling idea with a great central image, and it's fleshed out enough to deliver the stinger without leaving it looking like a rough sketch. This is the sort of thing I hoped might come out of this contest, and the sort of thing I was worried I might not actually see. Good job.
