Threat level: Neutralized?

14 Jun 2017 04:05

Why is SCP-3130 considered Neutralized if they don't know what happened to it? It could be anywhere in the world, so I don't really know if the threat level being Neutralized really makes sense. It wasn't Neutralized as far as anyone is concerned, but rather it escaped containment and took an entire wing of a Foundation facility with it. It should have kept it's anomalous powers, so the Neutralized threat level doesn't make sense to me. I did like the tone of the SCP article.
