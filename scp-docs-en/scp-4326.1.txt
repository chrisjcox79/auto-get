Originally written for the Short works contest, I've had it on ice until I figured out a good way to retool it. Hopefully people find this to be interesting.

Image credit to Faminepulse. Thanks to unclenicolini, Gishface, OthelloTheCat, weryllium and everyone else who helped this come into existence.

unfinishedpost

unfinishedword

Item # : SCP-4326

Object Class : Euclid keter

Special Containment Procedures : at the time of this report there is no way to fully contain SCP-4326, the longest lasting tactic is too encase SCP-4326 in a vinyl case and to administer 220 volts of electricity every 3 hours.

emergency breakout procedures : in the event of SCP-4326 breaking out of containment all SCPs containing mechanical parts are to be put on immediate lock down if failed to do so then all D-class personell will be used as shields.

description 

Whether you like it or not, history is on our side. We will bury you!
