If this was your first attempt at cosmic Horror then you definitely succeeded more than most. The only nitpick I have with that part of the story is that it wasn't particularly scary to me in the way most of the Lovecraft works I read were. It just lacked that moment where a glimpse of the horrific and unimaginable is revealed in my opinion. This might have been the blunt force trauma but it just didn't do it for me enough.

And I found it a bit excessive to use MTF Nu-7 () since there isn't really anything in the article to warrant use of a full battalion strength special force with tanks and the like.

It still was a very enjoyable read though. It kept my attention and my interest until the very end and left me wanting more about it. The release of the D-Class to normal society was a very nice touch that I haven't seen much on this site before.

+1
