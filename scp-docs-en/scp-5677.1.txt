General note, I believe standard format for clinical documentation for numbers that don't have associated units is to write out numbers less than 10, and put digits for numbers 10 or greater. And always put numbers for stuff with units. So for example:

Two apples
16 wildberries
Three sticks of goat butter
7 ounces tabantha wheat
10 cups cane sugar
etc.
