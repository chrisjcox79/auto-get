Youspoilers

Aattacks!

For the record, the only tabletop RPG I've played consistently was Heroscape, back when it was a thing. Ranged characters FTW.
DD fans, see if you can spot all the Easter eggs.
This SCP is effectively trying to improve itself based on player interaction.
At first, all it could make were regular animals and bugs. When Foundation staff started to play, it started to learn.
Thing is, it adapts/improves faster than the Foundation can get satisfactory documentation written. Hence the addenda pew pew pew more info every quest.
The Foundation doesn't know what it's capable of, nor are they certain what the thing wants.
So they keep playing.

Also, everyone should go watch the Acquisitions Incorporated campaigns. Chris Perkins is my hero. Perkins DM is best DM.
