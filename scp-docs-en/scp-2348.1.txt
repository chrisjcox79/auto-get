Headcanons

18 Sep 2019 15:40

Spoilers?

+dropdown

-collapsible

The anomaly is semantic, similarly to SCP-1539. Anything that passes through is redefined as “sky”. Cognitohazardous and ontokinetic elements are also involved.

And now for 1

+dropdown

-collapsible

SCP-2348 is a sky deity. This is no mere Zeus/Jupiter or Ra, however. This is a conceptual sky deity. This thing is a physical manifestation of the concept of “sky”. Apparently, this thing is such a powerful manifestation that it redefines objects passing through them to “sky”. It also draws in the sky around it, explaining the sub-anomalies. For the purpose of anyone around it, it is the entire sky. The “hand” imagery is due to human failure of comprehension. 2348 is shaped like a manipulative body part, incomprehensible to humans and rationalized as a hand. The pointing form is due to the manipulative body part moving into a manipulation phase, from the sky taking action. This is basically a sky god as Lovecraft or maybe anafabula, god of destruction.

Footnotes
Wheel of Fortune in the intro.
