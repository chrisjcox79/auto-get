Oh man. This was a neat skip.

Its a relatively simple effect, but the fact that Laika not only is still ticking despite Soviet Space era dickishness (ya sent the dog up there to die, ya bastards) but also acting helpfully really tugged on some heart strings.

The addendum also does a really good job tying this skip to the history of space travel, and leaves a lot to the imagination. Did Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin play fetch with her on the moon? Do astronauts on the ISS have a Laika just chilling around? These are questions I have after reading this skip.

Enthusiastic +1.
