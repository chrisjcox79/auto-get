SCP-610-L6

17 Apr 2009 22:45

I have a tremendous plan for the final log of SCP-610. I want it to be lived instead of me just writing it. So what I'd like to get together are interested people, perhaps five to six, who use the IRC chat and are vaguely familiar with the RP. There will be no pre-written characters, it will be faceless people so it will not affect the standard RP. I will issue gear and orders and narrate, but there will be little to no die rolling. This will be RP as I know it in its purest form. I describe, players react. The ultimate fate of the team and their actions will comprise the final log and the consequences thereof.

I know there is some 'Nnnnnh' about the RP touching the wiki continuity but this will not be 'That' RP. It just might use that channel to commence, but none of those rules or settings or events will have bearings on this adventure. This could just as easily been done in another format but the RPG channel is pre-existing so is really the best one to use for it. So if you're interested, PM me. The only real requirement is finding a time for the people who offer to converge so regulars will be preferred.
