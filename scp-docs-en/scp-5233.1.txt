Author Post!

01 Dec 2020 05:28

Big big big thanks to my lovely reviewers in chat: Pratten, Savan, Calibri_Bold, Mars, chaucer345, and Karakatt for all your wonderful suggestions to better develop the backstory here! Also, thanks to those in chat who provided the names for the two kids who summoned Mr. Wiggy.

Image source is here: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Common_earwig_(Forficula_auricularia)_on_thumb.jpg

Thank you to Calibri_Bold for making this neat reading/reaction video for 5233! https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=o166TMQJdn4

Notes:

- All of Wiggy’s interview excerpt dialogue consists of words found in the Wikipedia article for Earwig: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earwig

- Why earwigs? 1. When I was in elementary school, my friends and I found a very large pincher bug on the playground and admired it. Some other kid picked it up and tore off its pincers to keep as a souvenir. That image has stuck with me for over a decade. I can’t smush earwigs anymore. Also last month I found one in a box at work the other day and I let him go outside and it reminded me of that incident. 2. Quoth Wikipedia, “Earwigs are among the few non-social insect species that show maternal care.” Pinchy pinch moms are good moms. There’s a lot of neat facts about earwigs.

- Why Arizona? During my background research, I found this: https://cals.arizona.edu/yavapai/anr/hort/byg/archive/earwig.html so I just went with Arizona.

Best wishes to everyone in this crazy year. To everyone who has the opportunity to nurture a life, no matter how small, and those who teach others to do the same, you are the hope of the world.
