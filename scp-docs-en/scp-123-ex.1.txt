I got the interest in doing a Chicago Spirit article ever since reading the first 40 pages of Public Enemies: America’s Greatest Crime Wave and the Birth of the FBI, 1933-34 by Bryan Burrough, and the idea from a comment left by PeppersGhost on SCP-3036 about the idea of someone not wanting to leave the Foundation. This came from a combination of those two interests.

Thank you to Ninevolt, Peppersghost, DrChandra, Ember16, BlueBloodStains, Kakroom, and Avocadonut. Couldn't have done it without you!
