Well, this sure has been a long time coming. I have always found Bass Strait to be an inherantly creepy place, with a huge number of unexplained disappearances, like Prime Minister Holt and Frederick Valentich and hundreds of ships. This is an SCP I've been wanting to make for ages, and is all about what made it so wrong.

Big thank you to Sebarus, without which this would not have been possible.

I am not too concerned about what slot this piece gets, but I suppose I'll list 5006, 5005, 5040, 5007 and 5019 for my slot picks. Otherwise, I'd just like to be as close to the 5000 slot as possible.
