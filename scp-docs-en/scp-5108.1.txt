07 Mar 2020 23:55

I like this article already but it has a lot of potential areas to explore that go unmentioned. What does MKDalton do for the Foundation that it couldn’t do already? What is the cognitive fate of the souls trapped in the machine? Can they even think or feel anything? Does anybody even know if they can? The machine is clearly an evil, but does the Foundation negate that evil with what they use it for, or do they use evil to do more evil? What are they watching??

Of course none of these answers need to be answered explicitly, but as it stands I feel these unknowns are neglected to poor effect. Right now there is a really good setup in an obviously MKUltra-inspired CIA/Foundation interaction which is good enough on its own to carry an article, then we quickly learn the Foundation was able to finish the CIA’s dirty work and subverts the Ethics Committee and then the article ends with little fanfare after that. There should to be a greater arc in justifying the machine beyond the Ethics Committee walking away with their tails between their legs because something is deemed simply “essential”!
