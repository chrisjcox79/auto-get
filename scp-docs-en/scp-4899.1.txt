Fold

15 Sep 2018 06:49

Starts off very strong, but is ultimately a bit of a let down.

The images themselves don't progress meaningfully, aside from beginning to focus on Foundation sites - so it's essentially a visual rehash of what was already cleanly conveyed in the description. Due to how sparse the addenda is, it's far too easy to simply gloss over them and head right smack into the final image in the first section. This has the effect of ruining any possible tension - it's got no time to build towards that point, and the image did not elicit any kind of reaction from me.

Following that, having a similar message come back-to-back cheapens the effect. I don't feel the final image carries the impact it is supposed to.

I don't think you need to add a whole ton to this. It doesn't need bloat, but it could do with some substance - something that might imply the intent of this thing, or otherwise give it some kind of unique flavor. A bit of description, and perhaps some formatting to better space apart the images to give them a bit of breathing room, might go a ways to strengthen the pacing here.
