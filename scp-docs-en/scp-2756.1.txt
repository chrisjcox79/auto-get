13 May 2014 12:28

I certainly don't dislike it - the idea of everyone having horrific mutations and just trying to live with them is actually kind of sweet, in a weird way. I can't quite put my finger on what it is, but there's just something that's keeping me from happily upvoting this one. You've got my +1, but I was tentative to do it.

Also, you should remove the black boxes on the description. Personnel should be able to read the containment details and know exactly what needs to be done; as it stands now no one will have any idea as to what site anything should be sent to. Overall you want to avoid redaction, expungement, and black boxing anything in the containment procedures, as that's the information any site personnel can access in an emergency. There's no help on attempting to contain something if you can't see how you're supposed to do it.
