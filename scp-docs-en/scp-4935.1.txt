23 Feb 2019 18:33

I'm so happy to finally see this on the site. I mean, ok Captain Kirby embracing an end of death article is literally the least surprising thing ever, but still.

I'm happy with what you did with the exploration log especially. I think it gives a nice addition of flavor to the Akot people in general, which builds the world nicely.

But overall what I really like about this piece takes end of death and makes it feel more grand. Generally, I tend to write this canon from a more personal perspective, focussing on a single character and letting readers extrapolate that character's experiences. I see this approach taken a lot in other EoD stuff as well, which I personally enjoy because I love character based narratives, and I don't want to see that trend stop.

However, this doesn't really take that route. It uses End of Death to tell a story that leans into its sci-fi elements more. The lore and the world wreak of more classical plot-driven science fiction stories, and I love to see that new approach applied to the canon in a way that still fits with the overall bleak and gray vibe that is exemplified in the rest of End of Death.

Also, as a side point - I like the irony of the Foundation essentially writing history reports on the future.

So yeah. Good stuff my man. Good stuff.
