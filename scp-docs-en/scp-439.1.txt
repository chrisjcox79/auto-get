12 Sep 2010 11:41

one of the things I like is the fact that we have no idea how long these bugs have been doing this. They may have been evolving like this for millions of years, they may be extraterrestrial in origin, or they may be an escaped genetic experiment. Who knows?

I think the reason only one has been found so far is because as frightening as they are, the bugs are pretty fragile. They don't swarm or have appreciable toxins, they aren't very tough and they're very picky about habitat. Location is important for narrative plausibility because they need an area where victims are plentiful but not prone to seeking medical assistance. A doctor sees a bug in your chest, he's gonna take it out.
