Downvote.

11 Sep 2019 21:26

+here

-HIDE

under a Class-33 cover story .
附加记录：    

Under the Class-33 cover story. Reads quite a lot better, at least to me.

resembling a Cancer productus
附加记录：    

My preferred way to writing animal names is the layman's term, followed by the scientific term in brackets. For this, it would be 'resembling a red rock crab (Cancer productus)'.

It possesses an extremely slow metabolism and hunger system
附加记录：    

What's a hunger system? Do you mean digestive system? Or do you mean that it only becomes hungry very occasionally? If it's the former, use that term instead. If the latter, this part can probably be cut, as it's shown in the next clause of the sentence.

Addendum 4327.1  Discovery
附加记录：    

This is admittedly more of a personal opinion, but I dislike the use of italics here, and also the use of the '.' in the addendum. Generally, I would format things like this as Addendum XXXX-1:  Discovery.

Although sound is present in the footage, speech is unintelligible due to poor sound quality.
附加记录：    

I don't like the repetition of 'sound' here. Maybe replace the first usage of it with 'audio'?

Minor issues aside, I'm just not the biggest fan of the concept. It's a big crab, which is cool. But the narrative doesn't really do much for me. Militaries get scared of the big crab, so they kill it. There's not really much nuance.

I appreciated the historical context, but it still didn't do quite enough for me. I'm sticking with a novote downvote for now.
