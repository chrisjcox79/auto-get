Thank you very much, I appreciate it. Also yes, Day Breaks is a huge influence on the creation of this idea. It's such an effective doomsday article for its visualization of the apocalypse the sun creates, I felt even someone trying to spoil it to the Foundation would find it hard to sell on words alone. So I'm glad a few people have said they picked up on the reference without any direct reference to it in the article itself, its how I wanted it to work.

In addition to other sun related articles listed in the piece itself and elsewhere on the site, I felt this one could easily slot in with any of those canons while remaining unqiue on its own and I'm glad to see it has accomplished that.
