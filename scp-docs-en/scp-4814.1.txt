Junior Staff - Site Criticism

19 Oct 2019 21:57

Holy heck, this was good!

Tanhonyrambles

TURNOFF

A few things that I especially enjoyed in the article:

SCP-4814 is the collective designation for nine trees, designated as SCP-4814-1 through SCP-4814-9, that are biologically similar to standard human women in all manners except form.
附加记录：    

This is a great opener. Not only do you get this wonderful sense of whiplash halfway through the sentence, but it also conjures up a really vivid image in the mind.

After a brief background check, it became clear that Mr. Makson is a certified butcher.
附加记录：    

Ugh, this gave me shivers. This says so much with so little.

SCP-4814-1, -3, -5, and -6 have recently become visibly engorged in their lowest branches. Similarly, SCP-4814-2 and -4 have formed large lumps along their trunks. These newfound abnormalities, in addition to previous symptoms of nausea and menstrual spotting, confirms that SCP-4814-1 through SCP-4814-6 are pregnant.
附加记录：    

We started off with that wonderfully vivid and gnarly image, and we end with one that matches it. The mental image of pregnant trees comes to the mind surprisingly easily, especially with the way its described here, and the body horror it evokes is a superb way to bring the article to a close.

I’m not so familiar with Ambrose Restaurants, but if this SCP is any indication it’s something I’m going to have to look more into - the body horror is one thing, but the delving deeper into the human soul that’s hinted at in the letter is really intriguing!

A hearty +1!
