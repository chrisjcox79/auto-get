So the idea that I was trying to convey is the way it messes with your biology is just a byproduct of being around it too much, hence why the head researcher, who I figured would be up close and personal with it more than anyone else assigned to it, was farther along than any of the other staff. Those effects are unrelated to any of its other anomalous properties.

While I liked the story - loved its concise and effective method - I disagree that the Head Researcher of a project would, by default, by the one in most proximity to it. Especially with a potentially memetic or cognito-hazardous  like this.

I think either:

a) It should be noted, and considered slightly odd, that Dr. Martinez was in such constant close-proximity to SCP-3565 (perhaps due to morbid fascination, perhaps it is another of SCP-3565's effects)

OR

b) It should be a lower level researcher, or perhaps a dedicated sanitary/custodial worker (janitor,  time - the others can have similar abnormal births, but this staff member can have the Martinez Experience.
