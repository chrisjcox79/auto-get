DRIVE SAFELY KIDS

20 Jul 2017 04:17

I've been doing a lot of short articles lately. This is one of those - conceived and written up within six hours. While driving home from the gym I saw my brother and waved to him. Then I thought about how weird that looked and was like - damn it would be even weirder if there were eyes in the palm of my hand. Plus I've wanted to write a tale now where the MTFs and the D-class survive by being competent.

Many thanks to Modern_Erasmus, and not_a_seagull for critique.

I hope you enjoy! See more of my work on my author page!
