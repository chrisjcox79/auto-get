Fold

05 Feb 2015 19:49

The presidential references are definitely tongue in cheek and meant to be silly from a narrative point of view. The other side is that, in the world of the SCP itself, they are designed to be recognizable to an American living around the beginning of the 21st century. Presumably something mighty bad is coming, and the copies (if they exist) may contain valuable intel on the nature of some upcoming calamity. The use of sequential US Presidents also serves as a countdown. Each title means at most an eight year period (presumably), meaning every election is one term closer to A Very Bad Thing.
