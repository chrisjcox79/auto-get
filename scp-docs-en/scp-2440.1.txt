05 Mar 2014 06:09

This paragraph is everything I hate about naming conventions. Don't give us a three part SCP with identical sounding names when each part is so completely different. There is no reason to give the infected their own designation. There is no reason to give the memetic agent its own designation either.  Boom. Now at the end of the one sentence I know what all three parts of the SCP are and I only have one designation to keep track of.

Plus, briefly twice in one sentence?

individuals twice in one sentence? (not to mention, what the hell else would an organization be composed of but individuals)

Clean up your language; this reads like it's trying too hard to sound  and comes off as sloppy and bloated.

edit: Plus dude look at your containment procedures:  I don't think a dedicated MTF makes much sense for this SCP either. A SCP that is likely to require regular armed intervention and specialized tools merits an MTF- this does neither.
