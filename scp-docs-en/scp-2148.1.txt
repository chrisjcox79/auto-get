I am actually choosing my words pretty poorly tonight. It's not that I hate them, it's just that if you take them out of their context, what are they really? For example, if I'm a newcomer to the site, and this is the first thing I read, I'm going to be pretty confused. It lacks the back story (instead provided by other articles like it) that other skips work very hard to build in themselves. And don't get me wrong, I love wondertainment, just not this particular splinter of it. The reason of my posting on this one in particular is I feel it is the least interesting and narrative giving one to date. Think about it. Take 1048, another wondertainmnt object. It manages to include a new, interesting concept, provide interesting exposition about said concept, and wrap itself up with a satisfying conclusion. I feel this article is simply a bridge, as to me it only includes a slight amount of exposition. No true beginning, no real end, it is just making way for future works. And that's fine and all, but I dont necessarily think that belongs on the mainlist.

TL;DR: I see nothing wrong w/ the page itself, (although it's not the best, for the reasons mentioned) I just don't think it should be man list.
