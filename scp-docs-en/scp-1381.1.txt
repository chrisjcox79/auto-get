short version: felines are small cats, felids are ALL cats. longer version: big cats are pantherines, and necessarily not felines (pantherinae and felinae are the two subfamilies of family felidae). small cats are everything that isn't a lion, tiger, jaguar, or leopard of some sort - pumas, lynxes, cheetahs, cervals, anything with  at the end are felines.
