01 Dec 2016 10:35

I like this, but are the footnotes describing what adipocytes, organelles, and endoplasmic reticulum are really necessary? I understand that not everyone who reads them understand what they mean, but it ruins the immersion a little bit, because I imagine the people who work at the Foundation are pretty smart, and know what they are.

Also, just another little biology term, you said that endoplasmic reticulums synthesise lipids; you're partially right, if you want to be more technical, say that it's the smooth endoplasmic reticulum, which is the one that synthesises lipids. The other one, the rough endoplasmic reticulum, is responsible for the synthesis of proteins (via attached ribosomes), and packaging of said proteins.

Other than that, very good first SCP, take my upvote :)

Backstory! Woo!

03 Apr 2017 20:50

Funny thing, when I first started working on the original concept for this (three or four overhauls ago), the similarity to the Adipose was the first thing people brought up. I hadn't heard of them before that day, and I've tried my darndest to differentiate from that concept, but when the goal is 'happy fat', there's only so much you can do.

The original seed of this idea came from my little brother, who hasn't seen any Dr. Who either, for what it's worth, but the end result is a lot different than what he came up with.

Glad you liked it! And I might write more about these things when I have both motivation, and an idea at the same time.
