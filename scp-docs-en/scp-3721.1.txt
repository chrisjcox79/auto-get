I can’t hear you if you’re calling out my name

I can’t see you and I’m feeling just the same

I can’t see you, I can’t hear you

I’ll believe it when I die

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

I hate the Nazi Superscience trope. I hate hate hate it. Nazi technology was built off slave labor, prone to horrible malfunctions, and merely meant to solidify the Reich's power. That's not to mention their hatred of theoretical physics, to the point of outright rejecting reality.

This is my take on the Nazi Superscience trope.

Thanks to IAmTheOoga (sorry for forgetting you D:) for the advice!

First image from here.

translatornotes

Dokoni

As far as dialogue is concerned, don't be afraid to cut and remix if the emotions aren't coming through in a more literal translation. The communication logs are meant to convey feeling over information.

If you are gonna remix, I ask that you keep Zellweger's use of the k-slur (or equivalent in your own language) to a single instance. These are words that lose gravitas if repeated.

A big part of this SCP is understated horror. Keep the dark bits brief yet sudden.
I don't know if y'all have wikiwalk, but feel free to ask me if [BRANCH-SPECIFIC SCP] would make for a neat crosslink.

 and I'm not Robert Frost.
