Fold

25 Jun 2014 10:36

If you have no experience writing an article, I'd strongly recommend that you try writing a  article first. -001 proposals are much harder to write than a regular main-lister. That said, there is no special process to write an -001 proposal. You write it, and you post it, except the URL, instead of SCP-XXX, would be something like Fried-Cyanides-proposal, and you'd link it on the proposals-for-scp-001 page.

But, don't do this. If you want to write an article, get a feel for the format and tone and everything first before attempting an -001 proposal.
