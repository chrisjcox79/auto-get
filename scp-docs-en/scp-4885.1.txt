This is quite good in concept and execution, but I honestly think it still has tons of phrasing and word choice gaffes that ought to have been rooted out in crit. In a lot of places, this article falls into the trap of using complex language to sound clinical where simpler language would suffice.

SomeExamples

SomeExamples

Currently, it is only theorized that SCP-4885 is contained. To further elaborate, SCP-4885 is contained within an unknown Class-17 Containment Chamber in an unknown site.
附加记录：    

-

SCP-4885 is theorized to be contained within an unknown Class-17 Containment Chamber in an unknown site. In the event that SCP-4885 breaches containment
附加记录：    

SCP-4885-1 instances discovered by far2.AIC will have specialized Foundation carrier drones to be dispatched and attempt to grab the body, and carry it to Location i, using a specialized AI to take routes that minimize the chance of unwanted civilian interference.
附加记录：    

-

SCP-4885-1 instances will be recovered by specialized Foundation carrier drones and relocated to Location i, using a specialized AI to minimize the chance of unwanted civilian interference.
附加记录：    

In the event that Location i's whereabouts are discovered at large, the next personnel located within Document PAINT-THE-TOWN-YELLOW will establish the next Location i, and their immediate family will be notified of their death.
附加记录：    

This is not a word choice gaffe, but it's worth nothing that the rest of the file doesn't explain what this document is.

This entails that SCP-4885 wears a horizontal red and white striped shirt, a red and white bobble hat, and jeans. However, a noticeable difference in appearance from the character is the entity's paler skin and the lack of eyes.
附加记录：    

-

Like the non-anomalous character, SCP-4885 wears a horizontal red and white striped shirt, a red and white bobble hat, and jeans. In contrast to the character, the entity's skin is notably paler and its face lacks eyes.
附加记录：    

M-2 begins to violently groan, all while holding their stomach.
附加记录：    

-

M-2 begins to groan and hold their stomach.
