01 Sep 2013 19:23

I always loved this one.

For starters, the containment procedures are creative, thorough, and reasonable. The description is clear and explains everything that needs to be explained. The concept itself is unsettling and evokes very interesting imagery. This is one of the SCPs that got me hooked on the site.

That said, there are a few issues that make my upvote more hesitant than it could be:

Use of  once.
Lots and lots of scare quotes (ten total, nine of which I would not use).
Weird expungement of ultraviolet effects, although that doesn't bother me TOO much. (On that topic, it seems to me that something to do with its ultraviolet appearance or X-ray emission could be the cause of the apoptosis.)
Unnecessary incident log at the bottom.

If these were addressed, this would be among the SCPs I might show to people to introduce them to the site.
