Fold

03 Oct 2012 13:18

a normal loaf of bread
附加记录：    

This breaks tone for me. I have no idea what you're trying to get at with the word 'normal', especially associated with bread. Are you talking rye bread? White bread? wheat bread? Bread made with potatoes, a baguette? Normal is just such a vague word that assumes the reader knows what the narrator is talking about. I hate it. Bread comes in such a variety that having no other specifications besides 'normal' is like trying to describe a scented candle but you only refer to the scent as 'normal'.

SCP-613 is a piece of ███████ brand pad paper

Instances of SCP-613-1 separated by slicing or tearing, where the mass of the separated segment is greater than 50 grams, will form disparate identities distinct from the parent instance of SCP-613-1. Instances of SCP-613 produced in this manner have been observed to engage in erratic movements and vocalizations indicative of physical pain.
附加记录：    

Look man. SCP-613 is a pad paper. An instance of SCP-613 wouldn't speak unless SCP-613 speaks and vocalizes. This confuses me, unless you're trying to say instances of SCP-613-1 produced in this manner talk and move and whatnot. Because otherwise you're mixing up terms and making this confusing to read what you're talking about. Look over what you refer to as SCP-613 and SCP-613-1 again in case you accidentally crossed the streams.

Instances of SCP-613-1 can vocalize and achieve self-locomotion through unknown means, and are capable of sensation
附加记录：    

This is again very vague statements. What, do they float? Fly through the air? Roll along the ground? Just slide across the floor? What sensations, squishing, cutting, poking, heat, pain, cold?

around the world
附加记录：    

Better tone: 'internationally' is a wonderful world.

around the globe
附加记录：    

See above. This 'around the world/globe' stuff reminds me of a children's book. But that may just be me.

Overall, the concept appears like it needs more detail. Description is vague at best in critical parts, missing altogether in others. The idea right now is sapient bread that has mental problems if risen at nonroom temperature (what the fuck is room temperature considered anyways? 20 Celsius? Maybe write that down). It can be sufficiently weird I guess to make an interesting SCP, but right now falls short because you have details that may be interesting but never go into them and thus frustrates me (the reader). Downvote for now.

Living the dream, or dreaming the life?
