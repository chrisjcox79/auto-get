Fold

18 Jun 2010 04:29

More dangerous does not equal better.

The site is littered with things that kill you because.

To the foundation, this is EITHER: A sapient entity made of superheated plasma akin to that found in a star, that has somehow decided to be a cat, OR: A cat that has become superheated plasma, yet retaining it's feline consciousness.

Now, I don't really have a true backstory for this little guy, but if you're interested, the bits I *do* have are thus:

It wasn't originally a cat. EDIT: That is to say, in-universe, the Plasmacat here was plasma first, and then took on the cat shape.

The 'overfeeding' test was a rather sizable explosion, as it violently expelled the excess mass and energy

The 'cutting' test resulted in another sizable explosion, this time as a defensive mechanism.

There are two reasons I made it a cat. One, yes, the SCPfuel image was the inspiration, though if that was all, I could change it easily. The second reason is more important, and is because of my limitations as a writer. I just don't know enough about the reactions of any other animals to portray normal behaviors in text, which is what this writeup basically requires. It's easy to write yourself stupid, but it's, while probably not impossible, at least very difficult to (convincingly) write yourself more knowledgeable than you really are. If somebody wishes to change it to something besides a cat, and you can keep the writing to at least the standard I set (which is good for me, but probably well below the levels of most veterans here - And though that likely sounds like I'm trying to suck up, that isn't my intention), you're welcome to do so…I'd just like to reserve veto power over your changes, if I may.
