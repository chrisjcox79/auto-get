It's been a very long time since I wrote something for the site, but it's good to be back.

This concept has been something bouncing around in my brain for a bit, all starting when I saw this SCP-1319, an excellent skip by Tanhony that I did not know treaded similar ground until recently, but which served as a small guiding rod for tweaks.

Many thanks to ARD, Roget, MalyceGraves, Dr Akimoto, and a few scattered other souls on IRC (including #thecritters) for helping me to refine it into this version. Many thanks to spikebrennan as well for the fantastic name.

+Observations

–block

This execution was very much inspired by the idea of a literal bodily divorce, with the unfortunate flesh being the child left in the middle.
Students of Greek mythology will know that Cassandra was a rather doomed prophetess of Troy who could only make predictions that no one will believe. Ioannou's friend is, in my headcanon, of the same general mold, except he can only make predictions that are wrong but extremely believable.
