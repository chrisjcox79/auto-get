I'm a bit iffy on this one. The ending, as McB highlighted above, is a powerful gut punch. There is also some good fridge horror in the following line:

youre saying you can make me one of those fancy dolls?

dado can make u toy yes

And the actual  from the dado body horror wackiness, to the showcasing of a typical dado double-meaning. I just can't see why Ricardo would respond to that particular ad, though. I can see him being desperate, but it doesn't even seem adjacent to what he was looking for. While this is definitely one of the more thoughtful dado stories I've read, I personally don't find that the dado and father aspects (which are each great on their own!) mesh together well.
