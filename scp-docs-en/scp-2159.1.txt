Allow me to extend my thanks to various people: wheatmiser, TyGently, Dr_Leonard, AndarielHalo among others.

This article references the following articles:

SCP-1132-J (Head-Cannons) by Aelanna
SCP-2615-J (Clap Your Hands) by DreamwalkerFae
SCP-damej-J (damej robit) by Faminepulse, djkaktus, and Decibelles

And what is the damned thing anyway

+block

–block

All the iterations share a common theme: . SCP-2159 is the incarnation of headcanon, so everyone perceives it as headcanon.

Isaac Netero fights via something called the Type-100 Guan Yin Bodhisattva, while Graduation is based on Canon in D.

However, over time, these headcanons become superseded by Word of God that incorporates the opinion of the so-called  into the finalised canon.

Iteration 0 is technically a vajra, a ritualistic item that represents both lightning and diamond. That is the explanation for some of the traits surrounding SCP-2159 (density, fulgurite etc.). It literally fell to the Earth, that's for sure.

As for the unexplained context let's just say that a (trickster?) deity wants to make a point about perception.

And Halo, I'm sorry. I will admit that something sentient and with souls can be affected. However, I find hard to believe that AI technology would be that advanced by 2004 and camera-based tsukumogami are impossible since tsukumogami don't go well with electricity (or electrical appliances, in general).
