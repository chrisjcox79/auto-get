spoilers,sweetie

–block

Actually, I'm envisioning this as a case of 2 anomalous thingies cross-testing in the wild. I mean, given how many things are out there, it's kind of inevitable that some would encounter each other before the Foundation or a GOI came across them.

In this case, they were originally the injury-causing referee, who would just vanish in the crowd once players had been injured, and the ambulance, which would hybridize anyone inside the back when the sirens were running, with the secondary effect that people didn't notice the hybridization. The ambulance showed up at one of the referee's games and their properties resonated, making them into a larger, more dangerous SCP-level thingie. So now it's in its current form, PLUS  people forget about the ambulance once it's delivered the victims. Notice that it completely drops out of the document after delivery, when the Foundation would certainly want to investigate it.
