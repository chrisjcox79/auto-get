17 Jan 2020 18:22

I'm a fan of -EX skips, but this didn't do it for me.

Exploring the strangeness around particle physics is certainly a good place for exploring the strangeness of the normal world, but I wasn't a fan of the execution here. Asking random anomalies for information about quantum properties doesn't make much sense, and the SCP-three blackbox that was used for crosstesting isn't hinted enough to make much sense here. Why this anomaly in particular?

Furthermore, one of the critical parts in an explained article (at least in my view), is how the Foundation discovers it was non-anomalous. Here it's primarily , without much of an justification.
