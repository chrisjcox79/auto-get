As a skip, the idea of document-affecting SCP isn't exactly new. The article doesn't expand through the idea much further than, say, 732 or that one skip that made the documentation into a poem either, as well as the -J that made all documentation become superfluously convoluted. I'm not saying that this is necessarily inferior to them, but they are comparable enough that this article can't be as unique as it could have been.1

But as an interpretation of the contest theme? Fuck yes. I still think this article could grow to be more than it is right now, but +1 all the way from me.
