So, what's going on here is this: relative time speeds up as containment agents approach the center of the anomaly. Before we knew there was someone walking out of there at a significant fraction of light speed, we'd already sent agents in to correct the problem.

So we sent new agents in to stop them. The problem is, it's not clear that they can. Because the agents rushing to stop the first containment team slow down as they approach, it's not clear that the Foundation will be able to tell early containment teams not to realign the artifacts responsible for the anomaly. If they do, whatever SCP-1272-06 is will come rushing out of the singularity with enough speed to blow a hole in the site and anything even vaguely nearby.
