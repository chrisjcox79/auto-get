I like the idea, but what throws the article off for me is a handfull of wonky or run on sentences.

Some examples:

+crit

-crit

At some point in an approximately one month period immediately after an instance of SCP-2809-1’s parent or parents die,
附加记录：    

Shouldn't  be past tense here?

If an instance of SCP-2809-1 clicks on an instance of SCP-2809-2 they are taken to the website of Quondam Holidays. Quondam Holidays is designated as SCP-2809-3.
附加记录：    

I feel like this could be shortened into one sentence, as  repeated twice sort of seems odd to me.

Investigation into Quondam Holidays is ongoing but little information has been discovered about them at this time apart from that gathered from interviews with SCP-2809-1 instances and their reviews of Quondam's service.
附加记录：    

A bit of a run on here. Maybe something to make it less wordy, such as

Investigation into SCP-2809-3 is ongoing, with most information about them gathered from interviews of SCP-2809-1 instances and online reviews of SCP-2809-3.
附加记录：

I see exactly where you're coming from. The interview just isn't going to work for everyone. I'm sorry you're in that camp 'cause you've always given good feedback in the past but I'm leaving it as it is.
