It's the final note that killed it for me. The Foundation has much safer things underneath much more stringent security. The only things allowed to exist in the wild are those we cannot catch or move.

The main predators of the woodpecker include wild cats, foxes, rats. snakes and large birds although many other species of animal will prey on the woodpecker too.
附加记录：    

You're ruling out that groups of predators dying from facial and digestive acid burns might cause notice from external groups. Due to both concentration and breeding rates, their displacement of actual prey birds in the local ecosystem would have drastic ramifications. Sudden introduction of lethal prey would easily wipe out predator populations. This would lead to explosion in other prey animals, or an explosion of woodpeckers (once they are no longer preyed upon, if biting one isn't lethal).

At what point do they develop their acidic cocktail? Many predators specialize on eggs and chicks. If they're poisonous as eggs, the impact is going to be more severe.

But a Project Administrator who blew off a founded, data-backed researcher's concerns would not be an administrator for long. If the replacement rate of the imitated species is as fast as you imply, this could cause a major ecosystem disaster, leading to a massive violation of the Status Quo.

-1. The horror is there. The Foundation isn't.
