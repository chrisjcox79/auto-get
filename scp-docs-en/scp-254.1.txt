18 Sep 2010 15:15

I'm not sure what to make of this one… It's an interesting idea, and you've written it out properly, but somehow it fails to capture my imagination… Well, in this sort of situation, I usually try to make progress by asking a lot of questions.

1. So, NOBODY takes note of the strange new employee? Neither customers nor staff thinks there's anything odd about his presence? If that's the case, how do we even detect him? Everyone would take his presence for granted, and not look into his background even after he started going crazy.

2. What does he look like if photographed or viewed through a video camera?

3. Incorporeal or not, there's a lot of different things that could be done to try to physically manipulate 254-1. What has been done? What has worked?

4. Theoretically, you could do whatever you wanted with the 254 plaque as long as it isn't activated. Why not have two (and only two) researchers take it out in a trailer to the middle of a desert and experiment on it there?

5. Was "Employee of the Month" an intentional pun?

6. "If 254-1 is fired within approximately 20 days from the start of its decline in performance, 254-1 simply leaves the area and disappears." Okay, well, what happens if you wait it out?

7. What happens if you use SCP-148 on it?
