ACT II

06 Mar 2018 03:24

VARAXOUS enters stage right

Why should we let them sing?

VARAXOUS: Greetings all! Wonderful turnout we have here.

So absorbed in their cheers and jeers.

Applause

Have you ever thought about why it sings?

VARAXOUS: Thank you, thank you! Now, as much as I would love to take all of the credit, all I did was add some words and fight some owls for a good dream. While Darkstuff was an absolutely crucial piece of the puzzle, without the wonderful help of everyone involved, this wouldn't have had turned nearly as amazing as it did. I'd like to thank Tanhony, Weryllium, CaptainKirby, Ninevolt, thebillith, not_a_seagull, and PeppersGhost . Without their help, there wouldn't be any lights, any set, hell, not even any actors! So please give them, and everyone else involved in this wonderful production, a big hand!

USE YOUR EYES AND LOOK UP. TRULY SEE.

Pause to wait for the applause to subside

The owl's view is so far and so wide.

VARAXOUS: Now I'm sure you're all tired of the sweaty, skinny person standing in front of you all preventing the play from starting. So without further adieu, let the play

That sometimes they forget to check their own feathers.

VARAXOUS takes a large bow

A dream plucked away from one of the 12.

VARAXOUS: Begin!

What lies in the center?

Curtains fall in front of VARAXOUS and every light turns off except for the ones focused on the stage.

theykeeponimprovingtheykeeponrevising

justletmehideaway

DON'T

STOP

LOOKING

It's not a party until you summon a lesser demon.
