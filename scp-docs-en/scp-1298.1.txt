The point is the old doorstep baby trope gone horribly weird. Not everything has to murder/drive everyone crazy.

My thought process was roughly that this is what the well-meaning version of SCP-1377's creator(s) does. Whatever it is that makes these things, it doesn't really  humans, as displayed by the nonsensical letters, and is trying to cheer up those sad people on the TV. By delivering a little chunk of the uncanny valley straight to their doorstep.

Also, imagine what recovery must be like in instances where the distraught couple have taken the little buggers in. Anything you put in just remains inside, sloshing around, heated by the , going stale. And yet they'll fight the Foundation's agents over them, not due to an anomalous effect but just human nature.

Basically, it's like an SCP version of the Toynbee tiles.
