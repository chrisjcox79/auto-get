Overall, I enjoyed this (except for maybe the test logs), but there's just one little thing my over-analytical computer geek mind found hard to overlook.

SCP-4446 can not be deleted or edited. Any attempt to do so is prompted by an alert, that sufficient administrative rights are needed, even if executed as an admin.

Admin isn't the highest permission level; root is. Also, why not just wipe the hard drive? That doesn't require any permissions at all.
