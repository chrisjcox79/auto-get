Minor Typos/Grammatical fixes

19 Nov 2019 18:48

Workers will not leave SCP-4651 voluntary  and will vanish if forcible extraction is attempted, re-emerging the next day.
附加记录：    

Voluntary is an adjective.

Voluntarily is an adverb.

Furthermore, workers have proven unable  to recall details of a life beyond their employment, instead placing extreme value on their standing within the company.
附加记录：    

'have proven' vs. 'have been proven'

The lab boys are doing the proving. They proved a fact about the rations.

A fact about the rations has been proven.

Right now, your sentence reads like the workers are doing the proving, but you probably mean that the research is proving something.

Beyond that, it was a fun, short read. Thank you for sharing.
