This is probably obvious, but in case it is not: this article fits into the overarching narrative I have started with SCP-5512.

►◄

▼▼

Watts was the sole survivor of the massacre of the Society of Yorkshire Thaumaturgy as described in SCP-4612.
Watts’ obsession and drive to discover the origins of 4612-A/B led to the destruction of Clifton: as 4612-B told him to do better, and Watts instead spent 30 years killing and robbing people, in order to fuel his lifestyle and find out the secrets of 4612-A. If he hadn't tried to find out about the origins of 4612-A, then Watts and the town would have lived longer.
The destruction of Clifton occurred less than 30 years after the massacre in York, and 4612-B is still incredibly enraged by the death of his father.
