Big Picture / Conceptual Critique

Heyyy! This is good! The only things I can say are that I think that the RAISA part wouldn't have been handled by RAISA. That would have been put in with the rest of the documents, just not sure of why it was done in this format. But other than that, this is simply good. It's a story about humanity, not new to the Foundation, but done in a very fun and saddening way. Really felt like real people.

Oh, there is one other thing. The format for the  bit didn't make much sense to me. The article was good enough for me to take it in stride, but I raised eyebrows. Is there no better way to show that Noah is talking in an inhuman fashion? What is this supposed to represent in the SCP file?

Anyways, that's a +1, take it and have a good day.
