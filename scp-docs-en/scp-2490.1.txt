Well, it's not bad, but I'm gonna have to downvote:

+block

–block

SCP-2490 refers to a modified human being, believed to be Chaos Insurgency Special Operative Alpha-19
附加记录：    

I personally don't like excessive references to GOIs, and this scip seems to ride on the GOC stuff in the addenda. That's just my opinion, though.

SCP-2490 will pursue the target for anywhere between several hours and several months, often showing itself to the target from a range before fleeing. As a result of this behavior, targets usually express lack of sleep, extreme paranoia, introversion, and a phobia of mannequins.
附加记录：    

 would work, too.

We have a lot of scips that stalk, and a lot of mannequins- I'm strongly reminded of The Maybe-There Monsters. both of which are creepier than this.

targets are found unconscious, with puncture marks in their skull. MRI scanning reveals that brain tissue from the cerebellum, hippocampus, cerebral cortex, and random sections of the frontal, parietal, and temporal lobes has been removed and replaced with a fluid similar to SCP-████'s secretions.
附加记录：    

Loaves of the brain, neato.

vampiring brains out is pretty cool. The reference to SCP-blackbox's secretions replacing the brain strikes me as unnecessary, like it's not adding anything to the scip- unless i'm missing something. SCP's are usually supposed to be able to stand alone, and referencing other scips (real or not) can weaken your article. Same goes for GOIs and stuff, usually.

Following an attack, a random SCP that the target previously worked with will disappear
附加记录：    

Ok, cool, it's eating the headpudding and then using the stolen memories to steal itself some scips? that's kind of a big deal.

SCP-2490's head is composed of a single, oblong beige capsule, on which two eyes have been painted and no other facial features are present. Its body consists of multiple white, jointed segments. SCP-2490 possesses claws that appear to be grafted to its hand pods. Based on target testimony, however, most observers perceive it as an ordinary human being.
附加记录：    

yes, but how tall is it? does it have four legs? where is its head? is it humanoid? whaaaat are ?

possesses claws that appear to be grafted to its hand pods
附加记录：    

Grafted, like, surgically? are there seams or welds or something? Have they actually been added to the skip, or do they just  to?

Overall, it's well-written but similar to other scips and relies too heavily on things that aren't itself. So, uh, do with that what you will.
