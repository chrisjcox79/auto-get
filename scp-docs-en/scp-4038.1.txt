This is weird enough for me to upvote, but I did notice a few things that could use adjustment:

SCP-4038-C will then begin muttering in their sleep in a polyglot mixture of approximately 150 languages, some of which have long been extinct

 sounds a lot better.

appears to be highly correlated with the numbers of languages the SCP-4038-C individual speaks

However, upon being touched by a waking human the tongue begins to writhe in seeming panic before expiring
附加记录：    

How does a tongue die? Does the tissue undergo actual necrosis? Or does it just fall back into the mouth, which can result in the tongue being swallowed?

One last quibble: why isn't this Keter-class? The vector of infection isn't known, and who's to say the infection at the music festival was an isolated occurrence? This could easily breach containment.

Still, this is weird and awesome. Tongues hating humans? +1.
