Well, the suggestion was mostly in service to that terrible mistress dramatic conflict BUT I do think the essential difference is that SCP-105 is allowed to be much more open with using her abilities (she also has to work with Senior Psychopath, however). From the early manifestation of SCP-808's abilities, Alice probably incorporated the companionship of uplifted electrical devices the same way other introverted children might form deep relationships with books. It may be deep down, simmering and subconsious, but I think the Foundation's denial to Alice of any electrical device would be a strong source of animosity from Alice.

I think that Iris could serve as good example to Alice, and that the two would most likely form a bond after a few encounters. They are just so alike, and are so otherwise isolated, it's implausible they would avoid forming a bond. The evolution and nature of that bond (as well as its missteps) would be interesting to see.
