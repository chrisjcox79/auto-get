This doesn't work for me for a variety of reasons.

+block

–block

The description contradicts itself , there are various clinical tone breaks, the mtf dialogue is cliche and bordering on cheesy, the exploration logs didn't do anything particularly cool or interesting and didn't sustain tension, the diary entries were melodramatic, etc, etc.

Most disappointingly of all, the hook of having whoever enters use the phrase before entering was not used and did not play into the story at any point in time. That was a very good hook that was unfortunately abandoned. Another disappointment was the failure to explore this sarkic-abrahamic fusion you've got going on. From what I understand this is the first in a series, but SCP articles should be able to stand alone.

Entities inside being hostile but also asking for help (?)
