15 May 2017 21:00

The ambiguity is intentional. They think they have an explanation for the Doc's death, and they could be right. After all, someone could have just come along and stolen the abandoned boat, and there's plenty of animals in the wilderness who would have started making his body look half eaten.

Or is the monster real after all?

Originally I was going to post it as a main list, because I wrote it as 'the monster is real', and the meme effect is making them think it's explained, but I tried to leave it pretty up for interpretation. So in-universe, it's explained (or assumed to be explained, based on the conclusion you draw), thus it's in the 'explained' part of the database.

The mainlist version of it would have ended with a note telling Dr. R. to stop moving the document out of explained and back on to the mainlist, but I like the current ending better.
