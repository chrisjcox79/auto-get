SCP-5733: Knife. Scream. Cut to Black.

06 Nov 2020 23:45

Inspired by a love of '80s slasher movies and horror movies in general. This idea first came to me a few days prior to Halloween in the midst of a marathon of the aforementioned movie genres.

This is my first SCP in over 2 years, a massive thank you goes to all those who offered critique and guidance: Snapdragon133.

+Spoilers

-Spoilers

SCP-5733-1 takes on the identity of whoever is watching SCP-5733. It's why -1 is always one step ahead, why it knows thaumaturgy in test 28, and why there are two instances in test 017.
