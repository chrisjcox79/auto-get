Re: New Edits, Based on Feedback

25 Apr 2018 14:32

she isn't really, that big a threat. Which is why I initially figured containment procedures weren't that big of an issue.
附加记录：    

The Foundation doesn't contain anomalies because they're dangerous. The Foundation contains them because they're anomalous.

Having a teleporting person at Site-17, which is frequently used to house a ton of low-concern anomalies, is a security and safety liability. I personally believe that the Foundation would be much more likely to keep this entity in a separate location that doesn't have as much stuff in storage that the entity shouldn't know about, or just go the convenient route and just keep her sedated so she can't move around and cause additional problems. The Foundation has plenty of manpower, sure, but it's also going to be efficient with it.

It honestly feels like there's preferential treatment being shown to the entity for no reason, which breaks my suspension of disbelief. You can have the SCP entity roaming freely, but not in an area full of locations that require classified access. You can have Foundation staff be nice to her, but not in a way that goes against the organization's basic modus operandi. And it kind of seems like you as the author are getting really attached to the character on a personal level, so you might want to try and switch perspectives when handling containment.
