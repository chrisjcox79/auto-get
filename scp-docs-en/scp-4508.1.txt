Junior Staff - Site Criticism

06 Oct 2019 12:28

I enjoyed this!

Tanhonyrambles

TURNOFF

Just a few things that jumped out at me:

The Parazoology Division sends patrols to the area of discovery on a monthly basis. Any otter populations will be observed and contained if necessary.
附加记录：    

Containment procedures are always the best hooks, in my opinion, and these lines work excellently for that purpose. They contrast wonderfully with the previous description of the items physical containment, and they hint at the otterocious events that are to come.

The lucidity of SCP-4508-1 is currently unknown.
附加记录：    

Coming back to this after reading the entire SCP is just chilling, and it’s a good example of horror that works not only on the first read, but even better on subsequent ones.

I hit a weird looking dog or somethign, idfk what it was… dead now
附加记录：    

Had weirdest fucking dream. Got attacked by seals or some shit… when I woke up my room was a mess. W E I R D
附加记录：    

The inability to identify the animals as otters as first is a nice bit of comic relief, and it also helps us chart how Thomas begins taking the situation more seriously when he accurately identifies them. Nice inclusion!

12:17pm|Rich: s p o o k y
附加记录：    

The tone in these text exchanges is very nice - I can picture this as a conversation between me and one of my friends, only I would never get my life ruined by otters.

help
附加记录：    

I didn’t think I could be scared of an otter, but the picture here manages to do it. Just the way it’s reaching out to the camera gives me chills. Very nice choice of images here.

Overall, I really like this one. A wonderful mix of horror and humour that leaves the reader wondering just what the hell happened here, while following its own internal logic of vengeful otters.

+1
