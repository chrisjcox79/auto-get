16 Feb 2016 05:44

Ok, I'll bite (as in, upvote).

For me, this falls dangerously close to the line between expungement being interesting and annoying.You certainly throw a lot of clues around, but for a lot of them it's hard to tell if you're hinting at a grander structure or using cheap parlor tricks (e.g., mandala imagery, seances, possession with bizarre language/behavior/medical consequences) to jazz up a boring idea.

It's not that I doubt that there is a neat idea behind it, it's just not clear to me from this article. Ultimately I'm left feeling like I've just finished the intriguing but indeterminate pilot to a TV series, and that is not a good feeling to have about a single article.

The things I did quite enjoy include the descriptions of what things were like inside the location, the Theta Prime reference (if intentional), Manu 13 as a character, and the conversation about it being sent along. Those were enough for an upvote, even with my reservations.

(The new red text redaction language gets an honorable mention, but I can't push the needle too high on that one because it is ultimately just another flavor of redaction, and that's kind of my central beef.)
