BOLDPOST HAS ARRIVED

03 Sep 2018 09:02

I always wanted to build on Operation HEALTH's activities, and I don't think the well of wellness-related horror is dried up, so I decided to make this happen. Next up, something with heart surgery! And probably solvents!

The bit from the end of the monologue is taken from the flavor text of Heidar, Rimewind Master, which has stuck with me for the better part of a decade and that I've only now found the right opportunity to pay tribute.

The interview feels a little weak to me; the subject comes off as whiny and entitled. I realize that's probably the point, but I think the implication this creates is that the anomaly just makes you so pompous and dumb that you engage in self-mutilation to prove a point. It might be worth it to think about pulling back on the interview a smidge and implying that the anomaly causes an obsessive aversion to discomfort, rather than a principled aversion; people do this because they feel like they have to, without necessarily being able to justify this behavior.

That might be counter to your aim here, though.

Regardless: the idea of an anomaly that conflates a failure to experience pain with wellness is interesting, and the sheer self-destructive behavior it promotes is fun and creep-ifying.
