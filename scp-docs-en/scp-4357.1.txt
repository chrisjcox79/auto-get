28 Sep 2019 07:04

Thank you! I’m a bit sad about this piece’s performance as well. They can’t all be winners, of course, but I’m more proud of this article than some of my much higher-rated pieces. I wrote it largely as an exercise in storytelling through dialogue, and regardless of the score, I’m happy to see that it worked!

I think part of why it didn’t take off is that it’s much lighter on horror than many scp’s. It’s always important to know your audience when writing, and it looks like I missed the mark there.

If you liked it, I’d suggest checking out SCP-4170. It’s much less dialogue heavy, but I wrote it as a character study.
