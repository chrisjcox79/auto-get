27 Jul 2017 21:58

Way too over the top. Making the effect immunological, but able to resist memetics, makes no in-universe sense. Making it spread magically by proximity means there is no way it would be confined to Norway after almost a year (people from Norway do travel). I also fail to believe that the Foundation has the power to institute martial law worldwide (or do half the other things the containment procedures suggest), nor that this would be a logical approach in context. At some point the veil is blown, and executions (or shutting off global communications!) just makes things less secret, not more.

This SCP just felt ridiculous, and as others have said, way over the top. In this SCP:

The Foundation loses it secrecy from the world
The Foundation's entire O5 Council is killed
International Email, calling, texting, and all social media sites are somehow eliminated
Every single country is quarantined.
Society itself more or less falls apart and all countries are governed by Martial Law set by The Foundation's shadow governments
The Foundation leads a genocide against the Ethics Committee for some reason?

In my opinion, that's just too much. A simple allergy caused the end of the worldas we know it? I can't buy it. Downvoted.

Edit: I'd also like to mention that I feel like it seems unfinished as well. You're basically leaving the world in a state of disrepair with no resolution in sight.
