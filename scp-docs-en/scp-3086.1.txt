Excerpt of novel used is from Lord Foul’s Bane, first book in The Chronicles of Thomas Covenant, the Unbeliever by Stephen R. Donaldson.

Major Update: Audio Format has been uploaded! Leaving the text versions for people who can't listen and are willing to suspend disbelief. 

Oh god, and I was just a recording guy before

Super thanks to

Ba55

Yossipossi

Modulum

for speccing this before posting in the forums and on Discord.

Is the audio fighting you? Here's a YouTube link:

https://youtu.be/Dg-9H6WuRBA

+

-sense

If you've seen me around the site, it's because I have recorded Audio Adaptations of dozens of SCPs. When I came up with this concept, I realized two things about it that I wanted to make sure didn't happen.

1: I didn't want my first SCP to only be good based on the audio gimmick alone, so having it be  as we are used to on site is a good way to make sure the writing holds up and let suspension of disbelief that these are recorded do its job for now.

2: Letting the story go on the wiki text first allowed me to make any edits if anybody happened to spot something I didn't see or if the community came up with something that improves George vastly. It's far easier to edit a text than an audio post.

Now that the Audio Version (The True Canon) is complete, I'm leaving the logs up for people who can't listen to it because of being in public or because it's eleven minutes long.

and

+SCiP

-so

A: What if Fred was malevolent and had SCP-3043's powers?

B:

Audio Log 1: Hey, we have this dopey anomaly, let's poke it with a stick.

Audio Log 2: Nope, we should not have done that.

C: What if a fictional character just wanted people to care about it's creator?

A Tumblr Post of SCP-3086 Fan Art and that’s very exciting.
