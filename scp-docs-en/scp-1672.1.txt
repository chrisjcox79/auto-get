As I said in the chat, I have the same problem with this as I have with many of the AWCY? scps: if you have the power to directly and forcibly alter someone's patterns of thought , why are you using it to directly alter perceptions of people, in order to suggest a message, that might cause other people to think about things and change their own perceptions to what you want to convey? Hell, the entire concept renders all art meaningless, but that's neither here nor there: these people want people to think a certain way. They are narcissistic psychopaths who place no value on the lives or identities of other human beings. They have the capacity to directly and forcibly alter someone's thoughts. Why are they being so circuitous about it?

Art must allow a viewer to find their way to the meaning of the piece, however the exhibition is meant to prompt that.
