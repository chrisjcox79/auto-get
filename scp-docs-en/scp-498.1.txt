Reminds me of an experience I had with the same model clock radio as in the picture a few days ago. For an hour, I was hearing something that sounded like running water in an adjacent room and I thought maybe they'd left the tap on. It turned out, the clock radio was on and set between two stations. Every single button that I pressed except Snooze had absolutely no effect. When I hit Snooze, the noise stopped for five seconds and then came right back.

I unplugged it. The noise was still there. Then I noticed it had a battery backup. After I removed the battery… it kept making the noise! For two seconds, while it still had some residual charge. Before the noise finally stopped, I was about to put the thing down and get a hammer.

Long story short, I'm upvoting, just because this showed up less than a week after my experience with a haunted clock radio.
