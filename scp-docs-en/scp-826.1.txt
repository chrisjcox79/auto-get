Seconding the Experiment Log. Potential test materials: a book with no setting, like a corporate annual report; a book with no apparent library, study, or such location; a poetry collection; a coloring book; a reference book, like a dictionary, phone book, or technical manual; the script from a play; an orchestral score; and (oh gods I'm gonna get crucified for suggesting this) Cassie.

Other questions:

If 826 doesn't kick in till the subject leaves the room, does it only transform empty rooms?

As the subject leaves the room, can he tell what the containment room transforms into?

Can subjects communicate with the outside world via radio or satellite?

How do we know what happens to subjects stuck in the book when 826 resets?

Does time within 826 flow at the same speed as the outside world?
