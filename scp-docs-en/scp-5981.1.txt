The purpose of the incident logs are twofold:

To give some depth to the reality. In my opinion, there are a lot of anomalies that are solely used to carry a story. This is fine, but a lot of the time it doesn't feel fleshed out. I intended for this to be a change of pace; I want to show you what happens when you poke it with your finger.
The ending, where someone gets pushed into a nuclear reactor in real life.
