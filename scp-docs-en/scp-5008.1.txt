Silence will fall when the question is asked

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

So, how is it that a paragovernmental organization can have unlimited funding, hundreds if not thousands of massive research and/or prison facilities, diplomatic ties to every national government, agents in every police force and media outlet in existence, space programs beyond count, and a literally apocalyptic human rights record, and yet remain totally unknown to the public?
