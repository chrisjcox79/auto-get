Another SCP of mine

07 Nov 2018 19:20

This is now the 8th SCP I've written, and (hopefully) the 4th that will stay up on this website. This SCP took me a bit longer than usual to write, as I've been busy with college, but I'm glad to announce 4250! I'd like to thank all of you once again, and please, leave feedback! I'm always looking for ways to improve!

Also, the pens are my own, as are the samples, so there's no issues of copyright there. Feel free to read this article with the assurance that I have not infringed on any content in crafting this SCP for you all to read!

27 Sep 2019 17:47

I really enjoyed the read. I liked that you created a skip that allowed for you to write several types of stories while still maintaining a professional write up. I have seen more than a few entries use blue text to show updates to the log, but I believe this is the first time I have seen all these different colors being used. It works very well, is creative, and exceedingly unique among SCP entries.

Some points I think could be worked on:

test logs for all the pens
more information for the black pen. The [DATA EXPUNGED] does make sense in context, but perhaps you could have a code that says  and provide small details.
The interview could be improved a bit. As Damian Uck pointed out, if the pens were pretty simple to make, why not make another set?

All in all, a strongly unique entry, with some room to be fleshed out. It's a +1 from me.
