I'm not sure what's going on but there is that itch that there is an underlying something to everything happening here, and I can't deny that a lot of the sheer wtfness has me interested. Gonna withhold on voting until things click.

EDIT:

Okay,out.

Spoilers?know.

This is a play on redactions and how information can be obfuscated by them. The Foundation isn't redacting any information here (beyond the FM band); the location is literally named  are hiding crucial information needed to understand what is happening, and with that information hidden the Foundation can't figure out any rhyme or reason to this.

This is essentially the Foundation dealing with redactions that aren't their own. At least, that's what makes the most sense to me.

If I'm correct then this is very clever, and the fact that the article could keep me interested enough to actually think deeply on it is good, but how obfuscated this was and how it doesn't really go any other directions with the idea is holding back an upvote. I'll need to mull this over.
