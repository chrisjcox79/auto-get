So.. if I got this right..

+understand

-wrong

So, SCP-3980 is a contagious Infohazard, that makes everyone who is both alive and ‘infected’ by it appear dead to all outside viewers, and anyone dead and ‘infected’ appear alive. Any conversations with dead 3980-1 instances is just the speaker’s head filling in words (The corpse isn’t actually saying anything, but the person ‘talking’ to it hears what they thing the corpse would say if they were.. not a corpse). The vector of transmission is someone realizing that the people infected by 3980 are alive/dead, despite appearing dead/alive (The three words that Kim ‘says’ in the interview are ‘I am dead’), which explains why the Foundation explicitly states “there were no survivors”, and seemingly unnecessarily bury the entire site in cement.
