Feedback!

+block

–block

There's a seed of a cool idea here, a roundabout literally becoming a desert island for those on it, but for me the execution is off.

For one, the language is clunky and somewhat hard to follow. It was kind of fuzzy for me what exactly the boundaries of the effect were, and when or where certain things triggered. The descriptions aren't clear enough for me to really be able to visualize what this actually looked like, which is a problem since the visuals are 90% of the SCP.

It also lacks a hook. It's just a traffic circle that makes you hallucinate. The bit about people emerging from cars looking like they're emerging from sharks is cute, but it's focused on too heavily; that kind of bit works best as an aside in a log, not as a main focus of the article.

I like the concept, but I didn't enjoy the read

Best of luck!
