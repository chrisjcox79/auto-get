Fold

14 Mar 2013 15:31

I wasn't too crazy about the whole insectoid aspect to it, as I feel like something more mammalian would fit with these features. I also agree with a lot that's been said up above about the 15m range and the 1/16th shrinking (or shrinking at all, as I think its size would be a neat obstacle for it to have to overcome), but I don't have a problem with its initial size being 150kg. As for its eating, I think if it's going to eat so much, you should elaborate more beyond having it gain more lures and 'detach itself from its additional mass', which is really vague when you don't clarify what that mass is (the lures? body fat? I don't even think insects gain body weight like that), or how it detaches from it. I also have to agree with Anax on the whole human body thing. If it's going to impersonate a person, I think it should at least sockpuppet the body with its tongue or something, rather than completely remake it after digestion.

The journal entries are also kind of narmy and expected. I mean, I've tried making journals before, and never would I bother to use ellipses in said journal. That's so much wasted effort for a sentiment/tone I'd be able to recognize myself.

I'm going to neutral vote because I like the direction its taking, but I'd really like you to try revising it a bit.
