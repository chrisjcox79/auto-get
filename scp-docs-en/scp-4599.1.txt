27 Dec 2018 22:26

I couldn't really get into this one. It's dark and disturbing, but in a way that feels more just mundanely uncomfortable rather than uncanny or suspenseful. Topics like sexual abuse are hard to address in supernatural horror and I don't know if this article really has the sense of gravity to pull that off. In general, the ATF scips make me a bit wary because I can't shake the feeling that they fall back on negative stereotypes of anime fans and furries in a way that doesn't really subvert those tropes and just winds up in this awkward zone between goofy/ironic and disturbing.
