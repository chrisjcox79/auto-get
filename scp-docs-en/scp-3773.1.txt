Meow-thur Paw-mentary (Im so sorry)

11 Jul 2017 19:29

This has been in the works for a little while, well over two months, I think. And it's the first skip I've posted since the 3000 contest. Oliver in this is based off of a cat I owned of the same name, who vanished two years ago. This is dedicated to him, as sappy as that sounds.

This is set in the S  C P, as has Agent Ewell. Dr. Kola is the only one I haven't written into the canon yet.

Special thanks to SunnyClockwork and all others that helped critique this (my brain's frazzled from heat and travel, so if you helped to critique this, tell me and I'll add you here.)

11 Jul 2017 19:43

This is a tough one.

This is one of the rare SCPs that aims for an emotion other than fear or curiosity. It's heartfelt and touching and I like that.

With that said, it feels a little too nice for my taste. Everything works out fine, it really was just trying to helpplus, it pushed my suspension of disbelief a bit that the Foundation would be fine with her just playing with a cat after clearly developing an emotional attachment. Again, though, this humanizes the entire research staff in a way that really appeals to me, so that's sort of a point in its favor.

After much consideration and internal debate, I think I like more about this than I don't. +1.
