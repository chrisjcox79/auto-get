So this is my entry for Cliche-Con, A Spooky Scary Murder Monster. I play it pretty straight, there's nothing here that really elevates it above the trope, but I hope it's at least mildly interesting.

This was inspired by a creepypasta, The Thing That Stalks the Fields. It's not a particularly good creepypasta, but for some reason it stands out in my memory.
