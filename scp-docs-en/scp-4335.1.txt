Re: Author Post

17 Mar 2019 02:25

The terrain issue was me having a lack of time and ability; if I wanted to terraform it to be like the picture, it would have taken hours, and every hill around the entrance would need to be modified to fit in nicely.

The MKA is put there with the assumption that no civilian should ever actually face off with the anomaly. The docs are there because they know that civilians always nose around where they shouldn't, and that if a civilian got on the monster probably obliterated the chamber anyways. Or at least, that's my headcanon.

The chicken farm was pretty much laziness on my part tbh. I knew that lighting up the area around the spawners would take way too much time, so I started brainstorming any possible idea that would generate enough blocks, and decided upon the chicken farm. Of course, this has its own downsides; putting in all the chickens via eggs takes a lot of time, so it's more convenient in some ways and less in others.

Thanks for checking it out!
