I was hoping to avoid that because it kinda takes some of the fun out of it for me but I'll consider it. Might edit this post later tonight with a (spoilered) explanation.

EDIT: My explanation, feel free to come up with your own.

AncientAliens?

Nope.

A long, long time ago, a man was in love with a woman. The woman did something that pissed him off beyond belief. He made a deal with the devil or some similar entity ( is a reference to this deal via a reference to Tom Walker and the Devil, which I was reading in English at the time) in order to get back at her for what she did.

The details of this deal were that Mr. Angrydude would get the opportunity to kill this woman in every different reality/universe/dimension/whatever. These corpses are known to the Foundation as SCP-1646-2; they all come from a different universe. In exchange for the deal, Mr. Angrydude has to dispose of the bodies in our universe at a place of his choosing, to prove that he's still doing his work. He chose the West Coast of the United States because, at that time, that must have seemed like the edge of the universe.

Also he gets to be immortal to get to carry this out, and as a side-effect of, y'know, interdimensional travel, we get the physical symptoms that he experiences, primarily the albinism and bleeding blue. Although the latter was actually a pun on blue blood originally, since my headcanon originally has this guy as a member of the upper class, so this might count as back-justification.

Probably not as cool as y'all were hoping for, but it's what I got.
