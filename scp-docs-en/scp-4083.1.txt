Anyone who has spent time in a college town in the US will be familiar with the sort of building that houses SCP-4083. Three or four stories of grey vinyl siding, concrete or plank stairs in the middle of the building. The outside view will be asphalt, dead grass, and either pine trees or rhododendrons. This was inspired by that and also a Sigizmund Krzhizhanovsky short story I liked.
