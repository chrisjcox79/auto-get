Deja vu, anyone?

07 Jan 2017 01:10

DISCLAIMER:

Iposted

Wellthing.

This article is a rewrite of one from 'xenomorph666'. The original article is below, it was deleted. The user offered it to me for a rewrite, because I had an idea on how to work it. They seemed to be cool, and seemed like they really, really, really wanted to contribute to the wiki, thus I offered them co-authorship.

After a couple brainstorming sessions where I explained our goals, we got started. It quickly became apparent that they did not understand the thrust of the article, and each part they were assigned had to be culled. This was partially due to their inability, partially due to their failure to grasp the concept whatsoever.

The final product contains 100% my writing, 100% my vision. Their contribution was inspiring me with the title 'meat maze'. and that is literally it. Because I am far, far too nice, I let them post it anyways, since their article inspired it in the first place. I had zero issues sitting back as co-author, as I hoped they'd at least learn from the process and successfully contribute a solo work in the future.

That was of course until the user decided they needed to troll the wiki, including vandalising several sandboxes to include porn (mine included) and sending users porn and such through PMs.

I'm not comfortable covering for them anymore. They had no part of this. With permission from the Rewrite Team, I reposted.

Item #: SCP-XXXX

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-XXXX is located and contained in Area-40. The entrance to SCP-XXXX is constructed of reinforced steel with an electro-release lock mechanism. Five armed guards must be stationed near SCP-XXXX in case of the escape of SCP-XXXX-1.

Since ██-██-████ , no human subjects are allowed to enter SCP-XXXX under any circumstances.

Description: SCP-XXXX is an long sets of hallways constructed entirely out of organic matters. The length and size of the structure is unknown, but it is estimated to be ██ km long. SCP-XXXX appears to have an abnormally high regenerative ability as it is able to regenerate large chunks of tissues in seconds.

Creatures designated as SCP-XXXX-1 will form on the wall of SCP-XXXX from time to time. SCP-XXXX-1 specimens resembles a large, red, skinless, humanoid fetus with an abnormally large mouth at first. SCP-XXXX-1 at this stage are seen eating nearby objects. As SCP-XXXX-1 matures, SCP-XXXX-1 will slowly lose the ability to see as fleshy growth will began to cover their eyes, rendering them blind. SCP-XXXX-1 will become more hungry as it matures, causing them to attack nearby creatures or objects and consume them. SCP-XXXX-1 takes around two hours to mature. Observation shows that SCP-XXXX-1 becomes less intelligent as it matures.

If a human subject comes into SCP-XXXX without full body protection (skin exposed, etc), SCP-XXXX will rapidly grow tendons and ensnare them onto the wall. SCP-XXXX will then absorbs the victim onto the wall as subjects goes through a stage of  Subjects will slowly transform into a fetus as intelligence decreases. After the transformation, subjects will be converted into SCP-XXXX-1, SCP-XXXX-1 made from human subjects will be designated SCP-XXXX-2. SCP-XXXX-2 seems to retain most of their memories at first and suffer from severe polyphagia, making interviewing possible but dangerous. All SCP-XXXX-2 instances must be terminated two hours after their transformation.

SCP-XXXX was discovered at the construction of Area-40 when a group of miners broke into a section of SCP-XXXX, resulting in the release and attack of several SCP-XXXX-1 specimens. All escaped SCP-XXXX-1 instances are terminated and SCP-XXXX is sealed off.
