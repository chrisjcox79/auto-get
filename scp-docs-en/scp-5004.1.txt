- Undertale

This is my entry into the 5k contest. It's a little something different and I know some of you are going to hate it but it was a ton of fun to write and I don't regret it even a little bit.

This started from me overhearing  at a party last year, so there's that.

Image links in the header, thanks to the handful of people who read some of that. It's been a wild ride and I'm really happy with how things turned out.

Thank you everyone!

SIDE NOTE : in all the excitement of the last few days I totally forgot to attribute the person who provided the absolute lynchpin to this whole thing, daveyoufool. I cannot even dream of being able to capture the spanko voice, but Dave has come through in the clutch everytime I've required his dark talents. Thanks Dave!

Preferred slots: Closest number to 5001 that hasn't already been taken. 🌵
