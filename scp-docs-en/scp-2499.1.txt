Author Overview

18 May 2014 00:23

Thanks to CryogenChaos, Devereaux, TwistedGears, EdAWACSdenyY, HatMan, and the other members of chat for looking at this.

Forit:

SPOILERS

The stars themselves (or should I say “stars?”) are dancing to the music of The Planets. Each stage of the pulsating period for the SCP matches the various movements of the suite itself.

For example, the first movement of the suite, entitled “Mars - The Bringer of War” is a big, dramatic, intense, and fast piece that is full of pomp and grand order, much like a call to arms. Therefore, the movement of the stars matches that in its order, speed, and intensity of motion.

A good basis is, tempo=velocity, style=movement patterns, intensity=luminosity. There are other connections as well, but telling all of those would spoil the fun. :)
