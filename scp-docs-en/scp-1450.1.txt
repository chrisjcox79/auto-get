01 May 2012 00:50

I feel it's justified in being an SCP for several reasons:

A. It can eat and shit latex paint. That's theoretically something that could be evolved, but consider we only started using latex paints in the last few decades

B. It hunts people. It's specifically designed to inhabit urban areas and hunt humans. There's a word for that: Bloody dangerous. It's two words, and now my colon is clenching a little because there's a lot of abandoned buildings around here. (What have I done?)

C. It's got no identifiable genus. That means there's nothing to tie it to the rest of the naturally evolved species on earth. This thing appears to have just POPPED into existence.

Given all that, I feel like the foundation would be justifiably paranoid enough to start burning it out of existence while studying a small sample.
