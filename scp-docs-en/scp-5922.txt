Special Containment Procedures:  Foundation webcrawler ANGELA.aic is to monitor the Internet and news outlets for cases of sudden heart failure, with particular focus on deaths of individuals sharing an online space. ANGELA.aic is to regularly query chat room logs gathered by ORPHEUS.aic in search of similar messages and scan them for potential cognitohazards.

Description:  SCP-5922 is a cognitohazardous message capable of inducing heart failure in a subject that perceives it. SCP-5922 was discovered after an event that occurred on 2020/09/22 and caused the deaths of eleven individuals between the ages of 18 and 26 in a period of 7 minutes. All subjects were found to have been communicating in an online chat room at the time of death.

On 2020/09/25, containment teams located Alexandre Castro, a non-anomalous civilian involved in the incident. Mr. Castro was found in a state of considerable psychological distress, and has since been amnesticized and provided with grief counseling.
