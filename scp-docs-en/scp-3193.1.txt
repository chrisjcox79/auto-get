04 Feb 2020 17:43

While I was writing the logs, I was unsure whether I wanted to give a conclusive ending to their story. But I can give you a couple of ideas.

1. They both sacrificed themselves.

An arrow to your chest is a pretty dangerous thing. Without proper medical care, you could die pretty quickly. And since there's no  with Jack, (or Julie) you could reasonably assume that he died fighting whatever was in that control room.

2. They were both treated for their injuries, amnesticized, and released.

This is a pretty cut and dry response. Standard operating procedure.

3. They were recruited.

This is also a pretty viable answer. Even without training, they're both very resourceful. And since this isn't a one-time incident, you'd want experienced people who are immune to 3193 assisting in containment.
