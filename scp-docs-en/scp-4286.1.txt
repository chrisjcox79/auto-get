+Nitpicks

-Nitpicks

These bones will seamlessly integrate themselves into the body's skeletal system. SCP-4286 affected bones will produce blood cells at a rate consistent with standard human bones. SCP-4286 affected individuals have shown a notable resistance to disease, with most individuals being completely cured of their previous skeletal ailment two months after their SCP-4286 occurrence. SCP-4286 affected bones have shown remarkable strength, with instances withstanding more than 5000 newtons of force without damaging. All attempts to surgically implant instances of SCP-4286 affected bones have resulted in rejection from the host body.
附加记录：    

This bit here kind of feels almost like a  section about the anomaly, with a lot of sentences starting in a similar manner (e.g. SCP-4286 affected [something]), leading to the section coming off as disjointed (heh puns).

Also, incredibly small nit to pick, but in what way can the instances withstand 5000 newtons of force? If I hit the top of a femur, it can take somewhere around 26700 newtons before breaking, but if I hit the side of it, 4000 newtons can do the trick. This is the difference between these bones barely being able to hold most average body weight versus being stronger than a regular bone.

SCP-4286-1 then reaches into its hat and produces a small bandage stylized with a cartoon heart. This bandage is removed from its packaging and adhered to the child's arm. SCP-4286-1 gathers the items that were removed from its hat placing the hat on its head. SCP-4286-1 then bends over the child, touching its mouth to the child's forehead in a kissing motion.
附加记录：    

Gonna be honest, this right here is what makes the article. It's so wholesome, strange, and just generally WTF in the best kind of way. Really, the whole log is awesome.

00:02:14 - Black smoke rises from below the camera, obscuring the view of the room.
附加记录：    

With the above praise, there is a minor caveat. Not a fan of the anomalous black smoke to obscure the camera feed. I really would have preferred if he appeared and disappeared via zany circumstances, like coming out of a toy chest and disappearing into the closet, for example. Overall a minor gripe, but the black smoke feels a bit too out of nowhere for this anomaly.

I wasn't so sure about where this was going by the end of the description, so the introduction of a skeleton in a party hat playing doctor and knocking over toys came totally out of left field in the best possible way. +1 from me.
