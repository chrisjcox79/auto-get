21 Sep 2018 16:51

They refers to David Kincaid, as Okoro was never given the designation SCP-4080-1, only PoI-3283. And presumably, this document was written sometime before the end of the re-run period, so there is no telling what will happen once the series hits the end of SCP-4080-1's life again.

From my understanding, this is more like a serialized TV show rather than a weird record of this guy's life for an unknown civilization, whether alien or based on some other sort of spatial/temporal anomaly. When a show has its series finale, that show only repeats old episodes, regardless of the child-series. (Think Breaking Bad to Better Call Saul, or Doctor Who to Torchwood, there are thousands of examples) So maybe other frequencies are broadcasting some sort of spinoff of PoI-3283's life, but it's not something we know about at the time this document was put into the SCP database.
