Item #:  SCP-5547

Clearance:  3

container-class:  euclid

disruption-class:  ekhi

risk-class:  notice

threat level:  red

Special Containment Procedures:  SCP-5547-1, SCP-5547-2, and SCP-5547-3 are held in a 10x10x5 meter containment cell connect to 2 (two) standard humanoid containment chambers. Should any instance of SCP-5547 misbehave, threaten SCP-5547-1 with thermal weaponry until they stop. When a discussion event occurs attempts to convince them to bring things back with them or take things can be done with the approval of an O5, such attempts are to stop if SCP-5547-3 claims . SCP-5547-1 and 2 are to hand over any item it brings back but may request them for future narrative events. After a narrative event a d-class is to be sent in to retrieve the SCP-5547-4 instance, which will then be added to the site breakroom. Should a death event occur SCP-5547-2 is to be transferred to a 5x5x5 meter secondary containment chamber filled with water for one hour with in five (5) minutes.

Description:  SCP-5547-1 is a red type capable of altering it's biology to a high degree showing the ability to replicate traits of several anomalous lifeforms. It primarily takes the form of an underweight Caucasian male teenager and in this form displays sapience and sentience. SCP-5547-1 displays extreme thermophobia do to thermal weaponry causing several death events. Death events are the events that occur when SCP-5547-1 dies. Five minutes after it's death a gaseous substance will fly out of SCP-5547-2 and start releasing a unknown amount of energy for approximately 40 minutes resulting in an instance of SCP-5547-1 with all memories of the previous instance. During discussion events they spend most of their time listening, making simple suggestions to SCP-5547-3. During narrative events they have shown anomalous knowledge, however they have not been confirmed to use this knowledge outside of them. SCP-5547-1 is generally friendly and talkative, often talking to SCP-5547-2 or asking SCP-5547-3 for snacks and to help in clean the containment chamber.

SCP-5547-2 is a light blue crystal ball with a radius of 30cm and a mass of 50 kilograms that constantly produces EVE, SCP-5547-2 is at least as intelligent as an adolescent human but do to it's aggressive behaviour this can't be can't test further. SCP-5547-2 is made of a substance that is indestructible, non conductive, and completely unreactive. SCP-5547-2 can manifest SCP-5547-2A which is theorized to be made of the same substance as SCP-5547-2 within 10 meters of it and stay in the same position relative to it. It can move SCP-5547-2A with it's thoughts however the substance will disappear if farther than 10 meters from SCP-5547-2 and can't directly interact with SCP-5547-2. It uses SCP-5547-2A to attack anyone near it other than SCP-5547-1 or 3. During discussion events it is very critical while offering little advice. The object is sarcastic, and openly hostile towards everyone except SCP-5547-1. In narrative events it shown high levels of thaumatology capabilities in SCP-5547-1's possession.

SCP-5547-3 is a underweight Caucasian male teenager with the ability to alter reality with a method unknown to the foundation to an unknown degree. During discussion events it will usually suggest a human or humanoid with knowledge of the anomalous, and two anomalies which it comes up with ideas for stories including them and SCP-5547-1 and 2. During narrative events he stays in the real world and handles the side effects of narrative events, only appearing in narratives if something goes wrong in it.

Discussion events normally occur a week before a narrative event, however they have also lead to the disappearance, changes, destruction, and appearance of anomalies and people with knowledge of the anomalous. SCP-5547-3 always starts the conversation, SCP-5547-1 listens quietly to him, and SCP-5547-2 points out lack of originality, inconsistencies and missing information. For an example of a discussion event see addendum 1.

Narrative events consists of the disappearance of SCP-5547-1 and 2, the humanoid mentioned, and depending on unknown variables instances of the anomalies mentioned. SCP-5547-3 will then use it's reality altering abilities to act in place of the humanoid, and depending on the anomaly act in it's place, explain to those associated with the anomaly it is safe, or nothing. After a time varying from 5 minutes to 6 months SCP-5547-1 and 2 return in the containment cell, unless a death event occurs in during the narrative event then only SCP-5547-2 returns. The Humanoid returns as it was either before or at the end of the narrative sapience beings claim they got to choose, however they keep memory of the event either way, if the humanoid is not sapience it is always returned to how it was before the event with no memory of it reappearing where SCP-5547-3 claims they belong. Anomalies that are not in SCP-5547-1 or 2's possession or destroy return to where SCP-5547-3 claims they belong, anomalies destroyed don't reappear, anomalies in SCP-5547-1 or 2's possession at the end of the evw return in SCP-5547-1 or 2's possession. On the desk in one of the standard humanoid containment chamber connected their containment chamber SCP-5547-4 will appear. SCP-5547-4 are books written in English about the items that disappeared in the narrative event that caused it. SCP-5547-4 instances have no anomalous properties other than their origin. For an example of a narrative event see addendum 2.
