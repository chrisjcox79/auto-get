26 May 2020 20:40

I've decided to no-vote this. The punchline feels like it doesn't have adequate setup and flies in the face of the rest of the article's tone. While that disjointed tone is the point of the joke and the punchline ostensibly appears to be , it ended up being rather predictable. This may have been more impactful if I came to this article without knowing it was supposed to be a -J. Super serious and wrathful tone from the unnamed character + knowing this is a -J = me expecting the unnamed character to be pissed about something petty. Ultimately, it doesn't surprise me or subvert that expectation and the expectation itself wasn't enough to carry this for me.

Edit: per the other commenters, I very much enjoyed the buildup you provided. It added a sense of dread incrementally in a manner that would make Stephen King proud. Unfortunately, it still feels like a disjointed end tacted on to a wonderful progression of tension.
