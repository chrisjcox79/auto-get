26 Jul 2013 16:44

Well there's only two designations:

SCP-1828 is the shapeshifting box
SCP-1828-A are the glass models, some of which are octopus-shaped

The regular miniatures don't get designations because they are/appear to be completely non-anomalous.

As for the shapechanging nature of the box: Right now it's in your home, reflecting the floorplan of that level of the building (including other apartments, if you're in an apartment block), at 1/12 the size of the floorplan. Before it next updates, you put it in a garden shed in the backyard, and during the next update the whole thing shrinks to show just the shed, at 1/12 scale. Then you take it to your high school and during the next update it expands to reflect the floor plan of the school, at 1/12 scale. The update is instantaneous, so it's not like you can see it shrink or grow, it's just becomes a different size during the update.

As for the octopodeal things, who said that they're manipulating you? ;-)
