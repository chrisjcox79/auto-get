13 Feb 2011 12:56

It's not a meme, though. This falls under the umbrella of "weird mind shit". This is not an idea of any kind.

The vector for SCP-405 is the subject's final mental vocalisation, identified by most observers as a "Death Scream." This vocalisation occurs at the moment of death, regardless of cause, and has a range of at least 200m, although the more advanced the disease the greater the range. Individuals who hear this telepathic signal are infected with SCP-405.
附加记录：    

This right here, this mechanism of spread completely precludes it being memetic.

Addendum 405-01: Request use of SCP-148 in containment rooms. -Agent ██████

Denied, SCP-148 stocks are low enough as it is and we can treat anyone caught in the radius of a SCP-405 termination. In addition memetic readings  predict terminal phase activation is a strength ████ signal, requiring ██ of SCP-148 to shield. -O5-█
