Edit:

The Scene:  A normal, small-town Wendy's restaurant a few hours before opening.

The Cast:  The Opening Shift Manager, a wildly depressed person who simply can't be mustered to give a fuck anymore. The Opening Shift Chef, some teenager who's just here for the summer.

Enter Opening Shift Manager , preparing for their work day.

Opening Shift Manager  notices something strange as they walk to their office: the entire menu at their restaurant has changed. Curious, and worried, they go through their kitchen, seeking answers. Eventually, they open the Freezer .

Inside the Freezer , there are several Chains  and Meathooks . The Freezer  has no apparent bottom.

Opening Shift Manager : (sighs) Why does this shit always happen to me?

Exit OSM  to their office.

Enter Opening Shift Workers 

Opening Shift Chef  goes to the Freezer  while the other workers notice the changes to the menu. OSC  notices Chains  and Meathooks , shrugs, operates Meathooks  to retrieve Demon Flesh , begins grilling while whistling nonchalantly.
