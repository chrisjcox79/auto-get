Edit: On why this is icky. There are three possibilities for this anomaly:

1: The doorknob is unnaturally attractive and reams of Site-19 staff are already overt sexual harassers. Which, gross.

2: The doorknob compels people to sexually harass it. Which, super-gross.

3: The doorknob is SO attractive that it MAKES people harass it. Which, hi there victim-blaming.

And no, the facts that it's 1) an inanimate doorknob and 2) a joke SCP don't make that better. I might look past it if the joke were drop-dead funny, but it's not.
