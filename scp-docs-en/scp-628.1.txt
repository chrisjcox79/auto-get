Inspired by a large sycamore on my college campus whose broken-off upper trunk has been protected with a bit of sheet metal that looks a lot like a clarinet mouthpiece. Thanks to pooryoric and MisterFlames for advice.

Some of the sources I used to develop this article can be found below:

+Sources

-Sources

Acoustics Monitoring Program, a potential source of sound clips

One of the initial inspirations

Also, I would be very grateful if someone with a sound-editing program could take a good recording of Bach's Organ Fugue in G Minor, the "Little Fugue", and transpose it down several octaves and halve the tempo.
