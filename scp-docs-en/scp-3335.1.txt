Long sections of this come across as entirely overblown. This is particularly true of the dialogue sections - the first interview feels like every possible cliched phrase was worked into it, with neither party sounding like a realistic human, and the third is heavily committed to a very tired trope. The mission log could be cut down to about a third of its current length without losing any important information, and it would probably gain in realism and tension - too much time is spent on preliminaries that are distracting and don't serve to create a connection to the MTF characters.

In addition, the  category), meaning the story ends before it can get interesting.

Overall, this is a very large amount of text to tell a  story (and zombies is the right analogy, on the basis of loss of agency and compulsion to spread the effects). That's not new ground, and the work that's been put into this isn't really enlivening the idea.
