29 Jun 2012 18:04

It's good, but Nobody/The Breath feels tacked on, really extraneous to the item itself.

Possibly this is because I still don't get what you are hinting at above. What am I looking for in the picture? I see four figures, a man brandishing a sword (presumably Alexander), a second man, a woman, and either a midget or a child standing on a pedestal. I'm not picking up on anything that relates to either body parts or the personalities/functions that seem to go with them. Unless you are saying that there is signifigance in the mere fact that there are 4 figures and four aspects not counting breath. But that seems really insubstantial and leads to no interesting implications.

Second, -1 is objectively four different personalities. They refer to themselves by separate names and each has a consitent behavior pattern that is markedly distinct from the others. Wether these personalities are part of a single entity (the ghostly equivalent of multiple personality disorder) or are 4 separate things that happen to time-share a magic walkie-talkie is irrelevant. The fact that the research team has formed competing schools of opinion on the matter has no place in what is ostensibly a formal briefing.
