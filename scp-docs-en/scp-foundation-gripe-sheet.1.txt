This is a collab tale and is open to those who feel inspired to add reports of their own.

Based off of the airplane gripe sheet that's floating around the internet. The reports are short, almost curt, and are humorous, though to varying degrees.

If you get an idea, go for it, but don't post half-baked ideas as I will edit this for quality control. If unsure, feel free to run an idea through the comments, via chat, or PM me.

If a post is on the fence, I may try to edit it, though others just deleted. Imagine a humorous maintenance related situation within the Foundation and try to trim it down to one sentence to describe it and one sentence to fix it.

tl;dr bullets for adding reports

Don't force weak idea.
Keep the reports short. Super short.
Limit 10 per page. Start a new page after that.
New reports are to be added to the bottom of the latest page.

To attribute to this tale:

_ is based on : http://www.scp-wiki.net/scp-foundation-gripe-sheet
