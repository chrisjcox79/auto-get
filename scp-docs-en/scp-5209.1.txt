So, we got Earth B where the video takes place, and Earth A is our normal Foundation. The team in Earth B is sent to survey the church that was messing around with some sort of snake people vodou. During their time there, the Way is opened and the snake chick comes to Earth B and the dude is taken taken through the Way. What isn't in the article is that the Way led to Earth A and that's where the vodou snake people monsters are from. When the dude was brought through the Way his camera automatically connected to Earth A's SCiPNet and uploaded all its data. That's why the log ends when the way closes, the connection to Earth A's SCiPNet was lost.
