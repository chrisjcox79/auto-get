04 Oct 2018 01:52

I think the main issue with this one is that it's too short for its own good. There's no time for build-up, to get the reader off-guard - it just goes bang, bang, bang, bang, bang, the turns coming right away and with no subtlety. I'm left feeling like I needed to invest nothing in this, I didn't really have a chance to get disturbed or even confused.

Granted, I'm glad that there are SCPs that aren't sprawling epics, which even if they work can be a bit of a long haul to read, but this one felt like it needed a little more depth to it. Something to get me pulled in before it tried to mess with me.
