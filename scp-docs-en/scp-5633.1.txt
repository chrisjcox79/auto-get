23 Jul 2020 17:51

I'm gonna novote this SCP for now, mainly due to me not really understanding what's happening for the most part (or at least the Experiment Log 5633-█-█ part of the SCP).

+issues

-issues

The formatting of the dates seem to switch around between Year/Month/Day, Month/Day/Year, and Day/Month/Year. I'd suggest to be consistent and only use 1 date format.
I think I understand that something changed history, but it started to get confusing as it went on. I can understand that there's time travel-wackery going on though.
The Experiment Log 5633-█-█ part specifically confuses me the most. To me, it seem to slowly not make sense.
You begin to introduce new instances of SCP-5633, but never really explain what they are. Like who or what are SCP-5633-β, SCP-5633-δ, SCP-5633-γ, and SCP-5633-Γ? When I was writing this, I thought that they could be Foundation members, but I don't think I got that until after.
I do like the ACS header, but it changes fairly quickly. I had to wait for it to go through a few times before I knew what each version said. I'd suggest slowing the switch speed of the header (assuming you can)

BOO! This was actually written as an Exquisite Corpse.

23 Jul 2020 18:03

So, all of y'all who are saying it is incoherent or difficult to understand. there's largely a reason for that. This was actually NOT written by only me. In fact, I only  wrote the Containment Procedures. From there, it was written as an Exquisite Corpse by all the authors mentioned in the original credits.

We wanted to initially post it to see how the reaction would be without this knowledge and it was not too far from what we expected :p

It was fun to see, though!

If you guys have any theories as to an internal story or coherent logic to the whole thing, stick with it! Maybe there is! I hope the knowledge of this doesn't ruin the article for anyone.

Thanks guys!

PS. We started writing this back in mid January so the Exquisite Corpse contest totally stole our secret idea from us! /s
