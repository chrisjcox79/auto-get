05 Jan 2019 20:36

I think I get the general idea, but there seems to be an unnecessary level of obfuscation both on the description and the logs which is just confusing me as to what's actually going on. If the foundation have figured out a problem with the level of access granted to the pen and with the trustworthiness of its researchers and experimental personnel, to the extent that it's caused an O5 to be removed from office (correctly? otherwise?) one would expect that the events leading up to it would be covered in better detail, with clear redactions where you don't want to name names or whatever rather than the data simply being omitted.

It's interesting, and there's a clever idea here, particularly in that it seems to be more of a semantic threat than an actual reality altering one (ie it changes how people perceive things, and we've seen with e.g. The Forest Where Names Have Power that this can really, really mess things up, even within a Foundation investigation that's otherwise worked out that there's *something* wrong) but because I reach the end of it feeling baffled, and that I'm baffled by deliberately incomplete writing, it's a no-vote for now.
