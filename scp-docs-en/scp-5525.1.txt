First skip I've written in over three years, this one's about the US government finding and looting Atlantis.

To start with, some crit credits. A lot of people glanced at this, so if I forgot your name, let me know:

A Random Day
ch00bakka
Anorrack
minathewitch

Second, some NOTES:

The list of missing superweapons here is meant to serve as a potential hook for future articles (skips, tales, goi formats), not unlike the Little Misters list. My hope is to eventually build a much larger and more detailed view of AQUARIAN DISCO and their history by offering snippets in each superweapon article, and in the process also provide a look at some of the political and diplomatic interactions that go on behind the Veil. If you're interested in taking a stab at one of these, hit me up.
There's more to the containment here than just the blockade, but the Foundation isn't privy to all of the details of the GOC's activities.
SCPS Jump Jacked and Lunatic Leap (as well as Daring Dash) are all clandestine aircraft carriers and submarine tenders  they look like normal civilian ships, but can hold one or two submarines in internal holds, and carry a few wings of aircraft. The GOC vessels are straight-up warships, probably comparable to a light cruiser.
