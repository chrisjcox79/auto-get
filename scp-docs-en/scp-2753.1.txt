I really like this as an AWCY object. I feel like their usual thing (douchebags who kill people with art to show off how smart and edgy they are) is tough to pull off without making them just seem like comic book villains.

This particular item really nails it for me though  booby-trapping a convention of other anartists to send some vague and pretentious message is AWCY at their worst(/best), but has an internal logic that makes sense to me. I feel like it displays a pettiness and insecurity that really fleshes out the motives of the group as a whole.

I was actually thinking about this, and had an idea:

Addendum XXXX.2:  On 15/05/2015, several IntSCPFN1 servers experienced critical crashes simultaneously. Analysis of data found the crash occurred as a result of an update made to an SCP file which invalidated several cross-links to that file.
