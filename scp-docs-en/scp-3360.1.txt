This is really good! The idea of art being made by thought is an incredible idea, and you executed it very well. Especially with the idea of , that really draws sympathy to the SCP and its creator for simply wanting to do good.

Not to mention the surrealness of the blind D-Class's art piece, it feels very appropriate that a blind person would think in mostly blacks, but also that the piece itself has to do with running into things and shattering, almost as if it's a literal interpretation of how the blind person thinks of his blindness (A curse that causes me to run into things, and each time it feels worse and worse and I break more and more). Very well done! +1

I really feel there was a missed opportunity here to give good characterization to the entity which has a decentralized identity, never being able to create its own art while always being filled with the ideas of other people. While I did enjoy the visceral and well-described imagery of the test logs it ultimately didn't do enough for me. I also like the note from San Francisco Jones but I think the content is less interesting than if you had put the effort into writing from the perspective of the SCP. It's well put together and enjoyable, but I still find it lacking. No vote.
