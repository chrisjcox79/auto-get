Death to the Prototype

18 Jun 2018 18:22

Gear's Proposal is one of my favorite 001 proposals, and I've wanted to write something for it. But the biggest appeal of that article is how mundane and simple it is - how do you honor those original themes of that article?

By killing it off.

At the same time I was getting around to finishing this, Magnus's OldCon was ending. I realized that this had something literally dying of old age in it, and a little tweaking of the middle logs solidified the themes of growing old and growing up  it's not just the Prototype aging here, but the Foundation as a whole.
