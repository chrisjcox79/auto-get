01 Jan 2013 11:01

This is one of my favorite themes among SCP-class containment articles. The idea of this group of odd mechanical entities existing, then being confused to their own existence, then manifesting the 'pauper's post' as a political idea among anomalous entities, rather than being innate in their nature, is awesome.

The world is odder still than we understand. There are things that live outside our understanding, and they have ideas, and factions, and political arrangements, and all sorts of things, beyond our little politics. Whatever made these metal men did not make them for this, no more than who ever made us fleshy men made us for empire or tyranny or whatever we do in our day to day lives. We exist, as they exist, and as both mayhaps always have and always will exist, and try to piece together meaning for why we do so. This is their attempt to find meaning.
