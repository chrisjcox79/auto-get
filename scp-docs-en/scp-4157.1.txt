Closecrits

There are occasional issues with clinical tone throughout the article. For example:

SCP-4157-2 instances are aggressive and sporadic, and will act with hostility towards anyone/anything in their surroundings upon arrival.
附加记录：    

could have been phrased much more clearly. And the footnote about the GoI sounds, frankly, as though it were written by the GoI. It could be presented as a quotation from some source document, or set up with additional support elsewhere as a memetic effect, but as written it comes off as kind of sloppy.

The video log at the end was pretty much exactly what I was expecting based on the lead-in. There was definitely horror and there was definitely comedy, but for me they never really found a balance point that stuck. Instead of being creeped out or amused, in the end I was just sad that this delusional anomalous artist and/or businessperson is out there serially hurting dogs (and possibly people) because they've convinced themselves that that's a good thing to do.

Upvote!

24 Dec 2018 19:15

So, I agree those issues should be fixed, but I upvoted this because (A) the article is creative and fun and (B) I'm so tired of clinical tone being critiqued without useful suggestions to fix it.

So I'm going to provide some suggestions. Take them or leave them. I'm going to give your version followed by a suggested version. Now, given that I've been pinged a lot of clinical tone myself, maybe take with a big grain of salt, but I figure it can't hurt.

+block

–block

Before I start, I'll note in passing that it's a little odd that we learn about the video the researcher got and but not the SCP-4157-2 instance itself.

Anyhoo, on to the suggested edits

This is to occur until the SCP-4157-2 instance following the call manifests.
附加记录：    

Individuals are to be kept away until the SCP-4157-2 instance following the call manifests.
附加记录：    

Current investigations into GOI-4998 are underway, although tracking of the number on SCP-4157-1 yields no results to viable locations for a front organization for GOI-4998.
附加记录：    

Current investigations into GOI-4998 are underway, although tracking the number on SCP-4157-1 instances have yielded no viable locations for front organizations for GOI-4998.
附加记录：    

SCP-4157 events have only been shown to occur in North America, with SCP-4157-1 instances only appearing in areas and at times where no video surveillance and/or human viewing is currently present.
附加记录：    

SCP-4157 events have only been known to occur in North America, with SCP-4157-1 instances only appearing in areas and at times without video surveillance or human witnesses.
附加记录：    

Most SCP-4157-2 instances are hostile and sporadic, but do not survive for long periods of time after their initial appearance due to all recorded specimens showing some degree of distortion of organ, muscle, and bone structure compared to normal canines, resulting in major organ failure within the first day of manifestation in 85.6% of SCP-4157-2 instances. 44.7% of the previous percentage are SCP-4157-2 instances that are deceased upon arrival to the individual's household.
附加记录：    

Living SCP-4157-2 instances are hostile, but do not survive for long due to distortion of organ, muscle, and bone structure compared to normal canines, resulting in major organ failure within the first day of arrival at the delivery residence in 85.6% of cases. 44.7% of the SCP-4157-2 instances that die on the day of arrival are deceased even before opening the box.
附加记录：    

As a courtesy to our readers on mobile devices, please collapse long posts. ~Zyn

I disagree with mayoculpa about the description of the GoI. It's short, but it gets the point across, so I have no suggested edit there.

Overall, great SCP, and don't let the dado comparisons get you down. I'd love to see more from the MAKE WISH FOUNDATION. Merry Christmas, and have another +1! :)
