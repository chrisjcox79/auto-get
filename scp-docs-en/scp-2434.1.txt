Fold

07 Oct 2014 10:59

I understand your sentiment. However, these cars have a purpose when they do a spacetime jump to arrive here, at Earth. Firstly, doing a jump in their world is illegal. Second, you also have to remember that these cars are wrecked when they're being interviewed, so they also risk that. Third, it is implied that cars are purposely trying to travel back to this time.

Aside from -12, all other cars interviewed have a motive to travel back. -17 is an outcast, -19 wants to see its mother, and the aircraft was hijacked by some radical traffic cabs.

If such a risk is taken to escape from one world to another, you would want to tell your story objectively.

As a final note, the interviews serve as our only indication towards what  might be like. There's no way to travel to their world, so interviews are the next best thing.

I hope that clears up an issues. If anything else doesn't make sense, feel free to ask.
