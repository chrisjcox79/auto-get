Beautiful, but one thing:

You say, "Inhaling SCP-534-1 and SCP-534-3 is lethal. Like SCP-534-1, SCP-534-3 appears to have an indefinite lifespan." This is a little confusing, as it could be read to say that, individually, inhaling either -1 or -3 will result in death. I believe what you mean from context is that inhaling the two while clumped will result in death.

It's so minor that you don't even have to fix it. I figured out what you meant, so others most likely will as well.

Also really happy to see more from Prometheus labs. As it is, I think only 148 mentions them in the main SCP series.
