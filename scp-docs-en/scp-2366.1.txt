What about when people have conflicting goals?
附加记录：    

This catalyzes teamwork and cooperation, it doesn't literally apply goals . Note that it helped both competing basketball teams as well both the escaping D-Class and the security team that stopped them.

Let’s say a foundation representative and 5 or 6 members of different groups of interest are all given the goal of finding a way that all of them can coexist without any of their goals being compromised.
附加记录：    

Each team would have enhanced cooperation. This would probably mean that the lone Foundation operative is at an increased disadvantage. Unless they could bring the parties to negotiate, in which case it would probably make negotiations go more smoothly and the  goal would be more swiftly abandoned then it would have otherwise been.

I'm sorry that it didn't for you. It is intended to add quite a lot, actually, from a fridge horror perspective. The anomaly itself is well-contained, has a relatively limited and rather harmless effect, and the tests show that it has very limited potential for weaponisation. But this one item recontextualises everything - suddenly this anomaly has great potential to enable the destruction of life as we know it. Does that change the implications of the Native American ? How could the other items (the paper, the traditional remedies) be similarly misused?
