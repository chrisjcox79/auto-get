Damn, this was a fun read.

Test log based articles arguably haven't been in fashion for a while, but I think this does a bang-up job utilizing the simple premise to an entertaining if not outright horrifying extent (the switching of the broken/unbroken dichotomy must have been particularly messy).

The twist at the end is fairly predictable given the content of the article but is executed rather well. Given such articles as WJS's 001 proposal on the site, the execution of this dichotomy switch could be as simple as switching what the meaning behind the list is, which leads to fun potential tale ideas.

+1. Well done.
