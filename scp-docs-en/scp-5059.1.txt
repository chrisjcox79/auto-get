TRANSFORMERS - ROBOTS IN DISGUISE

This is a tribute to 80's nostalgia, and in some ways SCP wiki nostalgia as well. Ever re-watch a film from your childhood and just, really wanna live that drama, have those powers, go on those adventures?

Well maybe if you're lucky your dream will come true.

Image is taken right off of Flicker under CC By 2.0. You can find it here.

Many people to thank. The usual folks had eyes on this over time, Cerastes, Fullham, Akimoto, Graves. Also CityToast gave a test read, and another fellow in 19 right before it went up.

This might be the most ridiculous thing I've ever written to completion in service of the Foundation wiki. I hope it makes you all as happy as it made me to write it.

I really enjoyed this for the description and the first two test logs, however by the third one it felt like it was starting to slip into torture porn more than serving any descriptive purpose. I think cutting the second test log entirely, and dialing back the rest just a tad would make this a more palatable experience without compromising the horror that works.

Everything here is working phenomenally well but the testing logs take just a bit too much time wallowing in how awful this effect is. While that's delivering on what the description promised, there's a certain portion of it that just feels like horribleness for its own sake and not developing what's already been established. That's a fine line you have to walk in an SCP like this, and this does it for the most part, but you're going to have to dial it back there about 20%.

Whether you like it or not, history is on our side. We will bury you!
