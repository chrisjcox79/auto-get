Eh, this is a story about a drawing that communicates. We already have one that I can think of off the top of my head, and doubtlessly multiple others that I can't. This plays the tired concept pretty much exactly straight, with the exception of some logs that add nothing but length.

There are some attempts at feels stapled on for no apparent reason. In the best case, feels can add emotional depth and even recontextualize an article; this just goes for -style pathos, without giving the reader any reason to care.

-1. I would suggest running this by the forum/chat again. One angle I wish you had gone with was the fact that the  there is plenty of stuff to explore if you chose to go with a nuanced and well-researched  portrayal of mental illness and/or dementia
