I actually was thinking that idea of a morality shift might be a good angle to play this. I'd drop the "mindless violence" bit, and frame the compulsion to share the word this way —

They can deny the compulsion. They can resist it, ignore it. But people affected know that sharing this word is the right thing to do. It's the moral thing to do. People who don't share the word because of the resistance from other people feel guilty.

I'd let people be rather canny about hiding their affliction to further their goals. It's not about being forced to do something. It's about that wanting to, needing to, and feeling righteous in doing something that would terrify me about this SCP.
