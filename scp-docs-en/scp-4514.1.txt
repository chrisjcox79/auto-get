WERE BACK BABY

03 Sep 2018 22:25

It's been what? Like 3 months or so? Well hopefully this will be worth the wait. Season 2 of End of Death HERE WE COME!

Also, cool trivia thing, if you convert each digit of 4514 to a letter based off of its position in the alphabet, you'd get 4 = D, 5 = E, 1 = A, 4 = D. So really this is SCP-DEAD. :P

I'd like to thank all my critiquers: UraniumEmpire, DrChandra, Veieidaheimer, Croquembouche, Othellothecat, A Random Day, Trennderdios, and AnnRKiss.

Also the code for that little HTML collapsible is stolen from Kalinin's SCP-2888, so thanks for that!

Update: ok I ended up scrapping kal's code for a sexier version Croquembouche wrote, so thanks to both of you for the idea and implementation respectively.

Here's to the fear of forever. This is just the beginning, and the near is not nigh.

A good example of crosslinking and hopefully a sign of things to come.
