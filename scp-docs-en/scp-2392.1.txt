This is my attempt at short horror.

InspirationSCP-2392

2392,Ike

Got inspiration for this during my camping trip - getting late at night and we were looking for a place to camp, so we pulled over at a spot called Soudan Station.

Creepiest place I've ever been to - right next to the highway but hidden by really dense shrubs, only lights within a few twenty kilometers was our own headlights, and even those seemed like they couldn't penetrate the darkness. It was dead silent too, but in a really unsettling way. We let off some fireworks and shoved off - as we left, a nearby sign was illuminated by our headlights, the sign of some arse writing on it with blood red paint becoming evident.

So I wrote this as we went to the next place.
