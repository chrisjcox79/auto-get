15 Jan 2020 06:01

Having a big header with a mandatory collapsible at the start annoys me, it feels a bit like waving  in the reader's face and adds nothing to the story, obviously people are just going to click on it straight away, unlike other collapsibles where you can choose how much text you want to be presented with as you read.

I think this is quite a good article. It was well paced and had a nice amount of technobabble without getting too dense. However, I have mixed feelings about it as a contest entry - I agree with the above comment about this feeling grand and probably being the most  -000 effort, if you think -000s must be a Very Important Thing which link in with other Very Important SCP Lore Things, and the high rating presumably reflects that. The problem is that it peters out a bit at the end. I don't really get a sense of something that interesting being contained in the box other than Something Spooky and World-Ending, of which there are dozens on the site.

I don't think the lackluster thing in the box would matter as much if this was being judged as a normal article. It's a catch-22 - this article is good because it builds up a big impressive -000 feeling, but it does this too well and there isn't a payoff. Novote makes the most sense to express this I guess.
