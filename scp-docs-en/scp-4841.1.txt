Minor nitpick: if you want to describe where the village is located, you should mention the federal subject, not the district. Districts in Russia are second-level administrative divisions, similar to American counties, and often have identical names in different regions.

Just say the village is in Krasnoyarsk Krai, it will convey more information for anyone familiar with Russia.
