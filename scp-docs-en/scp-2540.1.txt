Actually , but...

07 Sep 2014 06:20

Ok so this is the result of a challenge between myself and Paroxysm, where we each would give the other a concept and see what we can do with them. The one he gave me was of his first article, a lime what is also a Tipler Cylinder.

If you're not sure what a Tipler Cylinder is, I don't blame you! Neither did I. So here's the Wikipedia link. Hope it helps!

Once again, so much thanks for everybody who looked at this and had an opinion, notably Athena_Grey, TwistedGears, CryogenChaos, and Paroxysm.

As far as the duel is concerned, I'm going to consider it null and void, since neither of us posted our articles by the deadline. Still, this was fun to write, and I hope you enjoy it! Your comments, critiques, and criticisms are always welcome :)

Edit: The main image is from here: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Dimension_levels.svg?uselang=ru and the little insert of a lime is my own, released under Creative Commons.
