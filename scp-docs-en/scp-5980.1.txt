Thanks to:

Tyumen

For reviewing this here cannibal boi for me on IRC, and thanks to cybersqyd, and the folks on IRC for helping me get over my anxiety about posting it!

+Inspiration

Shhhh

Ethan Chin is based off someone I knew that worked in a place called Fudruckers in my homestate, New Jersey. Fudruckers is kind of this 50's style malt shop with touch of Five Guys and some weird, dank asthetic, which is how I imagined Cannibal Chin's to be.

Cannibal Chin, or Ethan, is related to one Gytha Chin, who will be featured in the upcoming Tale - TNP2: Guns Akimbo. There's a whole family of psychos and strange folk in this part of the SCP universe, so be on the look out.

Dr. Andrews also makes an appearance here, and in this skip. He'll be important later.

That's all for now!

Edit: The photo I added is an original, and I release it under the appropriate licenses.

~ M.D.R.
