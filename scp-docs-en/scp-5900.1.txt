As others have said, this article does a solid job of keeping the technobabble from being incomprehensible nonsense. I was able to get a good grasp of what the techies here were up to, and the implied technologies being used were fascinating, especially the potential for literal computer wizards at ICSUT. Additionally, the note at the end was particularly satisfying, as it lets us know who pulled the trigger on the outage, but raises several more questions for the reader to ponder.

All in all, a good SCP article. +1.
