26 Apr 2018 02:21

Well, there are two types of eclipses.

A solar eclipse is one possibility, in which the moon temporarily passes in between the sun and the earth, blocking the light of the sun partially or totally.

A lunar eclipse is another variant of an eclipse in which the earth blocks the light to the moon. A lunar eclipse is sometimes referred to as a blood moon.

And that is what happens during an eclipse.
