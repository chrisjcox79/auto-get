Yet again I bamboozled the bread with promises of crit. No matter. Crit waits for no man.

+Gripes

-Gripes

SCP-4966 is to be kept within a modified humanoid containment cell sized appropriately for a domestic cat to move freely.
附加记录：    

To be honest, I don't really think the domestic cat bit is necessary. Surely if a humanoid could live semi-comfortably, so could a house cat, no?

This cell is furnished with
附加记录：    

Should be .

Balaenoptera musculus1
附加记录：    

I personally prefer using parentheses for common names next to the scientific name, but meh maybe that's just me. This is where I pick nits, after all.

The only physical traits of SCP-4966 unable to be altered are it’s existing facial features and gray coloration.
附加记录：    

Maybe should be 

An adult male Crotalus horridus
附加记录：    

Oh hey one of my skips references timber rattlesnakes too! That's kinda cool!

an anal fin
附加记录：    

This begs the question: Does Tubbioca have an anus? How can one have an anal fin with no anus? /s of course

Alces alces Americana
附加记录：    

Why is it that random ass skips end up explaining people's usernames to me? Did it for procy twice and now for alces. Funny how that works.

a raid on Site-██
附加记录：    

Bluh, nitpick but I always preferred a number over site blackbox myself.

I’m not a organ psychic
附加记录：    

Not sure if this is a quirk of Tubbioca speak or if this was a typo, so I'm just gonna point it out.

For its role in discovering the informants, SCP-4966 was provided a bag of Tostitos brand tortilla chips.
附加记录：    

This is fantastic. Better than even the Foundation Pat on the Back Award, I think.

This was just a fun and cute article, and it was great. The development of the mannerisms of the plushie combined with that interview at the end and the foiling of a dastardly CI plot made for an interesting ride from start to finish.

Take my +1, and my apologies for not being able to crit this before posting.
