**SCP-5366 - Until death do us part and every day after** - Author Post

10 Nov 2020 23:58

 - Ernest Hemingway

This entry was inspired by my own fears of not being able to communicate and be heard. To me, the idea that you could be just out of reach of someone hearing you but still be unheard is the most horrific thing possible. Sprinkle in some paralysis themes and add a touch of torturing your loved ones and this will be what comes out. I hope you enjoy it!

Critters in no specific order: Researcher Brayan

Very tentative upvote. It's executed well enough for my tastes, but there are some glaring issues in here that I'd like to point out:

SCP-5366 is described as a worldwide phenomenon with a variety of different effects; yet, the only case described is that of a singular GOC agent. The jump from the Description to the rest of the article is very jarring. If SCP-5366 was just , it would feel much cleaner as a whole.
The leading line  serves no overall purpose and could be safely axed.
.
The dialogue feels very one-sided. I know that's the point, but it makes me wonder if allowing Lynas to hear Peri would make the SCP stronger.

Like I said above, the unsettling execution (even if it gets a bit too close to SCP-2718 for my liking at points) wins my upvote, but these issues distracted me from that somewhat.

11 Nov 2020 02:21

I have taken the advice of VoidLady and have added the line into the ConProcs.

To address the other notes, I had originally had Lynas being able to hear Perl but it became too convoluted and long to just describe why he could hear her but she couldn't hear him. This choice also comes from my own fear of being locked into my own prison just out of reach from the rest of the world. To me, it is more horrifying to be able to yell and make sounds but have no response from the outside world.

I also chose to use only this one example because I wanted to get across that this is happening to everyone that visits their loved ones, but the Foundation is only really calling attention to this one couple because they are getting information from them. To include more examples felt like it would just be the same thing happening to multiple people and wouldn't contribute to the overall storyline.

Thank you for all the feedback and the +1! :)
