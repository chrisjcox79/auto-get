First off, kudos on picking a pistol that isn't a Glock, Luger, or 1911. It's nice to see someone do some actual footwork in picking out a firearm to use as the basis for an effect.

Second, the effect is interesting enough that I'd like to see an expanded test log in the future, since I doubt the Foundation is just going to go off what happened before they acquired the item.

Third, a question: does the effect activate only when the firearm is held in the hand or does holstering or just having it in contact with flesh work equally? (I can buy someone sleeping with a pistol under their pillow, but I'm having trouble suspending disbelief for someone actually holding it in their hand while sleeping)

At any rate, +1 from me.
