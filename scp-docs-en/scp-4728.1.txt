Junior Staff - Site Criticism

11 Jan 2020 01:19

I quite like this, even if it’s a little rough around the edges in some technical aspects - but I feel like those need to be ironed out a bit before I can feel confident upvoting.

+whatever

-whatever

On her coat is a badge identifying her as Kiara Scott, an Oncologist at a local medical center.
附加记录：    

I’m not 100% sure, but I feel like ‘Oncologist’ doesn’t need to be capitalized here.

SCP-4728-A-1 
附加记录：    

The grammar here is really wacky, with sections like “Hey there Circuit Breakers today we're going to talk about DVDs which is a new format and people are using it to store all of their real life movies on it, and animated movies as well.” and “Well the thing is you never actually take things out of storage! when you  something out of the disk, that something gets deleted.” really needing some more punctuation and breaking up a little (The ‘when’ after storage should be capitalized here as well.)

SCP-4728-A-2 
附加记录：    

I really like the way Kiara talks about moving data here - the idea of deleting it being impossible because something can’t go nowhere really gives us the sense that something else is being discussed through the lens of data, even if we don’t know exactly what.

And the running motif of data being moved around - even if I'm not 100% on what it actually means - definitely gives off the sense that there's something deeper being talked about here, and that sense of intrigue really helps the article.

Novote for now.
