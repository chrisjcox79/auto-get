+1, but

The Ethics committee is supposed to serve as a check/balance on the power of the O5s, and ensure ethical treatment of sentient and sapient SCPs (excluding outstanding circumstances, like SCP-2312. Usually, the Ethics Committee is overridden by the O5 council, or their review is exempted by a Thaumiel classification, due to the anomaly's importance to the accomplishment of the Foundation's mission, but in the case of SCP-4375, there are more powerful, complicit reality benders to fill this role, and SRAs, which invalidates the grossly unethical actions detailed here.

OPERATION XI |  

Subject:  Foundation enemies

Description:  SCP-4375-1 was used to terminate traitorous Foundation members and implanted Chaos Insurgency members.

Notes:  [DATA REDACTED]
附加记录：    

I enjoy a good conspiracy-esque narrative, and this is just that: but it clashes with the general operating methods of the Foundation.
