Loved this in the chat and I love it here. What turns this from an article I would downvote to an article I would upvote is the note from the Director - this SCP is pretty boring and low-priority, but it's self aware about it and uses that fact to its advantage.

You've also of subverted the trope of  by making the note solidify the object's lack of importance. The note has such personality and it conveys a story really well - you can feel the head researcher's frustration with his higher-ups and his helplessness in solving the object's mysterious qualities.

As it is, this SCP is a really solid example of worldbuilding in the Foundationverse. Upvoted :)
