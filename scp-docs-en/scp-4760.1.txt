Haha! I'm so happy to see this posted.

First off, well done with how you handle Anderson. While I have typically set my pieces involving him in the 90s to modern day, pushing this back to the era of the Cold War gave it a certain cloak and dagger feel that reminds me of some of the works from The Coldest War. Additionally, the explanation for Anderson's transformation from man to machine is fitting, as is his realization for how much he has fucked up in this scenario.

That aside, this article once again shows how well you have mastered the craft of writing SCPs, my friend:

The pictures present make the article pop.
The exploration log is brief enough to keep from dragging but has enough content to serve as a solid drive to continue reading.
The video recordings from the activation scene with Dark are emotional and vivid, as well as a solid way of tying in quite a few SCP articles and other GoIs/characters.

Hearty +1. You did a great job here and it really pleases me to see Anderson with someone else at the helm.
