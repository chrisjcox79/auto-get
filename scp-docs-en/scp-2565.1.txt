Item #:  SCP-2565

Object Class:  Keter

Special Containment Procedures:  Until such a time in which Neutralisation Proposal SCP-2565  can be enacted, SCP-2565 and SCP-2565 instances are currently contained in seperate Biohazardous Humanoid Containment Cells at Bio Site-18. As per standard biosecurity procedures, personnel entering the containment cells of SCP-2565 are to wear Level A HAZMAT suits in order to avoid becoming contaminated with SCP-2565.

Currently, SCP-2565's waste products are to be stored in sealed steel containers and buried underground at Secure Disposal Area 18 to avoid contamination or containment breach of SCP-2565.

Description:  SCP-2565 is Agent Allison Eckhart, a 31-year old woman who was formerly a field operative for the Department of Analytics. Aside from a minor excess of sodium concentration in its blood, SCP-2565 does not appear to possess any physical anomalies. Prior to containment, Allison Eckhart had worked for 5 years as a Level 2 Anomalous Object Recovery agent. SCP-2565 did not possess anomalous properties during this time period.

SCP-2565 is an autosynecdochic semantic pointer, believed to be the result of an artificially induced conceptual fractal. Because of this, SCP-2565's internal organs and waste products also possess the property of  and are thus perceived as Allison Eckhart. As an example, simply attempting to take blood samples from SCP-2565 has led to over three hundred complaints to the Ethics Committee from researchers assigned to SCP-2565.

Addendum Allison Eckhart-1:  Members of GoI-139 () carried out an attack on Secure Disposal Area 18, presumably for the purpose of obtaining SCP-2565's waste products. Due to the small size of the group, it is believed that all members were contaminated in the process. During the attack, 36 security guards and 13 processing personnel were also contaminated. Their remains have been incinerated to avoid further spread of SCP-2565.

Site Director [NAME HERE] has expressed deepest regrets over the casualties involved in the incident. The families of the casualties involved have been given appropriate reimbursement for SCP-2565 conversion.

Addendum Allison Eckhart-2:  Embedded Foundation assets in meteorology offices report multiple SCP-2565-contaminated cloud formations over the Pacific Ocean, suggesting the successful atmospheric dispersal of SCP-2565's waste products. As a result, over 100 liters of SCP-2565-contaminated water has been dispersed over the coastline of Australia.

Following continuing civilian casualties due to ingestion of SCP-2565-contaminated water, a Broken Masquerade scenario has been established due to the high-publicity nature of the casualties. It is estimated that at SCP-2565's current rate of waste product emission, a total AEK-Class Allison Eckhart End-of-the-World Breathability Scenario will occur within 40 years.

Plans for off-Earth evacuation of the remaining non-SCP-2565 population have already been set up, although the Global Occult Coalition has also proposed a plan for destruction of SCP-2565. It is believed that this proposal would carry a non-zero risk of an SK-Class Total Semantic Failure Scenario along with the neutralisation of SCP-2565.
