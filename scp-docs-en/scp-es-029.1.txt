Note from the author:

This SCP was written more than 3 years ago, when the Series III was developing, when I was a (even more) casual writer infatuated by the recent GoI formats and lore, especially the shaping of the CotBG history. As my second SCP, I tried to create something in my homelands about how GoIs can seize the opportunity of low Foundation assets to develop their operations.

Sadly, the authors who gave me feedback on this piece are completely inactive as of today. So I thank them for their feedback whenever they are now, and to all the ES branch for their reception. Also thanks to Dr Gears for the creation of the GoI and SCP-217, and the Rapid Nomenclature Shift GoI2014 team for granting us their vision of a more modern, expanded CotBG.
