There is quite a bit I like about this article.

I like the idea of Foundation  types testing out a school yard game, and that the effects of the anomaly are far from permanent. This easily could have gone down a more grim path, which in my opinion would have ruined the charm the article has in its current whimsical form.

I feel like the Foundation could easily reverse engineer this into a temporary immobilization effect if they were so inclined, and the mental image of a Foundation agent capturing an SH member by starting a game of Foot Pope is pretty great.

Finally, this article takes place in Portland, OR, which always wins bonus points from me.

+1 Zyn. This is a really nice, and refreshingly brief, article.
