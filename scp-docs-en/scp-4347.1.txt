Nice work. Very charming scip overall.

CRITHERE

GETHERE

I still feel the jump between anomaly 1, it's unusual level of intellect, and anomaly 2, the fact it can headbutt your consciousness out of your body, is narratively too disconnected. It still needs a stronger lead-in or lead-out line to connect the threads.

An interview log with the ranger who'd had an out-of-body experience would have also helped. Right now, after Video Transcript 4347-A, it just sort of carries on. The actual anomalous effect is too abstract: you only say it happens, we never get a first hand account of it.

You could have made room for this account by trimming down the Ranger Mclean log a bit. It gets a bit long-winded at times.

Still, I enjoyed reading this. It could have used more work, but I still give it a +1.
