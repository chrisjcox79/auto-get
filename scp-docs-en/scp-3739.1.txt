spoilers

spoiledmilk

Well, we're given early on that in the description it's essentially somewhere from this place called the Noosphere, think of it like an atmosphere of thought based around the consciousness of others, exists. And its leaking in bovine based memes. The secretion of this almost metaphysical milk product is described in the descriptions.

Which then leads to test logs that help configure an abstract on various archetypes found within this realm of dream/thought, loosely inspired by Carl Jung's teachings on the archetypes.

It then briefly goes over the PoI known as Maat Mohamed who had suffered from some sort of experience with the goddess Bat during the Suez Canal incident earlier.

Which then follows various logs that helps illustrate these archetypes in action and at play as the piece escalates out of control towards the ending where the CEO, M.M. (from the aforementioned sentence) influencing the population of dreamers under Moosphere, Inc.'s employment as expressed in the interview with Drauss.

I hope that helps with some clarifications on the narrative points.
