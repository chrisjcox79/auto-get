I want to thank tupacofficial for reviewing this concept all the way back in October of last year. Y’all really helped solidify this concept into something way better than what I originally was going to do. I also want to thank Discord users Pedagon and fwimmygoat for giving me feedback on the completed draft.

I originally started the draft for this article back in October, and for some reason didn’t get back to it until just recently. I’m glad that I got back to it though.

EDIT: Wow, thanks for all the upvotes! To any other new SCP authors out there, you are going to get an article to survive on the wiki if you keep trying! Don't worry, my first few concepts were horrible (Exploding watch, Infinite ketchup). Keep getting feedback and reading essays on the Guide Hub. I cannot preach enough how important feedback and research is for this kind of thing.
