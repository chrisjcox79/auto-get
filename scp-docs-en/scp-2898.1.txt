Hmm, this is tough. I like the idea behind this, and liked the version I read in chat. The new final addendum is helpful in many ways, as it reinforces the most important factor in the creation of the anomaly, and makes the emotional angle more explicit. But that's also what is making me wary - the writing there is almost obvious in how manipulative it is intended to be, meaning that the technical workings are showing through a bit. On the other hand, that is a very hard thing to fix (and I don't have any obvious solutions off the top of my head, apart perhaps from removing one or two repetitions of ), so I suspect it's not worth messing with now that you've posted and are getting upvotes. No-vote from me for now, but this may well be something that I come back to later, and marvel at why I haven't already upvoted.
