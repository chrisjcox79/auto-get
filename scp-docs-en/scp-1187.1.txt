Fold

23 May 2012 06:07

1. I think that any time you designate an , it should be given an appropriate designation, ex. Incident 1187-a

2. There are a lot of unanswered, but likely answerable questions in this.

Physically: Is it destructible? Are all components factory spec for the make and model? Are the components made of standard materials? Does it use fuel at the expected rate? Does the effect end if the unit runs out of fuel?

Operationally: Is the force generated by the wheels effectively unlimited (that is, does the ground move at 40km/h regardless of the mass being moved)? Does the anomalous effect occur on inclined ground? Does it occur if SCP-1187 is propped sideways against a wall (thereby moving the wall)? If it's placed on an inclined plane, will it roll down the hill, or will the hill roll up? Does the answer depend on whether the unit is running or not?

I wouldn't want you to include answers to ALL of the above questions, but answering a couple of them might add some meat and SCIENCE to the article ^_^
