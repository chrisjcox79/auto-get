Fold

30 Dec 2012 16:08

I have to agree that the concept is solid, but the execution is slightly lackluster. I recommend deleting this before it's officially deleted and polishing it for a little while more.

The thing that got me (positively) was that it sort of reminded me of the idea of blood diamonds. You know, precious gems mined in a war zone and sold to finance the combat, but people keep buying them anyone because it's pretty and sparkling and every kiss begins with kay! This is just a little more literal, and some people might think that's a failing, but I don't think so. Because the true horror of this SCP isn't that there's a liquid that turns blood into crystal, although that's pretty horrifying, it's that people keep buying the crystals anyway. And that's why I think the MCD connection isn't tacked on, but perfectly appropriate.

Upvoted for now, hopefully you can polish out any issues and turn this around, because the concept really deserves it!
