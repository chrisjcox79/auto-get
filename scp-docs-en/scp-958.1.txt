So, here we are. With a new picture, new effects, and new worldbuilding, we have a rewritten General-Beep. This rewrite actually means a lot to me; the old 958 was the first skip that stuck in my mind as something that didn't quite  and being able to give it new life years later to continue my Wondertainment/Factory stuff is one of the more satisfying things I've done here. Made all the better by Tanhony being a super cool guy and letting me do it before the thing was even in deletion range.

Thanks to Anaxagoras and Decibelle for convincing me to rewrite the thing wholesale rather than try to incorporate some of its older writings. A huge, massive thanks to Roget for pushing me to change up the -B (Rip Corporal-Boop) - it really resulted in a much better article, and ended up being a bit closer to the old version because of it. Thanks to Randomini and kaktus for making me do an interview, and more thanks to Randomini for helping me tighten it up.

The original article, its image, and its discussion pages can be found in the Files of this page.

Edit- Behold! I received more awesome art from Sunny!
