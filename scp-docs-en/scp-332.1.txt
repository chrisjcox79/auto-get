Some questions, comments and nitpicks =o)

27 Sep 2012 04:32

500 square meter area of exclusion
附加记录：    

Not sure what this means. Might be better to describe the exclusion zone in terms of distance from the edge of the football field  or something.

a 5 meter tall and 1.5 meter thick concrete fence
附加记录：    

That sounds more like something you'd call a wall rather than a fence.

all site personnel are to monitor the length and intensity of the sound produced by SCP-332 from soundproofed observation areas
附加记录：    

If they're soundproofed, how can they monitor the sound from within them?

30 humanoids
附加记录：    

Assuming that we can see them, can you give more detail than just ? Are they animated corpses? Planet-of-the-Apes monkey-creatures? Lizardmen?

SCP-332 will also emit sound
附加记录：    

What kind of sound? Do they play their instruments? Screech like the pod people in Invasion of the Body Snatchers? Hoot like gorillas?

if a noise between 15 and 80,000Hz is made
附加记录：    

That's a huge range, human hearing tops out around I think 15-20k-ish.

The camera is picked up and pans around the room. Several deceased persons are visible in frame, including the original cameraman
附加记录：    

What did they die of? They're still in the room, I'm assuming they didn't get trampled?

I'm not voting atm, it feels like it still needs work to me. I must say that the unidentified person picking up the camera and panning around after everyone in the room is dead freaks me out, in a good way. I'm also pretty sure we've never had an anomolous marching band before, so that's a plus.
