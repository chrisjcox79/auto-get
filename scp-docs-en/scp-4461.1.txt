An SCP that deals with heirarchy, structure, infohazards, and an unqualified gatekeeper of destruction.

This tale went through several iterations but focused on three concepts:

An infohazard so dangerous that it would be cataclysmic for any person qualified to deal with it to actually deal with it
How to contain an infoahazard without arousing suspicion in anyone at all
How a  would handle doing the toughest part of working for the foundation: killing the innocent because the alternative is far worse.

Thank you to ghostchibi and corpseofbixby for feedback and crit!

Addenda:  I apparently forgot to thank cybersqyd for crit as well. I apologize deeply for this oversight as it was unintended.
