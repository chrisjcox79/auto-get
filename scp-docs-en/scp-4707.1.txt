I figured that a lot of people would dislike the popups, especially the more technically-minded people since they would be a lot more underwhelmed by this sort of thing. About the SSL, I used that specifically because Wikidot does not have it. It was meant to be a more comedic nod to that fact, as well as a possible headcanon for how the SCP escaped. TLS is more commonly referred referred to as SSL. In order to help less technically-minded readers understand the joke1 without having to look things up, I used the more common term instead of the correct one.  is also not only not a common term, but a completely bogus one. There is no term considering there are no effective deep learning viruses to my knowledge.
