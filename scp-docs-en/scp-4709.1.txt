I felt as if this went on longer than it had to. The early logs didn't pull me in and not enough happened to keep me engaged with them throughout. Things didn't really get interesting until the very last log, with everything else before it feeling a bit generic. I also feel as though the metaphor could have been developed or hinted much better throughout the piece instead of just lore dumped at the end. All in all, this isn't a terrible piece, but it falls short of what I would want it to be for the above reasons.

Though I will say, the dialogue is pretty good throughout, and I for one am glad that an MTF doesn't immediately start cursing and screaming and pissing and shitting themselves when one of their fellows gets anomaly'd.
