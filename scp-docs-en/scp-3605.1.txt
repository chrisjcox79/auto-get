Notes

12 Mar 2018 23:39

The expungement here is designed specifically to address many criticisms people have about it in general, specifically that it's lazy or a cop-out. Much like 2317, I tried to use redaction as a way to create buildup for the final connection between 3255 and 3605. If I wanted to, I could fill in the redactions for all but one of these (which that is I will leave for the reader to figure out).

So in a way it's not truly redacting, at least not out-of-universe, since most of what was hidden is revealed at the end. Hope it worked well.

I'm otherwise fully satisfied with how this turned out, excepting one element: the order of the documentation. I debated a lot whether or not I should put the data or the journals first, and ultimately decided on the latter because I think it works better as a lead-in to the strangeness of the photos. At the same time, the final few logs seem more like a conclusory note for an SCP than the end of a section.

Dunno. Evidently enough people like it the way I wrote it for it to stick around, so I guess I'll leave 3605 as is for now.
