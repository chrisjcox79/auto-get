10 Sep 2020 00:21

A bit of technical critique, your description of using positron emission tomography to describe the method for identifying Y-class entities doesn't make sense and is described incorrectly. Positron emission tomography requires a radioactive tracer to be injected into the subject, which of course wouldn't work on non-corporeal entities. You may want to choose a different method for identifying the entities.

The Prince talks in old japanese instead being form the heinan period (he should be speaking in early middle japanese instead)

Second the sword

Why call it an odachi?
