to keep guard over SCP-5062.
附加记录：    

Probably a better way to say this but it's not a big deal.

The room contains a king-sized bed, a writing desk, a mini-fridge and a nightstand containing a copy of the King James Bible.
附加记录：    

Comma after mini-fridge

The size and specific form of these scissors will vary depending on the trapped individual, but are consistently at least 7.5cm long.
附加记录：    

Don't think you need this comma.

Following exit, the victim may experience additional symptoms including:
附加记录：    

Something about  feels wonky
