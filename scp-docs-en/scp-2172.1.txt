16 Sep 2014 23:04

The simple fact behind my decision to use Humes here is that 2172's reality destabilization is wholly irrelevant to the object's interest. It's a good reason for the Foundation to keep it hanging instead of putting it in a boring old box, and that's all it needs to do.

[EDIT: A bit more on what I mean by this: Humes are a fundamental part of my writing toolkit in this continuity because to otherwise describe what happens when this object is taken out of an area would take an expungement (ugly, cliché) or a hundred or so words on what exactly happens, which would distract from the focus of the piece.]
