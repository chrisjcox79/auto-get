Thoughts

09 May 2019 20:27

When I read the containment procedures and description of this object I really didn't like it. I thought that the concept was really boring .

You should add an incident report or two where agents find these things or their victims in strange places. Like if an agent investigated a missing persons case and walks into that missing person's attic. When he opens the attic copious amounts of water just pour out and he finds a blue, drowned corpse in the attic. That would really round it out. It'd be really cool to see another related SCP that explains their origins and objective. You got my vote!
