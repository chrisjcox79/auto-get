This is just delightful. I honestly couldn't have cared less about the D-Class escape plot, that was the least fun part of the article for me. What worked was that the culture of this serial killer online society felt fully-realized and complete. The  line was great, and where I upvoted, even if the subsequent stuff with Moriarity was a bit pedestrian. The ending is another high note. What works here is fantastic enough that I didn't mind the ho-hum D-Class story, but this would be an all-time classic if that stuff was cut out in favor of something that shows off the great characterization and originality you seem to be tapping into when writing the site's motley crew of characters coming into their own separately from the Foundation.

Whether you like it or not, history is on our side. We will bury you!
