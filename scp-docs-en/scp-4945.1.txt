24 Jun 2019 14:42

Interesting, but down voting because I found the dialogue between the dad and foundation to be really thin. First thing I would say if I had been separated from my son 12 minutes ago?  etc. And happily agreeing, without thought, to your son living in what is essentially captivity/mental institute for the rest of his life? It wrecks the immersion for me.

Also, the foundation seems way off tone here. Why so friggin' nice? Why announce your entire secret mission to a POI after 46 seconds of internal conversation? Why are they being so nice to the -1 creatures in the second instance? At no point did anyone say that harming them causes the boy any distress, and we all know if the foundation is anything, it is brutally efficient - bullets are numerous and cheap.

I guess I essentially had an issue with the tone throughout - it seem too simple, too focused on the happy ending, too committed to the  theme.
