01 Dec 2011 01:03

While I do like 769, I think I like this one better, although I think the execution makes the two different enough in regards to what kind of horror they are. The scope of 769's horror is that it's something the Foundation has thrown away thousands of people for, and gotten next to nothing they care about in return, but it's not the sort of thing that most people would use without some sort of prompting. 1062's horror, at least in my opinion, is that it's so innocuous, and was left in a semi-public place where lots of people would have the chance to be unwittingly exposed to it, went undetected for a period of time that could be up to a bit under three years, and who's to say there aren't more out there?

TL;DR: 769 is  horror.
