-text

[So we have a timeline tampering story. And we have something that either forces people to give up or potentially kill themselves if they have deeper beliefs. It took me a while for my brain to work and while pooping on the toilet I thought: what if this is the cause of Hitler's suicide? This could be the  theorycrafting that is often common.

But this opens up a whole can of worms. This means that the timeline we are possibly is already a  timeline than whatever was the alternative (where Nazi Germany doesn't give up as fast, where Hitler does not commit suicide). But if tampering through time is the goal, why not kill Hitler as a baby? Why not get him accepted into art school? The answer is the events of the war (as we know it) needed to unfold for some reason. The last document alludes to the project being necessary for the Foundation/reality as it is. Perhaps similar to the real life Operation Paperclip post WW2 where Nazi scientists were used to make great advances in rocketry, space, etc. perhaps Nazi occultists/scientists were also incorporated into the Foundation! Maybe..

Then there is the question of why is this sort of program STILL continuing post war? Why is it beneficial for people to align with the US, the UK and ? If you look at the wiki page for the Allies of World War II they list 5 major combatants: US, UK, France, China, USSR. In fact these OK that's nice, quick history question: what nations are on the PERMANENT security council of the United States? Oh snap.

I'm confused as to the Level 3 Mimetics. #1 and #3 look like they might be some reverse psychology propaganda. (Telling the populace nothing is wrong when everything is clearly going to shit giving the year 1945). #2 is confusing. Wilhelm in google brings up tons of Nazi's, a couple of german emperors. I feel #2 has some sort of key to  this SCP if it even can be solved but I have no idea how to approach it. Bicycles were a big part of espionage both for Nazi collaborators and Resistance members. Case Blue was a Nazi army plan that failed in 1943. Dunno what else to probe here.

Also Mussolini was overthrown in 1943 the year this project was started. Hmm. Turned his population against him too?

Is this why people subconsciously also group things they don't like as  and etc. because by associating certain ideas or terms with Axis powers it allows easier propagation of ideas? ]
