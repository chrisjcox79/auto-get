In one of his stories (, I think), Larry Niven went into some detail about the problems of momentum and potential energy involved in long-range teleportation (unless the two terminals are at the same distance from the center of Earth's mass, allowing for the possibility of masscons under one or both platforms, there's going to be a change in potential energy of the item as well). His Jumpshift Corp. used handwave tech to transfer excess momentum to floating platforms in various bodies of water.
