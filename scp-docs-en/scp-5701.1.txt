+Show

Original

SCP-5701-# instances are any photographs created by SCP-5701. If any person, henceforth referred to as  or have a noticeable regret of a past action, said SCP-5701-# instance will change to depict a scene of the subject making said mistake or regret. Additional specifics of the behavior of SCP-5701-# instances can be seen below:

Revised

SCP-5701-# are photographs created by SCP-5701. If a human subject is photographed by SCP-5701, the next time the subject performs an action they regret or consider to be a mistake, the SCP-5701-# in which they were photographed will be altered to depict that event. Edit: Just figured I'd explain what I did and why if that's needed.

Dr. Mason isn't interviewing; he's interrogating. He'll get harsh, but that's the point.

Dr. Dawkins is trying to get PoI-5701-3 to calm down. He needs to have a level of sympathy to keep him cool because a panic of any kind would just instantly  shut the interview down.
