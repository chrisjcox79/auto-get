Somewhere in South End when you were fun

you took my hand and you made me run

up past the prison to the seafront.

You climbed the cliff edge and took the plunge.

Why can’t we laugh now like we did then?

How come I see you and ache instead?

I had this idea at random today thanks to that song. So, I'm paying tribute to my home state of North Carolina with a coldpost about a shrooms-eating, acid-tabbing, DMT-huffing agender1 redneck with self-actualization issues. Oh, and their friend, who is a ball of flesh and dirt.
