Nothing much anyone else hasn't said. The thing that's really great here is the length; you establish an impactful, tangible theme that sticks with the reader past - whenever. It has duration, which is kind of amazing given the space in which it's conveyed. There's like entire volumes on the site of documentation and crap that failed to make me feel as much as you did here. I'm a little disappointed you didn't follow through with what seemed like an obvious test log - watch the car for as long as possible - but considering the final product it's probably better you didn't. The melancholy imparted by the sparseness of it is depth enough to make it satisfying.
