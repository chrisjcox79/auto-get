Bonjour! Ce commentaire de lauteur sera entièrement en français. (Not really)

09 Aug 2018 09:34

So, this has an interesting story behind it basically, about a week ago, the user Dr Lekter from the French wiki contacted me, saying that the French site had an interest in translating some of my works, as well as basing works off of the translations. I gave them the go-ahead, and immediately started looking for something I could translate in return.

I happened upon the Group of Interest SAPHIR, whose backronym is the least-weird thing about it. It's already been featured in DrGemini.

That is the tip of the weirdness iceberg.

SAPHIR is more than its simple premise DrGemini has done amazing work with SAPHIR, including making up SCP-281-FR, for anyone that's curious. In a word, this Group is Truly, Truly, Outrageous, and I fell in love with the concept.

Right as I was finalizing edits on this, I saw that DrGemini commented on the forum thread for this, so color me giddy. Glad you approve!

Thanks to all those who critiqued it, which includes but is not limited to DrGemini for helping me with French translation issues throughought my research on SAPHIR, and to Gemini for giving this their blessing.
