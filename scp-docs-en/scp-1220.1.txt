Test Subjects: Dr. █████████, D-426/1220

Native Language: English, American derivation

Word Spoken: Morpheme ███ compounded by Morphemes ██ and ███ 

Result: Anomalous zone briefly formed, with a radius of 2m. Everything within the zone was blasted away from subject with an estimated force of ███ N.
