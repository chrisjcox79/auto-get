This article is not about colonization, cause the intention of the baseline Foundation was not to colonize but too collaborate with the extra-dimensional Foundation but due too lack of foresight they accidentally brought something that was harmless and mundane from their dimension that caused a mass extinction in another. Since the baseline wasn’t using any force or had any plans to conquer this story is not a full repeat of the colonization of the New World that happened in our baseline. Instead it is a cautionary tale saying that when we discover new life, new worlds, or in this case a new dimension we must take the upmost care to prevent harmful cross-contamination as even day-to-day things that are only pests to us can be agents of mass destruction to a civilization that evolved separate from those things. The biggest tragedy of this all is that it could have been easily avoided if they used standard decontamination proceeders on either side of the dimensional portal or better yet completely seal of the portal chamber at first and have the base line bring their own oxygen and equipment to negotiate wirelessly and if in person contact or exchange of goods ever needed to happen then the use of the most rigorous decontamination protocol you have to ensure that a tragedy like this never happens.
