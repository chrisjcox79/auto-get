This is really fun, it feels like Rimworld turned into an SCP. On that note though, I kinda wish that the gamey-ness of it was fleshed out a bit more. My favorite part was definitely the person that got designated as food, but I wish there was more of that kind of thing going on with this. I also find the characterization of the player a bit strange. I can’t tell if he is just a complete asshole before this that develops a god complex the instant he got power or if the anomaly caused this in him. Not liking his job just doesn’t feel like strong enough motivation for him to become this much of a monster.

Overall though I thoroughly enjoyed it, and it makes me very excited for more Arcadia stuff. +1
