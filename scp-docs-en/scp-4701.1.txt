Oh hey, here I am doing a site crit thingy!

+line

-line

Bob Dylan and members of Old Crow Medicine Show have been designated PoI 4701-1 through -11.
附加记录：    

You've got a double space between .

I feel like more specific terms should be used other than . Something at least referencing viral popularity or record sales might be better suited? Also, you ended the sentence before closing the quotes.

singer, songwriter and poet
附加记录：    

I personally am an oxford comma guy, but I guess its fine if you aren't, because not including the comma here doesn't change how the line is interpreted.

as he appeared in 1972
附加记录：    

Is there a specific trait or feature of Bob Dylan that allowed us to identify this as what he looked like specifically in 1972, or is that specific year inferred from the release of the song in question?

pinning the object to the spokes
附加记录：    

I know its a dead body and all, but it kinda feels strange to call it . Not quite sure why to be honest. I don't necessarily think there's something inherently wrong with it, but it doesn't feel right, ya know?

rotating it between seven and eleven times at close to 200 kph.
附加记录：    

This genuinely reminds me of like when you would stand on a pot in Skyrim, and the physics engine would take a shit trying to figure out what belongs where, and that memory makes me chuckle.

Overall, this was just straight up wacky and I enjoyed myself throughout. Gonna be a +1 from me!
