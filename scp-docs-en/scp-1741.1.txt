27 Jan 2014 12:39

A minor nitpick here (seriously, minor, but it bugged me):

SCP-1741-C is a male humanoid of European descent, dressed in zoot suit.
附加记录：    

And

Background study has revealed that Alan Pearce went missing in 1946.
附加记录：    

Okay, so Alan Pearce (who 1741-C resembles) disappeared in 1946. While a zoot suit is from that era, it's unlikely he'd be wearing one. They were usually worn by younger men for nightclubbing, and were usually seen as extravagant and flashy - especially during the wartime, when there was fabric rationing.

So maybe it could be a plain men's suit in a dark grey? Or a trouser/sweater vest/shirt combination? Or, since it could be said he's a bit of a carny, plain brown trousers and a rumpled white shirt with folded up shirtsleeves.
