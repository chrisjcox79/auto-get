First thing in, along with the picture, and I'm almost immediately hooked. Most SCPs have containment procedures that of course make you think , which can stir up a great number of emotions, and this immediately caught my eye as one of the better examples of how to perform this function. This is a great  hook, in my opinion. What would necessitate changing from net launchers, which can actually work, to mock tranquilizer rifles?

It was very nice to me how it seemed to be wishy-washy about whether or not it wanted you to be afraid, or at least that's how it seemed to me. Most SCPs start you off with a sense of unease just by the nature of the site and the stuff you're reading, and very rapidly you can adjust - is this a funny skip? a sad skip? a scary skip? I wasn't sure which one of these I was going to get, which was one of the things that made me keep reading, and then when it wrapped up, I let out a nice little .
