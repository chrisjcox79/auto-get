KentuckyCriticisms

ThoughtGood

Due to the destruction SCP-1858 may cause
附加记录：    

I don't think this is a necessary line, especially considering it doesn't particularly match what comes after it.

to be under remote satellite surveillance at all times
附加记录：    

You see?  doesn't match very well for me, at least in terms of describing how to contain this thing.

All entrances and exits leading to Route 35 are to be permanently closed off from public access
附加记录：    

According to Wikipedia... US Route 35 is 663 kilometers long (412 miles), has 83 major intersections, and passes through three states.

Now, perhaps not all are easily accessed, and perhaps it is not the most traveled road, but to simply say  stretches my suspension of disbelief just a bit too far. I may be nitpicking, but I think you could stand to be a little more creative with your containment.

inspected for any barriers and/or debris that can prevent SCP-1858
附加记录：    

Could instead of can

SCP-1858 consists of two (2) entities
附加记录：    

I would think  would be a better word choice than entities, especially considering you call them vehicles every other time.

license plates of both vehicles have been obscured with duct tape
附加记录：    

How do we know it's duct tape? Did we get a really clear picture with the satellite or did Researcher McQueen jog out onto the road after it went by and do a visual check?

manifest approximately eight (8) kilometers apartkm/h
附加记录：    

Do they start from a standstill or at speed? How quickly are they accelerating?

collision will be followed by an explosion
附加记录：    

What kind? Gas? C4? Nuclear? There's a good variety of explosion types

undetermined strength of the explosion
附加记录：    

I'm not an explosionologist, but this seems like the sort of thing the Foundation would be able to figure out, especially if it happens with any amount of regularity

If the usual route of either component
附加记录：    

First entities, then vehicles, then SCP-1858-1 and SCP-1858-2, now components. Consistency is a plus.

is deliberately obstructed
附加记录：    

What about unintentionally? What constitutes obstruction? Would a branch or something that they need to briefly change lanes to avoid count or does it have to cover the road?

The test logs and/or incident reports
附加记录：    

You mention  In these tests, the cars appeared somewhere else. What exactly did were they testing? Did they intentionally block roads to see where the cars would go, or did they screw up and not clear the road properly? Was it an incident because there was unexpected test failure, or because there was civilian death, or both, or neither?

A disturbing trend SCP-1858 appears to have a preference to re-locate onto roads containing heavy traffic and flammable and/or explosive material
附加记录：    

It only involved explosive/flammable material in one of four tests, nitpicky, but I don't think you can call that a trend.
