I find myself asking what unauthorized D-class were doing in the (presumably monitored or at least locked) enclosure in the first place, let alone during 'restricted hours.'

Locking the doors to the enclosure between 8:55 p.m. and 6:05 a.m would prevent this kind of thing from happening again, and would cost nothing to implement.

Is the implication that the D-class were somehow  to the enclosure? If so, this is a perfect opportunity for an Incident Report adding a touch of the human perspective.

Regardless, the preparer of this report should at the very least mention that having the D-class in there was unauthorized, and an investigation is under way to determine how or why they got in there.
