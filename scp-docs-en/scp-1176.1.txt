A good SCP, but I would like to point out a few minor inaccuracies:

1. The name Abdel is not in fact a single name: Abd means slave or servant, and el means  in this context. Therefore, Abdel ibn Salah is not a correct Arabic name, since the prefix Abdel must only come before one of God's names (hence Abdallah- God's servant).

2. late classical Hellenic- you are most likely referring to the Hellenistic period here, rather than the late Classical one. In this later period the Greek culture was most influential in Egypt, which seems to where the coffin is from.

It is nice to see the picture in a good article though, good show.
