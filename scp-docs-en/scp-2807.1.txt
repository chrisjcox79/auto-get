This one’s a pretty interesting. It reminds me of Hallway 4, D-Class Dorms, Site 14, which is a good thing and a bad thing.

A good thing in that the psychic/spiritual scars of past D-Class hell infuses and congealed into a force that persists even worse than the events that bore it.

A less good thing in that I feel the D-Class Hallway is a little more subtle, this one is alright in its own way.

This one’s also a lot more… robust, in-universe, than the 479. It’s evident something exceptionally wrong happened here. Exactly what is unclear even to the Foundation.

I like it.

Alright, so that blackboxed skip in the description suggests that the Foudnation recovered another item from inside SCP-2807, likely an anomalous object that was stored there after being recovered from the local area. SCP-1215 is just such an object. As such, I am recommending the following edits:

Changing this:

SCP-2807 is located in Eastern Turkey, near the ancient ruined city of Ani.

SCP-2807 refers to Site-04 in its entirety except for the control center, which exists separately from the rest of site. SCP-2807 refers to a previously abandoned Foundation storage facility, estimated to be more than 200 years old, and most likely abandoned after the [REDACTED] event. Despite several breach attempts, the storage facilities inside remained intact. SCP-████ and several archives were recovered.
附加记录：    

To this:

SCP-2807 is located in Eastern Turkey, near the ancient ruined city of Ani.

SCP-2807 refers to Site-04 in its entirety except for the control center, which exists separately from the rest of site. SCP-2807 refers to a previously abandoned Foundation storage facility, estimated to be more than 200 years old, and most likely abandoned after the [REDACTED] event. Despite several breach attempts, the storage facilities inside remained intact. SCP-1215 and several archives were recovered.
