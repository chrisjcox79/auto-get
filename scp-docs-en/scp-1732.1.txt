 is what the Foundation is doing. The Foundation also has to deal with the fact that the historical record has already been edited and tampered with for 1700 years before they even got involved in the game, and they can't very well just un-redact it all and hope nobody believes the well-documented historical facts that they've revealed.

My purpose in writing this article was to take a look at what impact an SCP entity would have had in pre-modern times when there wasn't any such thing as a secret league of super-scientists to capture and study it and keep it from getting out into the world - and it seems to me that if people today can believe that a man 2000 years ago rose from the dead and turned water into wine, they can believe that a talking lion 1800 years ago was emperor.
