I like this (and definitely am upvoting it). Lest anyone question, I did give blessing for this to implicitly or explicitly reference 1050.

Don't get me wrong, I like the possibility that the Foundation may have found more information that might have to do with 1050. That said, I have misgivings about the 1050 losing its ambiguity. In other words, in 1050 I try and clearly demarcate what the Foundation knows (fact) and what the Foundation thinks (speculation). If something comes out and attempts to definitively say , I'm going to oppose it. So, I encourage making the apparent link a little more speculative - the Foundation can't know definitively that 1980-5 is anything like 1050's Destroyers. I'm not sure how to go about doing that, but that's my thoughts.

Also, I do not mind (or think it would detract) if you redact  to the blackbox state. Just saying.
