Mabuhay!

26 Nov 2019 03:55

This is a rewrite of the previous SCP-3906 that I wrote for JamCon '18. Given the 24 hour time constraint, I wasn't able to take the article quite as far as I wanted to. It's a piece that's really close to my heart, though, and I felt it was deserving of a more fleshed-out treatment. To see the original and its comment thread, please look under 'Files' at the bottom of the article.

Numen is a subclass that refers to deific anomalies.

Special thanks to CadaverCommander for some very helpful feedback on this.

If SCP-2190 didn't make my roots obvious, this one certainly does!
