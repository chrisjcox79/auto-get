Re: Jesus christ.

I appreciate it! For me, this is one of the most terrifying things: Not just being at the mercy of human indifference, but being so desperate for contact that you'll do anything to appease those who just don't care. You'll strip yourself of every last shred of dignity, just for the chance that you'll be acknowledged  without relationships, we starve. And when you're starving, you'll eat anything. Even yourself.

It's a place I've been a couple of times. I think I've moved beyond it (I hope!), but I'm at least familiar with that sort of head-space, and it still scares the crap out of me to think about it. I was trying to channel it here.
