Fold

13 Dec 2008 01:59

it's my understanding that SCP-109 is a normal canteen, except for the fact that it is bottomless.

1. Water balloon test - Because SCP-109 has no means of water propulsion other than gravity, it cannot fill a water balloon. I would also like to remind all personnel that

2. Electronic test - Other than the slight metal content of SCP-109, it acts as normal water and the results are the same with water from a normal tap.

3. Dumping in polluted river - Test parameters are unclear, dumping the water into a polluted river will dilute it with sufficient time, dumping the whole of SCP-109, with the means to retrieve it, will dilute the river and will cause the river level to rise until SCP-109 is retrieved.
