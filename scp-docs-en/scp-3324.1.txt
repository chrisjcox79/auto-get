If you're interested in my other work, here is my author page

My second SCP, let's hope it does at least as well as my first.

Big thanks to whomstdveWorm for discussing the concept with me.

Big thanks to Stallmantic for helping me to determine if my usage of tachyons was plausible.

But BIGGEST thanks to PeppersGhost for the extremely detailed and insightful criticism, that has elevated my idea so much. Seriously, the ending Ghost helped me develop is 50x better than what I had in mind at first.

This is inspired by my own experiences with kidney stones. I pee in a sieve (the very sieve in the image no less), because if the stone is causing me this much suffering, by god I get to look at it up close after it comes out! But, what if this strange behavior was a cause, rather than a symptom
