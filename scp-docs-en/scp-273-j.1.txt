Glad to see this on the mainsite! Solid +1 from me here since I've liked this since it was in the draft stage.

Results: No physical change was observed in D-6745. Carolina Reapers fished out of subject's mouth with difficulty.
附加记录：    

This line is hilarious and sums up the premise of the SCP pretty perfectly. I remember being in chem lab and having to write overly detailed reports at the end (and we'd get docked points if we left out steps like rinsing out glassware!) and this made me chuckle because of the absurdity of the scene that would definitely be something the Foundation would still do and mention. Good mix of serious science and silly science, imho. Well done, author!
