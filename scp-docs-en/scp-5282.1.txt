20 Feb 2020 18:38

Nouns are not necessarily physical objects with concrete referents. Nouns can refer to abstract concepts, like 'concept', 'word', 'language', or 'noun'.

Did you know that there are words that form other words when you invert them? Can she create a spinning TOP, or will she only get a POT?

or will she get marijuana?

When you have an SCP that involves natural language parsing, these are the sorts of questions that you should be thinking of. If you don't, someone else will.
