Re: Nitpick about the text.

13 Nov 2010 01:43

:) It does describe a piece of Earth technology, yes. I don't want to give too much away until the article's been up a little longer.

Considering that the text is entirely romanised, I'm not sure why it would have to be in unicode. I mean, it could and there'd be nothing wrong with that, but both are just ways of encoding data in binary. My idea is that the original binary code is converted to ascii-compatible text by a decrypting algorithm. SCP-328-01-ARC (not real) contains that original document, which is written in several languages. SCP-328-01 is the same document with all the recognised languages converted into modern english.
