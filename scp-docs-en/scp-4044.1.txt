The inspiration for it was actually Lily's 001 proposal; the image of the universe just giving a moment of kindness and beauty as a parting gift to life on Earth, and the Foundation responding by putting aside their ruthlessness just for a moment to take it in, are pretty far outside of my usual headcanon but they're nice to think about. The idea of the Foundation being confronted with a smaller moment of no-strings-attached love and kindness from their normally terrible world, and meeting it halfway by containing the phenomenon but doing something decent themselves in return, stuck with me and this came out of it soon after. It sat in my sandbox for a while, but yesterday seemed like a good day to post it.
