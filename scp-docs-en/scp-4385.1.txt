Site Criticism Team - Staff Review

23 Sep 2019 14:15

Taking a look since the votes on this one seem fairly divisive.

+Crit

-Crit

If SCP-4385 is confirmed MTF Pi-1 () are to be deployed to the location of the call and determine the situation.
附加记录：    

comma after 

SCP-4385 is a tech support phoneline
附加记录：    

phone line

SCP-4385-1 is a voice of indeterminate gender and possesses the ability to manifest objects1 inside the subject they are talking to, terminating them in the process.
附加记录：    

I feel like there is a better way to word this. As it stands, this is a rather weak sentence given that it was the line to tell us what -1 was.

Oliver:  Excuse me, anyone on?
附加记录：    

I feel like this isn't how a call center interaction would go. Most times, the technician answers first. If you want Oliver to say something first, perhaps make it clear that the line connected but nobody spoke for a few seconds.

What do you mean not the line for jokes, my TV has been blank for the past five days and all you people keep saying is reboot. News flash, it doesn’t fucking work.
附加记录：    

This needs to be split up into multiple sentences to be grammatically correct.

Good morning. May you please state for your call?
附加记录：    

Is this intentionally incorrect? If so, you don't really emphasize this enough in the previous log.

Several inhibitors have been placed on you for your protection and the enforcers, several more are being transferred.
附加记录：    

 is a super awkward way to convey this information.

Death was caused by internal bleeding and acute stress reaction.
附加记录：    

This could be reworded for clarity and clinical tone.

SCP-4385-1: Oh nice. I hope I get to live as long as you do.
附加记录：    

Missing space after 

I'm gonna cut it about there. The dialogue up to now has been fine, but is mostly flat and wooden. These call center dudes don't have much of a personality besides sounding almost like an Invader Zim ripoff, and they would need a serious tonal overhaul to make me like them.

Overall, my main issue with this is that the pacing is, frankly, flat out bad. There is 0 escalation of any kind present throughout the piece. From the first log, we are told that these kill you, and given an example of how. Then we get another. And another. And then a kid. And then another kid. Then the agent. Killing adults escalating to killing kids and then a neutralized anomaly is dreadful story development, and didn't hold my interest in the slightest.

And that leaves me with the ending Why a foundation agent literally told a confirmed extra terrestrial that they have established contact with an alien planet with intelligent life is beyond me, and that is the exact opposite of what the Foundation should do in this scenario. It makes no sense and totally rips me out of the story.

The above points led me to a strong downvote.

SoullessSingularity, for any questions or concerns relating to this review.
