Doors.

This is a collab between Weryllium and I, which I never thought I'd be saying to be honest. But it was a blast watching our different styles work together to make something just batshit crazy and fun. 10/10 wery recommend.

Alright, so now formalities. We are incorporating the following clichés:

Door To Another World
Thing That Does A Thing Really Well
Cryptic Interview Followed By Interviewer Suffering
