The poetry of nature is never dead.

This is an entry for the 2019 INT GoI Contest for Team Inkblots. The primary authors of this article are Gabriel Jade, but our other team members helped significantly as well! It was quite the journey being able to work with these fine individuals and I've managed to learn a lot about writing, both individually and cooperatively, by working with them.

As a reminder, votes on this page do not count towards the contest; if you wish to contribute to the Inkblots' total votes, you must do so on this page's counterpart.

Thank you for reading!
