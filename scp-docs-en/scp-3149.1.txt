02 Mar 2018 05:21

I understand what you're saying here, but I just want to explain my reasoning here a little. The use of monkeys was, to me while writing, intended to be another layer of dehumanization towards the authors on the part of Westhead. Not only are they being forced to work in abysmal conditions, in bodies that aren't even theirs, but they're not even human bodies. Its humiliation, another way to break them down and make them do what they're told.

It's fine if that aspect of the skip didn't quite work for you, but I just wanted to explain where I was coming from with it.
