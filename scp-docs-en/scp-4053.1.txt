Saw this on young and under 30.

+block

–block

I like the imagery with the polaroid coming out of the statue's mouth. The first half of this did a great job of setting the mood. The second half was a letdown; all that great buildup seems wasted on the ending, which reminded me of the zombie-ish monster people from stuff like 28 Days Later or Doom. I might be missing something, but it doesn't seem to fit with the setup, and I kept expecting some kind of twist or turn that never came. No vote from me, since there were still parts of the beginning I liked and the writing was solid overall.

Edit - also, welcome to the site, and even though I didn't vote on it I still want to say congratulations on what looks to be a successful first article!
