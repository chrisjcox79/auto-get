22 May 2017 08:03

If you ended the article on that interview, it might have some impact when one realises that it is SCP-2000. Maybe how that ended up in Mount Olympus might not be immediately clear.

The Level 5 document does not really add much, since the interview handled the reveal better. The Greek myth reference can hold more water if the instances generated have stronger parallels to Greek mythology. Currently, they seem to be humans of varying unusual traits that can bend reality.

Perhaps the main point in the Level 5 version is to state that they are using reality benders to fight the instances off. I understand you have thematic reasons for it, but the development of the story here still did not convince me that reality benders are needed.
