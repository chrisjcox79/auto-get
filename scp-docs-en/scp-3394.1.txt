Reading this SCP brought to mind another very similar SCP. SCP-2470 is also a consciousness which, when it learns of or infers the existence of concepts close to it, negatively acts upon them. These two anomalies are very similar in that regard, and could be crosslinked.

While the method of this detection is unknown, it appears that SCP-3394 is only able to detect things which it has been able to logically deduce the existence of.
