I like this concept, but I have a couple points to raise. There's some simple number and date redaction throughout that seems unnecessary (the date of the Iraqi Freedom crash, the number of people extracted in the last interview). You also have some it's/its confusion throughout, remember that  is the possessive word.

I also think the way that the anomaly is triggered - that is, how aircraft are chosen for the  of helicopters - is not explained well, and anyway doesn't make a lot of sense since there is time wankery going on. Quoting:

SCP-3539-1’s appearance will be identical to that of the helicopter that suffered a crash resulting in total crew loss nearest to SCP-3539 at the time of transmission.
附加记录：    

This is clunkily constructed, and also not true if it's pulling helis from the future. Consider something like:

SCP-3539-1 appears identical to a helicopter that has crashed, resulting in total crew loss. SCP-3539 tends to select recently crashed aircraft, though exceptions have been made (see Addendum 3539.1).
附加记录：
