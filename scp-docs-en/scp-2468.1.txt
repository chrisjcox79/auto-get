Also, couple minor problems.

SCP-2468 tends to appear in populated yet open areas, including urban parks and town squares. The subject has shown a tendency to revisit places which have resulted in a successful yield.
附加记录：    

Why is it referred to suddenly as subject after being called entity for two paragraphs before? Makes it sound as if you're talking about something else.

Upon reaching a distance of thirty-five centimeters from SCP-2468, victims are lifted by an unknown force to a height of approximately two meters, and dematerialize completely, along with SCP-2468.
附加记录：    

This sentence is confusing, straight after describing calls for help, it suddenly talks about victims. I suggest this replacement.

If a living organism of sufficient size ([describe limits, author]) approaches within 35cm of SCP-2468, it is lifted by an unknown force to a height of approximately two metres, then dematerializes, along with SCP-2468.
