About Stage Four

05 Sep 2012 16:41

The idea is that SCP-1806-1s are having their brains re-wired to interact with a completely alien reality. The progression is hinted early in the log as we see the victim begin showing signs of synesthesia and issues communicating what he's perceiving. () The progression is cognitive as well as sensory, and when it's complete, we can presume that our victim is as isolated from our world as we are from the one he's perceiving. Not only in terms of the senses, but in terms of abstractions like language. That word salad makes perfect sense, we just don't have the cognitive superstructure to understand it, anymore than we can see what's watching us from behind the shadows.
