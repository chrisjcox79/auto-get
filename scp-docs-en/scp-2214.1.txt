Fold

13 May 2016 03:20

I don't know. 2188 and this are different in terms of scope of story. It's hard to, in an SCP article, expand on the cumulative effects changing an object in 2214 can do without some serious and thorough exposition that would assuredly not be entertaining to read. It would also probably be beyond the scope of many writers. By keeping it simple and constrained like this, one is able to fill in the gaps in their head and apply the object to their headcanon as needed. 2188 works more on a human scale, which is how it can get away with dumping information and narrative. This is fine as it is, and it's a good example of a short article with story and interesting detail.
