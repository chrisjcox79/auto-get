this actually makes perfect sense. What is a child, when you separate yourself enough from the idea, but a parasite? That's all it is. It feeds off of you indirectly(unless you're a woman and carrying it, and thus, it is feeding off of you *directly*) and returns nothing (physical) to the host while it is still in its juvenile state.

However, this goes a step further. The people affected stop seeing their children as, erm, their children. They see them -just- as parasites. Things living off of them in bodies that are, honestly, something of a poor recreation of our(fully formed and adult) own. We love them because they're cute to us, as is any  creature with the same proportions. The large head to body size, the eyes, a well-fed/healthy looking frame.

I honestly think this article might work better if the bit about the possibilities of aliens was taken out. Aliens are creepy, but not nearly as creepy as this just showing us the natural order of things.
