I care least about some temporal shenanigan object that needs a screw to contain it. I care little about the staff shoving their way onto O5 by abusing the anomaly. But that ^^ right there is what got me invested in this site. Anomalies are neat, and character stories make you feel; but a well-written article highlighting that the SCPF has this kind of checks-and-balances system in place, or that actually shows them as a functional, multinational organization designed to save the planet and the inherent need to constantly protect the system, wins my vote every time. The SCPF, in any headcanon where they're global and nigh-on all-powerful (I mean, they usually win, right?), would have to be an immense bureaucratic undertaking, and imo there's not nearly enough material on this site that takes that into consideration.

(see I said I get all fangirly over stuff like this!)
