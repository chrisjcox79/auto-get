Mathematics is a human construct; we invented and worked it to apply to our universe, so that the principles of it can be used to understand our universe more comprehensively (so, even if we prove that 1=0, nothing will actually change).

But if there's another species of intelligent life that's aware of their place in the cosmos, they probably have a parallel system, so it's not a matter of 'keeping the theme going'. They're life and sciences revolve around the number 11. They probably have another word for 11, and a system of math like our base 10. This is also kind of insightful when you think about what these aliens would be like in a social sense. They're probably very ceremonial, religious even, when it comes to 11. Just a thought. What I'm saying is, they probably have numbers too. Presumably the rest is just targeting.

You try on the lower level animals to make sure that the quirks of your 'communication disease' are all lined out. Then you move on to humans. It's how you beta test. It's responsible.

The point is, they're trying to communicate with us. It's why they started infecting more people with a higher incidence rate. They can (maybe they just do) enter close enough to us to communicate once every 11 years, and they seem to be trying to schedule and plan with us.

More to the point, what about the stuff that only involves 11s because we say it does? 11th floor, fine. They can count (though who knows if they would have started at 0 or 1 in floor numbering?). I also really, really don't believe that the Foundation would accurately get all 11^3 people in the Quito outbreak, or that they got precise numbers for a scattered outbreak across three different countries over a year after the fact. The WHO doesn't document literally every person affected by outbreaks; they estimate. And they aren't massively burdened by the requirements of secrecy.
