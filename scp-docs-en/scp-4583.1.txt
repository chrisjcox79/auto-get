-Crit

SCP-4583 is an anomalous, highly contagious strain of Influenza A (H1N1).
附加记录：    

Noting that Type A Influenza is a classification of the flu virus but it contains multiple different strains of flu. H1N1 is a Type A Influenza virus, and was made popular as such from the 2009 Swine Flu scare, but there are others. If this is a swine flu, you may be better served calling it  or something like that. Your info isn't wrong, but it wouldn't be how I would say it necessarily.

Every mistake I'll make for the rest of my life is sitting here in front of me and I can do nothing about them.

This is fucking horrifying, I love it.

Sufficient quantities of SCP-4583-T are released into the bloodstream by osmosis for its anomalous processes to manifest.
附加记录：    

By definition, osmosis is referring to the diffusion of water. The proper term should probably be diffusion.
