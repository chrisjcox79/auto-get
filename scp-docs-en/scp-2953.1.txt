This article was largely inspired from a few words from Steeltitan, which inspired me to do this up. Also, thank you to the aforementioned two and others such as Solo, Aiden Eldritch etc. for looking this through.

Attributions are:

IJAMEA was from SCP-2937 by Mazyrian

 was from this article in the GOC canon, compiled by Clef

SCP-953 (Polymorphic Humanoid) by Clef

And while it is cool to make shapeshifting bullets, they are meaningless before good old-fashion military-grade weapons.

So this implies that 953 may either be Tamamo-no-Mae that the IJAMEA have awakened OR a random kumiho caught by them and it played along the IJAMEA's misconceptions. It does give insight on why 953 is so malicious to people in general and that she is beyond a generic monster.

If you wish to, you may read between the paragraphs

could you explain a little more why the transformation happens the way it does?
附加记录：    

TL;DR, the rock is processed from a fox spirit and contact with it changes one's appearance to look like a fox spirit (commonly portrayed in Chinese-Japanese-Korean myths as a human-fox hybrid).

The prominence of female subjects and the focus on the breasts make it read as fetishy to me
附加记录：    

I will counter your claim of 

Today, we proceed to test the effect on a live human should a small portion of Sessho-seki be extracted. As expected, today's volunteer responded positively upon contact with Sessho-seki. As with yesterday's volunteer, he  will be given relief off his earthly tethers for his  troubles.
附加记录：    

Although, given that Japanese language usually don't use gendered third person pronoun, it is likely to be undefined.

But of course, you might be referring to Tamamo-no-Mae with this description:

She was in human form partially, with her nine red tails wrapping her otherwise nude body.
附加记录：    

Well I am only going to put readers further into the suspense of disbelief if I made her manifest clothes magically. It is within possibility that IJAMEA to give her clothes. But I woudl regard her nakedness to be an allusion to the fox spirit's traditional role as a seductress (a role Tamamo-no-Mae performed on her myth).

And onto your second clam on , I would like to remind you that 'breast' is an organ of the human body. While it does attract a certain sexual connotation, it is the official scientific term for the organ (and Wikipedia gave me nothing else to work with). To say 'chest' is too broad and barely defines anything.

Ah, it is good to highlight concerns and I will sate your curiosity at least.

Transfigurations are more prominent and occur at an increased rate in female subjects than male subjects.
附加记录：    

Since the original entity is a female, a female body would be more receptacle to the transformation than a male.

and the extra breast growth/extra teats.
附加记录：    

Red foxes tend to have four pairs of them, and it references SCP-953 (which has been compared to a red fox). 'Teat' was used since it connotes animals, as compared to 'nipple' (usually implying human).
