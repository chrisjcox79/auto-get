02 Mar 2011 13:37

Overall, I like this concept. Not exactly a new idea, and not terribly memorable, but cool and creepy all the same. A few things:

I'm not a fan of that bit at the end about how it started ramming into vehicles when boxed in, especially since it apparently perfectly content to sit around because of a parking boot. I think I prefer the idea of this simply being a machine doing what it was built to when able, not some malevolent force of destruction.

This is a bit long. You go into heavy detail with both recovery and, more problematic, why it isn't special. When reading an SCP, I assume that it is completely normal until you tell me otherwise; you don't need to spend a paragraph describing how everything operates completely normally. You could cut out pretty much the second half of that paragraph and it would work just fine. That being said, if it uses a standard diesel engine, where does it get fuel?

Also, you really need to fix the formatting on the second photo.

So yeah, there's my two cents.
