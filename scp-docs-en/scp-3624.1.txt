I like the concept behind this one - it's actually pretty refreshing. A horrible ritual meant to create a hostile monster ends up creating a series of beneficial, albeit deformed pets. That said, there are a number of tone issues (particularly in the dialogue) that lead to my current downvote - I encourage you to keep editing this, however, because it's got potential.

+examples

-examples

It involves a number of requisites, most commonly being animal body parts, although on a single occasion a human arm was successfully utilized.
附加记录：    

A human arm is an animal body part - scientifically speaking, there shouldn't be anything particularly special about it. It's also an odd sentence in general. You mention a number of requisites, then only specify animal body parts. I suggest altering the sentence to something along the lines of:

Each SCP-3624 ritual utilizes a selection of material components as a base, most frequently a variety of freshly severed animal body parts. No pattern has been observed in the components selected for each ritual; practitioners appear to be largely opportunistic. These body parts are then stitched together in a shape that roughly resembles a predatory animal (see Addendum 3624-01 for a breakdown of each observed attempt).
附加记录：    

Though I'm not particularly satisfied with that either. I was staring at it for about half an hour before deciding a suggestion didn't necessarily need to be perfect.

附加记录：    

This dialogue is somewhat unnatural. , which comes off as jarring at best. This would better fit the tone you're conveying with his words.

附加记录：    

And so on. Unfortunately I don't have the time to be completely comprehensive, but I hope that selection helps, at least.
