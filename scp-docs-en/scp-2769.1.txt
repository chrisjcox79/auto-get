The nonstandard item number thing is very much feeling like a flavor of the month at this point, and it's becoming harder and harder to not just downvote on sight when I see these.
I'm also beginning to feel that way about weird cognitihazards that affect their own documentation. I'm starting to tire of bells and whistles. Give me some writing.
Maybe there's some thread running through this that elevates it above lolrandom wackiness for its own sake. If there is, I'm not seeing it, and this isn't giving me anything as a reader to convince me to look all that hard for it. There's no initial grounding and I'm unconvinced that there's anything here. The assumption should never be that the reader is obligated to untangle everything that you've laid out, independent of the setup.
Exhibit #1,233,924 of a thing that inexplicably talks and of all the things it could sound like it sounds like a smart alecky teenager.

Can't say I'm feeling this one on any level.
