Manti Te'o's girlfriend as a joke SCP is a format that has been used a couple times already with varying degrees of success, such as SCP-3999-J and SCP-007-J; however, I daresay that the former's success was primarily due to being a parody of 3999 and the latter's to having a reasonably funny dialogue.

I think this would work a lot better if it really leaned into the joke, possibly by tying into the Antimemetics Division or the general prevalence of amnestics - ideally, using the joke to skewer one of the various memory-related story concepts on the site in general.
