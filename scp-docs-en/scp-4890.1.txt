31 Jul 2018 04:31

Eh. Paint me underwhelmed.

The anomaly here is pretty stock Wondertainment shenanigans, which is a little bland as the article doesn't push the GoI in any particularly interesting directions, but rather seems to tread over the same whimsical ground often covered by these articles.

While the Hindenberg Disaster and Assassination of Ceaser were fairly clever test log entries, the rest fell flat and seemed more like an attempt to link out to other historical objects without much of an attempt to build a substantial narrative connecting the pieces.

Finally, the twist at the end featuring SCP-1000 has just been done so much before as to take all weight out of that particular twist.

-1. Perhaps if there had been more logs of the quick, witty quality as the ones mentioned above I'd be a little more willing to let other aspects of the article slide, but as is you don't do much here for me as a reader.
