I considered an  story in a later draft, but when I started writing it got bogged down with details and I eventually scrapped it. Admittedly, I took the easy way out by killing Collins and cutting off the test logs.

Part of the problem is that I wasn't sure what should happen if the person escapes. Does the cave continue to mimic them? Because then you basically have a permanent (though tiny) earthquake. Or it could just deactivate - this is a little boring but a satisfying escape log would make up for it.

I'm writing something else right now, but I'll probably come back to this in a few weeks and rewrite the ending.
