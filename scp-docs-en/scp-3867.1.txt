Okay! So this whole ordeal started late last night (as most things do) when Captain Kirby and I were talking. I jokingly do 'Panda Watch 2k19' updates on whatever mundane things my dog is doing, and he commented that it would be a fun SCP idea to have a website that shows you the last thing your dog did. We brainstormed this one out together and I wrote it while he continued to pour good ideas into my noggin.

So hooray, Captain Kirby and nico collab. Woo!

All pet pictures are courtesty of Alces_alces respectively.

and the Jamba Juice is from here https://www.flickr.com/photos/12739382@N04/2415197699

I'm happy to see that people are enjoying what was kind of just nico and I spitballing at like 1AM!
