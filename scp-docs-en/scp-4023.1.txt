Fold

22 Sep 2018 16:28

I understand what this is trying to do here, but a lot got lost in the execution. I'm almost inclined to believe that this was a coldpost for a few reasons.

+reasons

-reasons

It seems like you bit off more than you could chew, putting 23 anomalies into one article and redacting half of them. Really, within the entire article, only like 4 or 5 of the SCP-4023-# entries are really even anomalous by their own right (and even fewer could actually stand as SCPs on their own), a good chunk of them are filler [DATA EXPUNGED] entries to take up space (with a handwavey in-universe explanation to boot), and the rest of them could be listed under addenda while still retaining the meaning of the article.
It is too damn long while trying too hard, making it too ambitious for its own good. It ends up failing to deliver on the promises it makes and builds up, leaving the reader feeling like they wasted their time reading a story with an unsatisfying/incomplete conclusion.
SO. MUCH. REDACTION. Seriously, it's all over the place, and it's in places where it really isn't needed, or is even given to the reader anyways, either upon a highlight or just through reading a later collapsible.
Despite failing to come up with 23 related SCPs (which appears to be the intention of the article, as well as the focus of the main format screw), a few of the articles that were mostly fleshed out were fairly pointless from a storytelling perspective. A ritual where a level-3 researcher has to murder some personal effects of the O-5 Council is just so out of left field, and it's just left hanging there. Same goes for the album. Literally none of that besides the number 23 had anything to do with the rest of the article. It read like a sandbox idea that you weren't comfortable with shoving into its own spot on the mainlist.
Lastly (and perhaps most importantly), there are a LOT of grammatical errors all over this thing. I get having a few mistakes here and there, but there are so many that it almost makes me think you pulled a Red Bull fueled all-nighter to crank this thing out. A line-by-line critique would help a lot with this.

-reasons

TL;DR: Basically, this reads like something I would find in the draft forums, not like a finished work. -1
