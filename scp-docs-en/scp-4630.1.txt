I knew to expect something special with the 4630-2, but that twist took me by surprise. Well done, great first scip! +1

+Nitpicks

-Nitpicks

You have some formatting things that bug me. In your interview logs you add a horizontal separate before the [END LOG], which isn't correct. It's also inconsistent with the [START LOG]. I would also recommend making the [XX LOG]s bold.

Also I would remove the horizontal separator before the first audio log and replace it with an  or something. The horizontal separators between interviews are not necessary in my opinion, quote blocks by their nature offer a visual separation between items.
