1) Why is it Safe? Euclid or even Keter seems more appropriate given the fact SCP-3423-1 can appear anywhere.

In a seemingly random interval ranging from 3 to 24 hours, the location of SCP-3423-1 will instantly change.
附加记录：    

Note: Neither the interval of change nor the location is random.
附加记录：
