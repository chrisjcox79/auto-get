Sometimes, some things are just a wild goosechase...

29 Jun 2020 15:07

Hello everyone! This is Veralta once again, presenting a story about chasing geese, eggs that hatch into Paris, and eccentricity (well-meaning and nostalgic by the way) when you have the money. Enjoy!

This was actually the product of a site-19 chat joke concerning 'French Eggs', which was a part of a spoof concerning the 'Partridge in the Pear Tree' song.

Special thanks to DrAkimoto1, forbiddenquest, DianaBerry, fairydoctor, kidf, barredowl, Calibri_Bold, anorrack, Naveil, Marcelles_Raynes, theycallmetim, Ekronak, Meska and jornax. You really helped me bring out Gastoneu's character and the weirdness of hatching Paris:)
