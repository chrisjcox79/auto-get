25 Jul 2018 02:39

I was going to pass this one up, but it seems to be getting a lot of attention. If you want to save it, I think it just needs some context. Where they were found, and a log that shows they tried more reasonable procedures that failed because one of them produced a portable hole from their pocket, and let themselves out of the containment locker, etc. It needs more of the juxtaposition between the silly but wily, slippery objects, and the serious, high security facility they're contained in. If you don't include more representation of the facility and reasonable documentation, then a lot people are going to see this as a failed joke scp instead of an interesting and unique main series scp.

Optionally, un-blunt their mallets so that their antics become dangerous and actually scary. If you don't keep the don happy, he cartoonishly drops a piano on you that you didn't notice was hanging there. Only, much less cartoonishly, your body crushes like a mortal human body.

That's an object that needs secure containment procedures.
