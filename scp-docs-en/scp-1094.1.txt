Mmkay, some questions n' comments.

1. Why is the Site number redacted? You can just pick a number at random, go to whichever page on the wiki details the canonical Site numbers, and make sure it isn't one of those. In actual containment situations, it would be important to know the Site number for the object, in case someone needed to get to it.

2. The Containment Procedures show no mechanism in place for actually testing the object, even though it has been fairly lightly-researched Or add in something in the containment procedures about how one would go about testing it, either one.

3. Do you mean for the pivotal event in the article to happen in 2019? I would consider moving it back to slightly in the past rather than the future; while I have never minded articles that appear to be written in the future, a lot of people tend to think it breaks immersion.

All in all, not a bad article, but it doesn't quite grip me enough for an upvote. No vote for now.
