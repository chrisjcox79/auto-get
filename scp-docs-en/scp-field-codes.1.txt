11 Dec 2019 23:12

That's awesome to hear they have made it to your wall irl. I've had people discuss working them into different stories or mediums, some interesting comment short fiction written on Reddit. Only really recently have I seen them brought up more than just sharing the original.

Some Chinese producers made listings on Amazon with the symbols on shirts. An SCP game included one as an Easter Egg. Fans made joke edits or their own symbols. I finally cleaned up the image and made listings for shirts and posters myself. Now BoneWorks has several of the symbols in the game and I'm trying to make sure they properly credit me for the originals.

It's been an interesting ride since I first posted these on dA and on the wiki forums.
