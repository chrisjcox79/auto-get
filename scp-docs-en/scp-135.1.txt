CANCER DOES NOT WORK THAT WAY! GOODNIGHT!

12 Feb 2013 22:00

So, speaking as a biologist, if you're giving people cancer as a result of exposure to this SCP then any cells you remove from the area of effect should likewise continue growing uncontrollably with a nutrient source. Meaning, this SCP must cause irreversible changes to gene regulation in the cells. The alternative is that cells only grow uncontrollably while in the AoE of this SCP, in which case the cancerous growths would revert to their previous characteristics upon removal from the SCP and all you'd have is a test subject with some really nasty-looking benign neoplasias or maybe a good teratoma or two.

Re: CANCER DOES NOT WORK THAT WAY! GOODNIGHT!

15 Feb 2013 00:44

Yes, SCP-135 induces irreversible changes to gene regulation of cells. Being in the AoE additionally removes the need for a nutrient source and causes the cells to multiply at an unnaturally fast rate. When removed from the AoE, the affected cells are still cancerous, but they revert to acting like non-anomalous cancer cells.
