Titled, 

Grief is not a new theme for me to explore in my SCPs. I'm not sure what that says about me, but, writing is an important way for me to work through my emotions and the contents of the articles don't necessarily reflect what I am trying to work with. But I have seen grief take its toll.

This story resonates with the idea thatyou yourself are still apart of the equation. Until you address the rot at your core, you can changed a million variables and never change your circumstance.

Don't run from your grief, you'll just die tired.

Thank you for your read.
