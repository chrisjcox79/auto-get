17 Nov 2015 22:59

Fantastic. I'll admit to a certain amount of bias in favor of the concept, being a longtime baseball fan, but even beyond that, this is quite clever, humorous without being stupid, and built around an internally consistent view of the alternate world in which it's set. You clearly have a vision of the world in which this story takes place, and it's allowed to leak out naturally through the details of the log. Good piece of writing and storytelling, and I'm happy to support it.

Favorite parts:

The entry for the Chicago Orphans was hilarious; the mental image of the Cubs being kicked out of Wrigley and still trying to play was pretty funny.
The San Francisco Ghosts  to escape an assassination plot.
I'm assuming the San Diego team is a reference to this.
The references to real-life defunct franchises like the Highlanders, Pilots, and the Colt .45s.

One question:

Conspired to fix the 1919 World Series, which they lost in a four game sweep, to the confusion of all involved.
附加记录：    

Wouldn't the Black Sox winning the Series be the more confusing outcome?

Overall, nice work, and one of my new favorites.
