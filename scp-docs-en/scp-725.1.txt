25 Feb 2010 16:31

You missed a few instances where you should have blacked out or changed your SCP references, ie. SCP-XXX and ?? where you wanted to put in the blackout ink. I like the concept, but like many SCPs the description has too much unneeded information. From what I've seen the description should be a detached observation of the object. IE, it's a parroting humpback whale that can mimic sounds in X ranges, and appears to have a memetic effect on other whales.

The details of it's capture and observations as to it's need to find new sounds should probably go into addenda or testing logs.

For a first go though, I like it, but then I tend to like biological SCPs.
