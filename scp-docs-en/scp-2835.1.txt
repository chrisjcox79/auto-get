24 Jul 2016 16:44

I like the idea of an angry animated pelican who hates being stuck in a bad cartoon, and Paddy's characterisation is pretty amusing. I also like the presentation of the link with the WENR-TV incident, because it evokes the sort of things you'd see in discussion of conspiracies or paranormal events quite well.

Unfortunately, making Paddy able to kill people is extremely predictable and ruined the article for me. The dehydration mechanism is seemingly totally arbitrary, and the fact that Paddy can go around killing large numbers of people for very little reason makes this yet another 'super-petty anomalous god-being', which is a story I've seen too many times to interest me. It also undermines Paddy's characterisation as insecure and desperate for human validation because of how bad his cartoon is, because he has superpowers. It'd work better as a story if Paddy was either comically impotent or vindictive but limited in his abilities.
