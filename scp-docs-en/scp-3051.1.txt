Authors Note

18 Jul 2017 19:02

Thanks to Spookypizza, Maliceaforethought, Lexinator, and Gaffney for crit on this. Also thanks to DrMagnus for the amazing department report formatting code. If you enjoyed, feel free to check out my other stuff here: http://www.scp-wiki.net/modern-erasmus-author-page

https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Aerial_view_of_the_Capitol_Hill.jpg

https://openclipart.org/detail/122833/all-seeing-tech-eye

Images are both public domain

As someone who works pro bono as a human rights lobbyist irl, this is straight from my own frustrations. I've wanted so many times to really show people the suffering their actions are causing, in the belief that if they only really knew then they'd change. Considering Manna doesn't exactly have the restrictions a normal ngo does, I believe it only follows that they'd eventually act on the same frustration.
