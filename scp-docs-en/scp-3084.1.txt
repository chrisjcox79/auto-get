The last picture was honestly the most creeepy for me, and it acted as a marvelous punchline. It looks like somebody with a cloak pulled over their head. The last scene of SCP-3084 is the men pulling the old woman's cloak over the young woman's head.

But this creates more questions than answers for me. Where did they find her? Why does it look like she's in a lake? What's with the VCR stuff? Is this another film anomaly? 701? 993? 1981?

All in all, great skip.
