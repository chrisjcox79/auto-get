The directness of the final note sapped it of any credulence, making it into an overbearing and unearned emotional beat that did not resonate in the slightest. I might have appreciated the sentiment had it not strayed from the established behavior of the anomaly, at the very least then it wouldn't have came off as quite a strong case of authorial intrusion.

It made me unable to remain immersed, sending what may have been a sentimental moment into the throes of narmy talking down to the reader.
