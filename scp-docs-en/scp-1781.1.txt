Nope. Nope nope nope. I strongly disagree. This does not need to be a -J, and probably wouldn't have succeeded if it were. Many main list articles have comedic elements, but work perfectly as regular SCPs because they present the article in a believable way. There's an entirely different set of standards for joke articles, and this doesn't meet them. It's silly, but it's not laugh-out-loud funny from start to finish, nor was it intended to be. It's just an anomaly that creates situations which can humorous, but also highly disturbing.

21 Jul 2014 20:54

I like the idea all right, but I feel like the observation log didn't sell the article as fully as it could have. There was a lot of potential to push the ideas much further, ramping up the weirdness and interest. Why not have Hydra!Weaving grow extra Weaving heads? Why not have Bone!Weaving act according to the physical properties of a regular bone? Why not have him in the role of a woman? Or as the T-Rex in Jurassic Park? Or as Gandalf's hat? What about animated films? What about theatrical documentaries like Earth? Why not show the consequences of what happens when a film is played that already has Weaving in it? This strikes me as an SCP that is wholly supported by the accompanying log, and right now that log feels too weak to hold up the rest of the article (kinda like the Siskel  Ebert skip before its rewrite). +-0

Also, it was very considerate of the SCP creator to make sure that the backstory-explaining notes regenerate like that. (Also also that is a very, very good photoshop.)
