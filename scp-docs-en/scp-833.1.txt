Pointless expungement is when [DATA EXPUNGED] is just thrown in casually as a lazy attempt to make something interesting, there is no build-up to it and it doesn't fit the SCP at all.

Expunged data (in my opinion) should be used for self-replicating memes or things too terrible to even think of (or usually a mixture of the two), this is because that (again, in my opinion) [DATA EXPUNGED] is where the data is completely and utterly deleted due to said reasons but possibly a hard-copy exists somewhere, in a very big safe.

As I can't possibly imagine that this SCP can create any sort of death meme or do something utterly terrible that warrants an expungement it just ruins the entire SCP for me because all I can think of is the person that wrote it did so thinking: 

Note #1: For proper uses of [DATA EXPUNGED], see SCP-923.

Note #2: For something that manages to be really mysterious without a single [DATA EXPUNGED], see SCP-055.
