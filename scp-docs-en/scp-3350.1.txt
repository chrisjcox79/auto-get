Feedback!

+block

–block

The concept has real merit, but parts of this felt kind of awkward to me.

For one, this is a very long article that basically boils down to . You've got a lot of extraneous details that don't feel like they add much.

As well, I wasn't really sold on the whole  it didn't really fit with the rest of the article (for me at least).

Finally, there's awkward wording in a lot of places. For example:

Field agents must search residencies actively at the hours of 2 and 8 AM on a daily basis for SCP-3350 instances to prevent discovery by any nearby residents.
附加记录：    

In addition to slightly clunky language, you never mention which residencies are being actively searched. I assume, from later in the article, that it's specifically this small town in Canada, but that should be made explicit in some way (even just by adding the phrase ).

I want to like this one, but it needs cleaning up.

Best of luck!

I liked the idea and its implications, but here's some things that got me kinda confused:

+questions

-questions

Instances anomalously materialize almost instantly within a standard mailing depository corresponding to a household at predicted faster-than-light speeds.
附加记录：    

The delivery process is always unnoticed, and will not manifest if under surveillance of any known type or under direct eye contact of other sapient beings.
附加记录：    

If you can never watch the delivery process, how can you predict that it can materialize itself at faster-than-light speeds?

subjects are able to change the physical construct of their bodies by applying slight pressure to an area of the body.
附加记录：    

Do the subjects discover that functionality through trial and error? Because it doesn't seems a lot intuitive for me. It might be just me, but if I used this DVD, I would probably take it out of whatever reading device I used it, before I could figure out how it worked by myself.

Anyway, I'm just a newbie, and I'm not necessarily criticizing you, but rather just have some questions that maybe already have a reasonable explanation.
