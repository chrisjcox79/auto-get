+again

-that

Someone recruited a craftsman to produce goggles that can be used to determine certain aspects of people's souls (the fact that animals have them too is secondary). They fucked up in their background check of this individual though, thinking he was reliable to work with. He wasn't. Didn't get that this wasn't some kind of Halloween joke and ended up shipping an anomaly through the post, which then made it end up in Foundation care. Whoever ordered these things, and whatever their plans for them are, they aren't content with loose ends. And they'd probably kill to have a mole inside the Foundation to get their goggles back.
