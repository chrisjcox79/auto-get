The only thing I would change is to add a little more to the [DATA EXPUNGED]. At the moment, it just seems like a blank spot. I would put a tiny bit in there to push people onto a certain train of thought (pun absolutely intended). For example, "Some notable and reoccurring cars are a horse transport car with horse corpses hanging from ropes made of sheep tendon, a tanker car filled with a mixture of crude oil and human bile, a large transport car filled with stacks of keys made of many different materials, [DATA EXPUNGED]."

changed to

"Some notable and reoccurring cars are a horse transport car with horse corpses hanging from ropes made of sheep tendon, a tanker car filled with a mixture of crude oil and human bile, a large transport car filled with stacks of keys made of many different materials, and a passenger car containing the dismembered remains of several well known [DATA EXPUNGED]."

You could probably think of a better hint to put before the [DATA EXPUNGED], but I think that the little clue will direct people to darker, scarier thoughts than simply having them insert their own fears entirely.
