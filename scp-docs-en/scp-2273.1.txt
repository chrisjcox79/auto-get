I wrote a lot of stuff about why things were the way they were in the parallel universe Alexei comes from, but that draft was boring as Hell.

Moarspoilers

–block

Basically, the biggest difference between Alexei's world and our world is the fact that the war between humanity and SCP-1000 wasn't as severe in his world. Instead of getting bombed back into the Stone Age, they were just knocked off their high horse. Their cities and technology were left intact, but they went underground, literally. When WWI came around, SCP-1000 was reminded  When they saw that we were divided on such intense ideological and nationalist boundaries, they decided to play both sides in order to get payback. They've been keeping that game up at the time of Alexei's recovery for 70 years, just letting humanity wipe itself out.

Does that help?
