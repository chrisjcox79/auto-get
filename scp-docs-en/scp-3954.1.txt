Preface: I HAVE NO EXPERTISE WORTH MENTIONING. Take my ideas with a respectful grain of thought. That being said I think I can give helpful very subjective anecdotal comments.

I thought this story was amazing. About halfway through I was thinking of all the emotional trauma you could display through it and low and behold you threw some in at the end. I actually would have been fond for a shorter description and more logs about the SCP PTSD-style reliving memories or even more addiction logs. Everyone in the story felt very human, which is great, especially since you weren't forcing your Dr. type characters to seem human through what they say. Basically, great indirect characterization. It's 2 AM my time, but I thought I'd see if there were any good Yau30s and I can say I was not in the slightest bit disappointed.
