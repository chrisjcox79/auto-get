This has a lot of tropes swirling around on it. I don't mean to say that they're poorly combined, but that I don't think they're advisable at all. So, this is:

- A magical vigilante with perfect knowledge.

- Its solution to sex offenders is literally what they already try to do with sex offenders, except in this case it works perfectly, never hits an innocent, and presumably doesn't have long-term health outcomes. Forgive me if I don't find  to be a clever ironic comeuppance.

- It also gives them alternative pleasures that act as standard-issue cursed wish-granters/compulsion effects. Which can probably kill them, because execution is fine too, right? Also, I get the feeling that you mean these things are pleasurable but not fetishes/sexual, but the language there should be more distinct.

- All of this is bookended by a meme and an internet fad/movement.

So yeah, it's a bunch of bad tropes stitched together, amounting to  I'm all for GAW tackling sex offenders, but this just feels like a wish fulfillment power fantasy. -1
