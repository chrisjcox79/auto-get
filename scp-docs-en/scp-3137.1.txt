I feel like having a collaborative log would detract a little from the spookiness of this mysterious application and its creators. In my opinion, a lot of SCPs with large collaborative logs are known more for the log's contents than the SCP itself. For example, 914 turns things into other things, but the focus is on the series of inputs/outputs rather than the machine itself. Maybe having a log would turn the focus of this SCP from the notepad and its creators, to simply a list of . Maybe it'll all work out though.
