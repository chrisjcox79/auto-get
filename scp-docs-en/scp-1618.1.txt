Oh Bastet. (I seen this silly pop up in the SA thread, so I actually bother to address it)

Why Duchamp's Fountain - because it looks like an urinal?

Seriously - the focus of this comes from the quote cited at the bottom, which calls for a golden toilet of some sort, and given various considerations (executives inside the building being primary target, urinals being outside rather than closed in cabins making the leaflet message somewhat more noticeable,me simply liking the picture of the gilded urinal, and it being the first thing I thought of when off to make the SCP) I chose an urinal. However, the whole object wouldn't change a single bit if it was an actual toilet.
