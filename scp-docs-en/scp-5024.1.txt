This is my first try at giving feedback to a post and I can't think of a better one to do than this one. I loved every bit of this. The concept was really interesting and original, probably because it's so hard to describe something made up of just information, but you pulled it off.

The first thing that comes to mind is that this feels more like a tale than a SCP. You could try splitting it into the bare anomaly and then a spinoff of tales involving it, but I don't know if the SCP would be half as good without the added tale aspect. Addressing the  I said before, please do that. I want to see and know so much more about these characters. I want see more of the magic they are using and know how it works.

Honestly, I am not a good enough writer to try and improve this. I do hope that a good enough writer does come by and gives you feedback to make this even better than it is now. Once again, please write some Tales with these characters. With my first forum post, you also have my first upvote. +1 all the way.
