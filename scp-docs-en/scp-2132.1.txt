Its a little confusing, I'll be the first to admit that.

Spoilers

–block

Inspiration-wise, I work with kids and I'm constantly astounded by the things they come up with. Like, kids are so weird and they can come up with such cool stuff. I'm very jealous. This also stems from my dad, who's one of those guys it's impossible to get presents for because he just goes and buys himself anything he wants. The stuff he wants us to get him are tickets to things, experiences. Human beings will never have everything they want, even after getting every experience, they'll always want something more specific, more catered, more unique. I also think numbers stations are creepy.

MCD disposed of him and replaced him with Yuki. We'll see how long she lasts.
