Hey, thanks for the feedback, and the interest. I appreciate that more than anything else!

+Answer

-Hide

Good eye with the 271 thing. So 2.71828 is an approximation for the mathematical constant e. It's an irrational number, like pi, that shows up all over math/nature/physics. It's found in population growth algorithms, computing interest accrual, and practically everywhere in calculus. It's an important number to our universe.

One thing you may have never thought about is that humans use a base 10 numbering system (0s 1s 2s 3s all multiples of 10. The aliens don't use multiples of 10. They use e, and so their structures naturally show multiples of e for the same reason ours show multiples of 10. This isn't important to the overall theme of the skip, its just a little something I threw in because I find it interesting and I was hoping people might ask about it and/or be intrigued.

You are right about the Sycamore aliens. They are alien maintenance men who noticed some weird signal coming from the Earth site and showed up to check things out. They just found a bunch of meddling earthlings messing things up they do the prudent thing stun the Earthlings and remove all the obsolete equipment just to make sure the Earthlings can't meddle. It's like a maintenance guy showing up at a Nuclear silo and seeing a bunch of ants eating the wires of a now-obsolete system so they safely remove the wires knowing that the ants can't really hurt anything else. Except that they're wrong, because these ants have dangerous SCPs at their disposal and actually do end up setting off the nuke.

You are probably right about the specific audience. I just don't know what to do about that. It's a sciency SCP, cuz science is my thing. You are right that this wouldn't make a good SCP-2000

Thanks again for the interest!
