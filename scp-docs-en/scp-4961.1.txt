Thanks to Chritin (or whatever their new wikidot name is) for their valuable input during the creation of this article, to Ayers for helping me overcome hurdles with the license compatibility for the images used, and to the real Ruta, Radek, and Ana for an awesome summer.

This one’s been glaring at me from my archives folder for weeks now, so I decided to finally polish it off and post it. Taking a summer job in the middle of nowhere with little to no internet connection can really you’re your creative drive, it turns out. Pair that with a friend’s loss of a beloved furry friend, and it all takes a darker turn. I hope you all enjoy!

Note:  The font for the letter section of the article might not load properly for mobile users and might display as a default font with odd spacing instead, but otherwise it loads normally on computers.
