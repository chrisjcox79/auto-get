There's an online first draft1 pair of books called The Salvation War by Stuart Slade that deals with a very similar premise, and goes into detail about how it may play out.

Long story short, Hell (and Heaven) get their asses kicked six ways to sunday by humanity. It seems that mighty angels and terrible demons aren't a match for cruse missiles, tanks, jet fighters, and nukes.

1. Story is, it got leaked/pirated before it was finished, and gained a perception of being a money sink afterwards by publishing firms. The planned third book was never completed because of this. Piracy is bad, mmm'kay?
