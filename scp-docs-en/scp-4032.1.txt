Gas explosion

04 Nov 2018 13:18

This is one of those few occasions where I know exactly what it says under redacted, but it's redacted for a good reason:

+block

–block

The sloth's digestive tract is closed up and can't handle the increased pressure, so the sloth explodes. It was redacted because I, the author, and the foundation in-universe, are leary to describe in detail the gruesome death of a cute pet, unless a researcher needs the exact details.

Even in real-life, hardened people that are accustomed to death tear up at a dog's death, and sloths are especially cute. But yes, they explode like a sloth-shaped firecracker.
