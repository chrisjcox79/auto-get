Brackish; water, slightly salty or briny/distasteful or unpleasant. So no, that isn't misused.

It's not a cheese that makes you eat cheese until you die; that's a side effect of the initial infection. The hook consists of two things, the first being that the cheese recreates itself through infected subjects, and second being that an unknown individual or group of individuals is spreading it with malicious intent. I'm sorry that I haven't made this clear enough.
