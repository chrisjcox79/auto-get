To expand on my thoughts, I guess that I think the concept is fine: a haunted funhouse/house of mirrors is always cool. However, there's no real cohesion, no underlying theme, nothing to guess at. The SCP is just a standard haunted funhouse. It seems to be randomly screwing with people who enter in completely random ways. The SCP doesn't seem to have any goals or motives, just random spooky stuff. I think you need it to have some difficult-to-ascertain motive that we can partially guess at. Maybe someone was murdered/molested/[REDACTED] by some cult there and they want everyone to know. Maybe its inhabited by an inter dimensional mirror alien or a psycho cult clown.
