That's why I said this is such a great SCP. It's a simple concept, but done in a way that leads to a great deal of discussion possibilties.

From a psychological standpoint, this dog could be viewed as the personification of death. It could be evil, it could be good, it could just be a thing that happens, or it could be that this thing is just a remarkable coincidence. The point is: we don't know.

It took me a while to understand that, but that's why I like this SCP so much. It makes you think.
