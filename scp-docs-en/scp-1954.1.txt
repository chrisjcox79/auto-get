Interesting. As soon as I seen the bad title , I downvoted but something about this one made me remove the downvote.

There are heavy failures of tone here, and I don't understand how putting allspice (unless you mean deodorant) in meatballs , or inviting a clown to a birthday party is counter-intuitive, but the idea itself isn't bad, and I enjoyed reading it.

Overall ,not a bad job. I'm looking forward to this getting fixed up.
