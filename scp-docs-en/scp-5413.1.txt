Interesting SCP

I am not the author nor am I a sikh but here is my interpretation of it. If I am wrong about something or missed some thing please correct me. If someone want to do a declass on reddit you may use my interpretation

Based on the author's comment sounds like an allegory on alcoholism and effect of alcohol on faith and life in general.

Mild sedative and euphoric effects.

Motor issues, such as a loss of balance and poor

Digestive and excretory issues, such as vomiting and dehydration.

Respiratory and circulatory issues, such as aspiration pneumonia, cyanosis, and respiratory depression.
附加记录：

Knowledge of SCP-5413's layout obtained by viewing SCP-5413 in person carries a mild compulsory and amnestic effect
附加记录：    

The effects of entering the Gurdwara does sound like the effect of alcohol on the body. I wonder have the Class D used for exploration had their blood tested?

The door to the right leads to the Langar hall. Seven vats for food are present in the sitting area. Two are filled with hot, cooked meat, and two others contain yeast, barley, and grapes
附加记录：    

According to the footnotes a Langar is  and having meat there does sound a bit sacrilegiou. The other vat contains ingredients for beer (yeast and barley) and wine (yeast and grapes).

300,000 rupees
附加记录：    

That is expensive which is about 300,000 rupees in 2020 usd

The only set of human remains in SCP-5413 is a male child's skeleton, approximately seven years of age, in a prostrated position in front of the box. Syringes jut out of its arms.
附加记录：    

I am not sure how a Syringe jut out of his arms. Was it impaled into any specific bones? Is there clothing on the skeleton? Maybe that is an allegory of children overdosing on their parent's drugs?

Attempting to empty a nearby water pitcher causes a rose-colored mixture to dispense, able to flow past what the pitcher can contain. Testing has revealed it to be a mix of Amrit, goat's milk, and blood.
附加记录：    

The closest I can find to Amrit a brand of alcoholic bevereage. Does the foundation have the capability to find out what is the specific brand of alcoholic beverage?

A disciplinary paddle and ruler are present on the desk, both still warm.

A child's screams and sounds of a liquid being consumed can be heard, and are the only details early subjects were able to recall.
附加记录：    

Sounds like these two are connected. Was the skeleton a result of the child getting beaten by a drunk?
