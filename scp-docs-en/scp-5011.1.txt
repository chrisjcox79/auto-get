WE MADE IT.

This SCP would not have been possible without the incredible Caro, who had the idea and did the real leg work of making the documents look good. She deserves far more credit than I do. Also thanks to shaggy for making sure the ending didn't suck, Dexanote for providing Bridge's line, and Tanhony for providing much-needed moral support, as well as Elogee Fishtruck and everybody else who read this in its evolving phases.

The text version is here if you have trouble reading parts of it, use reader software, want to translate or anything else. For any translator trying to recreate the look of the documents: soak in coffee for two minutes, making sure there's an even spread. Sprinkle some coffee grinds, then bake in the oven until the edges of the page curl up. It's time consuming, but boy is it worth it.

Thank you for reading :)

Images were created by:

ASCI header is by viperfishy

Joe Shuster's signiature is wikimedia commons.

All other image work done by Caro and I and scanned at Walgreens.

If we do not achieve the greatest underdog victory in history we would like the earliest available Series VI slot.

Whether you like it or not, history is on our side. We will bury you!
