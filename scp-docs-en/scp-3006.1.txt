Yay! Another contest entry!

I would like to thank minmin, WJS, Sirens, DrBleep, ARD, Silber, TyGently, Cyantreuse, and anyone else I am forgetting who helped me out with this entry. You guys rock.

This wasn't originally going to be what I was writing, but I was stuck and had this fresh idea that I think is better than what I was going for. Hope you guys enjoy it!

inb4 downvoted for memes

e: Also thanks a lot to Deci for fixing my SCP-3000 numbering 

+block

–block

So let's players have always captured my interest on a content production level because of how grinding the schedule must be. As someone who, by choice, ground out a shit-ton of content over a short period of time, I know what burnout feels like. So I wanted to capture that feeling through literally crushing creators spirits by the exponential expansion of their content. Once the contest is over I'd like to add some more examples to the test list, at least two or three more. The content creator being affected in an interview is also contributing to a subtext of the horror in being a public figure and having people create things you might find distasteful.

Whether you like it or not, history is on our side. We will bury you!
