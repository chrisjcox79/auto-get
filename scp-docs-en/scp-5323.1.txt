My first SCP. I fancy myself a screenwriter because dialogue is my strong suit. I chose X323 because 323 is an area code for the Los Angeles area—213 is the area code for Hollywood, but I was beaten to that number, so I'll take what I can get.

I won't lie and say I'm using PENTAGRAM because of any reason other than trying to avoid using the Chaos Insurgency; but it all works out. I'm quite fond of the idea that the occult branch of the DoD used the Red Scare in 1950s Hollywood as an excuse to abduct and experiment on low level blacklisted figures. If I get anymore good ideas, this may be the first of a  series.

Fresh

23 Apr 2020 00:26

I love this SCP.

You recognized your strength and twisted the medium to suit it. The infohazard at play was fresh and the ending helped recontextualize the whole piece. I particularly love the us of The Pentagram as an antagonistic force, very refreshing to see a lesser used GOI.

I do agree that most of Alan's personality is only interesting because of the almost noir touch to it. I would like to see him have a little bit agency in his story. It feels as if he's just being pushed around by one organization to the next. Alan is visually interesting but it's hard to connect his difference to his ability, it seems a little random given the context.

All in all, it feels fresh but needs a little bit of polish. You get my upvote.
