Fold

18 Jun 2015 10:05

I'mmmmmm tentatively upvoting, I suppose.

It's got three pretty simple narrative elements: 1) a document through which 2) an entity is screwing with the Foundation via 3) doomed neutralization ideas.

It's a simple narrative and most of the space is taken up by the proposals, but I think it works. (It's rare for me to enjoy someone writing hate notes to the Foundation, so that's an achievement.) Also, I think there's a good balance with the crosstesting, with you providing enough additional narrative that you aren't just riding their coat-tails.

The image and the lulz explanation nearly killed it for me before I got into the meat. I mean, makes sense to have an insultingly childish cover in context, but it still threw it off.

I have some more in-universe issues (For example, if the documents are Level 4 or higher (so O5) and they think the SCP details are accurate, who do they have reading this?), but I don't know of addressing them world help.
