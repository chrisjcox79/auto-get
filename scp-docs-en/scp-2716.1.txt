This is really bizarre. I can't quite wrap my head around a galactic scavenger hunt/carnival game. But it's really well written. The pacing is what makes it work, specifically. The description and containment procedure set up something really weird but it spends just the one addendum to make it work, and then wraps everything up very neatly with the message from O5-8. It's very trim, very breezy. It ramps up the weirdness, sticks the landing, and doesn't overstay its welcome.

I'm feeling a strong +1 on this one.
