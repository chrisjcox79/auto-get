Fold

06 Dec 2020 23:03

Hi Author, I've noticed a few typos.

The current will be worth as much as the gold making up the consumed 5083-1's body.
附加记录：    

Should  here be currency? This sentence also doesn't read well (in my opinion). I think it'd be better phrased as:

+block

–block

Individuals who have consumed portions of 5083-1 will receive a sum of currency

proportional to the quantity of gold they ingested.
附加记录：    
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

O5-11: (pauses) …Apolagies.
附加记录：    
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

This is a really imaginative SCP, but the main problem I have with it (and why ultimately I decided on a neutral vote) is the reaction of the Overseer Council. In the description you state:

Unless they have undergone at least Level 3 memetic agent resistance training, any

further people that see 5083-1 after initial manifestation will gain the same desires

to consume them.
附加记录：    

For me, I find it hard to believe that field agents and MTFs would have this memetic resistance training, but not the O5 Council. I know the O5s don't operate in the field, but they are, after all, the most important people within the Foundation, and not people you want compromised.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

O5-4 runs towards Alpha-1-5, twists his face into a cognitohazardous facial expression
附加记录：    

I'm not sure what this is meant to mean? Are you saying O5-4 is contorting his face in a way that makes it a cognitohazard?
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Ultimately, I was more interested in Operation Lifeboat than the skip itself. Moreover, I don't feel like Eunike 185 added anything to the story. In my opinion, if it was removed it it wouldn't change much (unless Operation Lifeboat and Eunike 185 are linked?)
