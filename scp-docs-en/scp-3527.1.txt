Re: Admin Post - Info - Open

06 May 2018 14:34

I had no idea that this skip (3994) had even existed prior to this admin post. I had simply thought of the idea because of my interest in the cliche extraterrestrial, being practical blobs of goo that try to be humans. I had simply combined that with the thought that they could be very incompetent, as we have not been invaded yet, and that could act as an explanation (as in why they have not succeeded yet).

As described in my author commentary on my author page, I was also listening to music and I wanted to incorporate that into my skip. Which combined with the incompetent thought and the music thought to form what if they just like our music, and that's why they haven't fully invaded as they want us to keep making more?

Coming from the author, this is entirely a coincidence, but I would be more than happy to change the concept if that is what's needed.
