It's a collection of ghost statues. They follow the movement of the earth, but matter moves through them without interaction. They're made of many kinds of (incorporeal) matter. They escaped from a global disaster in another universe. The same disaster may be in early stages here. And they're Russian.

It's nicely written, and atmospheric, but there's no implied larger canvas than the one given. Why are they statues? Why are they intangible? Why do they follow the earth? There's no answers, just mystery.
