Ok, some parts of this are like, really cool, but I feel that there was a much better way to show a lot of this stuff rather than tell it.

Personally, I love the idea of a society being unable to think past the present. However, I think that idea in particular would be much stronger if you like say1 could have been left more vague, and not stated as explicitly because I feel that takes a lot of the mystery out of the piece.

Like, all around this feels like a clever idea, but I just feel like the delivery of the cleverness could've allowed you to demonstrate the horror of SCP-11711 better, because I feel like that horror would have added a lot more to the piece to take it beyond a cool time-travel idea.
