Thank you very much!

SpoilersAnswers

-Close

The wave and frequency mentions are meant to hint at SCP-4940 being psionic in nature, allowing it to accomplish the anomalous activities it does.
The info on SCP-4940-1 is classified to hide SCP-4940's latent personality and full sapience, for morale reasons, as well as the fact that it is a recent discovery, which raises more questions than what lower-level researchers should know about the company the object was found from.
It is not false. The information is included due to researchers presumably conducting tests allowing SCP-4940 to fully develop. It's implantation into a brain is an alternative route to it's development.

I think there's an interesting story here, but it's stretched too thinly over the prose. It's clear that you put a great deal of work and love put into this, but I think it may have stayed in the proverbial oven too long and gotten overwritten. You've got some solid concepts here, and they'd be carry a lot more punch if written in more concise terms.
