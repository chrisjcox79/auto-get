I didn’t know SCP-4469 and only after your mention I read this article. Maybe at first sight can look similar, because both objects are abnormally durable, but the mechanism associated is very different; in the case of the toy it cause a weakening of intermolecular forces of objects around it, which increase the fragility of this objects, while the effect associated to the rose is a “deviation of entropy”, impeding that the object to deteriorate or withering but increasing in return the entropic decay in the objects and organisms around it (i.e. the “aging” of these)
