SCP-2873: Another Human Weapon

19 Oct 2017 04:37

Companion piece for SCP-3033. Why strip people of their autonomy to make them weapons when you can grant weapons free will?

Thanks to GreenWolf, Decibelle, ARD, Dexanote, fieldstone, and everyone else that took a look at this in its drafting period.

I have my doubts on whether or not Anderson Robotics entering the field of guided missiles is in line with the GoI, but I'm willing to overlook that on the sheer strength of the morbid humor of the piece. The tragedy of indoctrinating what are essentially preteens into becoming suicide bombers and their subsequent brief flashes of insight before death goes so far that it wraps all the way around from deep tragedy to wicked comedy. It doesn't deliver any meaningful political insights, but it doesn't need to.
