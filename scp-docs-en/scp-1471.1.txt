I want to emphasize this, even though Anaxagoras mentioned it in passing: This SCP works in part because of what it does NOT do. It doesn't crazy people to death (unless you decide in your head that it does), and SCP-1471-A does not jump out at the end and [REDACTED] people. It relies on the creepy picture and the idea that you're going to be seeing this thing, and have it be part of your life.

I completely expected some obvious cliched thing to happen at the end, and happily upvoted when it didn't. Good lesson for new writers.
