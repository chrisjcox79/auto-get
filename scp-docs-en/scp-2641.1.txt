almost feels like a rite of passage

12 Aug 2018 21:53

Commentary/Info

–block

My obligatory fungal/contagion and tale/SCP article, cast from an admittedly heavily and dense medical perspective, which I understand is a strength and a weakness that I own, but that I also try to ride the margins of. I understand the CSS theme, the offsets, the technicality of the work will be a turn off for many. Your viewpoints are justified and understood. I am who I am however and works are those in pockets of severely technical niche-work, and I am accepting of the relative unconcern that comes with it.

In the hands of an author who isn’t a pathological provocateur, this would have been a normal SCP and an offset of separately-published tales.
