The God you believe in is not only not good.

That God is cruel.

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

In 9th grade, I watched a documentary about the Russian Revolution, which ended on what was clearly meant to be a feel-good note with the Russian Orthodox Church canonizing Nicholas II. I'm thankful my ancestors, refugees from the Tsar's cruelty, weren't around to see such a thing.

Thanks to JackalRelated for the crit.

Part three of Christ In Scarlet.
