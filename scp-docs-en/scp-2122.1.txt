So re-reading it I think I (kind) of get it.

Spoilers?

–block

So this is an interrogation room where the interrogator can do anything, without consequences (since at the end both persons return to the original state), as long as there is no accountability (this must be the point of the different sentence between test A-8 and A-9). But how reliable is the description? Since the anomalous properties only manifest with two people, and the only way to know what happened is with those people's testimonies, there's actually basically no information detailing what happened; and if the exposure to 2122-1-D results in mental alterations, it can also very well alter the interrogatee so as to not reveal it either. And I guess incident 7 shows that the physical alterations make them super-strong or something similar.

Hm, exposure to 2122-1-D makes for extremely strong and obedient people I guess the implication of attachment S9 is that the Foundation is making some super soldiers? 2122 would not only (or maybe even mainly) be an interrogation chamber but an indoctrination one.
