Thanks to daveyoufool and spookman (don't know your wiki account) for feedback.

+Commentary

-Commentary

This one's been in the works for a while. It is two things.

First, it's a tribute to the music of Zedd, most especially Clarity thrown in at the end. I've written fanfiction based on music before and wanted to see if I could apply the same technique to SCPs. Time will tell if it's successful.

Secondly, this is a reaction and do-it-better of one of my biggest pet peeves: dream skips. I absolutely detest dream SCPs because there's no way to independently verify what happens to test subjects in them. So here we have something that plays the dreams for you instead of the sleeper having to remember them.

As for the story behind this, I think I'll keep it close to my chest and let the article speak for itself.

EDIT: Also this is now SCP-2676, because that's apparently a thing you can do and I'm mega-OCD.
