05 Apr 2018 06:36

So I enjoy the talk show transcript. It really makes your SCP seem more personable and interesting. I do think you need to rework the SCP -1 and it's second stage instances as I felt kind of confused there. Do SCP-1 instances teleport to the studio to view the show live? Or do they simply stay at home and get effected? I think if you had them in their second stage still being intelligent, and proclaiming the benefits of the show, that would be better. Perhaps you can have some incidents of Foundation Personnel shutting down radio broadcasts and Internet webpages, created by these instances to encourage people to watch?

Maybe have only 'live' shows cause the anomalous effects to humans, recorded episodes could lack the anomalous effects. Hinting that its something in particular about the live show, that affects people? Like hearing Tyal'k'nec is the trigger, but whatever that is, gets lost or scrambled in recordings? I'd also suggest changing SCP-3926-1 in stage 2, to a new designation, maybe SCP-3926-2? And lastly I found it a little strange that Amnestic's aren't used on people. Would they be effective at curing or reversing the effects of 3926? Would they work on Stage 2 instances?

I've given you a +1 vote for now. I'm looking forward to you expanding on this SCP more, I like it.
