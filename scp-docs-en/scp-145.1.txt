That still doesn't make her .

• The phone may be her only hope at contacting the outside world; you don't know what's on the other side.

• She probably doesn't know/remember how she got there or any of the other details. Or she might be giving them that information in her attempts to plead for help; we weren't given the full details of what she's saying.

They do say they attempt to track the call and it isn't possible.

I'm going to go ahead and NOT ASSUME that she's stupid. If she would deliberately minimize her chances of survival, yeah she'd be stupid. But there's a lot of mystery to this article, and it's a very strange to assume without sufficient evidence that someone is purposely withholding information that may free them from something like this.
