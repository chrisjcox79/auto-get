Fold

01 Jun 2012 20:40

The difficulty there is that the newborn baby has to be born at pretty much the exact same time as the current host dies, and that would be pretty complicated for the Foundation to completely control - we'd have to keep ready-to-pop women hostage constantly and regulate air/naval traffic through wherever the isolated containment region would be. That's what I was alluding to at the end with the request from Dr. Samesh - he'd like to implement something like that, but the Ethics Committee isn't quite comfortable with the logistics of putting it into operation.

We don't know what happens if there's no newborn available at time of death - maybe it dies permanently, maybe it takes over an adult body, maybe something even worse - and considering how dangerous this person can be outside containment, we don't want to find out.
