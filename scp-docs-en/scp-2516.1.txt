I initially downvoted the first revision of this article, but have changed my vote to an upvote. It has a few problems (like why Taured itself isn't considered anomalous), but I found myself feeling so very fucking sorry for someone as lost as -1. From his point of view, this is the fucked up anomalous world. And yes, it's been explored in other articles, and this may not be the most perfect execution, but if you make me feel sorry and I want to keep reading, you get an upvote.

One thing though:

The passport contains stamps from airports located in Paris, Taured, Tokyo, The United States of America, and Germany, these stamps match the ones used by the associated country.
附加记录：    

This sentence sounds off, since Taured isn't recognized as a country and thus doesn't have a stamp recognized in this reality construct to match it against. Maybe change to:

The passport contains stamps from airports located in Paris, Tokyo, The United States of America, and Germany, matching the ones used by the associated country. It also contains a stamp for the Taured National Airport.
