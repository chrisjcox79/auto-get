Yeah, I was afraid of UTD comparisons, but I don't think it's all that similar. Under the Dome takes place in southern Maine, while this is in northern Maine. This happened because of a fuckup with an interdimensional portal, while UTD is Lovecraftian children treating people like toys. The Dome in UTD is like the Dome in the Simpsons Movie, while this is like an intangible semi-permeable membrane. Plus, the people in UTD didn't suffocate instantly.

Fun fact: the only Stephen King stories I've read are Under the Dome and The Stand, although I've seen the film adaptation of The Mist. All other resemblance is coincidence.
