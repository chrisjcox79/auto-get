This is an interesting event, but (1) I don't really understand how the translation to English occurred, (2) given the translation, the Foundation doesn't seem to have updated its description to reflect information from the contents of the transmission (which is convenient for narrative purposes but improbable in-world).

One solution to (2) might be to write up the SCP/description for the Keter anomaly, then present the translated transmission, and then present an updated and revised SCP/description that reclassifies the anomaly as Neutralized. Note that the revised SCP need not take the veracity of the transmission's contents for granted, but they should at least be noted.
