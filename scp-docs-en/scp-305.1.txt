27 Mar 2010 15:47

I like this concept a lot, and can't help but wonder if the spacetime anomaly is artificial, and actually intended to keep 305 contained. FullHazard's idea to change the designations (305 for the anomaly, 305-1 for the entity inside, 305-2 for its spawn) should definitely be implemented, for consistency with other SCPs such as SCP-076. Other than that, I'd downgrade it to Euclid (it'd be Safe if it weren't for the possibility of escaped child entities on the loose) and trim the article, particularly in those containment procedures. Parts of the containment procedure really belong in addenda, and others are just excessive amounts of detail. Remember, this is supposed to be a quick-reference document for someone who may need to figure out what to do about this SCP in a life-threatening hurry - the most necessary information goes first, and the least immediately-useful stuff comes last (such as in addenda).
