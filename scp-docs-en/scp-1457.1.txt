I think there's a difference. Also I like this a lot, and I like it pretty much purely for the journal.

The telepathic spider was about a spider getting wigged out when it was talking to the equivalent of eldritch beings from beyond the veil (and said beings actually sympathizing with the spider on account of also dealing with things that are equally beyond their own ability to understand).
