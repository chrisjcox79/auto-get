05 Feb 2013 21:01

Problem:  There is a potential error in this SCP's terminology.

Explanation:  You mention, in the 'Stage 3' section that 'infected subjects display extreme hyperphagia'. The term I believe you intended to use is 'Polyphagia'. Both are conditions characterised by excessive hunger, however Hyperphagia is a subset of Polyphagia believed to occur due to damage or lesion on the Hyperthalmus, whereas Polyphagia is a more generalised term usually caused by metabolic pathobiologies. Unless the virus does indeed cause brain damage, in which case I recant this statement.

Additional:  I enjoyed this article.
