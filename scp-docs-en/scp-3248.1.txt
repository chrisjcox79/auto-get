Timer Expired. Edits made as discussed below. The author may revert at any time.

+block

–block

Rereading this article for Wikiwalk, I was reminded of SCP-453, in that we have social gatherings on alternating loops. It would not be unreasonable for the SCPF to look into these as potential deviations of the same phenomenon. As such, I am suggesting the following:

Editing this:

All SCP-3248-1 events have been nearly identical, save for the occurrence of an SCP-3248-2 event. At some point during the festivities, one of the partygoers will murder Dr. Sullivan. The method of murder and the partygoer that commits the murder has differed for every SCP-3248-2 event. The partygoers will react with shock, and will attempt to call authorities or, in some cases, attempt to leave. The SCP-3248-1 event will always end at midnight.
