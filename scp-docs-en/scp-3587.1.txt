Tentative +1!

FEEDBACK

–block

Initially, I was pretty set on a no-vote; I both 1) expected cannibalism, and 2) expected that to be the end of it. I was curious when the antler'd 3587-2 showed up, and pleased when she murdered everyone who constructed the radio. I was even more pleased by that final entry, which took things in a completely different  direction.

That being said, I described this as a tentative upvote for a reason! There's a lot of places I feel like this skip could be tightened up  shorter skips need to be as clean as you can make them, there's a a lot of dangling threads here that you could snip (probably after the contest). Some examples:

SCP-3587 is a deserted tropical island, located 53 kilometers off the coast of ███ █████ and referred to by locals as the , due to the dangerous environmental conditions present there.
附加记录：    

This reads better with fewer clauses:

SCP-3587 is a deserted tropical island located 53 kilometers off the coast of ███ █████. Due to the dangerous environmental conditions it presents, locals refer to it as .
附加记录：    

SCP-3587-1 is the collective designation for (at the time of writing) two hundred and sixty three instances of Lionel Hodgeson, a fisherman who became stranded on SCP-3587 in 2014 following a severe thunderstorm in the area.
附加记录：    

That parenthetical pops out of no where; it works better at the end:

SCP-3587-1 is the collective designation for Lionel Hodgeson, a fisherman who was lost at sea during a severe thunderstorm in 2014. At the time of this writing, there are two hundred and sixty three instances of SCP-3587-1.
附加记录：    

It is currently unknown how Hodgeson was able to enter SCP-3587 without being repelled.
附加记录：    

It is currently unknown how Hodgeson arrived on SCP-3587.
附加记录：    

So on, so on. Oh, one more big one, because it's at the end:

SCP-3587-1 instances display no visible signs of sapience, instead crawling on all fours and consuming prey dragged to the beach by SCP-3587-2 in an animalistic manner. Body language suggests SCP-3587-2 is pleased by this development.
附加记录：    

You definitely don't need to tell us 'in an animalistic manner'  the fact that he's displaying no signs of sapience and crawling on all fours tells us everything we need to know. IE:

Remaining SCP-3587-1 instances no longer display signs of sapience. They crawl on all fours and consume prey dragged to the beach by SCP-3587-2. Body language suggests SCP-3587-2 is pleased by this development.
附加记录：    

I'd also seriously consider cutting that final line (about 'body language'), because I think it's 1) implied, and 2) works better through implication.

One more thing: I don't think you need an invisible barrier. Invisible barriers always strike me as bad writing; they're obviously magical. Instead, use what you've got: 'due to the dangerous environmental conditions'  anyone who tries to approach the beach strikes the rocks surrounding it and drowns. Anyone who tries to fly in gets blown away, hits the rocks, and drowns. Etc.

Anyway, that's it; I was pleasantly surprised by the ending of this, and enjoyed it quite a lot!

Good luck with the contest!
