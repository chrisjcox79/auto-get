But your picture on my wall, it reminds me...

12 May 2019 13:17

After 5 Months of work and absence and work on this, it is finally done.

Big thanks for conceptual and writing critique and help goes to: TheMightyMcB

(Phew what a list, hope I got everyone. Could not have made it without you guys!)

And special thanks  to Lord_of_Laugh for photoshopping the image of the kitchen. (Minor edits by me.)

I also want to thank SCP Café Podcast Episode 20!

The idea for this came to me on the day after new years eve, laying in bed in a friends house.

I looked up at their wall full of travel photographs and thought to myself  And that was that. Feels good to have my first article be my favourite kind: A thing what does a thing, that hopefully works!

This will probably not be the last you will read of me! Hope you enjoyed!
