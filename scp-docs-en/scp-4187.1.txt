Here In Mother Russia, Burger Eat You!

05 Jun 2019 19:43

Why are we still here? Just to suffer? Every night, I can hear it eating. The Whoppers. The nuggets. even The fries. The meat I've lost. The comrades I've lost. It's like they're all being cooked. You can hear it too, can't you?

What if you were the burger? Another little SCP, number buddying with the rest. Thanks to RadioactiveRADS, FloppyPhoenix, Rura, Henzoid, naepicfael, and 17 Incorporeal Brooms for critting.

Catch ya in the deep fryer.
