03 Mar 2014 19:27

I like the idea, but as other have pointed out there are noticeable flaws in its construction. The two biggest ones, for me, had to be Addendum's -B and -C. The interview comes off beneath Foundation standards. Assuming this is the first interview conducted with PoI-2015-0102 (why 0102, by the way? Shouldn't that correspond with the SCP-2015 variant he created?), it felt like the doctor wasn't giving the interviewee's answers any thought. Sure, he asked about the rumored extra-dimensional beings, but that felt like he was doing it out of obligation and not to deepen the Foundation's understanding of the SCPs, their creators, or the competition. I don't know if I'm conveying my feelings on it adequately, but I hope you understand my point.

Now as for the audio transcript, well Interesting at all, to be honest. My advice would be to scrap the audio log in its entirety.
