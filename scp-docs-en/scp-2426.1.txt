I liked this one a lot. I thought that the crazy luxury items were clever, the weirdly-similar but not quite employees were a nice touch, and overall it was something kind of unusual while still fitting into the mainlist landscape. I was all set to upvote.

Then I hit the logs.

The first one was sort of irritating, but I stayed my urge to downvote; it did convey the whole information-gathering side of the skip, so it did have a reason to be there. I didn't care for the  tone of the person being interviewed, but I suppose there are people who do talk like that, though it all still came off as fairly unnatural.

The second one, as people above have said, just kills this. Silly pulp magazine time traveling gangsters with a tendency to reveal all of the stuff they're doing in tidy, concise conversations. Any mystery that the skip had was neatly excised and replaced with a cheeseball future. It wrecked the reading experience to the point where I had to downvote.
