Pleased to Meat You

20 Mar 2018 04:46

This is a historic moment. To my knowledge, this is the first direct Xmas gifter-giftee collab! More personally, it's my first exploration and my longest work ever (for now!)

Our story begins on a cold December morning. DarkStuff had been selected as my gift recipient for the Christmas Exchange. I was moved by the selflessness of his request and wrote him a little something. He was very happy to receive it but did note many areas that could be improved. So we agreed to a collab, that my idea and his skills could be combined to make this work what it was always destined to be.

Fast forward a few months. After both tying up loose ends, we sat down and hammered out this draft together. I had a blast working with Stuff for this, and I hope you have as much fun reading as we did writing.

Big thanks to CadaverCommander for crit.

Do enjoy your visit.
