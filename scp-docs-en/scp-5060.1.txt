Well, here we are. Another X000 Contest on our doorstep, and another attempt to swing for the fences. As anyone who read Portland Vice could probably tell you, mystery is not my strong suit. So here I wanted to come up with something that got in, presented a mystery, and got out without too much time to bog itself down with chaff and other distractions. There will be plenty of novella-length entries posted, so I wanted to keep this one brief.
