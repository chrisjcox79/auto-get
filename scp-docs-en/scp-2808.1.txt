While I know that 2808 is incomprehensible even in-universe, I find it hard to attach myself to an SCP if it is defined (even as a concept).
附加记录：    

I suppose that's a risk I take, but I decided that withholding the information would be infinitely more interesting than any definite thing I put in there. I also wanted to demonstrate, rather than simply state, that 2808 is committed to posing an impediment to smooth bureaucratic functioning; I figured that refusing to pin it down to any conceivable form would do the trick.
