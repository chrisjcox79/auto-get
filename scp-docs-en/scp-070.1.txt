Ideally, I think this was intended as a direct subversion of Xman type SCPs. The guy has metal wings. That sounds awesome. But… he can't fly, they can't be removed, and they trap him by anchoring him to concrete.

I see the idea.

But it's written in such a way that the blatant badfic potential is highlighted here, and I think that's part of the problem. The whole bit about the wings reacting to his mood, or blocking bullets? Yeh. Giving the wings an obvious benefit ruins the type subversion.

I almost like this SCP, and I'm glad it's up for edit, because with a few changes it could be a solid entry.
