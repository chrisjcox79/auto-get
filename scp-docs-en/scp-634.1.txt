Fold

07 Sep 2012 06:14

I like it. First of all, I usually dislike mental effects, but this is how it should be, a single, unique effect, and other behaviors just result from it. It doesn't mysteriously compel people to put their hand in its bowl, or repeat the same task over and over, just makes them forget what they were doing.

I also like the teeth thing, it changes it from a-normal-thing-with-a-strange-power to a-logically-consistent-weird-entity. A normal goldfish wouldn't gain anything from removing a person's short term memory, but it's this things primary mode of sustenance. It shows you thought things through to their logical conclusions.

Also note that SCP-634 does not influence a person's or animal's natural instincts or wants, only affecting their memory of the current context of a situation. Because of this, SCP-634 would be unlikely to stop a rampage of an SCP which has a general inclination towards violence and a dislike of people, as those behaviors would continue regardless of context.
