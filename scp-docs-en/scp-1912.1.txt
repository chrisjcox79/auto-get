The first thing I do not understand, is why an active colony must be kept around in the first place. The SCP is the watch, not the colony itself. It would be much better to just kill all instances of SCP-1912-1 and just have SCP-1912 in some standard containment locker, which would make it  If there was no instances of SCP-1912-1, then they could not try to spread the nectar or whatever around to infect more people. The Foundation has no reason to keep any instances of SCP-1912-1, since they could just produce more if they felt the need. This would save a lot of expenses and D-Class as well.

The second thing is that it is very hard to believe that the researchers would allow the honey to be consumed by anyone after a few D-Class said it was good. (Like an above poster mentioned) I believe you were trying to pull off something like SCP-884, but it just did not work and felt more added on than anything.
