Seconding this. The end kinda comes out of left field. It's not built towards particularly well. I would pull back on some of the inane chatter which is consuming the bulk of the logs and try to interject some more direction or development for cola, because it's nit quite as focused as some of the other GAW interactions I've seen. It's a fine line between silly bs and doing things like revealing character and progressing story in chat logs, but I think it's tipping too far into trying to appear like a chat room discussion - so that those elements to make it a satisfying story have been sacrificed somewhat.
