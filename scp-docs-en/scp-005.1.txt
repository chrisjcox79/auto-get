15 Dec 2011 00:42

I like this one, too. I agree with those who say that not every SCP needs to be horrific or dangerous.

But it could do with some more detail. It raises various intriguing questions in my mind about what exactly happens physically when you use it. For mechanical locks, I can imagine it morphing to become the right shape to open the lock. But what about electronic ones? For a keypad, maybe it grows fingers that push the buttons in the right order. What about card-swipes? Fingerprint readers? Card swipes?

An experiment log detailing the effects on various types of locks would be interesting. The Foundation has surely conducted such experiments and recorded the results somewhere.
