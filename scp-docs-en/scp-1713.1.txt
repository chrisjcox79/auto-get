Levels nine and ten are storage for raw ores and materials. Automated carts and arms can be seen transporting materials taken from the surface and transporting them to the elevators for melting down at the upper levels.
附加记录：    

Wait, it takes supplies from the surface? In that case, wouldn't the containment procedure need to have a bit about preventing robots from getting out to scavenge for supplies? And wouldn't the factory have responded to having its supplies cut off?

Materials that have been located at this level include coal, iron ore, bauxite, silicon dioxide, copper ore, and unprocessed gemstones.
附加记录：    

This suggests that the factory is surrounded by mining tunnels, from which it mines all of its supplies. If that's so, then the containment procedure would need to include something about preventing anyone from discovering these mining tunnels, like buying up all the mining rights to the surrounding areas so no one else tries to mine there.

Fold

25 Apr 2012 06:07

A lot of work obviously went into this. It's not bad, but it needs a little polishing and I have a few questions.

-As noted, it's not very clear where the factory is getting its raw materials from - if they're mining it on their own it needs to be mentioned that the Foundation is trying to locate the source, and if the Foundation is providing it with raw material, they should probably stop.

 doesn't sound like the kind of term that belongs in an article, since the way these things are described doesn't really resemble a difference engine.

Pipes filled with a water-like fluid
附加记录：    

Either say  is imprecise and doesn't give the reader any useful information.

The radiation dosage at the core icosahedral structure has been measured in excess of 50 sieverts/hour and personnel are not to remain on this level for any longer than two hours - radiation dosimeter tags must be worn at all times to keep track of exposure time.
附加记录：    

8 sieverts is a guaranteed lethal dose. Forget two hours, they'll be lucky to last 10 minutes down there.

Objects include toys, weapons, personal vehicles, animals, implants, and, in one case, an artificial clockwork human head that could [REDACTED] . A wind-up key was located at the base of the skull. When the key was turned, the head began to speak, mentioning items such as how to create an effective lubricant out of human semen, safely dismantling the flytrap, and how to create a dessert cake from [DATA EXPUNGED].
附加记录：    

Why redact what the head is capable of doing, and then tell us what it's capable of doing? Also, an artificial intelligence that talks about how to make cake from unspeakable ingredients? It's been done.

Overall, not a bad entry, but it needs work. No vote for now.
