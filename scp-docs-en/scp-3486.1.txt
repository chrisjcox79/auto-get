22 Jun 2018 05:42

I remember when you first showed me this in our exchange. The idea was so unique yet so utterly horrifying that I was left speechless and quite frankly distraught, something of a rarity when reading drafts.

Now?

I'm fucking terrified.

A combination of Victorian horror and that god-awful surgery log combine in this article to form something straight out of the nightmares of a serial killer. The nice little endnote that they were all products of a woman trying to recreate her son? Fuck that. I'm not even considering closing my eyes in fear of seeing these horrendous abominations reattach plastic faces onto themselves.

Solid writing, a fantastic combination of styles and plot points, and just plain disgust really churned out a beautiful piece of work. Well done!
