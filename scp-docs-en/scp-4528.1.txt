Ok but the title explains all but im going to explain how much I love this article in more detail anyway.

Going through the  innocent or too unassuming. It looks like something I would wear everyday!

But plot twist! It actually has the ability to make things flammable and to perhaps set things on fire! That within itself isn't very strong, but there's this other element to it which ties to cults, and how its anomalous properties are only triggered when the wearer is a 7+ women

The article explores themes of womanhood and femininity and the, as Simone de Beauvoir puts it (I paraphrase)  and how it relates to society, religion, and cults. Maybe I'm looking too much into this article, maybe this isn't what you intended to convey with this piece of writing, and maybe you didn't expect a reference to political philosophies in the comment section of your writing.
