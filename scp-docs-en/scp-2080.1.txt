20 Oct 2014 04:45

I like this a lot: it's bizarre and creepy and tragic all at once. The reason for the argument between 2080-1 and his son is a bit heavy-handed but I guess it works. Honestly, you could just say they had an argument that was more heated than normal, that would have been enough.

Something that really struck me about the snippets in Document 2080-02a was the implication that dad was often too busy for his kid, promising to be there but not fulfilling those promises. These naturally led me to think about all times I'd wished there were two of me, to fulfil obligations and responsibilities fully. Likewise, there are moments when I've made a decision and realized I wished I could see how things would have gone for me on the opposite side of that decision - I imagine a huge fight with a kid would be one of those times, but it seems seeing the alternative play out is not working out too well for Mr. Herschel
