Just a few Technical FAQ!

Q.  Why can I just skip the whole survey and not put anything in?

A.  While I wanted verisimilitude to be really strong in this, I also did not want to needlessly irritate those that may be re-reading the SCP or accidentally refresh. As such, you are not required to fill in any of the entries unless you want to. The Foundation may be keeping track, though ;]

Q.  On offset/3 or offset/4, why does the  always give me an X?

A.  These are not the intended way to get to the next  so to speak. I did not give people the ability to move to the next part that way as you would miss out on parts of the SCP if you did so.

For offset/3, you need to get to the end of the Survey, press  and wait for the link that brings you to the next part.

For offset/4, you need to click the right arrow until you get to the final page. It will appear to glitch and the final link will, again, appear for you to click.

Q.  [Insert technical issue here]], can you fix it?

A.  I'd love to! Just make sure to give me some information so I can do so. I need Device you are browsing on (e.g. Samsung Galaxy S5/iPhone 8/Windows PC/etc.), as well as the Browser and Version you are using (e.g. Firefox 68.0.2/Chrome 76.0.3809.132/etc.).  For mobile devices, the phone version and the browser you are using should be enough.

Thanks guys! I hope I can get this to be a good experience for everyone. :]
