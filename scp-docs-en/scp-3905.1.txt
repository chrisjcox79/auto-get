This was a lot of fun  it skirts really close to -J material, but a few lines in it were too great for me not to ultimately decide to upvote.

FEEDBACK

–block

In all other instances where trash is deposited, SCP-3905 behaves identically to a non-anomalous trash can.
附加记录：    

This is a pretty great line (because it's both hilarious and something I can legitiately imagine the Foundation sincerely writing).

SCP-3905-B instances display signs of sapience
附加记录：    

you actually used the right term, too (as opposed to 'sentience'). O_O

There's a few spots here I think you can tighten up (probably after the contest) that I wanted to mention; feel free to take any of it or leave any of it  I think this skip is pretty great as is.

SCP-3905-B instances can perform many actions that would normally require arms, legs, or a greater height, such as limboing.
附加记录：    

Given that this is a list of anomalous properties exhibited by SCP-3905-B instances, I think you can just cut all this out and say this is what they can (HA!) do.

(e.g. “Feeling Hot Hot Hot” by The Merrymen, “Let’s Get It Started” by the Black Eyed Peas, etc)
附加记录：    

That 'etc' feels a little unclinical to me; I think the following would be just slightly stronger:

(Examples include  by the Black Eyed Peas).
附加记录：    

On the western end of SCP-3905-A, a plastic tube protruding from the ground and labeled “THE SWEET STUFF” continuously produces a substance believed by SCP-3905-A researchers to be alcohol.
附加记录：    

I think you can take out the crossed out portion, because really  who other than the researchers would be stating this belief?

SCP-3905-B instances apparently violate the laws of space-time while consuming the alcohol, as they have been observed to repeatedly consume far more than would be able to fit into a 355ml can without spilling any contents out.
附加记录：    

I feel like this can be made much more simple and clinical (also, you started the previous sentence with SCP-3905-B, so I'd avoid it in the next sentence):

These instances have been observed consuming volumes of liquid far greater than what a non-anomalous 355ml aluminum can would allow.
附加记录：    

This area is frequented less often than the western one, and SCP-3905-B instances who consume the contents of the tube expressing displeasure.
附加记录：    

SCP-3905-forcing the contents of the eastern plastic tube into them.
附加记录：    

Okay, that's a lot of edits there I know. Not all of that is super important, but 'involuntarily' seems like the wrong word, and there definitely needs to be an 'on' in front of 'based'.

These soda cans are inert and do not display the same anomalous properties that SCP-3905-B instances do. To date, no diet or sugar-free cans have become instances of SCP-3905-B.
附加记录：    

I feel like you can get this across much more simply:

These aluminum cans appear to be non-anomalous.
附加记录：    

Like, you don't need that last line  it's pretty strongly implied, and I feel like 'non-anomalous' is a good word to end on.

I really liked this  they're just things that I think would significantly tighten it.

Good luck with the contest!
