Are you afraid of god? You shouldn't be.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Another entry into the collection. This piece was made for the -ES wiki's I57 canon, and carries the BHL port of the canon theme. It also fits into my personal collection of series unnamed. Thanks to everyone who put up with this draft  there's so many of you I don't remember all the names, but a special thanks to Almarduk for harassing me into finishing this, DC_Yerko for making the canon this is for and giving me his blessing to write it and the theme, and Kirby and ARD for working through the ending with me until it wasn't garbage.
