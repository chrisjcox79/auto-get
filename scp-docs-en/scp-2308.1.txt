08 Apr 2016 16:44

This makes my head hurt. +1.

Fridge logic time (inevitable with any sort of time-travel). So, looking at this chronologically according to the base timeline:

In 2007, they were producing the 2007 vehicles and sending them back in time to 2006, while receiving the 2008 vehicles and selling them.

But then in 2008, they wouldn't have received any 2009 vehicles (since they didn't / wouldn't sell in 2008). Without the new inventory to sell, there's no revenue with which to manufacture the new inventory they didn't receive.

They use the revenue from the sale of the 2008 vehicles to produce the 2008 vehicles anyway, in order to prevent a time-paradox. But they know that once they finish, they will be bankrupt.

So, I'm not understanding why or how they produced the 2009 vehicles. They just never would have received them from the future, and thus would have known not to produce them (and not had the money to do so even if they wanted to, since they hadn't had any to sell).
