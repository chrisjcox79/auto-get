This one's pretty odd. A lot of people are complaining about how we're throwing D-Class into a meaty garbage disposal, but I think something is being missed:

-The Ethics Committee has prevented anyone from knowing what's going on down there for Some reason.

-The D-Class are intentionally given corrodable/biodegradable materials. Because We want the SCP to not get a tummy-ache?

-As you said, very inefficient methods are being used on this thing. However, as the rest of the article seems quite well-thought-out, this would seem to be by design.

-Since the Foundation has undertaken containment and started feeding D-Class to the SCP, the meaty-sheety thing has been growing in complexity by adding neural, sensory and structural (bone) tissue.

The Foundation's up to something here. This is one of those SCPs where it's obvious that the reader doesn't have O5 access, and is almost certainly being lied to by those more in the know. This article just doesn't shove it in your face like most do, which is refreshing, but it seems like most people need more of a shove in the right direction.

I'm not certain what the underlying secret of why the Foundation is actually feeding people to this thing is (or allowing them to be integrated into it, possibly still conscious afterward), and I think just a couple of textual hints in that direction would've put this article over the top for me. However, as it is, it's still a +1.
