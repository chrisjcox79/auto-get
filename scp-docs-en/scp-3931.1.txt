02 Sep 2019 10:29

Please don't make fake links. I personally don't consider wikidot markup to be a canon element, but even if you do, I read on mobile. They literally hurt my fingers.

Also I felt like this had an excessive amount of specific detail and was pretty short on story. All of the exact meters and factions of seconds and extensive containment procedures just left me impatient for the other shoe to drop, but it never did.

It's good that you gave us characters to empathize with. The scout leader's story was a great exercise in making me feel empathy for someone within the format of a report, and the expansion to staff was interesting, but all in all this feels more like a specific tone or feeling than a story. Sometimes that's all it takes, but here, for me, it wasn't enough.

Also I don't blame you if you hadn't read SCP-1008, but it is one of my favorite early SCPs, and it's also shorter than this.

No vote.
