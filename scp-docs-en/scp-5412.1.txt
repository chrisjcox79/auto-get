This was wild and I liked it a lot.

There were two mild issues I had. The idea of three drunk mates holding onto this ritual for nine minutes is a bit of a stretch for me, but it's really .

I found the body horror with the leg being severed off to be shocking and hilarious and awesome. But I found the bit about the dude being destroyed through his genitals to come off as sort of gratuitous.

Really good work though all the same. Dives in, gets out, justifies its existence plenty. There are a lot of logistical problems posed by this SCP that I appreciate you did not delve to deeply into solving. +1
