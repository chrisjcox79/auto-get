07 Apr 2020 17:59

Hey this scip needs more attention, it's real nice. I'd say my upvote was earned when it's shown the guy doesn't want to just chase you off the premises, but will literally follow you anywhere at slightly above walking pace to lecture you on trespassing. On that note, the incident log is great. I can only imagine all the guards being so confounded and worried as they fire shot after shot into this guy and he keeps strolling along, prying open security doors with an umbrella. On top of that, there's some nice mystery fleshed out over several addenda, really wondering what might be going on there, although not as much is suggested as I would've liked. Still good though.

The Liverpudlian bloke I especially like. Don't know what he is, but I love the way he speaks. He/It only cares about you tresspassing, and damn you sir for not heeding the signs. I wonder if he's really a person, or just a protection measure of some kind; the final log indicates some kind of intelligence. In fact, I'd like to see more test logs where they literally just try to talk to the guy and ask him things, point out there's no tresspassing sign, reason stuff out, see if that's possible. Or if multiple people go in at once, with someone to distract him. I wonder what he'd do if you got in a helicopter, though maybe that's pushing it.
