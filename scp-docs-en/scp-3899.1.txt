17 Jun 2017 18:24

First off, I want to emphasize just how much I love the premise of this. An eldritch entity masquerading as a demonic a-dimensional Mad Max impersonator is just excellent and the way you characterize it in the interview - as somewhat crazed, verging on manically nihilistic - is excellent and allows for an off-kilter feel to pervade the piece, even as it attempts to rectify its 'mistake' with Martha. I can't help but feel that this is unfinished, in that it cuts off abruptly and its ultimate purpose, the memetic effect and what it actually is all remain unresolved. However, I believe that's also to the article's credit: it's an effective example of a work-in-progress, of an item under steady research that is sure to progress in scope but in the meanwhile is a mildly disturbing, decidedly amusing automobile oddity.

+1 for riding shiny and chrome

some extras

17 Jun 2017 20:07

if anyone wants a little amplifying information, i'll spoilerize some tidbits for the curious

+spoilahz

you'vespoiled

SCP-3899 is a very old god. Not as old as some others, and definitely not as powerful, but still a god. It's a manifestation of freedom, perpetuity of motion, and unstoppability. A long time ago, it was the first stray particle. Then it was an asteroid. Then a comet. Then a landslide, a rogue wave, a charioteer, a taxi driver, and now a trucker, among others, whenever it has chosen a physical form.

The only things it cares about are motion, continuance, and the ability of thinking things to be free, and while it's generally not hostile, it will fight when it feels that oppression in significant amounts is evident, or if anything attempts to restrain it.

When we finally hit Foundation Ragnarok in whatever form that'll take, I see 3899 throwing in its lot with MEKHANE. Their goals are similar even if their implementation is wildly different, but when Yaldabaoth finally starts its big push, the Hauler will fire up and enact the only plan it's ever had, which is to [DATA EXPUNGED].

This is all fanon though, lol, which is why I didn't add it in. Not trying to exceed my grasp, just my interpretation. 3
