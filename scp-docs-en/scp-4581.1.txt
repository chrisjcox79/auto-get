SCP-4581: The 

03 Nov 2019 14:25

It's my first SCP! I've been reading the wiki for a long time and I'm thrilled to finally contribute. Many thanks go out to kinchtheknifeblade - for taking an early look, and for developing such a fun GoI!

A peek behind the curtain : A few months ago, I heard that someone had used copyrighted music to stymie a would-be propagandist, and I thought that was a brilliant nonviolent solution. Eventually, I realized that was the same kind of thing Gamers Against Weed would appreciate, and I started thinking about delivery media. I've always loved the audacious visual design of In God We Trust, Inc., and this seemed like an obvious match emerged from the ensuing back-and-forth.

There are a few deliberate themes in this piece: the omnipresence of the observer effect, culture clash, the fuzzy definition of  just a bit too well.

There's a few niche references scattered through this piece (the Tipper Gore joke being most obvious). I wonder if people will spot them?

Attributions : The cassette image is composited from three different CC assets:

Base: Ubcule's retouched compact cassette (CC 0);
Detail: Thegreenj's compact cassette (CC 2.5/ CC 3);
Logo: Tankist-777's anti-racist flag (CC 0).

I used alexadam's img-encode program to write the  joke onto an audio spectrogram.

Also, credit where it's due: the  alias is inspired by one of @mcclure111's jokes on Twitter.

Update : SCP-4581 now has an accompanying Tale, set in the immediate aftermath of steakshift's interrogation! I hope you enjoy stoners riffing on movie footage, and also deep emotional turmoil:
