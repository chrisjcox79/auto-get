23 Mar 2017 06:33

Usually I'm very keen on warship SCPs, but I'm pretty meh on this.

The anomalous abilities are that it's a sunken ship that remanifests, is super difficult to sink, and gets violent if you hail it. Plus the weather gets bad and there's a spooky glow. Also it says random slightly spooky stuff in Dutch that doesn't actually hint at a deeper or more interesting story.

That's about as generic as you can get for a sunken warship story. We've got at least two other sunken warships kicking around (SCP-1264) with a lot of the same imagery with vastly more interesting stuff going on.

The story about the attack and the pirate ship was rather cool and well-executed, but it doesn't make up for the rest of this being so bland.

-1
