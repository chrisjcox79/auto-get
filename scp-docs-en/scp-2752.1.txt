Thanks, and Other Matters

27 Feb 2016 01:26

Allow me to express my thanks to the people who reviewed it: Mistbourne, LordStonefish, Tuomey, Moohab etc.

And to address other matters in the article:

+block

–block

The 'Toʊ' in 'Robinson Toʊ Liz' is a rough phonetic estimation of the Japanese particle と (roughly meaning 'and'). Earlier drafts have it as 'and', but I decided I want to foreshadow it.

And yes, it is a Robinson list.

The last part (with the stamps) is a reference to the hyper-inflated banana money that Imperial Japan issued to Occupied Malaya in WWII (the regime is named Malai Baru). I will note that this article is set in the 70s, where the Japanese economic miracle was still a thing and Japanese money matters.
