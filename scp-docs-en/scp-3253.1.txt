I really like the idea, and the story of these two D-Class getting classified and abused by their researcher, who then reprimanded by the Foundation, it's neat. The execution could be better, there's a few extraneous details that don't need to be there, like the exact length of time for each pause in the interviews. Also, them being secret lovers really comes out of nowhere. I'm not opposed to the idea, I actually kinda like that this SCP may be fueled by the power of love, but the only indication before is that A is allowed visitation rights with B, and then it's just kinda thrown out in the interview. There were some references to it in the testing logs, so maybe with some rearranging it could be foreshadowed better.

I'll give it an upvote for being enjoyable, but it could be improved.
