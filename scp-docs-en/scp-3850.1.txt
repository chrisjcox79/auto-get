15 May 2017 21:04

You're a floating dead boy

with nothing left to destroy

just the water in your lungs

wished there by the ones you love
附加记录：    

A short exercise to keep my SCP-writing skills limber. Conceived within ten minutes, written up within three hours. Many thanks to DrBleep for review.

Extra special thanks to being the moon of my life his suggestions on improving the meat of the story, without which this would not be nearly as interesting.
