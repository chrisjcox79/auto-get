Fold

08 Jun 2015 19:15

Thank you for pointing those things out!

The reason for including 74 hours was that (to my knowledge) that is the maximum amount of time a human being can survive without water. It was included to show that the effect doesn't extend the lives of victims in any way – it doesn't need the affected people to remain alive. Reading back over it, though, it does look a little awkward. It's been updated.

The outbursts continue until the  is neutralized, either by exposure to the statue, or by being knocked unconscious or killed. That's been updated.

The reason no testing is allowed requires a little explanation.

Theis...

Thethis:

There is no way known by the Foundation to end the effect of the statue. Removing the victims from its line of sight will just make them attack their captors until they exhaust themselves (as in, literally are incapable of movement), then begin again – or return to the statue – when they regain enough energy; this cycle would continue until they died of natural causes or were terminated.

Presumably, the Foundation tried everything they could think of, but the testing was proving fruitless and was eating up D-class, so they just decided no testing was allowed, since if no one saw it everything was okay.

There is a way to end the effect in all surviving victims, which the Foundation is unaware of: destroying the statue. This would most likely have been brought up, but dismissed since if the statue was destroyed (or rather, sent to another dimension or vaporized, as pieces of it still retain its anomalous qualities when separated), no further research could be done on it.

This left the Foundation with a conundrum: there could be no more testing until a cure was found, and (though it was the only possibility left any personnel could think of) destroying the statue might not cure anyone either, and leave them unable to test it anyway.

What option did this leave?

Don't test it, just contain it and call the whole thing square.

So, yeah. Does that make sense? (Also, does it work?)

Thank you for your recommendations!
