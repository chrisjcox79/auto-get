Fold

18 Oct 2011 14:22

Fantastic. I thought it was going to be a boring non-linear geometry SCP, but then the last two test logs grabbed my attention. The possibilities are intriguing: this could be a door to parallel universes separate from each other by a few degrees; the farther you go, the farther you get from your own version of reality. Seeing altered versions of ones self raises existential questions, and the suggestion that we don't get the same person back after we send them through, but a slightly altered one, is very creepy (but I have a phobia of doppelgangers anyways).

All in all, I really enjoyed this SCP. Upvoted.
